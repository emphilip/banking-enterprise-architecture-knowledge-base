# Loan Origination Workflow

**Definition.** Loan Origination Workflow is the process spanning loan application capture, data collection, credit assessment, decisioning, and offer issuance through to loan booking.
**Also known as:** Loan Application Process.

## Relationships
- Loan Origination Workflow is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Origination Workflow depends on the Loan Origination capability.
- Loan Origination Workflow depends on the Credit Decisioning capability.
- Loan Origination Workflow causes Credit Underwriting.

## Details
Loan Origination Workflow gathers applicant and financial data, validates documents, routes the application through credit decisioning, and produces a structured offer for acceptance and booking. Actors include applicants, loan officers, and credit analysts. Systems involved include a loan origination platform, credit decisioning engine, and document processing.
