# Routing & Execution

**Definition.** Routing & Execution routes the order to venues or brokers and executes on best terms, capturing fills.
**Also known as:** Execution, Best Execution.

## Relationships
- Routing & Execution is derived from Trade Order Management.
- Routing & Execution is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Routing & Execution depends on the Brokerage & Trading capability.

## Details
Routing & Execution selects venues for the validated order, executes on best terms using market data, and captures execution fills. Controls include best execution, venue selection, and execution monitoring.
