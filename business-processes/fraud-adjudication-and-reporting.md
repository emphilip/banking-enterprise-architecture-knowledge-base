# Fraud Adjudication & Reporting

**Definition.** Fraud Adjudication & Reporting adjudicates the fraud claim, applies liability and refers for SAR/regulatory reporting.
**Also known as:** Adjudication & Reporting, Fraud Disposition.

## Relationships
- Fraud Adjudication & Reporting is derived from Card Fraud Handling.
- Fraud Adjudication & Reporting is defined in the Cards domain.
- Fraud Adjudication & Reporting depends on the Fraud Management capability.

## Details
Fraud Adjudication & Reporting adjudicates the fraud claim, applies Reg E unauthorized-EFT liability, and refers confirmed fraud for SAR consideration and regulatory reporting with a full audit trail. Actors include the Card Fraud Analyst.
