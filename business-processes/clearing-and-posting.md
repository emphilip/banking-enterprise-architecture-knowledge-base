# Clearing & Posting

**Definition.** Clearing & Posting matches the cleared presentment to the authorization and posts the transaction to the cardholder account.
**Also known as:** Presentment Posting, Clearing Match.

## Relationships
- Clearing & Posting is derived from Card Transaction Authorization.
- Clearing & Posting is defined in the Cards domain.
- Clearing & Posting depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
Clearing & Posting matches the cleared presentment to the original authorization hold, applies interchange and posts the transaction to the cardholder account with posting accuracy controls. Actors include the Card Operations Analyst.
