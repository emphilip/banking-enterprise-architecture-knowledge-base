# Risk & Affordability Assessment

**Definition.** Risk & Affordability Assessment evaluates the borrower against the 5 Cs, scores credit risk and tests affordability and debt-service capacity.
**Also known as:** 5 Cs Assessment, Affordability Review.

## Relationships
- Risk & Affordability Assessment is derived from Credit Underwriting.
- Risk & Affordability Assessment is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Risk & Affordability Assessment depends on the Credit Decisioning capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Credit Underwriter. Inputs are the decision-ready file; outputs are an underwriting assessment. Controls include credit policy, affordability (DTI/DSR) testing and scorecard governance.
