# Remediation & Attestation

**Definition.** Remediation & Attestation implements remediation and control changes for the obligation and attests that the change is embedded.
**Also known as:** Remediation & Sign-Off, Change Attestation.

## Relationships
- Remediation & Attestation is derived from Regulatory Change Management.
- Remediation & Attestation is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Remediation & Attestation depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
Remediation & Attestation consumes the impact assessment and remediation plan and produces implemented controls and an attestation record. Controls include remediation tracking, control implementation, attestation and audit traceability. The lead actor is the Regulatory Change Manager.
