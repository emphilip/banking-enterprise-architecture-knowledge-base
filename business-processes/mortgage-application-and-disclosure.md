# Mortgage Application & Disclosure

**Definition.** Mortgage Application & Disclosure takes the URLA/1003 application and issues TRID disclosures within regulatory timelines.
**Also known as:** Mortgage Intake, TRID Disclosure.

## Relationships
- Mortgage Application & Disclosure is derived from Mortgage Origination.
- Mortgage Application & Disclosure is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Mortgage Application & Disclosure depends on the Loan Origination capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Mortgage Loan Originator. Inputs are the Mortgage Application; outputs are a disclosed mortgage file. Controls include the TRID Loan Estimate (3 business days), URLA completeness and fee tolerances.
