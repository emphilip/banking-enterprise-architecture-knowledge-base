# Investor Profiling & Suitability

**Definition.** Investor Profiling & Suitability captures investor data, runs KYC for investors and assesses risk tolerance, capacity and suitability before opening the wealth relationship.
**Also known as:** Investor Profiling, Suitability Onboarding.

## Relationships
- Investor Profiling & Suitability is derived from Wealth Onboarding.
- Investor Profiling & Suitability is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Investor Profiling & Suitability depends on the Investment Advisory capability.

## Details
Investor Profiling & Suitability captures investor data and a KYC Profile, builds the Risk Profile, and determines a suitability outcome under MiFID II / Reg BI before the relationship is opened. Controls include KYC for investors, MiFID II/Reg BI suitability, and risk-profiling control.
