# Provisional Credit & Resolution

**Definition.** Provisional Credit & Resolution issues provisional credit, adjudicates the outcome and finalizes credit/debit within regulatory deadlines.
**Also known as:** Resolution & Credit, Dispute Closure.

## Relationships
- Provisional Credit & Resolution is derived from Dispute Resolution.
- Provisional Credit & Resolution is defined in the Cards domain.
- Provisional Credit & Resolution depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
Provisional Credit & Resolution issues provisional credit under the Reg E 10-business-day rule, adjudicates the dispute within the 45/90-day investigation deadline, and finalizes credit/debit with notice to the cardholder. Actors include the Dispute Resolution Specialist.
