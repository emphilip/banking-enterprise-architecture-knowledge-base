# Alert Generation & Routing

**Definition.** Alert Generation & Routing generates, deduplicates and routes alerts to queues and case management based on severity and typology.
**Also known as:** Alert Routing, Alert Orchestration.

## Relationships
- Alert Generation & Routing is derived from Fraud Detection.
- Alert Generation & Routing is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Alert Generation & Routing depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Alert Generation & Routing consumes a risk score and alert thresholds and produces a routed Fraud Alert. Controls include threshold governance, alert prioritization and routing rules.
