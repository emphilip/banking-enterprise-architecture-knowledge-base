# Statement Delivery

**Definition.** Statement Delivery is the rendering, delivery by print or e-statement, and archival of statements per the customer's delivery preference.
**Also known as:** Statement Distribution, E-Statement Delivery.

## Relationships
- Statement Delivery is derived from Statement Generation.
- Statement Delivery is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Statement Delivery depends on the Notification Services capability.

## Details
Statement Delivery takes the assembled statement and delivery preferences and produces a delivered Bank Account Statement. Controls include e-delivery consent and archival and retention.
