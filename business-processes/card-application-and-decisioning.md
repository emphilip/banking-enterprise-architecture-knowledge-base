# Card Application & Decisioning

**Definition.** Card Application & Decisioning captures the card application, runs KYC and credit/eligibility decisioning and sets the card account and credit limit.
**Also known as:** Card App & Decision, Issuance Decisioning.

## Relationships
- Card Application & Decisioning is derived from Card Issuance.
- Card Application & Decisioning is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Application & Decisioning depends on the Card Issuing capability.

## Details
Card Application & Decisioning takes the Card Account Application, applies KYC checks and credit/eligibility policy, and assigns the credit limit before establishing the card account. Actors include the Card Issuance Officer.
