# Cash Management

**Definition.** Cash Management is the process of managing corporate liquidity, collections, disbursements, and pooling to optimize working capital and funding positions.
**Also known as:** Corporate Cash Management.

## Relationships
- Cash Management is defined in the Payments domain.
- Cash Management depends on the Payment Execution capability.
- Cash Management depends on the Liquidity Management capability.

## Details
Cash Management aggregates account positions, forecasts liquidity, executes collections and payments, and applies pooling and sweeping to optimize balances. Actors include corporate treasurers, cash management product teams, and bank operations staff. Systems involved include a payment orchestration hub, liquidity management tooling, and the core banking ledger.
