# Closing & Mortgage Funding

**Definition.** Closing & Mortgage Funding reaches clear-to-close, delivers the Closing Disclosure and funds the mortgage.
**Also known as:** Closing & Funding, CTC.

## Relationships
- Closing & Mortgage Funding is derived from Mortgage Origination.
- Closing & Mortgage Funding is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Closing & Mortgage Funding depends on the Loan Origination capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Mortgage Closer. Inputs are the clear-to-close decision; outputs are a funded mortgage. Controls include the Closing Disclosure (3 business days), the clear-to-close gate and signing controls.
