# Ongoing KYC Monitoring

**Definition.** Ongoing KYC Monitoring refreshes KYC data on a risk-based cycle and re-screens and re-rates on trigger events to keep profiles current.
**Also known as:** Ongoing Monitoring, KYC Refresh.

## Relationships
- Ongoing KYC Monitoring is derived from KYC Verification.
- Ongoing KYC Monitoring is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Ongoing KYC Monitoring depends on the KYC Management capability.

## Details
Ongoing KYC Monitoring consumes the KYC Profile and trigger events and produces a refreshed KYC Profile and review outcome. Controls include risk-based review cycles, trigger-based refresh, perpetual KYC and re-screening. The lead actor is the KYC Analyst.
