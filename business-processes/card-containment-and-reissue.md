# Card Containment & Reissue

**Definition.** Card Containment & Reissue verifies the cardholder, blocks the compromised card and instructs reissue/re-credential.
**Also known as:** Containment & Reissue, Block & Reissue.

## Relationships
- Card Containment & Reissue is derived from Card Fraud Handling.
- Card Containment & Reissue is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Containment & Reissue depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
Card Containment & Reissue verifies the cardholder, blocks the compromised card to stop further authorizations, and instructs secure reissue and re-credentialing with token re-provisioning. Actors include the Card Fraud Analyst.
