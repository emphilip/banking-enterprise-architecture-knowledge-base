# Scenario & Capital Planning

**Definition.** Scenario & Capital Planning projects capital under baseline and adverse scenarios and assembles the capital plan for CCAR board approval and submission.
**Also known as:** CCAR Planning, Capital Plan Assembly.

## Relationships
- Scenario & Capital Planning is derived from Regulatory Capital Reporting.
- Scenario & Capital Planning is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Scenario & Capital Planning depends on the Regulatory Capital Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Regulatory Reporting Specialist. Inputs are capital projections, scenarios and RWA; outputs are the capital plan and FR Y-14 schedules. Controls include scenario governance, board sign-off and CCAR submission.
