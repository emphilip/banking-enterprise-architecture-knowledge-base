# Limit Monitoring & Escalation

**Definition.** Limit Monitoring & Escalation monitors exposures against limits, detects breaches and escalates them through governance.
**Also known as:** Limit Tracking, Breach Escalation.

## Relationships
- Limit Monitoring & Escalation is derived from Risk Reporting.
- Limit Monitoring & Escalation is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Limit Monitoring & Escalation depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Limit Monitoring & Escalation consumes the aggregated risk dataset and risk limits and produces limit-breach escalations. Controls include limit-breach detection, escalation thresholds and governance routing.
