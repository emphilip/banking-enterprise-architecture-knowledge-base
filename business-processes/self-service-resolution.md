# Self-Service Resolution

**Definition.** Self-Service Resolution is the sub-process that resolves self-service requests in-channel and escalates to assisted servicing where needed.
**Also known as:** Self-Service Handling, In-Channel Servicing.

## Relationships
- Self-Service Resolution is derived from Channel Servicing.
- Self-Service Resolution is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Self-Service Resolution depends on the Channel Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Channel Operations Analyst. Inputs are the authenticated session and the request; outputs are a resolved request or an escalation. Controls include escalation handoff control and accessibility.
