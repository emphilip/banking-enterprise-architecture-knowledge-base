# Channel Authentication & Orchestration

**Definition.** Channel Authentication & Orchestration is the sub-process that opens and authenticates a channel session and orchestrates context across channels.
**Also known as:** Session Orchestration, Channel Auth.

## Relationships
- Channel Authentication & Orchestration is derived from Channel Servicing.
- Channel Authentication & Orchestration is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Channel Authentication & Orchestration depends on the Customer Authentication capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Channel Operations Analyst. Inputs are the Channel Session; outputs are an authenticated session. Controls include SCA / step-up authentication and cross-channel context integrity.
