# Dormancy & Escheatment

**Definition.** Dormancy & Escheatment is the identification of dormant accounts, the issuance of a dormancy notice, and the escheatment of unclaimed funds to the state.
**Also known as:** Escheatment, Abandoned Property Handling.

## Relationships
- Dormancy & Escheatment is derived from Account Closure.
- Dormancy & Escheatment is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Dormancy & Escheatment depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
Dormancy & Escheatment takes a dormancy report and produces escheated funds and an escheatment report. Controls include dormancy rules, escheatment and CASS, and state filing deadlines.
