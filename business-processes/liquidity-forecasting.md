# Liquidity Forecasting

**Definition.** Liquidity Forecasting projects future cash needs and surpluses from positions, flows and obligations.
**Also known as:** Cash Forecasting, Liquidity Planning.

## Relationships
- Liquidity Forecasting is derived from Cash Management.
- Liquidity Forecasting is defined in the Payments domain.
- Liquidity Forecasting depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Treasury Cash Manager. Inputs are the cash position; outputs are the Cash Forecast. Controls include forecast variance review against actuals.
