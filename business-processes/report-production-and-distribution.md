# Report Production & Distribution

**Definition.** Report Production & Distribution produces board and committee risk reports and regulatory returns and distributes them to stakeholders.
**Also known as:** Report Production, Reporting & Distribution.

## Relationships
- Report Production & Distribution is derived from Risk Reporting.
- Report Production & Distribution is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Report Production & Distribution depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.

## Details
Report Production & Distribution consumes the aggregated risk dataset and produces the Risk Report and regulatory returns. Controls include report completeness under BCBS 239 Principle 8, board and committee reporting and regulatory submission.
