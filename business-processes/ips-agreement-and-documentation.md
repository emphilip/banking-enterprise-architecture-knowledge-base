# IPS Agreement & Documentation

**Definition.** IPS Agreement & Documentation produces, presents and agrees the Investment Policy Statement and delivers the required disclosures and suitability report.
**Also known as:** IPS Agreement, Advice Documentation.

## Relationships
- IPS Agreement & Documentation is derived from Financial Planning.
- IPS Agreement & Documentation is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- IPS Agreement & Documentation depends on the Investment Advisory capability.

## Details
IPS Agreement & Documentation takes the recommended plan and suitability report, produces and presents the Investment Policy Statement, and obtains the signed IPS with disclosures delivered. Controls include IPS governance, disclosure delivery, and client acknowledgement.
