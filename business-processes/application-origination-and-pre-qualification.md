# Application Origination & Pre-Qualification

**Definition.** Application Origination & Pre-Qualification captures the loan application, runs a soft pre-qualification and assembles supporting documents before processing.
**Also known as:** Origination Pre-Qualification, Pre-Qual.

## Relationships
- Application Origination & Pre-Qualification is derived from Loan Origination Workflow.
- Application Origination & Pre-Qualification is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Application Origination & Pre-Qualification depends on the Loan Origination capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Loan Officer. Inputs are the Loan Application; outputs are a pre-qualified application ready for processing. Controls include soft credit pull consent, KYC capture and data-quality checks before the file progresses.
