# Match Generation & Triage

**Definition.** Match Generation & Triage generates watchlist alerts from name and transaction matching and performs L1/L2 triage of potential matches.
**Also known as:** Alert Generation & Triage, Screening Triage.

## Relationships
- Match Generation & Triage is derived from Sanctions Screening Operations.
- Match Generation & Triage is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Match Generation & Triage depends on the Sanctions Screening capability.

## Details
Match Generation & Triage consumes party and transaction data and sanctions lists and produces triaged alerts. Controls include matching thresholds, three-tier triage (clear/review/block) and alert prioritisation. The lead actor is the Compliance Screening Officer.
