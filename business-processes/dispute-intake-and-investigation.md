# Dispute Intake & Investigation

**Definition.** Dispute Intake & Investigation registers the cardholder dispute, validates Reg Z/Reg E rights, gathers evidence and investigates the claim.
**Also known as:** Dispute Intake.

## Relationships
- Dispute Intake & Investigation is derived from Dispute Resolution.
- Dispute Intake & Investigation is defined in the Cards domain.
- Dispute Intake & Investigation depends on the Dispute Management capability.

## Details
Dispute Intake & Investigation logs the cardholder claim against a Card Statement transaction, confirms the claim falls within Reg Z 60-day billing-error and Reg E error-resolution windows, and conducts a reasonable investigation gathering the evidence package. Actors include the Dispute Resolution Specialist.
