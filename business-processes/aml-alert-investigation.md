# AML Alert Investigation

**Definition.** AML Alert Investigation triages and investigates transaction-monitoring alerts into cases, analysing transactions and source of funds.
**Also known as:** Alert Investigation, AML Case Work.

## Relationships
- AML Alert Investigation is derived from Suspicious Activity Reporting.
- AML Alert Investigation is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- AML Alert Investigation depends on the Transaction Monitoring capability.

## Details
AML Alert Investigation consumes AML alerts and transaction history and produces an investigation case and findings. Controls include alert triage, transaction analysis, source-of-funds checks and evidence standards. The lead actor is the AML Investigator.
