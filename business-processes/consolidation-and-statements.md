# Consolidation & Statements

**Definition.** Consolidation & Statements eliminates intercompany balances, runs consolidation across entities and prepares the consolidated financial statements under IFRS.
**Also known as:** Group Consolidation, Statement Preparation.

## Relationships
- Consolidation & Statements is derived from Financial Close.
- Consolidation & Statements is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Consolidation & Statements depends on the Financial Reporting capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Group Accountant. Inputs are entity trial balances and intercompany matches; outputs are the Consolidated Financial Statements. Controls include intercompany elimination, IFRS 10 consolidation and controller sign-off.
