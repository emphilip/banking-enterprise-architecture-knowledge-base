# Offer & Disbursement

**Definition.** Offer & Disbursement issues the credit offer, executes the agreement and disburses funds to the borrower.
**Also known as:** Offer & Funding, Disbursement.

## Relationships
- Offer & Disbursement is derived from Loan Origination Workflow.
- Offer & Disbursement is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Offer & Disbursement depends on the Loan Origination capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Loan Officer. Inputs are the Credit Decision; outputs are a disbursed loan. Controls include regulatory disclosures, four-eyes on disbursement and execution of the loan agreement.
