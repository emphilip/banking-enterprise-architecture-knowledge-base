# Early-Stage Collections

**Definition.** Early-Stage Collections detects delinquency, contacts the borrower and agrees a promise-to-pay in the early ageing buckets.
**Also known as:** Early Collections, Soft Collections.

## Relationships
- Early-Stage Collections is derived from Loan Collections.
- Early-Stage Collections is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Early-Stage Collections depends on the Collections & Recovery capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Collections Agent. Inputs are a delinquent account; outputs are a cured or arranged account. Controls include FDCPA-aligned contact rules, vulnerable-customer treatment and promise-to-pay logging.
