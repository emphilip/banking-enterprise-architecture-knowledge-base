# Period-End Adjustments

**Definition.** Period-End Adjustments identifies and posts recurring and one-off period-end adjusting and accrual journals before reconciliation.
**Also known as:** Accruals & Adjustments, Period-End Journals.

## Relationships
- Period-End Adjustments is derived from Financial Close.
- Period-End Adjustments is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Period-End Adjustments depends on the General Ledger Accounting capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the General Ledger Accountant. Inputs are accruals, prepaids and depreciation schedules; outputs are posted adjusting journals. Controls include journal approval and segregation of duties and period-end cut-off.
