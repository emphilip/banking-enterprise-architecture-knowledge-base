# Risk Capture & Registration

**Definition.** Risk Capture & Registration identifies and registers risks across categories from activities, loss events and emerging-risk scans.
**Also known as:** Risk Capture, RCSA Identification.

## Relationships
- Risk Capture & Registration is derived from Risk Identification & Assessment.
- Risk Capture & Registration is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Risk Capture & Registration depends on the Operational Risk Management capability.

## Details
Risk Capture & Registration consumes business activities and loss events and produces identified risks and Risk Register entries. Controls include COSO identification, taxonomy completeness and emerging-risk scanning.
