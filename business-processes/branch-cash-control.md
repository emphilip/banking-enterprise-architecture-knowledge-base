# Branch Cash Control

**Definition.** Branch Cash Control is the sub-process covering branch opening and closing cash controls, teller drawer and vault custody, and end-of-day balancing.
**Also known as:** Vault & Drawer Control, Branch Cash Balancing.

## Relationships
- Branch Cash Control is derived from Branch Operations.
- Branch Cash Control is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Branch Cash Control depends on the Branch Banking capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Branch Manager. Inputs are drawer and vault cash and system balances; outputs are a balanced branch and a reconciliation report. Controls include dual custody of the vault, drawer limits, and end-of-day reconciliation.
