# Statement Assembly

**Definition.** Statement Assembly is the determination of the statement cycle and the extraction of postings to assemble statement content with the required disclosures.
**Also known as:** Statement Build, Cycle Assembly.

## Relationships
- Statement Assembly is derived from Statement Generation.
- Statement Assembly is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Statement Assembly depends on the Account Servicing capability.

## Details
Statement Assembly takes the transaction ledger and cycle calendar and produces assembled statement content. Controls include Reg DD and Reg E periodic content and cycle consistency within four days.
