# Card Production & Fulfillment

**Definition.** Card Production & Fulfillment generates the PAN/credential, personalizes the card and fulfills (physical mail or virtual/token) the card to the customer.
**Also known as:** Production & Fulfillment, Card Personalization.

## Relationships
- Card Production & Fulfillment is derived from Card Issuance.
- Card Production & Fulfillment is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Production & Fulfillment depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
Card Production & Fulfillment generates the Card Credential, personalizes the EMV chip and card body, and fulfills the card by secure mail or virtual/token delivery, protecting the PAN under PCI DSS. Actors include the Card Issuance Officer.
