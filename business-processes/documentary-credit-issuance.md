# Documentary Credit Issuance

**Definition.** Documentary Credit Issuance takes the applicant's LC request, assesses credit and limits and issues and advises the documentary or standby credit.
**Also known as:** LC Issuance, Credit Issuance.

## Relationships
- Documentary Credit Issuance is derived from Trade Finance Processing.
- Documentary Credit Issuance is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Documentary Credit Issuance depends on the Collateral Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Trade Finance Officer. Inputs are the Letter Of Credit application; outputs are an issued Letter Of Credit. Controls include the credit-limit check, UCP 600 / ISP98 drafting and sanctions screening.
