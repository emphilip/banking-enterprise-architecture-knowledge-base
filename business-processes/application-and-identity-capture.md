# Application & Identity Capture

**Definition.** Application & Identity Capture is the capture of the account application, customer identifying data and consent, and the CIP identity verification at account opening.
**Also known as:** Opening Intake, CIP Capture.

## Relationships
- Application & Identity Capture is derived from Deposit Account Opening.
- Application & Identity Capture is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Application & Identity Capture depends on the Account Opening capability.

## Details
Application & Identity Capture takes the Account Opening Form and identifying documents from the Onboarding Specialist and produces verified applicant data. Controls include the Customer Identification Program, KYC, and TIN collection.
