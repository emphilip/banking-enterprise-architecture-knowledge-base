# Rate Application & Attribution

**Definition.** Rate Application & Attribution applies transfer rates to assets and liabilities and attributes net interest margin to business units.
**Also known as:** FTP Application, Margin Attribution.

## Relationships
- Rate Application & Attribution is derived from Funds Transfer Pricing.
- Rate Application & Attribution is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Rate Application & Attribution depends on the Asset Liability Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the ALM Analyst. Inputs are the FTP Curve and balance-sheet positions; outputs are transfer-priced margins and NIM attribution. Controls include margin-attribution reconciliation and premium-range limits.
