# Exception Investigation

**Definition.** Exception Investigation investigates, classifies and resolves reconciliation breaks and settlement failures.
**Also known as:** Break Resolution, Investigations.

## Relationships
- Exception Investigation is derived from Reconciliation & Settlement.
- Exception Investigation is defined in the Payments domain.
- Exception Investigation depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Reconciliation Analyst. Inputs are reconciliation breaks; outputs are resolved breaks with disposition. Controls include break ageing thresholds and an escalation workflow for unresolved items.
