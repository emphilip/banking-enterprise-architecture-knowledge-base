# Funding & Buffer Management

**Definition.** Funding & Buffer Management monitors LCR/NSFR buffers against limits and decides and executes funding and placement actions.
**Also known as:** Buffer Management, Funding Execution Sub-Process.

## Relationships
- Funding & Buffer Management is derived from Cash & Liquidity Management.
- Funding & Buffer Management is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Funding & Buffer Management depends on the Liquidity Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Treasury Analyst. Inputs are the liquidity position and regulatory limits; outputs are funding decisions and executed funding. Controls include LCR/NSFR limit governance, funding-authority limits and treasurer escalation.
