# Overdraft Fee & Notice

**Definition.** Overdraft Fee & Notice is the application of the applicable overdraft or NSF fee and the notification of the customer with the required disclosures.
**Also known as:** Fee Application, OD Notice.

## Relationships
- Overdraft Fee & Notice is derived from Overdraft Servicing.
- Overdraft Fee & Notice is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Fee & Notice depends on the Notification Services capability.

## Details
Overdraft Fee & Notice takes a pay-or-return decision and produces an Overdraft Fee and a customer notice. Controls include fee disclosure and the distinction between NSF and overdraft fee rules.
