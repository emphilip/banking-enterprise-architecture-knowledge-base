# Late-Stage Recovery

**Definition.** Late-Stage Recovery escalates persistent arrears to restructure, charge-off and post-charge-off recovery or agency placement.
**Also known as:** Late Collections, Hard Collections.

## Relationships
- Late-Stage Recovery is derived from Loan Collections.
- Late-Stage Recovery is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Late-Stage Recovery depends on the Collections & Recovery capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Recovery Officer. Inputs are a cured or arranged account that has re-defaulted; outputs are a recovery outcome. Controls include charge-off policy, settlement authority and collateral/repossession controls.
