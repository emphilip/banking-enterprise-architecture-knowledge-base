# Appetite Approval & Cascade

**Definition.** Appetite Approval & Cascade obtains board and CRO approval of the Risk Appetite Statement and cascades limits into business plans and monitoring.
**Also known as:** Approval & Cascade, RAS Approval.

## Relationships
- Appetite Approval & Cascade is derived from Risk Appetite Setting.
- Appetite Approval & Cascade is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Appetite Approval & Cascade depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Appetite Approval & Cascade consumes the draft Risk Appetite Statement and produces an approved Risk Appetite Statement with embedded limits. Controls include board approval, three-lines ownership and embedding into business plans.
