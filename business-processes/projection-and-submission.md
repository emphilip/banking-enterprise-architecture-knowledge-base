# Projection & Submission

**Definition.** Projection & Submission runs models to project capital and losses, obtains board sign-off and submits the capital plan to regulators.
**Also known as:** Projection & Filing, Capital Submission.

## Relationships
- Projection & Submission is derived from Stress Testing.
- Projection & Submission is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Projection & Submission depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.

## Details
Projection & Submission consumes the Stress Scenario and portfolio data and produces capital projections, the Stress Test Result and a regulatory submission. Controls include board sign-off, CCAR/DFAST submission and result documentation.
