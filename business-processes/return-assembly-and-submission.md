# Return Assembly & Submission

**Definition.** Return Assembly & Submission assembles capital-adequacy returns to the regulator taxonomy, validates, obtains board attestation and submits (COREP/FINREP, FR Y-14/CCAR).
**Also known as:** COREP Submission, Capital Return Filing.

## Relationships
- Return Assembly & Submission is derived from Regulatory Capital Reporting.
- Return Assembly & Submission is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Return Assembly & Submission depends on the Regulatory Capital Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Regulatory Reporting Specialist. Inputs are risk-weighted assets, own funds and taxonomy rules; outputs are the Capital Adequacy Return and the regulatory submission. Controls include taxonomy validation, FR Y-14 attestation and board/CFO sign-off.
