# Curve Construction & Governance

**Definition.** Curve Construction & Governance builds the matched-maturity transfer-pricing curve with liquidity-premium add-ons and governs methodology and curves through ALCO.
**Also known as:** FTP Curve Build, Curve Governance.

## Relationships
- Curve Construction & Governance is derived from Funds Transfer Pricing.
- Curve Construction & Governance is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Curve Construction & Governance depends on the Asset Liability Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the ALM Analyst. Inputs are market funding curves and behavioural assumptions; outputs are the FTP Curve. Controls include matched-maturity methodology governance and FTP curve governance with ALCO approval.
