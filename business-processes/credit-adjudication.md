# Credit Adjudication

**Definition.** Credit Adjudication renders the approve, decline or refer decision, sets terms and conditions and applies delegated authority.
**Also known as:** Adjudication, Credit Decisioning Sub-Process.

## Relationships
- Credit Adjudication is derived from Credit Underwriting.
- Credit Adjudication is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Credit Adjudication depends on the Credit Decisioning capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Credit Underwriter. Inputs are the underwriting assessment; outputs are a Credit Decision. Controls include delegated authority limits, four-eyes review and condition setting.
