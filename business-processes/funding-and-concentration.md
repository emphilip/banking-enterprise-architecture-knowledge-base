# Funding & Concentration

**Definition.** Funding & Concentration executes sweeps, pooling and funding transfers to balance accounts to target.
**Also known as:** Cash Concentration, Sweeping & Pooling.

## Relationships
- Funding & Concentration is derived from Cash Management.
- Funding & Concentration is defined in the Payments domain.
- Funding & Concentration depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Treasury Cash Manager. Inputs are the Cash Forecast; outputs are funding instructions executing sweeps and pooling. Controls include sweep parameters and approval limits.
