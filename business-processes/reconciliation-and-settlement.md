# Reconciliation & Settlement

**Definition.** Reconciliation & Settlement is the process of matching payment and ledger records, resolving exceptions, and finalizing the exchange of value across clearing and settlement systems.
**Also known as:** Recon & Settlement.

## Relationships
- Reconciliation & Settlement is defined in the Payments domain.
- Reconciliation & Settlement depends on the Payment Clearing & Settlement capability.

## Details
Reconciliation & Settlement ingests scheme and nostro statements, matches them against internal ledger entries, investigates breaks, and posts settlement positions. Actors include settlement operations and finance staff. Systems involved include a payment orchestration hub, the general ledger engine, and clearing connectivity.
