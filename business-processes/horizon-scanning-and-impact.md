# Horizon Scanning & Impact

**Definition.** Horizon Scanning & Impact scans regulatory feeds for emerging change, maps it to obligations and assesses business and operational impact.
**Also known as:** Horizon Scanning, Impact Assessment.

## Relationships
- Horizon Scanning & Impact is derived from Regulatory Change Management.
- Horizon Scanning & Impact is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Horizon Scanning & Impact depends on the Regulatory Compliance capability.

## Details
Horizon Scanning & Impact consumes regulatory feeds and the obligation library and produces a mapped Regulatory Obligation and impact assessment. Controls include horizon-scanning coverage, obligation mapping and multi-dimension impact assessment. The lead actor is the Regulatory Change Manager.
