# Interaction Handling & QA

**Definition.** Interaction Handling & QA is the sub-process covering agent handling and resolution of the interaction, wrap-up and quality-assurance review.
**Also known as:** Handling & QA, Resolution & Wrap-Up.

## Relationships
- Interaction Handling & QA is derived from Contact Centre Interaction.
- Interaction Handling & QA is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Interaction Handling & QA depends on the Contact Center capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Contact Centre Agent. Inputs are the routed interaction; outputs are a resolved interaction and a QA score. Controls include call recording and consent, QA scorecards, and AHT / FCR controls.
