# Complaint Intake & Triage

**Definition.** Complaint Intake & Triage logs an incoming complaint, acknowledges it and classifies and prioritises it for handling.
**Also known as:** Complaint Logging, Complaint Intake Triage.

## Relationships
- Complaint Intake & Triage is derived from Complaint Handling.
- Complaint Intake & Triage is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Intake & Triage depends on the Case Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Complaints Officer. Inputs are the Complaint Record; outputs are a triaged complaint ready for investigation. Controls include the acknowledgement SLA and an eligibility/jurisdiction check.
