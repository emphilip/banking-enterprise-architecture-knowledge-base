# Account Setup & Funding

**Definition.** Account Setup & Funding is the capture of the mandate and signature card, product setup, account creation and initial funding and activation.
**Also known as:** Open & Fund, Activation.

## Relationships
- Account Setup & Funding is derived from Deposit Account Opening.
- Account Setup & Funding is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Setup & Funding depends on the Account Opening capability.

## Details
Account Setup & Funding takes the Signature Card and product configuration and produces an active account. Controls include signatory verification and fee disclosure.
