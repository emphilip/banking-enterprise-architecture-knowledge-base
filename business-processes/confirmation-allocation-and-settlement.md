# Confirmation, Allocation & Settlement

**Definition.** Confirmation, Allocation & Settlement confirms and allocates fills across accounts and settles the trade on the T+1 cycle with custody reconciliation.
**Also known as:** Allocation & Settlement, Post-Trade.

## Relationships
- Confirmation, Allocation & Settlement is derived from Trade Order Management.
- Confirmation, Allocation & Settlement is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Confirmation, Allocation & Settlement depends on the Brokerage & Trading capability.

## Details
Confirmation, Allocation & Settlement issues the Trade Confirmation, allocates fills fairly across accounts, and settles the trade on the T+1 cycle with trade-vs-custody reconciliation. Controls include allocation fairness, T+1 settlement, and trade-vs-custody reconciliation.
