# Loan Collections

**Definition.** Loan Collections is the process of managing delinquent and defaulted loan accounts through outreach, repayment arrangements, hardship handling, and recovery actions.
**Also known as:** Collections Process.

## Relationships
- Loan Collections is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Collections depends on the Collections & Recovery capability.

## Details
Loan Collections identifies overdue accounts, prioritizes them by risk, contacts borrowers, negotiates repayment plans, and escalates to recovery or write-off where needed. Actors include collections agents, hardship teams, and recovery specialists. Systems involved include collections workflow tooling, the core banking ledger, and notification services.
