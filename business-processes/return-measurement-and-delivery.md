# Return Measurement & Delivery

**Definition.** Return Measurement & Delivery computes time-weighted returns versus benchmark with attribution and produces and delivers the Performance Report.
**Also known as:** Return Calc & Delivery, Reporting & Delivery.

## Relationships
- Return Measurement & Delivery is derived from Performance Reporting.
- Return Measurement & Delivery is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Return Measurement & Delivery depends on the Portfolio Management capability.

## Details
Return Measurement & Delivery computes time-weighted returns against the benchmark with attribution and produces and delivers the client Performance Report. Controls include GIPS TWR methodology, benchmark consistency, disclosure accuracy, and four-eyes sign-off.
