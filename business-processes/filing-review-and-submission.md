# Filing Review & Submission

**Definition.** Filing Review & Submission reviews compiled returns under four-eyes, obtains sign-off and submits them to the regulator within deadlines.
**Also known as:** Review & Submit, Filing Submission.

## Relationships
- Filing Review & Submission is derived from Regulatory Filing.
- Filing Review & Submission is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Filing Review & Submission depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.

## Details
Filing Review & Submission consumes the compiled return and produces the submitted return and acknowledgement. Controls include four-eyes review, deadline control such as the CTR 15-day deadline, e-filing and acknowledgement capture. The lead actor is the Regulatory Reporting Analyst.
