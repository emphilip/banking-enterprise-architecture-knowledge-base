# Investment Account Setup

**Definition.** Investment Account Setup opens the investment and custody account, executes the advisory mandate and funds the account ready for investing.
**Also known as:** Investment Account Opening, Mandate Setup.

## Relationships
- Investment Account Setup is derived from Wealth Onboarding.
- Investment Account Setup is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Investment Account Setup depends on the Portfolio Management capability.

## Details
Investment Account Setup takes the suitability outcome and account agreement, opens the custody account, executes the advisory mandate, and verifies funding so the account is ready to invest. Controls include mandate execution, custody account controls, and funding verification.
