# Appetite Definition & Limits

**Definition.** Appetite Definition & Limits defines risk capacity, drafts qualitative appetite statements and cascades them into quantitative limits and tolerances.
**Also known as:** Appetite & Limits, RAS Drafting.

## Relationships
- Appetite Definition & Limits is derived from Risk Appetite Setting.
- Appetite Definition & Limits is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Appetite Definition & Limits depends on the Enterprise Risk Management capability.

## Details
Appetite Definition & Limits consumes the strategy and business plan and risk capacity and produces a draft Risk Appetite Statement and risk limits. Controls include FSB RAF principles, capacity-versus-appetite alignment and the limit cascade.
