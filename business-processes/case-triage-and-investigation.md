# Case Triage & Investigation

**Definition.** Case Triage & Investigation triages alerts into cases, assembles context such as account history, and investigates to confirm or dismiss fraud.
**Also known as:** Triage & Investigation, Case Investigation.

## Relationships
- Case Triage & Investigation is derived from Fraud Investigation.
- Case Triage & Investigation is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Case Triage & Investigation depends on the Case Management capability.

## Details
Case Triage & Investigation consumes the Fraud Alert and account activity and produces the Fraud Case and investigation findings. Controls include triage decisioning, account-history review and evidence standards.
