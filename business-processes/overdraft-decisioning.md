# Overdraft Decisioning

**Definition.** Overdraft Decisioning is the detection of insufficient funds, the checking of opt-in and limit, and the decision to pay or return.
**Also known as:** OD Decisioning, Pay/Return Decision.

## Relationships
- Overdraft Decisioning is derived from Overdraft Servicing.
- Overdraft Decisioning is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Decisioning depends on the Overdraft Management capability.

## Details
Overdraft Decisioning takes a debit item and the available balance and produces a pay-or-return decision. Controls include Reg E opt-in and the overdraft limit.
