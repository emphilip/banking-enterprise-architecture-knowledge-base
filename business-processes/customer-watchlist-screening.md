# Customer Watchlist Screening

**Definition.** Customer Watchlist Screening screens the verified customer and related parties against sanctions, PEP and adverse-media lists at onboarding.
**Also known as:** Onboarding Screening, KYC Screening.

## Relationships
- Customer Watchlist Screening is derived from KYC Verification.
- Customer Watchlist Screening is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Customer Watchlist Screening depends on the Sanctions Screening capability.

## Details
Customer Watchlist Screening consumes the verified identity and current sanctions lists and produces screening results and potential matches. Controls include sanctions/PEP/adverse-media screening, fuzzy matching and list currency. The lead actor is the KYC Analyst.
