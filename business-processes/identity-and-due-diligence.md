# Identity & Due Diligence

**Definition.** Identity & Due Diligence proves applicant identity (CIP) and performs CDD/EDD and screening to assign a risk rating.
**Also known as:** KYC & CDD, Verification & Diligence.

## Relationships
- Identity & Due Diligence is derived from Customer Onboarding.
- Identity & Due Diligence is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Identity & Due Diligence depends on the Identity Verification capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the KYC Analyst. Inputs are the captured application; outputs are a cleared onboarding case ready for setup. Controls include identity proofing, CDD/EDD and sanctions/PEP screening.
