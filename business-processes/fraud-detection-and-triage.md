# Fraud Detection & Triage

**Definition.** Fraud Detection & Triage generates and triages fraud alerts from scoring/velocity rules and prioritizes cases for action.
**Also known as:** Detection & Triage, Alert Triage.

## Relationships
- Fraud Detection & Triage is derived from Card Fraud Handling.
- Fraud Detection & Triage is defined in the Cards domain.
- Fraud Detection & Triage depends on the Fraud Management capability.

## Details
Fraud Detection & Triage generates a Fraud Alert from real-time scoring and velocity rules, then triages and prioritizes cases for action against alert-prioritization controls. Actors include the Card Fraud Analyst.
