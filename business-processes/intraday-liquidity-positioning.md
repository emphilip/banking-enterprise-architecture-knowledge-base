# Intraday Liquidity Positioning

**Definition.** Intraday Liquidity Positioning aggregates nostro and central-bank balances and projects the intraday and short-term liquidity position.
**Also known as:** Intraday Positioning, Position Projection.

## Relationships
- Intraday Liquidity Positioning is derived from Cash & Liquidity Management.
- Intraday Liquidity Positioning is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Intraday Liquidity Positioning depends on the Liquidity Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Treasury Analyst. Inputs are nostro and central-bank balances and payment flows; outputs are the liquidity position. Controls include nostro reconciliation and intraday liquidity monitoring.
