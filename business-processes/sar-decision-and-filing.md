# SAR Decision & Filing

**Definition.** SAR Decision & Filing decides whether reasonable suspicion exists, obtains MLRO sign-off and files the SAR within deadlines.
**Also known as:** STR Decision.

## Relationships
- SAR Decision & Filing is derived from Suspicious Activity Reporting.
- SAR Decision & Filing is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- SAR Decision & Filing depends on the Regulatory Reporting capability.

## Details
SAR Decision & Filing consumes investigation findings and produces the SAR Filing and filing confirmation. Controls include the reasonable-grounds standard, MLRO sign-off, the FinCEN 30-day deadline, narrative quality and the tipping-off prohibition. The lead actor is the MLRO.
