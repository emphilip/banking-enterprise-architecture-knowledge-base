# Reporting Data Aggregation

**Definition.** Reporting Data Aggregation gathers and aggregates risk data across systems with controlled quality and lineage for reporting.
**Also known as:** Data Aggregation, RDA.

## Relationships
- Reporting Data Aggregation is derived from Risk Reporting.
- Reporting Data Aggregation is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Reporting Data Aggregation depends on the Data Warehousing capability.

## Details
Reporting Data Aggregation consumes risk data and the Risk Register and produces an aggregated risk dataset. Controls include BCBS 239 accuracy and completeness, data lineage and reconciliation.
