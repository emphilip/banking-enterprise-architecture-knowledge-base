# ATM Replenishment & Reconciliation

**Definition.** ATM Replenishment & Reconciliation is the sub-process that replenishes terminals via cash-in-transit under dual custody and reconciles dispensed versus loaded cash daily.
**Also known as:** Replenishment & Recon, ATM Balancing.

## Relationships
- ATM Replenishment & Reconciliation is derived from ATM Cash Servicing.
- ATM Replenishment & Reconciliation is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- ATM Replenishment & Reconciliation depends on the ATM Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the ATM Custodian. Inputs are the cash order, vault cash and EJ files; outputs are a replenished ATM and a reconciled GL. Controls include dual custody, CIT chain-of-custody, daily ATM GL reconciliation, and variance management.
