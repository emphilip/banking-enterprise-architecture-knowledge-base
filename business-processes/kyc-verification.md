# KYC Verification

**Definition.** KYC Verification is the regulated process of confirming a customer's identity and assessing risk through document checks, screening, and due diligence before and during the banking relationship.
**Also known as:** KYC Process.

## Relationships
- KYC Verification is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- KYC Verification depends on the KYC Management capability.
- KYC Verification depends on the Identity Verification capability.
- KYC Verification causes Customer Onboarding.

## Details
KYC Verification collects identity evidence, validates documents, performs sanctions and watchlist screening, and assigns a customer risk rating. Actors include compliance analysts and onboarding staff, with escalation to enhanced due diligence for higher-risk parties. Systems involved include document processing, identity proofing, and screening platforms feeding the customer record.
