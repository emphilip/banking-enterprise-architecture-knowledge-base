# Credit Underwriting

**Definition.** Credit Underwriting is the process of evaluating a borrower's creditworthiness, affordability, and risk to render an approve, decline, or refer decision with pricing and conditions.
**Also known as:** Underwriting.

## Relationships
- Credit Underwriting is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Credit Underwriting depends on the Credit Decisioning capability.

## Details
Credit Underwriting analyzes credit bureau data, income and affordability evidence, and policy rules to score and adjudicate applications. Actors include underwriters and credit risk officers, with automated decisioning for straight-through cases and manual review for exceptions. Systems involved include a credit decisioning engine and supporting risk and data platforms.
