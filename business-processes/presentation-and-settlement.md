# Presentation & Settlement

**Definition.** Presentation & Settlement examines presented documents against the credit terms and settles or refuses payment under the rules.
**Also known as:** Document Examination, LC Settlement.

## Relationships
- Presentation & Settlement is derived from Trade Finance Processing.
- Presentation & Settlement is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Presentation & Settlement depends on the Collateral Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Trade Finance Officer. Inputs are the presented trade documents; outputs are a trade settlement. Controls include UCP 600 Article 14 examination (5 banking days), discrepancy handling and AML checks.
