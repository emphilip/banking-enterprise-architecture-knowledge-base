# Order Capture & Compliance

**Definition.** Order Capture & Compliance captures and validates the order and runs pre-trade compliance against mandate, restricted lists and limits.
**Also known as:** Order Intake, Pre-Trade Check.

## Relationships
- Order Capture & Compliance is derived from Trade Order Management.
- Order Capture & Compliance is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Order Capture & Compliance depends on the Brokerage & Trading capability.

## Details
Order Capture & Compliance captures the Order Ticket, validates completeness, and runs pre-trade compliance against mandate, restricted lists and limits, producing a validated order. Controls include pre-trade mandate/limit checks and restricted-list screening.
