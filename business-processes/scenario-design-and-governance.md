# Scenario Design & Governance

**Definition.** Scenario Design & Governance designs coherent stress scenarios (supervisory and internal) and governs the models used under SR 11-7.
**Also known as:** Scenario Build & Governance, Stress Scenario Design.

## Relationships
- Scenario Design & Governance is derived from Stress Testing.
- Scenario Design & Governance is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Scenario Design & Governance depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Scenario Design & Governance consumes supervisory scenarios and macro assumptions and produces a Stress Scenario and validated models. Controls include scenario coherence, SR 11-7 model validation and second-line oversight under the three lines of defence.
