# Digital Application Intake

**Definition.** Digital Application Intake is the sub-process that captures the online application with save-and-resume, pre-fill and consent.
**Also known as:** DAO Intake, Digital Intake.

## Relationships
- Digital Application Intake is derived from Digital Onboarding Journey.
- Digital Application Intake is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Application Intake depends on the Digital Banking capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Digital Onboarding Specialist. Inputs are the Digital Application; outputs are a captured application. Controls include consent capture, accessibility, and abandonment mitigation.
