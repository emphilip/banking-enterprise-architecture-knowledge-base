# Account Setup & Activation

**Definition.** Account Setup & Activation creates the customer record, opens the product and activates access once due diligence clears.
**Also known as:** Onboarding Activation, Account Setup Activation.

## Relationships
- Account Setup & Activation is derived from Customer Onboarding.
- Account Setup & Activation is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Account Setup & Activation depends on the Customer Profile Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Onboarding Specialist. Inputs are the cleared onboarding case; outputs are an active customer profile and onboarded customer. Controls include an approval gate, four-eyes and activation logging.
