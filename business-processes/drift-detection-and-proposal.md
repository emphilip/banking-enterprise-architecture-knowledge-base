# Drift Detection & Proposal

**Definition.** Drift Detection & Proposal measures holdings drift against Investment Policy Statement targets and generates a rebalance proposal within tolerance bands.
**Also known as:** Drift Detection, Rebalance Proposal.

## Relationships
- Drift Detection & Proposal is derived from Portfolio Rebalancing.
- Drift Detection & Proposal is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Drift Detection & Proposal depends on the Portfolio Management capability.

## Details
Drift Detection & Proposal compares holdings to the Investment Policy Statement targets, quantifies drift, and produces a drift report and rebalance proposal. Controls include drift tolerance bands, IPS targets, and tax-lot awareness.
