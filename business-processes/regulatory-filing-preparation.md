# Regulatory Filing Preparation

**Definition.** Regulatory Filing Preparation gathers and validates data and compiles regulatory returns such as the CTR and prudential returns against templates.
**Also known as:** Return Preparation, Filing Prep.

## Relationships
- Regulatory Filing Preparation is derived from Regulatory Filing.
- Regulatory Filing Preparation is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Regulatory Filing Preparation depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.

## Details
Regulatory Filing Preparation consumes source data and filing templates and produces the compiled return and validation results. Controls include data completeness, template/taxonomy validation and reconciliation to source. The lead actor is the Regulatory Reporting Analyst.
