# Request Intake & Triage

**Definition.** Request Intake & Triage captures a service request, validates the requester and triages it to a priority and queue.
**Also known as:** Request Logging, Request Intake Triage.

## Relationships
- Request Intake & Triage is derived from Service Request Handling.
- Request Intake & Triage is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Request Intake & Triage depends on the Customer Servicing capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Service Advisor. Inputs are the Service Request Ticket; outputs are a triaged request ready for fulfilment. Controls include requester authentication and priority/SLA assignment.
