# Complaint Closure & Learning

**Definition.** Complaint Closure & Learning closes the complaint, captures feedback and performs root-cause analysis to drive improvement.
**Also known as:** Closure & RCA, Learning & Reporting.

## Relationships
- Complaint Closure & Learning is derived from Complaint Handling.
- Complaint Closure & Learning is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Closure & Learning depends on the Case Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Quality Assurance Reviewer. Inputs are the resolved complaint; outputs are a closed complaint and improvement actions. Controls include root-cause analysis, MI/reporting and record retention.
