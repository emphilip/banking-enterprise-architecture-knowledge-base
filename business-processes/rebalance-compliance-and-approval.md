# Rebalance Compliance & Approval

**Definition.** Rebalance Compliance & Approval pre-trade compliance-checks the rebalance proposal against mandate limits and obtains four-eyes approval before raising orders.
**Also known as:** Rebalance Approval, Pre-Trade Compliance.

## Relationships
- Rebalance Compliance & Approval is derived from Portfolio Rebalancing.
- Rebalance Compliance & Approval is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Rebalance Compliance & Approval depends on the Portfolio Management capability.

## Details
Rebalance Compliance & Approval validates the rebalance proposal against mandate and IPS limits, then obtains four-eyes approval, producing an approved Order Ticket set. Controls include pre-trade compliance, mandate/IPS limits, and four-eyes approval.
