# Digital Identity & Activation

**Definition.** Digital Identity & Activation is the sub-process covering electronic identity verification (eKYC), SCA enrolment, initial funding and channel activation.
**Also known as:** eKYC & Activation, Verify & Activate.

## Relationships
- Digital Identity & Activation is derived from Digital Onboarding Journey.
- Digital Identity & Activation is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Identity & Activation depends on the Customer Identity capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Digital Onboarding Specialist. Inputs are the captured application and ID documents; outputs are an activated digital customer. Controls include eKYC handoff, SCA enrolment, and funding controls.
