# Variance & Insight Reporting

**Definition.** Variance & Insight Reporting compares actuals to budget and forecast, captures variance commentary and produces the management accounts and board MI pack.
**Also known as:** Variance Analysis, MI Reporting.

## Relationships
- Variance & Insight Reporting is derived from Management Reporting.
- Variance & Insight Reporting is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Variance & Insight Reporting depends on the Financial Reporting capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the FP&A Analyst. Inputs are actuals, the budget and business commentary; outputs are the Management Accounts and board MI pack. Controls include variance commentary governance and CFO/board MI sign-off.
