# Capital Calculation & Aggregation

**Definition.** Capital Calculation & Aggregation sources finance and risk data and calculates risk-weighted assets and own funds for the reporting period.
**Also known as:** RWA & Own Funds, Capital Aggregation.

## Relationships
- Capital Calculation & Aggregation is derived from Regulatory Capital Reporting.
- Capital Calculation & Aggregation is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Capital Calculation & Aggregation depends on the Regulatory Capital Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Regulatory Reporting Specialist. Inputs are general-ledger data, risk exposures and own-funds data; outputs are risk-weighted assets and own funds. Controls include data-quality reconciliation and RWA calculation governance.
