# Valuation & Reconciliation

**Definition.** Valuation & Reconciliation values the portfolio and reconciles positions and cash, processing corporate actions before measuring returns.
**Also known as:** Valuation & Recon, Book Reconciliation.

## Relationships
- Valuation & Reconciliation is derived from Performance Reporting.
- Valuation & Reconciliation is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Valuation & Reconciliation depends on the Portfolio Management capability.

## Details
Valuation & Reconciliation values holdings and cash, processes corporate-action events, and reconciles internal positions and cash against custodian records, producing a valued and reconciled portfolio. Controls include valuation accuracy, position/cash reconciliation, and corporate-action capture.
