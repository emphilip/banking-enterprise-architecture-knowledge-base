# Property & Title Diligence

**Definition.** Property & Title Diligence orders valuation and title work to confirm collateral value and clear title before closing.
**Also known as:** Collateral Diligence, Appraisal & Title.

## Relationships
- Property & Title Diligence is derived from Mortgage Origination.
- Property & Title Diligence is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Property & Title Diligence depends on the Collateral Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Loan Processor. Inputs are the disclosed mortgage file; outputs are a valued collateral file. Controls include appraisal independence, title and lien search and flood determination.
