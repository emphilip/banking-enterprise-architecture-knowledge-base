# Client Discovery & Profiling

**Definition.** Client Discovery & Profiling gathers client goals, financial situation, knowledge and experience and risk attitude to build the Risk Profile.
**Also known as:** Discovery, Client Fact Find.

## Relationships
- Client Discovery & Profiling is derived from Financial Planning.
- Client Discovery & Profiling is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Client Discovery & Profiling depends on the Investment Advisory capability.

## Details
Client Discovery & Profiling completes the fact find, capturing goals and financials and producing the Risk Profile. Controls include MiFID II information-gathering, Reg BI care, and KYC freshness.
