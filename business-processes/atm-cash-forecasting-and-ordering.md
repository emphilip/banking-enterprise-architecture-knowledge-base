# ATM Cash Forecasting & Ordering

**Definition.** ATM Cash Forecasting & Ordering is the sub-process that forecasts per-terminal cash demand and raises cash orders and load plans.
**Also known as:** ATM Forecasting, Cash Ordering.

## Relationships
- ATM Cash Forecasting & Ordering is derived from ATM Cash Servicing.
- ATM Cash Forecasting & Ordering is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- ATM Cash Forecasting & Ordering depends on the ATM Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the ATM Custodian. Inputs are ATM usage data; outputs are an ATM Cash Forecast and a cash order. Controls include forecast model governance and idle-cash limits.
