# Inherent & Residual Assessment

**Definition.** Inherent & Residual Assessment scores inherent risk (likelihood times impact), assesses control effectiveness and derives residual risk versus appetite.
**Also known as:** Risk Scoring, Residual Assessment.

## Relationships
- Inherent & Residual Assessment is derived from Risk Identification & Assessment.
- Inherent & Residual Assessment is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Inherent & Residual Assessment depends on the Operational Risk Management capability.

## Details
Inherent & Residual Assessment consumes Risk Register entries and the control inventory and produces inherent and residual ratings. Controls include inherent and residual scoring scales, control assessment and residual-versus-appetite evaluation.
