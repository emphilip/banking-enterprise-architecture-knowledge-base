# Request Fulfilment & Closure

**Definition.** Request Fulfilment & Closure fulfils the requested change or service, confirms with the customer and closes the ticket.
**Also known as:** Fulfilment & Closure, Resolution & Closure.

## Relationships
- Request Fulfilment & Closure is derived from Service Request Handling.
- Request Fulfilment & Closure is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Request Fulfilment & Closure depends on the Case Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Service Advisor. Inputs are the triaged request; outputs are a fulfilled request and closed ticket. Controls include SLA tracking, escalation and closure confirmation.
