# Disposition & Recovery

**Definition.** Disposition & Recovery decides fraud disposition, recovers or reverses funds, files a SAR where required and feeds outcomes back to models.
**Also known as:** Disposition & SAR, Fraud Resolution & Recovery.

## Relationships
- Disposition & Recovery is derived from Fraud Investigation.
- Disposition & Recovery is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Disposition & Recovery depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.

## Details
Disposition & Recovery consumes investigation findings and the Fraud Case and produces a fraud disposition, SAR filing and recovery outcome. Controls include SAR thresholds and timelines, loss recovery and the model feedback loop.
