# Chargeback Filing & Representment

**Definition.** Chargeback Filing & Representment assigns the scheme reason code, raises the chargeback and handles merchant representment/pre-arbitration.
**Also known as:** Filing & Representment.

## Relationships
- Chargeback Filing & Representment is derived from Chargeback Processing.
- Chargeback Filing & Representment is defined in the Cards domain.
- Chargeback Filing & Representment depends on the Dispute Management capability.

## Details
Chargeback Filing & Representment assigns the scheme reason code, raises the chargeback within the 120-day filing limit, and handles merchant representment and pre-arbitration within the 30-day phase response window. Actors include the Dispute Resolution Specialist.
