# Plan Construction & Suitability

**Definition.** Plan Construction & Suitability identifies suitable products, sets the asset allocation, runs suitability and best-interest checks and documents the recommendation.
**Also known as:** Plan Construction, Suitability Determination.

## Relationships
- Plan Construction & Suitability is derived from Financial Planning.
- Plan Construction & Suitability is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Plan Construction & Suitability depends on the Investment Advisory capability.

## Details
Plan Construction & Suitability takes the Risk Profile and product universe, sets the target allocation, runs the suitability and best-interest checks, and produces the recommended plan and suitability report. Controls include suitability assessment, Reg BI care and disclosure, and conflict checks.
