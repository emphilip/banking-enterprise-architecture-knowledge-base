# Interaction Intake & Routing

**Definition.** Interaction Intake & Routing is the sub-process covering IVR self-service capture, caller authentication and skills-based routing to an agent.
**Also known as:** IVR & Routing, Contact Intake.

## Relationships
- Interaction Intake & Routing is derived from Contact Centre Interaction.
- Interaction Intake & Routing is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Interaction Intake & Routing depends on the Contact Center capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Contact Centre Agent. Inputs are the inbound contact; outputs are a routed interaction. Controls include caller authentication, IVR containment, and routing rules.
