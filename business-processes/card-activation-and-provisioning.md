# Card Activation & Provisioning

**Definition.** Card Activation & Provisioning activates the card and provisions it to digital wallets/tokens for first use.
**Also known as:** Activation & Wallet Provisioning.

## Relationships
- Card Activation & Provisioning is derived from Card Issuance.
- Card Activation & Provisioning is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Activation & Provisioning depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
Card Activation & Provisioning authenticates the cardholder, activates the personalized card and provisions network tokens to digital wallets, enabling first use under activation-authentication and wallet-provisioning controls. Actors include the Card Issuance Officer.
