# Identification & Beneficial Ownership

**Definition.** Identification & Beneficial Ownership captures CIP data and identifies and verifies ultimate beneficial owners and control persons for the customer.
**Also known as:** CIP & UBO, Onboarding Identification.

## Relationships
- Identification & Beneficial Ownership is derived from Customer Due Diligence Onboarding.
- Identification & Beneficial Ownership is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Identification & Beneficial Ownership depends on the Customer Due Diligence capability.

## Details
Identification & Beneficial Ownership consumes applicant data and the ownership structure and produces the CIP record and beneficial-ownership record. Controls include the CIP minimum-data requirement, the 25% UBO threshold and control-prong identification. The lead actor is the AML Compliance Officer.
