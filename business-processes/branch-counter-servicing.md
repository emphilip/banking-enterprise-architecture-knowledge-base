# Branch Counter Servicing

**Definition.** Branch Counter Servicing is the in-branch counter sub-process covering teller cash and cheque transactions and over-the-counter customer servicing.
**Also known as:** Teller Servicing, Branch Counter Operations.

## Relationships
- Branch Counter Servicing is derived from Branch Operations.
- Branch Counter Servicing is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Branch Counter Servicing depends on the Branch Banking capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Teller. Inputs are the customer request and cash or cheque; outputs are a completed transaction. Controls include identity verification, transaction limits, and dual control on large items.
