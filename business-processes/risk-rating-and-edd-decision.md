# Risk Rating & EDD Decision

**Definition.** Risk Rating & EDD Decision screens parties, assigns the Customer Risk Score, triggers Enhanced Due Diligence where high-risk and approves the CDD outcome.
**Also known as:** EDD Decisioning, Risk Rating & Approval.

## Relationships
- Risk Rating & EDD Decision is derived from Customer Due Diligence Onboarding.
- Risk Rating & EDD Decision is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Risk Rating & EDD Decision depends on the Customer Due Diligence capability.

## Details
Risk Rating & EDD Decision consumes screening results and customer attributes and produces the Customer Risk Score and CDD decision. Controls include risk-based scoring, PEP/EDD trigger, source of wealth and funds verification, and four-eyes approval. The lead actor is the AML Compliance Officer.
