# Budget & Forecast Preparation

**Definition.** Budget & Forecast Preparation constructs the annual operating budget and rolling forecasts with business-unit input and approval.
**Also known as:** Budget Build, Operating Budget Preparation.

## Relationships
- Budget & Forecast Preparation is derived from Management Reporting.
- Budget & Forecast Preparation is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Budget & Forecast Preparation depends on the Financial Reporting capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the FP&A Analyst. Inputs are the strategic plan, driver assumptions and prior actuals; outputs are an approved operating budget and forecast. Controls include budget approval and assumption governance.
