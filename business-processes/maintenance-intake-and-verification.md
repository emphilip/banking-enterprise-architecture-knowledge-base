# Maintenance Intake & Verification

**Definition.** Maintenance Intake & Verification is the capture of a maintenance request and the verification of the requester's authority and signatory status.
**Also known as:** Maintenance Intake, Mandate Check.

## Relationships
- Maintenance Intake & Verification is derived from Account Maintenance.
- Maintenance Intake & Verification is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Maintenance Intake & Verification depends on the Account Servicing capability.

## Details
Maintenance Intake & Verification takes the Service Maintenance Request and produces a verified request. Controls include signatory verification and authentication.
