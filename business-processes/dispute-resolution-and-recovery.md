# Dispute Resolution & Recovery

**Definition.** Dispute Resolution & Recovery resolves the case (arbitration if needed), finalizes credit/debit and recovers or reverses funds.
**Also known as:** Resolution & Recovery, Case Closure.

## Relationships
- Dispute Resolution & Recovery is derived from Chargeback Processing.
- Dispute Resolution & Recovery is defined in the Cards domain.
- Dispute Resolution & Recovery depends on the Dispute Management capability.

## Details
Dispute Resolution & Recovery resolves the Chargeback Case through arbitration where needed, applies the final credit/debit and recovers or reverses funds with final-credit accuracy controls. Actors include the Dispute Resolution Specialist.
