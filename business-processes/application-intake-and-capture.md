# Application Intake & Capture

**Definition.** Application Intake & Capture receives, captures and pre-checks a prospect's application and supporting documents before verification.
**Also known as:** Onboarding Intake, Application Capture.

## Relationships
- Application Intake & Capture is derived from Customer Onboarding.
- Application Intake & Capture is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Application Intake & Capture depends on the Customer Acquisition capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Onboarding Specialist. Inputs are the Customer Application; outputs are a captured application ready for identity and due diligence. Controls include channel authentication, data-quality checks and consent capture.
