# Real-Time Risk Scoring

**Definition.** Real-Time Risk Scoring scores inbound transactions and events in real time with rules and ML models and raises alerts for suspect activity.
**Also known as:** Detection Scoring, Real-Time Fraud Scoring.

## Relationships
- Real-Time Risk Scoring is derived from Fraud Detection.
- Real-Time Risk Scoring is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Real-Time Risk Scoring depends on the Fraud Analytics capability.

## Details
Real-Time Risk Scoring consumes transaction and event streams and produces a Fraud Alert and a risk score. Controls include real-time scoring, rule governance and model performance monitoring.
