# Risk Treatment & Ownership

**Definition.** Risk Treatment & Ownership decides treatment (accept, mitigate, transfer or avoid), assigns owners and tracks remediation.
**Also known as:** Treatment & Ownership, Risk Response.

## Relationships
- Risk Treatment & Ownership is derived from Risk Identification & Assessment.
- Risk Treatment & Ownership is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Risk Treatment & Ownership depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Risk Treatment & Ownership consumes residual ratings and produces treatment plans and assigned owners. Controls include treatment-decision governance, owner accountability and remediation tracking.
