# Complaint Investigation & Redress

**Definition.** Complaint Investigation & Redress investigates the complaint impartially, decides the outcome and delivers redress and a final response.
**Also known as:** Investigation & Redress, Resolution & Redress.

## Relationships
- Complaint Investigation & Redress is derived from Complaint Handling.
- Complaint Investigation & Redress is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Investigation & Redress depends on the Case Management capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Complaints Officer. Inputs are the triaged complaint; outputs are a resolved complaint with a final response. Controls include impartial investigation, redress authorisation and the final-response SLA.
