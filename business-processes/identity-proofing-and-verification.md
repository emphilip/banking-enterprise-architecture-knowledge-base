# Identity Proofing & Verification

**Definition.** Identity Proofing & Verification collects and verifies customer identity documents and PII against authoritative sources and biometrics to satisfy the Customer Identification Program.
**Also known as:** CIP Verification.

## Relationships
- Identity Proofing & Verification is derived from KYC Verification.
- Identity Proofing & Verification is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Identity Proofing & Verification depends on the Identity Verification capability.

## Details
Identity Proofing & Verification consumes identity documents and PII and produces a verified identity and CIP record. Controls include the CIP minimum-data requirement, document authenticity checks, biometric/liveness and authoritative-register cross-checks. The lead actor is the KYC Analyst.
