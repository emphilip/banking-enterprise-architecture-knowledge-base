# Balance Settlement & Deactivation

**Definition.** Balance Settlement & Deactivation is the disbursement of the residual balance to zero, the production of a final statement and the deactivation of the account.
**Also known as:** Settle & Close, Final Settlement.

## Relationships
- Balance Settlement & Deactivation is derived from Account Closure.
- Balance Settlement & Deactivation is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Balance Settlement & Deactivation depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
Balance Settlement & Deactivation takes an eligible closure and the residual balance and produces a closed account and a Closure Confirmation. Controls include zero-balance confirmation and record retention.
