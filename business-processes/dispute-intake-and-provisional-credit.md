# Dispute Intake & Provisional Credit

**Definition.** Dispute Intake & Provisional Credit registers the cardholder dispute, classifies the claim and issues provisional credit within regulatory timelines.
**Also known as:** Provisional Credit Intake.

## Relationships
- Dispute Intake & Provisional Credit is derived from Chargeback Processing.
- Dispute Intake & Provisional Credit is defined in the Cards domain.
- Dispute Intake & Provisional Credit depends on the Dispute Management capability.

## Details
Dispute Intake & Provisional Credit registers the cardholder dispute against a Card Statement entry, classifies the claim and issues provisional credit under the Reg Z 60-day billing-error and Reg E error-resolution timelines. Actors include the Dispute Resolution Specialist.
