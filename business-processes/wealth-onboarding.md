# Wealth Onboarding

**Definition.** Wealth Onboarding is the process of establishing a wealth or investment client, including suitability assessment, account setup, mandate definition, and initial portfolio funding.
**Also known as:** Investor Onboarding.

## Relationships
- Wealth Onboarding is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Wealth Onboarding depends on the Portfolio Management capability.
- Wealth Onboarding depends on the Customer Acquisition capability.

## Details
Wealth Onboarding captures the client profile and risk appetite, performs suitability and due diligence, defines the investment mandate, and opens portfolio and custody accounts. Actors include relationship managers, advisors, and onboarding operations. Systems involved include portfolio management platforms, CRM, and the core banking system.
