# Cash Positioning

**Definition.** Cash Positioning establishes the intraday and end-of-day cash position across accounts and currencies.
**Also known as:** Position Keeping, Cash Visibility.

## Relationships
- Cash Positioning is derived from Cash Management.
- Cash Positioning is defined in the Payments domain.
- Cash Positioning depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The sub-process is performed by the Treasury Cash Manager. Inputs are the Bank Statement Message; outputs are the consolidated cash position. Controls include position sign-off and intraday limit monitoring.
