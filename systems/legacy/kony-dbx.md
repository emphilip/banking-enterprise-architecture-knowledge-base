# Kony DBX

**Definition.** Kony DBX is a pre-acquisition multi-experience digital banking platform from Temenos (Kony) that delivered web, mobile, ATM and branch banking journeys before its technology was folded into Temenos Infinity.
**Also known as:** Kony Digital Banking, DBX.

## Relationships
- Digital Channel Platform depends on Kony DBX.
- Kony DBX is related to Legacy Online Banking.

## Details
Kony DBX provided a low-code, multi-experience development platform for retail digital banking, spanning native mobile apps, responsive web, and assisted channels. Following Temenos's acquisition of Kony, the Kony DBX technology was integrated into the Temenos Infinity digital banking platform. As an older-generation, often on-premise or self-managed deployment, Kony DBX is treated as a legacy digital channel system superseded by the cloud-native Temenos Infinity successor.
