# TSYS TS2

**Definition.** TSYS TS2 is a card-processing platform from Global Payments (TSYS) used by issuers to manage credit and debit card accounts, authorizations, and statements.
**Also known as:** TS2, TSYS.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on TSYS TS2.
- TSYS TS2 mentions Marqeta.
- TSYS TS2 is related to First Data Cards.

## Details
TSYS TS2 is a large-scale issuer processing system supporting card account management, authorization, and posting for many banks and card programs. It runs on traditional high-volume mainframe-class infrastructure operated by the processor. Its scale and reliability make it entrenched at incumbent issuers, while modern API-first card platforms compete for newer programs.
