# TCS BaNCS

**Definition.** TCS BaNCS is a financial-services product suite from Tata Consultancy Services that provides core banking, payments, and securities processing for banks and financial institutions.
**Also known as:** BaNCS.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on TCS BaNCS.
- TCS BaNCS is related to Infosys Finacle.

## Details
TCS BaNCS is used by banks, capital-markets firms, and insurers across many countries for core processing and transaction management. It is typically deployed on-premises or via TCS-hosted models and spans universal banking, payments, and market infrastructure functions. Its breadth makes it a significant system of record in large institutions.
