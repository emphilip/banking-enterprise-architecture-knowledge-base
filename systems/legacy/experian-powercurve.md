# Experian PowerCurve

**Definition.** Experian PowerCurve is a component-based automated decision engine from Experian spanning data access, strategy design, decision automation and monitoring across the credit lifecycle.
**Also known as:** PowerCurve, PowerCurve Strategy Management, PowerCurve Originations.

## Relationships
- Credit Decisioning Engine depends on Experian PowerCurve.
- Experian PowerCurve is related to FICO Origination Manager.

## Details
Experian PowerCurve is a modular decisioning suite whose components cover data access, strategy design, decision automation and ongoing monitoring across the full credit lifecycle. PowerCurve Originations, made globally available in 2013, extends this to origination decisioning and value-based offers, making PowerCurve a long-established, broadly deployed credit decision engine.
