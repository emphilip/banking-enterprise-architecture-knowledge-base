# QRM Balance Sheet Management

**Definition.** QRM Balance Sheet Management is a long-established on-premise ALM/IRRBB and balance-sheet-management system from Quantitative Risk Management used by large, highly-regulated banks for rate-risk, FTP and capital modelling.
**Also known as:** QRM, Quantitative Risk Management, QRM ALM.

## Relationships
- Asset Liability Management Engine depends on QRM Balance Sheet Management.
- QRM Balance Sheet Management is related to SAS Asset And Liability Management.

## Details
QRM Balance Sheet Management provides interest-rate-risk-in-the-banking-book (IRRBB) measurement, cash-flow generation, funds transfer pricing and capital/balance-sheet modelling for large banks operating under intensive regulatory scrutiny. As a legacy on-premise ALM platform it is now challenged by cloud ALM offerings such as Empyrean Sol ALM and Moody's Balance Sheet Management.
