# Temenos WealthSuite

**Definition.** Temenos WealthSuite is a holistic digital wealth platform from Temenos whose robo-advisor packages client profiling, asset allocation, rebalancing and goal-based investing for automated, hybrid or fully-advised journeys.
**Also known as:** WealthSuite, Temenos Wealth, Temenos Robo-Advisor.

## Relationships
- Investment Advisory Platform depends on Temenos WealthSuite.
- Temenos WealthSuite is related to Investment Advisory.

## Details
Temenos WealthSuite profiles investor risk tolerance and capacity, builds goal-based plans and generates suitable proposals, powering automated and hybrid robo-advisory journeys including asset allocation and proposal-to-portfolio conversion. As an incumbent advisory suite it is a modernization baseline against which newer AI-driven digital advice platforms position themselves.
