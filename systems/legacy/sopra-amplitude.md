# Sopra Amplitude

**Definition.** Sopra Amplitude is an established end-to-end core banking system from Sopra Banking Software (Sopra Steria), deployed across 200+ banks in 50+ countries, with a cloud-native, AI-enabled SaaS successor branded "Amplitude Up".
**Also known as:** Amplitude, Sopra Banking Amplitude, Amplitude Up, SBP Core Amplitude.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Sopra Amplitude.
- Sopra Amplitude is related to Avaloq Banking Suite.

## Details
Sopra Amplitude is widely deployed across Africa, the Middle East and Europe as a full-service core banking platform. Its "Amplitude Up" evolution is positioned by the vendor as a cloud-native, AI-enabled SaaS successor adopted by 50 banks within two years of launch. The platform covers account, deposit and lending processing for retail and universal banks.
