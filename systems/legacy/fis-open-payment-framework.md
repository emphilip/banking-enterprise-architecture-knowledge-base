# FIS Open Payment Framework

**Definition.** FIS Open Payment Framework is an ISO 20022-native enterprise payment hub from FIS providing parsing, validation, cost-based routing and warehousing across instant, high-value and ACH rails, long-deployed at tier-1 banks.
**Also known as:** OPF, FIS OPF.

## Relationships
- Payment Orchestration depends on FIS Open Payment Framework.
- FIS Open Payment Framework is related to Finastra Global PAYplus.

## Details
FIS Open Payment Framework is a mature, ISO 20022-native enterprise payment hub that parses, validates, routes and warehouses payments across instant, high-value and ACH rails. Long deployed at tier-1 banks, it provides cost-based routing and broad scheme coverage as an established on-premises platform that is a focus of payments modernization.
