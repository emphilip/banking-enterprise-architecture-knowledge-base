# IBM Cognos

**Definition.** IBM Cognos is a business intelligence and reporting platform from IBM used to build dashboards, reports, and analytics over enterprise data.
**Also known as:** Cognos.

## Relationships
- Analytics Platform depends on IBM Cognos.
- IBM Cognos is related to SAS Platform.

## Details
IBM Cognos provides enterprise reporting, ad-hoc query, and dashboarding capabilities long used for management and regulatory reporting in banks. It has traditionally been deployed on-premises against enterprise data warehouses. Modern self-service BI and cloud analytics tools increasingly compete with established Cognos deployments.
