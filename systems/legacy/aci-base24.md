# ACI BASE24

**Definition.** ACI BASE24 is a payment processing and switching system from ACI Worldwide used to authorize, route, and settle card and ATM transactions.
**Also known as:** BASE24, BASE24-eps.

## Relationships
- Payment Orchestration depends on ACI BASE24.
- ACI BASE24 mentions Volante Payments.
- ACI BASE24 is related to Legacy Payment Hub.

## Details
ACI BASE24 is a long-established electronic payments switch widely deployed for ATM and POS transaction acquiring, authorization, and routing. It has historically run on HP NonStop (Tandem) fault-tolerant hardware, with BASE24-eps offered as a later edition. Its on-premises, high-availability heritage makes it central to many banks' card-payment infrastructure and a focus of payments modernization.
