# Avaloq Banking Suite

**Definition.** Avaloq Banking Suite is a Swiss-origin integrated core banking and wealth-management platform from Avaloq (NEC), serving private banks, wealth managers and universal banks with transaction processing, portfolio management, risk and compliance.
**Also known as:** Avaloq, Avaloq Core, Avaloq Wealth.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Avaloq Banking Suite.
- Avaloq Banking Suite is related to Sopra Amplitude.

## Details
Avaloq Banking Suite is a long-standing on-premise incumbent core that combines banking transaction processing with wealth and portfolio management in a single platform. Now owned by NEC, it is also offered via SaaS and BPaaS delivery models. As an established core, it covers risk and compliance functions alongside account and securities processing for private and universal banks.
