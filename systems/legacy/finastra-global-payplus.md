# Finastra Global PAYplus

**Definition.** Finastra Global PAYplus is a modular multi-rail enterprise payments hub from Finastra with highly configurable workflows, ISO 20022-native processing and automated repair for high-value, mass and real-time payments.
**Also known as:** GPP, Global PAYplus.

## Relationships
- Payment Orchestration depends on Finastra Global PAYplus.
- Finastra Global PAYplus is related to FIS Open Payment Framework.

## Details
Finastra Global PAYplus is a modular, multi-rail enterprise payments hub supporting high-value, mass and real-time payments with ISO 20022-native processing. Its highly configurable workflows and automated repair restore non-STP payments to a processable state, making it a widely deployed platform that is a target of payments modernization.
