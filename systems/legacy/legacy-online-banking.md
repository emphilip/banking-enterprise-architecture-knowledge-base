# Legacy Online Banking

**Definition.** Legacy Online Banking refers to the category of older internet banking platforms that provide customers with web-based access to accounts, transfers, and payments.
**Also known as:** Legacy Internet Banking.

## Relationships
- Digital Channel Platform depends on Legacy Online Banking.
- Legacy Online Banking mentions Backbase.
- Legacy Online Banking is related to Legacy IVR System.

## Details
Legacy Online Banking platforms are typically monolithic web applications, often deployed on-premises and tightly coupled to the core banking system. They tend to have limited mobile responsiveness, dated user experience, and rigid integration. Modern engagement banking and digital channel platforms increasingly supersede these legacy internet banking systems.
