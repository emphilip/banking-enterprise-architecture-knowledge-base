# Cisco UCCE

**Definition.** Cisco UCCE is the Cisco Unified Contact Center Enterprise platform used to route, queue, and manage voice and multichannel customer interactions in large contact centers.
**Also known as:** Unified Contact Center Enterprise.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Cisco UCCE.
- Cisco UCCE mentions Genesys Cloud.
- Cisco UCCE is related to Avaya Aura.

## Details
Cisco UCCE (Unified Contact Center Enterprise) provides intelligent call routing, computer-telephony integration, and agent management for large enterprise contact centers, traditionally deployed on-premises. Banks use it for high-volume customer service telephony. Cloud contact-center platforms increasingly supersede on-premises enterprise contact center systems.
