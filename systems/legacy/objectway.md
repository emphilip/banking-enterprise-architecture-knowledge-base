# Objectway

**Definition.** Objectway is an established modular front-to-back wealth, asset and banking platform from Objectway whose Investment Advisory module enables a client-centric advisory process with omni-channel engagement, portfolio management and execution-only investing.
**Also known as:** Objectway Platform, Objectway Digital Wealth.

## Relationships
- Investment Advisory Platform depends on Objectway.
- Objectway is related to Temenos WealthSuite.

## Details
Objectway serves private banks and wealth managers with a modular platform spanning advisory, portfolio management and digital engagement, administering over EUR 1 trillion in assets. Its advisory module supports a client-centric process with omni-channel client engagement. As an established wealth platform, it is positioned alongside next-generation cloud wealthtech offerings.
