# Finastra LaserPro

**Definition.** Finastra LaserPro is a long-standing loan documentation and origination platform from Finastra for consumer, commercial and mortgage lending, with strong compliance document generation.
**Also known as:** LaserPro.

## Relationships
- Loan Origination Platform depends on Finastra LaserPro.
- Finastra LaserPro is related to Ellie Mae Encompass.

## Details
Finastra LaserPro is widely used by US community banks and credit unions to generate compliant loan documentation across consumer, commercial and mortgage lending. Its strength is maintained, compliance-current document packages tied to the origination workflow, positioning it as a documentation-centric legacy origination platform rather than a cloud-native end-to-end LOS.
