# SAS Anti-Money Laundering

**Definition.** SAS Anti-Money Laundering is an integrated anti-money-laundering platform from SAS that covers transaction monitoring, scenario detection, watchlist screening, case management and regulatory reporting on the SAS analytics stack.
**Also known as:** SAS AML, SAS Anti Money Laundering.

## Relationships
- Transaction Monitoring Platform depends on SAS Anti-Money Laundering.
- SAS Anti-Money Laundering is related to NICE Actimize.

## Details
SAS Anti-Money Laundering applies analytics-driven scenario detection and behavioural risk segmentation over transactions and customer activity to surface suspicious behaviour, with built-in watchlist filtering, alert triage, investigation/case management and SAR/STR generation. Traditionally deployed against an enterprise SAS analytics environment, it represents an established, on-premises generation of AML monitoring that cloud-native, AI-first platforms increasingly supersede.
