# IBM Db2

**Definition.** IBM Db2 is a relational database management system from IBM used by banks to store and process transactional and analytical data, including on mainframe platforms.
**Also known as:** Db2, DB2.

## Relationships
- Data Warehousing depends on IBM Db2.
- IBM Db2 mentions Google BigQuery.
- IBM Db2 is related to Oracle Exadata.

## Details
IBM Db2 is a long-established relational database available across z/OS mainframe, Linux, Unix, and Windows environments. In banking it frequently underpins mainframe core systems and enterprise data stores requiring high reliability. Db2 on z/OS in particular is deeply embedded in legacy estates and is often retained or migrated during data modernization.
