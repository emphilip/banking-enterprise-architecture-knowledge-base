# SAS Risk Management for Banking

**Definition.** SAS Risk Management for Banking is a long-deployed on-premises risk suite from SAS that combines analytics, modeling and data management for market, credit and operational risk, asset-liability management, enterprise stress testing and expected credit loss.
**Also known as:** SAS Risk, SAS RMfB.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on SAS Risk Management for Banking.
- SAS Risk Management for Banking is related to Oracle OFSAA.

## Details
SAS Risk Management for Banking is built on the SAS analytics stack and a shared risk data foundation, delivering market risk (VaR, sensitivities, stress testing), credit risk (PD/LGD/EAD and expected credit loss under IFRS 9), asset-liability management and enterprise stress testing. It is typically deployed on-premises as an integrated risk and finance platform across the bank's risk books.
