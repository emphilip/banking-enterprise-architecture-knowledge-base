# Fidessa

**Definition.** Fidessa is a benchmark sell-side equities trading and order-management platform from ION Group that unifies trade execution, OMS and middle office in a single automated, exception-based workflow.
**Also known as:** Fidessa OMS, Fidessa Trading Platform, ION Fidessa.

## Relationships
- Order Management System depends on Fidessa.
- Fidessa is related to SS&C Advent Moxy.

## Details
Fidessa connects 6,500+ buy-side firms, 600+ brokers and 100+ markets, providing brokers with a consolidated order-management and execution platform. Its exception-based workflow automates routine order handling so traders focus on exceptions. Now part of ION Markets, Fidessa remains a long-standing incumbent in sell-side equities order management.
