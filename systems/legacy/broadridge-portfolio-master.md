# Broadridge Portfolio Master

**Definition.** Broadridge Portfolio Master is a representative legacy wealth and asset portfolio recordkeeping and investment book-of-record category within Broadridge's wealth platform, historically covering advisor recordkeeping.
**Also known as:** Broadridge Recordkeeping, Broadridge IBOR, Portfolio Master.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management System depends on Broadridge Portfolio Master.
- Broadridge Portfolio Master is related to SS&C Advent Geneva.

## Details
Broadridge Portfolio Master names a generic category of incumbent wealth recordkeeping and investment book-of-record components, maintaining holdings, positions and account records for advisors and wealth firms. As a representative legacy recordkeeping category it stands for the kind of platform that modern cloud aggregation and reporting systems modernize.
