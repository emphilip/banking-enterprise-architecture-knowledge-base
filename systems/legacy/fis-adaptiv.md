# FIS Adaptiv

**Definition.** FIS Adaptiv is the FIS Enterprise Risk Suite (formerly Adaptiv) for advanced market and credit/counterparty risk analytics, real-time limits, PFE/SA-CCR/XVA and consolidated cross-book exposure.
**Also known as:** Adaptiv, FIS Enterprise Risk Suite.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on FIS Adaptiv.
- FIS Adaptiv is related to IBM Algorithmics.

## Details
FIS Adaptiv (now branded the FIS Enterprise Risk Suite) delivers advanced market risk and credit/counterparty risk analytics, including potential future exposure (PFE), SA-CCR regulatory exposure and XVA valuation adjustments, with real-time limit monitoring and consolidated exposure across trading books. It is deployed as an enterprise risk analytics platform at trading and banking institutions.
