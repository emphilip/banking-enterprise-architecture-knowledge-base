# Informatica MDM

**Definition.** Informatica MDM is an established multidomain master data management platform with on-premise heritage that provides match-merge, survivorship and stewardship for party data.
**Also known as:** Informatica MDM Hub, Siperian.

## Relationships
- Master Data Management depends on Informatica MDM.
- Informatica MDM is related to Reltio.

## Details
Informatica MDM (also known as Informatica MDM Hub and originally Siperian) is a long-standing multidomain master data management platform widely deployed on premises by banks to deduplicate and consolidate customer and party data. It offers configurable match-merge, survivorship and data-stewardship capabilities, and although a SaaS edition exists, its on-premise heritage and operational footprint position it as a legacy platform giving way to cloud-native master data services.
