# SAS Fraud Management

**Definition.** SAS Fraud Management is a fraud detection and analytics product from SAS that scores transactions and activity for fraud using analytical models.
**Also known as:** SAS Fraud.

## Relationships
- Fraud Analytics depends on SAS Fraud Management.
- SAS Fraud Management mentions Featurespace.
- SAS Fraud Management is related to FICO Falcon.

## Details
SAS Fraud Management provides real-time transaction scoring and analytics for payment and banking fraud, built on the SAS analytics stack. It is traditionally deployed on-premises and used by banks and card issuers for enterprise fraud detection. Cloud-native, adaptive fraud platforms increasingly compete with established analytics-based fraud systems.
