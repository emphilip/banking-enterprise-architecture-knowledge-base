# Moody's RiskFrontier

**Definition.** Moody's RiskFrontier is a credit-portfolio risk and economic-capital engine from Moody's Analytics that computes expected and unexpected loss, portfolio value, loss and capital distributions and concentrations for Basel II and Solvency II, valuation and risk-based pricing.
**Also known as:** RiskFrontier.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on Moody's RiskFrontier.
- Moody's RiskFrontier is related to Moody's RiskAuthority.

## Details
Moody's RiskFrontier simulates credit-portfolio outcomes to derive expected loss, unexpected loss and the full loss distribution, supporting economic capital allocation, concentration analysis, risk-based pricing and valuation. It draws on Moody's Analytics public-firm and private-firm EDF credit measures and is deployed as a quantitative portfolio-risk engine for Basel II and Solvency II purposes.
