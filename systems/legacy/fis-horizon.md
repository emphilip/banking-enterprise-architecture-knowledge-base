# FIS Horizon

**Definition.** FIS Horizon is an FIS deposit and loan core platform serving small and midsized banks, covering account servicing, transaction posting and reporting.
**Also known as:** Horizon, FIS Horizon Core.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on FIS Horizon.
- FIS Horizon is related to FIS Profile.

## Details
FIS Horizon is one of FIS's core platforms for small and midsized banks, sitting alongside FIS IBS in the vendor's portfolio and documented in the Kansas City Fed market-structure brief and FIS's NA community-bank core systems report. It handles deposit and loan account servicing, posting and reporting, and is a typical migration source toward cloud-native deposit cores.
