# Legacy Credit Scoring

**Definition.** Legacy Credit Scoring refers to the category of older rules- and scorecard-based credit decisioning engines used to assess applicant creditworthiness for lending decisions.
**Also known as:** Legacy Scorecard Engine.

## Relationships
- Credit Decisioning Engine depends on Legacy Credit Scoring.
- Legacy Credit Scoring mentions Zest AI.
- Legacy Credit Scoring is related to Black Knight Empower.

## Details
Legacy Credit Scoring systems apply statistical scorecards and deterministic business rules to evaluate credit applications, often built on older platforms with limited model agility. They are typically batch-oriented or tightly coupled to core lending systems and slow to incorporate new data sources. Machine-learning-based decisioning platforms increasingly supersede traditional scorecard engines.
