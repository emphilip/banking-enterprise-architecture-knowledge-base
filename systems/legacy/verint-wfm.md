# Verint WFM

**Definition.** Verint WFM is an established, often on-premise workforce management and workforce optimisation suite from Verint for forecasting, scheduling, recording and quality management in voice-centric contact centres.
**Also known as:** Verint Workforce Management, Verint WFO, Verint Impact 360.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Verint WFM.
- Verint WFM is related to Avaya Aura.

## Details
Verint WFM provides demand forecasting, agent scheduling, adherence tracking, interaction recording and quality management for contact-centre operations. Traditionally deployed on-premise around legacy voice infrastructure, it focuses on workforce optimisation (WFO) for inbound call centres. Cloud-native workforce engagement suites increasingly supersede this on-premise WFM/WFO footprint.
