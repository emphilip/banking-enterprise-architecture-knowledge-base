# FIS Global Plus

**Definition.** FIS Global Plus is an integrated, real-time, multi-currency portfolio accounting and custody/trust system from FIS, long used by banks and trust providers as the wealth investment book-of-record.
**Also known as:** Global Plus, Unity Powered by Global Plus.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management System depends on FIS Global Plus.
- FIS Global Plus is related to SunGard Asset Arena.

## Details
FIS Global Plus maintains portfolio positions, holdings and cash, computes portfolio accounting and multi-currency valuations, and serves as the trust and custody system of record for institutional wealth and trust providers. As a long-established platform it is frequently a modernization target, with firms migrating toward cloud-native, real-time book-of-record systems.
