# SunGard AvantGard

**Definition.** SunGard AvantGard is a legacy SunGard (now FIS) treasury and receivables suite, including the Integrity and Quantum treasury management systems, for cash, payments and treasury management.
**Also known as:** AvantGard, AvantGard Integrity, AvantGard Quantum.

## Relationships
- Treasury Management System depends on SunGard AvantGard.
- SunGard AvantGard is related to SAP Treasury And Risk Management.

## Details
SunGard AvantGard provided cash management, treasury payments, deal capture and receivables management through its Integrity and Quantum treasury workstations, widely installed across corporate and bank treasuries. As a legacy on-premise treasury suite it is now displaced by cloud treasury platforms such as Kyriba and GTreasury.
