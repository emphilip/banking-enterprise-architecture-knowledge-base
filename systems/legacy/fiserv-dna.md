# Fiserv DNA

**Definition.** Fiserv DNA is a core account processing platform from Fiserv used by banks and credit unions to manage deposits, loans, and customer relationships.
**Also known as:** DNA.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Fiserv DNA.
- Fiserv DNA mentions Mambu.
- Fiserv DNA is related to FIS Profile.

## Details
Fiserv DNA is an open, relational core platform widely adopted by community and mid-tier financial institutions, deployed on-premises or hosted by Fiserv. It supports an open development environment that lets institutions and partners extend functionality. As a traditional core, it is commonly considered alongside cloud-native alternatives during modernization programs.
