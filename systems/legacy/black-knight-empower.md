# Black Knight Empower

**Definition.** Black Knight Empower is a loan origination system from Black Knight used by lenders to manage the mortgage and loan origination workflow.
**Also known as:** Empower LOS.

## Relationships

## Details
Black Knight Empower (Empower LOS) is a loan origination system supporting application intake, processing, underwriting, and closing for mortgage lending. It is used by large mortgage originators and servicers, traditionally hosted or deployed in dedicated environments. Cloud-native, configurable origination platforms increasingly compete in the lending technology market.
