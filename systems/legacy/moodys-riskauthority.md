# Moody's RiskAuthority

**Definition.** Moody's RiskAuthority is a Basel I/II/III regulatory-capital and risk-data-aggregation and reporting platform from Moody's Analytics that centralizes credit, market, operational, concentration and liquidity risk data with regulatory and economic capital, ALM and stress testing modules.
**Also known as:** RiskAuthority, Fermat.

## Relationships
- Risk Data Aggregation depends on Moody's RiskAuthority.
- Moody's RiskAuthority is related to Moody's RiskFrontier.

## Details
Moody's RiskAuthority (formerly the Fermat platform) aggregates risk data across business lines into a centralized regulatory data store, computes Basel I/II/III regulatory capital and supports concentration, liquidity, ALM and stress testing reporting. It is positioned as a BCBS 239-aligned regulatory capital calculation and risk-data-aggregation solution, traditionally deployed on-premises at large banks.
