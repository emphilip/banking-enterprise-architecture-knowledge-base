# Siebel CRM

**Definition.** Siebel CRM is a customer relationship management application from Oracle used to manage sales, service, marketing, and customer interactions.
**Also known as:** Siebel.

## Relationships
- CRM Platform depends on Siebel CRM.
- Siebel CRM mentions Salesforce Financial Services Cloud.
- Siebel CRM is related to Pega BPM.

## Details
Siebel CRM is a long-established CRM suite, acquired by Oracle, providing sales, service, and call-center functionality, traditionally deployed on-premises. Banks have used it to manage relationships, cases, and campaigns across channels. Cloud-native CRM platforms tailored to financial services increasingly succeed on-premises Siebel deployments.
