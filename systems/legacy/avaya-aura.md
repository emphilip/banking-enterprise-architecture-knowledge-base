# Avaya Aura

**Definition.** Avaya Aura is a unified communications and contact center platform from Avaya used to manage voice telephony and customer contact channels.
**Also known as:** Aura.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Avaya Aura.
- Avaya Aura mentions Amazon Connect.
- Avaya Aura is related to Cisco UCCE.

## Details
Avaya Aura provides session management, telephony, and unified communications underpinning enterprise contact centers, traditionally deployed on-premises. Banks use it for call routing and agent telephony in customer service operations. Cloud contact-center-as-a-service platforms increasingly supersede on-premises voice infrastructure.
