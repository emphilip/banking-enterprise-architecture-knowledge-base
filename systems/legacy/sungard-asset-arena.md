# SunGard Asset Arena

**Definition.** SunGard Asset Arena is a legacy portfolio and investment management / investment accounting suite for asset managers and institutions, originally from SunGard and now part of FIS.
**Also known as:** Asset Arena, FIS Asset Arena.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management System depends on SunGard Asset Arena.
- SunGard Asset Arena is related to FIS Global Plus.

## Details
SunGard Asset Arena provides investment accounting and portfolio administration across the trade lifecycle for buy-side firms and institutions, maintaining positions, valuations and the investment book-of-record. As an incumbent on-premises suite it is commonly replaced by modern, real-time, cloud-based investment platforms.
