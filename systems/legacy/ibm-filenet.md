# IBM FileNet

**Definition.** IBM FileNet is an enterprise content management system from IBM used to capture, store, manage, and govern documents and unstructured content.
**Also known as:** FileNet.

## Relationships
- Document Management depends on IBM FileNet.
- IBM FileNet mentions Hyperscience.
- IBM FileNet is related to OpenText Documentum.

## Details
IBM FileNet (FileNet Content Manager) provides document repository, imaging, and content workflow capabilities, traditionally deployed on-premises. Banks use it to manage account-opening documents, loan files, and compliance records. Cloud content services and intelligent document processing increasingly augment or replace legacy ECM repositories.
