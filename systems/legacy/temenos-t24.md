# Temenos T24

**Definition.** Temenos T24 is a core banking system from Temenos used worldwide for retail, corporate, and private banking account and transaction processing.
**Also known as:** T24, Globus.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Temenos T24.
- Temenos T24 mentions Thought Machine Vault.
- Temenos T24 is related to Oracle FLEXCUBE.

## Details
Temenos T24, which evolved from the earlier Globus product, is one of the most widely deployed core banking platforms globally, used by banks across many countries. It is traditionally deployed on-premises and has been succeeded by the Temenos Transact branding for newer cloud and SaaS deployments. Its breadth of banking functionality makes it a major system of record in many institutions.
