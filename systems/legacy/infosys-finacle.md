# Infosys Finacle

**Definition.** Infosys Finacle is a core banking suite from Infosys (EdgeVerve) used by banks worldwide for deposits, lending, payments, and digital banking processing.
**Also known as:** Finacle.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Infosys Finacle.
- Infosys Finacle is related to Oracle FLEXCUBE.

## Details
Infosys Finacle is a broadly deployed core banking platform, especially across India, Asia, Africa, and the Middle East, serving large retail banks. It is traditionally deployed on-premises but also offered through cloud and managed-services models. Its component-based suite spans core processing, payments, and digital engagement.
