# Legacy IVR System

**Definition.** Legacy IVR System refers to the category of traditional interactive voice response platforms that use menu-driven prompts and touch-tone or basic speech input to handle inbound customer calls.
**Also known as:** IVR.

## Relationships
- Conversational AI depends on Legacy IVR System.
- Legacy IVR System mentions Kasisto.
- Legacy IVR System is related to Legacy Online Banking.

## Details
Legacy IVR Systems rely on rigid, menu-tree navigation and limited speech recognition, often deployed on-premises with telephony hardware. They are characterized by frustrating call deflection, deep menu structures, and minimal natural-language understanding. Conversational AI and virtual assistant platforms increasingly supersede legacy IVR with natural-language self-service.
