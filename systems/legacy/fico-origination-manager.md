# FICO Origination Manager

**Definition.** FICO Origination Manager is an origination decisioning solution from FICO that combines scorecards and decision rules to drive credit decisions and value-based offers during origination.
**Also known as:** Origination Manager.

## Relationships
- Credit Decisioning Engine depends on FICO Origination Manager.
- FICO Origination Manager is related to Experian PowerCurve.

## Details
FICO Origination Manager brings together FICO scorecards and configurable decision rules to automate approve/decline/refer decisions and generate value-based offers at the point of origination. It is positioned as an established, FICO-anchored decisioning suite, competing with component-based platforms such as Experian PowerCurve in the credit origination decisioning market.
