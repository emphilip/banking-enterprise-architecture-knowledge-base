# Pega BPM

**Definition.** Pega BPM is a business process management and workflow automation platform from Pegasystems used to model, automate, and orchestrate business processes and case management.
**Also known as:** Pega, PegaRULES.

## Relationships
- Workflow Orchestration depends on Pega BPM.
- Pega BPM mentions Camunda.
- Pega BPM is related to Siebel CRM.

## Details
Pega BPM, built on the PegaRULES engine, provides case management, business rules, and process automation widely used for servicing, onboarding, and operations workflows. It has traditionally been deployed on-premises, though Pega also offers cloud delivery. Lightweight, developer-centric orchestration engines increasingly compete with established BPM suites.
