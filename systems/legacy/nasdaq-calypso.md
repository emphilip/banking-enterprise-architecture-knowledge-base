# Nasdaq Calypso

**Definition.** Nasdaq Calypso is a front-to-back cross-asset capital-markets platform from Nasdaq (formerly Calypso/Adenza) for trade capture, processing, treasury and risk across derivatives, FX and fixed income.
**Also known as:** Calypso, Adenza Calypso, Calypso Technology.

## Relationships
- Order Management System depends on Nasdaq Calypso.
- Nasdaq Calypso is related to Linedata Longview.

## Details
Nasdaq Calypso is a long-standing bank and capital-markets incumbent covering trade capture, processing, treasury and risk for derivatives, FX and fixed income. It was formed via the Calypso–AxiomSL combination under the Adenza brand and is now Nasdaq-owned. The platform provides front-to-back lifecycle management across multiple asset classes.
