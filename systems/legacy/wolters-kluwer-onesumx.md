# Wolters Kluwer OneSumX

**Definition.** Wolters Kluwer OneSumX is a finance, risk, and regulatory reporting platform from Wolters Kluwer used by financial institutions to manage compliance and regulatory submissions.
**Also known as:** OneSumX.

## Details
Wolters Kluwer OneSumX provides regulatory reporting, finance, and risk management capabilities with maintained regulatory content across jurisdictions. It is deployed on-premises and via cloud delivery for banks meeting capital, liquidity, and statutory reporting requirements. Cloud-native regulatory reporting platforms increasingly compete with established suites.
