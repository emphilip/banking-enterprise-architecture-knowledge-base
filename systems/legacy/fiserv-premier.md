# Fiserv Premier

**Definition.** Fiserv Premier is a long-established Fiserv deposit and loan core platform widely used by US community banks for account servicing, transaction posting and general-ledger integration.
**Also known as:** Premier, Premier Core.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Fiserv Premier.
- Fiserv Premier is related to Fiserv DNA.

## Details
Fiserv Premier is one of the most widely deployed community-bank core platforms in North America, documented in the Kansas City Fed's market-structure brief on core banking services providers and FIS's NA community-bank core systems report. It supports deposit and loan account servicing, posting and GL integration, and is commonly weighed against cloud-native cores during modernization programs.
