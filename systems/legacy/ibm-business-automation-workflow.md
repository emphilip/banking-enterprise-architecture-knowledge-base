# IBM Business Automation Workflow

**Definition.** IBM Business Automation Workflow is a business process management and workflow engine from IBM, formerly IBM BPM and WebSphere Lombardi, for modeling, automating, and orchestrating human and system tasks.
**Also known as:** IBM BPM, IBM BAW, WebSphere Lombardi.

## Relationships
- Workflow Orchestration depends on IBM Business Automation Workflow.
- IBM Business Automation Workflow is related to Pega BPM.

## Details
IBM Business Automation Workflow combines the former IBM BPM and case management capabilities to coordinate long-running banking processes spanning human approvals and system steps, such as onboarding and loan origination. It is typically deployed on-premises in regulated institutions. Low-code and cloud-native process automation platforms increasingly supersede it.
