# Informatica Axon

**Definition.** Informatica Axon is an enterprise data governance platform from Informatica providing a business glossary, governance workflows, policy management, and data stewardship.
**Also known as:** Axon Data Governance, Informatica Axon Data Governance.

## Relationships
- Data Governance depends on Informatica Axon.
- Informatica Axon is related to Informatica MDM.

## Details
Informatica Axon Data Governance organizes business glossary terms, policies, and stakeholder responsibilities so that data ownership and stewardship can be tracked across the enterprise. Traditionally deployed on-premises, it is being superseded by cloud-native governance and catalog tooling as Informatica shifts toward its cloud data management portfolio.
