# Fiserv OmniPay

**Definition.** Fiserv OmniPay is an acquirer and merchant card-processing platform providing authorization switching, clearing, settlement, billing, funding, dispute management and reporting for processors and sponsor banks.
**Also known as:** OmniPay, Fiserv OmniPay Platform.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on Fiserv OmniPay.
- Fiserv OmniPay is related to FIS Card Management.

## Details
Fiserv OmniPay is an acquirer-side platform that switches ISO 8583 authorizations and processes scheme clearing and settlement files, calculating interchange and computing net funding positions with Visa and Mastercard. It provides merchant billing, dispute management and reporting for processors and sponsor banks, and is scheme-certified for acquiring traffic. It complements issuer-side platforms by serving the merchant acquiring side of card processing.
