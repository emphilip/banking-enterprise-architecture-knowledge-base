# Oracle OFSAA

**Definition.** Oracle OFSAA is Oracle's Financial Services Analytical Applications suite, an integrated risk and finance platform built on a shared data model covering credit, market, liquidity and operational risk, regulatory capital, capital planning and stress testing.
**Also known as:** OFSAA, Oracle Financial Services Analytical Applications.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on Oracle OFSAA.
- Oracle OFSAA is related to SAS Risk Management for Banking.

## Details
Oracle OFSAA delivers modular analytical applications — including Credit Risk, Market Risk, Liquidity Risk Management, Operational Risk, Basel regulatory capital, capital planning and enterprise stress testing — on a unified Financial Services Data Foundation. The shared data model lets banks reconcile risk and finance figures, and the suite is traditionally deployed on-premises against the bank's enterprise data warehouse.
