# SAS Asset And Liability Management

**Definition.** SAS Asset And Liability Management is a legacy SAS ALM / Risk Management for Banking module for interest-rate-risk, liquidity and balance-sheet analytics.
**Also known as:** SAS ALM, SAS Risk Management for Banking ALM.

## Relationships
- Asset Liability Management Engine depends on SAS Asset And Liability Management.
- SAS Asset And Liability Management is related to QRM Balance Sheet Management.

## Details
SAS Asset And Liability Management delivers interest-rate-risk, liquidity-risk and balance-sheet analytics on the SAS analytics platform, with scenario modelling and earnings/EVE sensitivity for ALCO. As a legacy on-premise ALM module it is now superseded by cloud ALM platforms such as Empyrean Sol ALM.
