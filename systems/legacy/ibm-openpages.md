# IBM OpenPages

**Definition.** IBM OpenPages is an AI-enabled, on-premises and Cloud Pak GRC suite from IBM spanning operational risk, model risk, third-party risk, regulatory compliance, policy, financial controls and internal audit.
**Also known as:** OpenPages, OpenPages with Watson.

## Relationships
- Governance Risk & Compliance Platform depends on IBM OpenPages.
- IBM OpenPages is related to Archer IRM.

## Details
IBM OpenPages provides operational risk management (RCSA, KRI, scenario analysis and loss events), model risk management, third-party risk, regulatory compliance, policy management, financial controls and internal audit on a single GRC data model, with Watson AI assistance for risk insights. It is deployed on-premises or on IBM Cloud Pak for Data as an integrated governance, risk and compliance platform.
