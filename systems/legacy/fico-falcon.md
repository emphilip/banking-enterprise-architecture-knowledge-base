# FICO Falcon

**Definition.** FICO Falcon is a payment fraud detection system from FICO that uses analytics and machine learning to score transactions for fraud risk in real time.
**Also known as:** Falcon, Falcon Fraud Manager.

## Relationships
- Fraud Analytics depends on FICO Falcon.
- FICO Falcon mentions Feedzai.
- FICO Falcon is related to SAS Fraud Management.

## Details
FICO Falcon (Falcon Fraud Manager) is a long-established card and payment fraud platform applying neural-network and analytic models to detect fraudulent transactions. It is widely deployed at card issuers, traditionally on-premises or via managed service. Cloud-native, real-time fraud platforms increasingly compete with established fraud systems.
