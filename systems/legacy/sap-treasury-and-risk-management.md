# SAP Treasury And Risk Management

**Definition.** SAP Treasury And Risk Management is a legacy ERP-embedded treasury module from SAP (SAP TRM / classic Cash Management) for cash management, transaction management and treasury risk.
**Also known as:** SAP TRM, SAP Treasury, FSCM Treasury.

## Relationships
- Treasury Management System depends on SAP Treasury And Risk Management.
- SAP Treasury And Risk Management is related to SunGard AvantGard.

## Details
SAP Treasury And Risk Management embeds cash management, money-market/FX/derivative transaction management and treasury risk within the SAP ERP/FSCM stack, integrated with the SAP general ledger. As a legacy ERP-bound treasury module it is now commonly superseded by dedicated cloud treasury platforms such as ION Treasury.
