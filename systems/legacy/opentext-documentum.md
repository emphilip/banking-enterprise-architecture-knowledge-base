# OpenText Documentum

**Definition.** OpenText Documentum is an enterprise content management platform, now owned by OpenText, used to store, manage, and govern documents and records.
**Also known as:** Documentum.

## Relationships
- Document Management depends on OpenText Documentum.
- OpenText Documentum mentions AWS Textract.
- OpenText Documentum is related to IBM FileNet.

## Details
OpenText Documentum provides content repository, records management, and document lifecycle capabilities, traditionally deployed on-premises. Banks use it to manage compliance documentation, contracts, and customer records. Cloud content services and intelligent document processing increasingly supplement or replace legacy Documentum repositories.
