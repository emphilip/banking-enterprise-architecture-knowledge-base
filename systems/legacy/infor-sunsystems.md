# Infor SunSystems

**Definition.** Infor SunSystems is a long-established financials and general-ledger accounting system from Infor that uses a unified-ledger model combining GL with AP and AR, plus bank reconciliation and global payment management.
**Also known as:** SunSystems, Infor SunSystems Financials, Infor FSM.

## Relationships
- General Ledger Engine depends on Infor SunSystems.
- Infor SunSystems is related to SAP ECC.

## Details
Infor SunSystems is widely used in financial-services back offices and combines the general ledger with accounts payable and receivable in a single unified ledger. It provides bank reconciliation and global payment/EFT management, supporting multi-currency and multi-entity finance operations. As an established on-premise financials package, it is now challenged by cloud-native financial-management platforms.
