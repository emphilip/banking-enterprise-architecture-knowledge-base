# SAP CRM

**Definition.** SAP CRM is an on-premise SAP Customer Relationship Management suite, part of the SAP Business Suite, for sales, service and marketing, now in maintenance and superseded by cloud CRM.
**Also known as:** SAP CRM 7.0, mySAP CRM.

## Relationships
- CRM Platform depends on SAP CRM.
- SAP CRM is related to Siebel CRM.

## Details
SAP CRM (also known as SAP CRM 7.0 and mySAP CRM) is the on-premise customer relationship management component of the SAP Business Suite, supporting sales, service and marketing processes integrated with SAP ERP. Banks deployed it for relationship and campaign management, but SAP has shifted its strategy to cloud customer-experience products, leaving on-premise SAP CRM in maintenance and increasingly replaced by cloud CRM platforms.
