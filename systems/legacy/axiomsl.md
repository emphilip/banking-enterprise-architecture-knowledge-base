# AxiomSL

**Definition.** AxiomSL is a regulatory reporting and risk data management platform, now part of Adenza, used by financial institutions to prepare and submit regulatory filings.
**Also known as:** ControllerView.

## Relationships
- Regulatory Reporting Engine depends on AxiomSL.
- AxiomSL mentions Regnology.
- AxiomSL is related to Wolters Kluwer OneSumX.

## Details
AxiomSL (the ControllerView platform) automates regulatory calculations, data aggregation, and submission across jurisdictions, traditionally deployed on-premises or hosted. Banks use it to meet capital, liquidity, and transaction reporting obligations. The platform is now part of Adenza, and cloud-native regulatory reporting solutions increasingly compete in this space.
