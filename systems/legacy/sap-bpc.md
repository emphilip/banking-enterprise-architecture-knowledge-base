# SAP BPC

**Definition.** SAP BPC is a legacy SAP Business Planning and Consolidation product for planning, budgeting and legal/management consolidation running on SAP BW/NetWeaver.
**Also known as:** Business Planning and Consolidation, SEM-BCS.

## Relationships
- Financial Consolidation Platform depends on SAP BPC.
- SAP BPC is related to Oracle Hyperion HFM.

## Details
SAP BPC unifies financial planning, budgeting, forecasting and legal/management consolidation on the SAP BW/NetWeaver platform, supporting currency translation, intercompany elimination and ownership calculation for the group close. It is a legacy on-premise consolidation engine increasingly succeeded by cloud CPM platforms such as Anaplan and OneStream.
