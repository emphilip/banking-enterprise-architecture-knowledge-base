# Fiserv Optis

**Definition.** Fiserv Optis is a Fiserv issuer card-processing platform for credit card programs, providing account management, authorization, billing and statementing, and is one of the major US issuer-processing platforms profiled for modernization.
**Also known as:** Optis, Fiserv Optis Platform.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on Fiserv Optis.
- Fiserv Optis is related to Fiserv OmniPay.

## Details
Fiserv Optis is an issuer-side platform focused on credit card programs, handling cardholder account management, ISO 8583 authorization, billing cycles and statementing for bank issuers. It is profiled among the major US issuer-processing platforms targeted for modernization by cloud-native processors. As an incumbent issuer system it supports large credit portfolios with established scheme certification.
