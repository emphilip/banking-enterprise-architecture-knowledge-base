# IBM Algorithmics

**Definition.** IBM Algorithmics is an Algo Risk market, credit and economic-capital analytics platform with a simulation engine, economic scenario generator and risk dashboards, acquired by IBM in 2011 and widely deployed at tier-1 banks.
**Also known as:** Algorithmics, Algo Risk, Algo One.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on IBM Algorithmics.
- IBM Algorithmics is related to FIS Adaptiv.

## Details
IBM Algorithmics (Algo Risk / Algo One) provides multi-asset market and credit risk analytics, counterparty credit exposure and economic and regulatory capital computation, driven by a Monte Carlo simulation engine and an economic scenario generator with consolidated risk dashboards. Originally an independent Toronto-based vendor, it was acquired by IBM in 2011 and remains widely deployed as an enterprise risk analytics engine at large banks.
