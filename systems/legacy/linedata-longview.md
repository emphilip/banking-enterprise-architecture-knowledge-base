# Linedata Longview

**Definition.** Linedata Longview is a buy-side multi-asset order-management system from Linedata that serves as the master record for intraday position keeping, orders, trades, allocations, pre-trade compliance and execution.
**Also known as:** Longview, Linedata Longview OMS, Longview Trading.

## Relationships
- Order Management System depends on Linedata Longview.
- Linedata Longview is related to Fidessa.

## Details
Linedata Longview supports institutional, wealth and alternative managers across the investment lifecycle, acting as the system of record for positions and trade activity. It embeds pre-trade compliance checks and connects order generation through to execution. As an established buy-side OMS, it is challenged by next-generation cloud-native OEMS platforms.
