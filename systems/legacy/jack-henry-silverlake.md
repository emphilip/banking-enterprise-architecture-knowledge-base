# Jack Henry SilverLake

**Definition.** Jack Henry SilverLake is a core banking platform from Jack Henry that provides account processing for community and regional banks, traditionally running on IBM Power servers.
**Also known as:** SilverLake System.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Jack Henry SilverLake.
- Jack Henry SilverLake is related to Fiserv DNA.

## Details
Jack Henry SilverLake (the SilverLake System) is a long-established core that runs on the IBM i / Power platform and serves many U.S. community and regional banks. It supports deposits, loans, and integrated ancillary banking services. Its on-premises and hosted deployment heritage makes it a focus of ongoing modernization and integration efforts.
