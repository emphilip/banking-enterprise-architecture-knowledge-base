# SS&C Advent Moxy

**Definition.** SS&C Advent Moxy is an established buy-side trade order management system from SS&C Advent for order generation, modelling and rebalancing-driven orders, and trade workflow on advisor and asset-manager desks.
**Also known as:** Moxy, Advent Moxy.

## Relationships
- Order Management System depends on SS&C Advent Moxy.
- SS&C Advent Moxy is related to SS&C Advent Geneva.

## Details
SS&C Advent Moxy raises orders from model and benchmark drift and manual instructions, supports allocation and trade workflow, and feeds executed trades back to portfolio accounting on advisor and asset-manager desks. As a long-standing desktop-oriented OMS it is frequently superseded by integrated, multi-asset front-to-back order and execution platforms.
