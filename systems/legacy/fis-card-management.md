# FIS Card Management

**Definition.** FIS Card Management is the FIS issuer card-management and processing platform, including the Payments One and former CMS issuer stack, covering credit, debit and prepaid card issuing, authorization and account servicing.
**Also known as:** FIS CMS, FIS Payments One, FIS Card Processing.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on FIS Card Management.
- FIS Card Management is related to Fiserv Optis.

## Details
FIS Card Management is an issuer-side platform supporting credit, debit and prepaid programs, with ISO 8583 authorization processing, EMV card issuing, billing and account servicing for banks and processors. It is one of the major US issuer-processing platforms profiled for modernization. As an incumbent system it runs high-volume card portfolios at established issuers.
