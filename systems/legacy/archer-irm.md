# Archer IRM

**Definition.** Archer IRM is a long-established, highly configurable GRC/IRM platform from Archer that centralizes enterprise, IT and operational risk, third-party governance, policy and compliance.
**Also known as:** RSA Archer, Archer GRC.

## Relationships
- Governance Risk & Compliance Platform depends on Archer IRM.
- Archer IRM is related to IBM OpenPages.

## Details
Archer IRM (formerly RSA Archer) is a modular, highly configurable integrated risk management platform centralizing enterprise risk, IT and operational risk, third-party governance, policy and compliance management on a common workflow engine. While extensible, it is associated with heavy implementation effort and a dated user interface relative to newer cloud-native GRC entrants.
