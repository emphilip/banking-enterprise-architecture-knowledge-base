# SAS Platform

**Definition.** SAS Platform is the analytics and statistical computing platform from SAS used by banks for data analysis, modeling, reporting, and risk and fraud analytics.
**Also known as:** SAS 9, Base SAS.

## Relationships
- Analytics Platform depends on SAS Platform.
- SAS Platform mentions Amazon SageMaker.
- SAS Platform is related to IBM Cognos.

## Details
SAS Platform (notably the SAS 9 / Base SAS environment) provides programming, statistical modeling, and analytics widely used in credit risk, fraud, and regulatory analytics. It is traditionally deployed on-premises with a proprietary language and runtime. Open-source data science and cloud ML platforms increasingly supplement or replace classic SAS workloads.
