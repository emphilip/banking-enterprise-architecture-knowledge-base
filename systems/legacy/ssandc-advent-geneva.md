# SS&C Advent Geneva

**Definition.** SS&C Advent Geneva is an established global portfolio management and accounting platform from SS&C Advent, providing full portfolio and investor accounting, NAV and trade lifecycle support for buy-side firms.
**Also known as:** Geneva, Advent Geneva, APX.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management System depends on SS&C Advent Geneva.
- SS&C Advent Geneva is related to SS&C Advent Moxy.

## Details
SS&C Advent Geneva maintains the investment book-of-record with positions, lots, cash, accruals and corporate-action posting, computing NAV and multi-currency valuations for hedge funds, asset managers and fund administrators. It is a widely deployed incumbent accounting platform that modern cloud-based investment systems increasingly replace.
