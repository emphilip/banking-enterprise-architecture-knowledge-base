# Mambu

**Definition.** Mambu is a cloud-native, SaaS core banking platform from Mambu that supports deposits, lending, and accounts through a composable, API-driven model.
**Also known as:** Mambu Core.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Mambu.
- Mambu supersedes Fiserv DNA.
- Mambu is related to Thought Machine Vault.

## Details
Mambu pioneered a composable banking approach in which banks assemble best-of-breed services around a lightweight cloud core. Delivered as a multi-tenant SaaS on public cloud, it exposes open APIs for product configuration and integration. This modernizes legacy on-premises cores by removing infrastructure management and accelerating time to market for new financial products.
