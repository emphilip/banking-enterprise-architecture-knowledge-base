# Adyen

**Definition.** Adyen is a cloud-based global payments platform from Adyen that provides a single integration for accepting and processing payments across online, mobile, and in-store channels.
**Also known as:** Adyen.

## Relationships
- Payment Orchestration depends on Adyen.
- Adyen supersedes Legacy Payment Hub.
- Adyen is related to Stripe.

## Details
Adyen operates a single, unified payments platform that combines gateway, risk, and acquiring on one stack, delivered as a managed cloud service with global reach. Its API-first design lets merchants orchestrate many payment methods and currencies through one connection. This modernizes fragmented on-premises payment hubs by consolidating the payment flow into a single cloud platform.
