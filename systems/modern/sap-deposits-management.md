# SAP Deposits Management

**Definition.** SAP Deposits Management is an S/4HANA banking module from SAP and SAP Fioneer that manages current, savings and fixed-term deposit contracts across their lifecycle, with product configuration, pricing and contract operations.
**Also known as:** SAP Deposits Management for Banking, SAP Transactional Banking, SAP Fioneer Core.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on SAP Deposits Management.
- SAP Deposits Management supersedes Legacy Mainframe Core.
- SAP Deposits Management supersedes FIS Horizon.

## Details
SAP Deposits Management runs as an S/4HANA banking module, offered through SAP Fioneer, that manages deposit contracts for current, savings and fixed-term accounts across opening, servicing and closure. It provides product configuration, pricing and contract operations on a modern in-memory platform, positioning it as a replacement for mainframe-era and regional deposit cores during core modernization.
