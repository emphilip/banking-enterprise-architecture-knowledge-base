# Orion

**Definition.** Orion is a cloud wealthtech platform from Orion Advisor Solutions for RIAs, broker-dealers, banks and family offices, combining portfolio accounting, CRM, trading, financial planning, compliance and embedded AI with TAMP and OCIO investment solutions.
**Also known as:** Orion Advisor Tech, Orion Advisor Solutions, Orion Wealth.

## Relationships
- Investment Advisory Platform depends on Orion.
- Orion supersedes Objectway.

## Details
Orion integrates portfolio accounting, the Redtail CRM, trading, financial planning, compliance and a client portal with embedded AI (Denali). It serves RIAs, broker-dealers, banks and family offices, supported by TAMP and OCIO investment solutions, and reports over USD 5 trillion in assets under administration. The cloud-native delivery modernizes on-premise wealth platforms.
