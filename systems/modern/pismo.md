# Pismo

**Definition.** Pismo is a cloud-native, network-agnostic issuer-processing and core-banking platform, acquired by Visa, covering card issuing, authorizations and a high-throughput ledger for all card product types via APIs.
**Also known as:** Pismo Platform, Visa Global Issuer Processing.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on Pismo.
- Pismo supersedes TSYS TS2.
- Pismo supersedes FIS Card Management.
- Pismo is related to i2c.

## Details
Pismo provides cloud-native issuer processing across credit, debit and prepaid products, handling ISO 8583 authorizations and a high-throughput, event-driven ledger exposed through APIs. As Visa Global Issuer Processing it is network-agnostic and scheme-certified, replacing batch-oriented legacy issuer platforms with a real-time, API-first stack that scales for large card portfolios under PCI DSS controls.
