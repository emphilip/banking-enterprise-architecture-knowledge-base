# Five9

**Definition.** Five9 is a cloud contact-center-as-a-service (CCaaS) platform from Five9 noted for strong outbound and predictive dialing alongside inbound omnichannel routing, IVR and workforce engagement.
**Also known as:** Five9 Intelligent CX Platform.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Five9.
- Five9 supersedes Cisco UCCE.

## Details
Five9 provides a cloud CCaaS suite combining inbound omnichannel routing, self-service IVR, and workforce engagement with industry-recognised predictive, progressive and preview outbound dialing for collections and proactive contact. As a cloud-native platform, it replaces on-premise enterprise contact-center infrastructure with elastic, AI-assisted voice and digital engagement.
