# Appian

**Definition.** Appian is a low-code process automation and BPM platform from Appian that combines workflow orchestration, business rules, case management, and RPA and AI to build and run enterprise process applications.
**Also known as:** Appian BPM, Appian Platform.

## Relationships
- Workflow Orchestration depends on Appian.
- Appian supersedes IBM Business Automation Workflow.
- Appian is related to Bonita.

## Details
Appian unifies low-code application development, workflow orchestration, business rules, and case management with built-in RPA and AI on a single platform. Banks use it for onboarding, servicing, and case-driven processes. This modernizes legacy BPM engines by accelerating delivery with low-code and a cloud-deployable runtime.
