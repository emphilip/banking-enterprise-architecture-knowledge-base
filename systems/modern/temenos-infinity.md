# Temenos Infinity

**Definition.** Temenos Infinity is a cloud-native, composable omnichannel digital banking engagement platform from Temenos that delivers customer acquisition, onboarding and engagement across web and mobile.
**Also known as:** Infinity, Temenos Digital, Infinity Engage.

## Relationships
- Digital Channel Platform depends on Temenos Infinity.
- Temenos Infinity supersedes Legacy Online Banking.
- Temenos Infinity supersedes Kony DBX.

## Details
Temenos Infinity is built on a microservices and API-first architecture, providing prebuilt digital banking journeys, an experience orchestration layer, and analytics for acquisition through servicing. It absorbed the Kony DBX multi-experience technology following Temenos's acquisition of Kony, becoming the successor digital channel platform. It is cloud-deployable and integrates with multiple core banking systems.
