# Bloomberg AIM

**Definition.** Bloomberg AIM is a buy-side order and execution management system from Bloomberg with decision support, order modelling and rebalancing, pre-trade compliance and broad liquidity-venue connectivity across multi-asset workflows.
**Also known as:** AIM, Bloomberg Asset and Investment Manager.

## Relationships
- Order Management System depends on Bloomberg AIM.
- Bloomberg AIM supersedes SS&C Advent Moxy.

## Details
Bloomberg AIM raises and models orders, runs rebalancing and pre-trade compliance, and routes across a broad set of liquidity venues and execution providers from within the Bloomberg ecosystem. Its multi-asset decision support and connectivity replace narrower desktop-bound order management workflows.
