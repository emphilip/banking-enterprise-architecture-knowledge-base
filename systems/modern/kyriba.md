# Kyriba

**Definition.** Kyriba is a cloud Liquidity Performance and treasury management platform from Kyriba providing multi-bank cash positioning, broad bank/ERP connectivity, AI cash forecasting, payments and FX/risk management.
**Also known as:** Kyriba Liquidity Performance, Kyriba TMS.

## Relationships
- Treasury Management System depends on Kyriba.
- Kyriba supersedes SunGard AvantGard.

## Details
Kyriba aggregates balances and transactions across thousands of banks and ERPs to give a real-time global cash position, drives same-day funding and concentration decisions, applies AI to short- and medium-term cash forecasting, and runs treasury payments and FX/risk management as a multi-tenant cloud SaaS. As a cloud treasury platform it supersedes legacy on-premise treasury workstations.
