# ION Treasury

**Definition.** ION Treasury is a treasury and risk management portfolio from ION Group (Wallstreet Suite, Reval, IT2, Treasura) spanning on-premise to cloud delivery, covering cash, dealing, payments and risk for corporates and banks.
**Also known as:** ION Wallstreet Suite, Reval, IT2, Wallstreet Suite.

## Relationships
- Treasury Management System depends on ION Treasury.
- ION Treasury supersedes SAP Treasury And Risk Management.

## Details
ION Treasury brings together the Wallstreet Suite, Reval, IT2 and Treasura products for cash and liquidity management, financial-instrument deal capture, treasury payments, hedge accounting and treasury risk across corporate and bank treasuries. Offered from on-premise through to cloud, it supersedes legacy ERP-embedded and on-premise treasury modules.
