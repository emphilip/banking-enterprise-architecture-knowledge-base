# Twilio

**Definition.** Twilio is a cloud communications platform (CPaaS) from Twilio with programmable messaging APIs for SMS, MMS, RCS, and WhatsApp, plus voice and email, used to deliver customer notifications, OTP, and alerts at scale.
**Also known as:** Twilio Programmable Messaging, Twilio CPaaS.

## Relationships
- Notification Services depends on Twilio.
- Twilio supersedes Legacy SMS Gateway.
- Twilio is related to Amazon SNS.

## Details
Twilio exposes programmable messaging APIs spanning SMS, MMS, RCS, and WhatsApp alongside voice and email, with global carrier connectivity and deliverability tooling. Banks use it for OTP, transaction alerts, and customer notifications. This modernizes carrier-bound on-premises gateways with elastic, API-first cloud messaging.
