# ValidMind

**Definition.** ValidMind is a cloud, AI-first model risk management and model governance platform from ValidMind that automates model inventory, risk tiering, validation testing, documentation and audit-ready reporting for traditional and AI/ML models under SR 11-7 and SR 26-2.
**Also known as:** ValidMind MRM, ValidMind AI Governance.

## Relationships
- Model Risk Management Platform depends on ValidMind.
- ValidMind is related to ModelOp.

## Details
ValidMind automates the model risk management lifecycle — centralized model inventory, risk tiering, automated validation testing and benchmarking, documentation generation and audit-ready validation reporting — for both traditional statistical models and AI/ML models. The platform is aligned to SR 11-7 and SR 26-2 model risk management guidance and is delivered as a cloud, AI-first solution.
