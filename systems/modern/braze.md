# Braze

**Definition.** Braze is a customer engagement platform from Braze for cross-channel, personalized messaging and journey orchestration across push, in-app, email, SMS/MMS, and WhatsApp using real-time behavioral data.
**Also known as:** Braze Customer Engagement Platform.

## Relationships
- Notification Services depends on Braze.
- Braze supersedes Legacy Notification Gateway.
- Braze is related to Twilio.

## Details
Braze orchestrates cross-channel customer journeys across push, in-app, email, SMS/MMS, and WhatsApp, driven by real-time behavioral and profile data with segmentation and personalization. Banks use it for engagement and lifecycle messaging beyond simple transactional alerts. This modernizes one-way on-premises notification gateways with data-driven journey orchestration.
