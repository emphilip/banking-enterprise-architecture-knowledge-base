# Camunda

**Definition.** Camunda is a process orchestration and workflow automation platform from Camunda that executes BPMN and DMN models to coordinate services and tasks.
**Also known as:** Camunda Platform.

## Relationships
- Workflow Orchestration depends on Camunda.
- Camunda supersedes Pega BPM.
- Camunda is related to Temporal.

## Details
Camunda provides developer-friendly process orchestration based on open BPMN and DMN standards, with a scalable workflow engine that can run cloud-native or self-managed. It coordinates microservices, human tasks, and decisions end to end. This modernizes monolithic BPM suites by offering a lightweight, API-driven, horizontally scalable orchestration engine.
