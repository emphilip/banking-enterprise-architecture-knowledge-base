# Episode Six

**Definition.** Episode Six is a modern, configurable issuer-processing and ledger platform, TRITIUM, enabling instant card issuance, payment processing, spend limits and merchant-category controls across card and wallet products.
**Also known as:** Episode Six TRITIUM, E6.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on Episode Six.
- Episode Six supersedes First Data Cards.
- Episode Six is related to Thredd.

## Details
Episode Six runs its TRITIUM platform to provide configurable issuer processing and a real-time ledger for card and wallet products, supporting instant virtual and physical card issuance, ISO 8583 authorization, spend limits and merchant-category controls. Its parameter-driven, API-first design lets issuers launch and adapt programs quickly, replacing rigid legacy card processors while maintaining Visa and Mastercard scheme certification.
