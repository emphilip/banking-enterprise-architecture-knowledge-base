# MSCI RiskMetrics

**Definition.** MSCI RiskMetrics is the RiskMetrics RiskManager calculation engine from MSCI delivering multi-asset VaR, factor, stress-testing, liquidity and counterparty-credit-risk analytics with regulatory reporting, used by banks and asset managers.
**Also known as:** RiskMetrics RiskManager, RiskManager.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on MSCI RiskMetrics.
- MSCI RiskMetrics supersedes IBM Algorithmics.

## Details
MSCI RiskMetrics (RiskManager) provides a multi-asset risk calculation engine spanning value-at-risk, factor-based risk decomposition, stress testing, liquidity risk and counterparty credit risk, with supporting regulatory reporting. Backed by MSCI's risk models and analytics, it is widely adopted by banks and asset managers and is a common replacement for legacy enterprise risk engines.
