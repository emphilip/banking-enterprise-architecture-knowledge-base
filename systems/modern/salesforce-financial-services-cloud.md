# Salesforce Financial Services Cloud

**Definition.** Salesforce Financial Services Cloud is a cloud-based CRM platform from Salesforce tailored for banking, wealth, and insurance, providing client relationship and engagement management.
**Also known as:** FSC, Salesforce FSC.

## Relationships
- CRM Platform depends on Salesforce Financial Services Cloud.
- Salesforce Financial Services Cloud supersedes Siebel CRM.
- Salesforce Financial Services Cloud is related to Backbase.

## Details
Salesforce Financial Services Cloud extends the Salesforce platform with industry data models, client and household views, and prebuilt processes for financial institutions, delivered as multi-tenant SaaS. It integrates analytics and AI for advisor and banker productivity. This modernizes legacy on-premises CRM by providing a cloud-native, continuously updated, industry-specific engagement platform.
