# Lithic

**Definition.** Lithic is a developer-first, API-first issuer processor providing programmable card issuing, money movement and precise debit and credit ledgers with full program observability, and offers a processing-only deployment.
**Also known as:** Lithic Issuer Processing, Privacy.com.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on Lithic.
- Lithic supersedes First Data Cards.
- Lithic is related to Thredd.

## Details
Lithic exposes programmable APIs for card issuing, money movement and precise double-entry debit and credit ledgers, with real-time webhooks for full program observability. It supports a processing-only model so banks can keep the issuing BIN while Lithic handles ISO 8583 authorization and tokenization, replacing batch-oriented legacy card processors with developer-friendly, real-time services under PCI DSS controls.
