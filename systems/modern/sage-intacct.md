# Sage Intacct

**Definition.** Sage Intacct is a cloud-native financial-management and ERP platform from Sage built around an AI-powered, multi-dimensional general ledger that audits transactions in real time.
**Also known as:** Intacct, Sage Business Cloud Intacct.

## Relationships
- General Ledger Engine depends on Sage Intacct.
- Sage Intacct supersedes Infor SunSystems.

## Details
Sage Intacct covers GL, AP, AR, cash management, revenue recognition and project accounting with real-time reporting. Its multi-dimensional ledger lets finance teams tag and analyze transactions across dimensions without proliferating account codes. Delivered as cloud SaaS, it modernizes on-premise financials packages with continuous availability and embedded AI.
