# GTreasury

**Definition.** GTreasury is a cloud-native treasury and risk management platform from GTreasury focused on cash visibility, cash-flow forecasting, bank connectivity, payments and FX risk for mid- to large-market organisations.
**Also known as:** GTreasury Platform, GTreasury TRMS.

## Relationships
- Treasury Management System depends on GTreasury.
- GTreasury supersedes SunGard AvantGard.

## Details
GTreasury delivers real-time cash visibility, cash-flow forecasting, multi-bank connectivity, treasury payments and FX risk management on a cloud-native platform, with open APIs for ERP and banking integration. As a cloud-native treasury and risk management system it supersedes legacy on-premise treasury suites.
