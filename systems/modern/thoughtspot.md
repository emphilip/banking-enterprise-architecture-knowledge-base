# ThoughtSpot

**Definition.** ThoughtSpot is a search- and AI-driven analytics platform from ThoughtSpot that lets business users query governed data through natural-language search tokens and build live boards.
**Also known as:** ThoughtSpot Analytics, ThoughtSpot Sage.

## Relationships
- Analytics Platform depends on ThoughtSpot.
- ThoughtSpot supersedes SAP BusinessObjects.

## Details
ThoughtSpot uses a search-token architecture that translates business questions into governed queries against the underlying data, returning answers and visualizations without manual report building. ThoughtSpot Sage adds large-language-model assistance for natural-language analytics. ThoughtSpot is positioned as a Leader in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Analytics and Business Intelligence Platforms.
