# Microsoft Power BI

**Definition.** Microsoft Power BI is a cloud-native business intelligence and analytics platform from Microsoft that provides self-service data modeling, interactive dashboards, and AI-assisted insights delivered as a managed SaaS service.
**Also known as:** Power BI.

## Relationships
- Analytics Platform depends on Microsoft Power BI.
- Microsoft Power BI supersedes IBM Cognos.
- Microsoft Power BI is related to Snowflake.

## Details
Microsoft Power BI modernizes traditional on-premise enterprise reporting suites by offering cloud delivery, native connectors to cloud data warehouses and lakehouses, natural-language Q&A, and Copilot-based generative-AI assistance. In banking it is commonly used for management reporting, risk dashboards, and regulatory analytics, replacing heavyweight legacy BI servers that required dedicated infrastructure.
