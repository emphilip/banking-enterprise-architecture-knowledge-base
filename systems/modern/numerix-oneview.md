# Numerix Oneview

**Definition.** Numerix Oneview is a cloud-native, SaaS market-risk and front-to-risk analytics platform from Numerix delivering real-time, on-demand multi-asset risk, scenario and regulatory analytics with elastic scalability.
**Also known as:** Oneview, Numerix Oneview for Market Risk.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on Numerix Oneview.
- Numerix Oneview supersedes Moody's RiskFrontier.

## Details
Numerix Oneview unifies pricing, market risk and front-to-risk analytics on a cloud-native, SaaS architecture, computing multi-asset VaR, expected shortfall, scenario analysis and regulatory metrics on demand with elastic, on-demand compute. Its real-time and consolidated risk views allow firms to replace on-premises portfolio-risk engines with a scalable cloud platform.
