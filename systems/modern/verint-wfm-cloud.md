# Verint WFM Cloud

**Definition.** Verint WFM Cloud is a cloud-native workforce engagement and workforce management suite from Verint providing AI forecasting, omnichannel scheduling, quality and performance management.
**Also known as:** Verint Workforce Management Cloud, Verint WEM, Verint Open Platform WFM.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Verint WFM Cloud.
- Verint WFM Cloud supersedes Verint WFM.

## Details
Verint WFM Cloud delivers AI-driven demand forecasting, omnichannel agent scheduling, automated quality management and performance/coaching tooling as a cloud-native workforce engagement (WEM) platform. It succeeds the on-premise Verint WFM/WFO footprint, extending workforce optimisation beyond voice to digital and omnichannel contact-center operations.
