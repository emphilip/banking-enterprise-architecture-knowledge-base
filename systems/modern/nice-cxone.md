# NICE CXone

**Definition.** NICE CXone is an enterprise cloud contact-center-as-a-service (CCaaS) platform from NICE with omnichannel routing, IVR, recording, quality management and integrated workforce engagement and analytics.
**Also known as:** CXone, NICE CXone Mpower, inContact.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on NICE CXone.
- NICE CXone supersedes Avaya Aura.

## Details
NICE CXone delivers a unified CCaaS suite covering omnichannel interaction routing, self-service IVR, interaction recording, automated quality management, and workforce engagement management with embedded interaction analytics. Delivered as a cloud platform, it replaces on-premise enterprise voice and contact infrastructure with elastic, AI-assisted customer engagement.
