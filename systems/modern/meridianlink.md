# MeridianLink

**Definition.** MeridianLink is a cloud loan and account origination platform from MeridianLink for credit unions and community banks, with pre-built bureau integrations to Experian, Equifax and TransUnion.
**Also known as:** MeridianLink Consumer, MeridianLink Opening.

## Relationships
- Loan Origination Platform depends on MeridianLink.
- MeridianLink supersedes Finastra LaserPro.

## Details
MeridianLink delivers cloud-based consumer loan and deposit account origination (MeridianLink Consumer and MeridianLink Opening) aimed at credit unions and community banks. Its pre-built integrations to the major credit bureaus and configurable digital application flows let smaller institutions launch online origination quickly, modernising on-premise origination systems.
