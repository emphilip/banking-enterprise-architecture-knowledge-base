# Glia

**Definition.** Glia is a digital customer service and unified interaction platform from Glia that combines voice, chat, video, messaging and AI for finance and insurance.
**Also known as:** Glia Interaction Platform, Digital Customer Service, DCS.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Glia.
- Glia supersedes Cisco UCCE.

## Details
Glia provides a unified (ChannelLess) interaction platform that blends digital customer service channels (chat, messaging, video, cobrowse) with voice and AI assistance, designed for banks, credit unions and insurers. By unifying digital and voice interactions in a single platform, it modernizes the siloed, voice-first on-premise contact-center model.
