# Feedzai

**Definition.** Feedzai is an AI-based fraud detection and financial crime prevention platform from Feedzai that uses machine learning to score transactions in real time.
**Also known as:** Feedzai.

## Relationships
- Fraud Analytics depends on Feedzai.
- Feedzai supersedes FICO Falcon.
- Feedzai is related to Featurespace.

## Details
Feedzai applies machine learning and behavioral analytics to detect fraud and financial crime across payments, account opening, and transactions, delivered as a cloud-capable platform. Its models adapt to evolving fraud patterns and support real-time decisioning at scale. This modernizes legacy rules-based fraud systems with adaptive, data-driven detection.
