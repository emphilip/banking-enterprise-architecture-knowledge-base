# Tableau

**Definition.** Tableau is a visual analytics and self-service business intelligence platform from Salesforce, known for interactive data visualization and exploratory analytics across Tableau Desktop, Tableau Server, and Tableau Cloud.
**Also known as:** Tableau Desktop, Tableau Cloud, Tableau Server.

## Relationships
- Analytics Platform depends on Tableau.
- Tableau supersedes SAP BusinessObjects.

## Details
Tableau lets analysts build interactive dashboards through drag-and-drop visual encoding, with live or extracted connections to a wide range of data sources. Tableau Server and Tableau Cloud add governed publishing, permissions, and collaboration. It is consistently positioned as a Leader in Gartner's Magic Quadrant for Analytics and Business Intelligence Platforms.
