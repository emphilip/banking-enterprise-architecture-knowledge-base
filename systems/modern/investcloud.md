# InvestCloud

**Definition.** InvestCloud is an AI-driven digital wealth and financial-planning platform from InvestCloud, including NaviPlan, covering goals-based, cashflow, tax and estate planning with advisor and client digital experiences.
**Also known as:** InvestCloud X, NaviPlan.

## Relationships
- Investment Advisory Platform depends on InvestCloud.
- InvestCloud supersedes Temenos WealthSuite.

## Details
InvestCloud profiles investors and builds goals-based and cashflow plans, running tax and estate planning and proposal generation through configurable digital experiences for both advisors and clients. Its NaviPlan engine and AI-driven personalization position it as a successor to incumbent digital advisory suites.
