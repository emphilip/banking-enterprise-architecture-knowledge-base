# Coupa Treasury

**Definition.** Coupa Treasury is a cloud-based treasury management solution from Coupa (formerly BELLIN) providing cash and liquidity management, forecasting, payments and financial-instrument management within the Coupa business-spend platform.
**Also known as:** Bellin, BELLIN tm5, Coupa Treasury Management.

## Relationships
- Treasury Management System depends on Coupa Treasury.
- Coupa Treasury supersedes SunGard AvantGard.

## Details
Coupa Treasury was acquired from the BELLIN Group in 2020 and integrated into the Coupa business-spend management platform. It delivers cash and liquidity management, cash forecasting, treasury payments and financial-instrument management as cloud SaaS. Integration with Coupa's spend platform connects treasury visibility to procurement and payables data.
