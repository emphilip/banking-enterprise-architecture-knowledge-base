# ForgeRock

**Definition.** ForgeRock is an enterprise identity and access management platform, now part of Ping Identity, that provides access management, identity governance, and directory services.
**Also known as:** ForgeRock Platform.

## Relationships
- Identity Access Management depends on ForgeRock.
- ForgeRock supersedes Oracle Identity Manager.
- ForgeRock is related to Okta.

## Details
ForgeRock offers a comprehensive identity platform spanning access management, identity lifecycle and governance, and a scalable directory, available both self-managed and as identity cloud. It supports complex enterprise and consumer identity use cases with fine-grained authorization. This modernizes legacy identity managers with cloud-deployable, standards-based, automated identity services.
