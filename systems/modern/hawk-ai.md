# Hawk AI

**Definition.** Hawk AI is a cloud-based anti-money-laundering and transaction monitoring platform from Hawk that applies machine learning to detect suspicious activity and reduce false positives.
**Also known as:** Hawk:AI.

## Relationships
- Transaction Monitoring Platform depends on Hawk AI.
- Hawk AI supersedes Oracle Mantas.
- Hawk AI is related to ComplyAdvantage.

## Details
Hawk AI combines traditional rules with machine learning and explainable AI to monitor transactions, screen for sanctions, and detect money laundering, delivered as a cloud-native platform. Its approach aims to cut false positives while improving detection of novel typologies. This modernizes legacy monitoring engines with AI-assisted, cloud-deployed surveillance.
