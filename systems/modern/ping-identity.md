# Ping Identity

**Definition.** Ping Identity is a customer and workforce identity platform from Ping Identity, delivered as PingOne, that provides authentication, single sign-on, and customer identity management.
**Also known as:** PingOne.

## Relationships
- Customer Identity depends on Ping Identity.
- Ping Identity supersedes IBM Tivoli Identity Manager.
- Ping Identity is related to Auth0.

## Details
Ping Identity provides cloud-based customer identity and access management (CIAM) with single sign-on, multi-factor authentication, and identity orchestration through its PingOne cloud. It supports open standards and flexible deployment for consumer-facing applications. This modernizes legacy identity managers with scalable, standards-based identity delivered as a managed cloud service.
