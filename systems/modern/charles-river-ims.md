# Charles River IMS

**Definition.** Charles River IMS is an integrated investment management solution from State Street (Charles River Development) providing OMS/EMS, portfolio construction, real-time position keeping, automated pre/post-trade compliance and performance attribution across asset classes.
**Also known as:** Charles River, CRD IMS, Charles River Development.

## Relationships
- Order Management System depends on Charles River IMS.
- Charles River IMS supersedes SS&C Advent Moxy.

## Details
Charles River IMS combines order and execution management with portfolio construction, real-time position keeping and automated pre- and post-trade compliance in one multi-asset workflow. As part of State Street Alpha it offers an integrated front-to-back alternative to standalone desktop order management tools.
