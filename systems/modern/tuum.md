# Tuum

**Definition.** Tuum is an Estonian cloud-native, API-first, microservices core banking platform with a modular accounts engine for current, savings and deposit accounts.
**Also known as:** Tuum Core, Modularbank.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on Tuum.
- Tuum supersedes Fiserv Premier.

## Details
Tuum, founded in Estonia as Modularbank, delivers a cloud-native, API-first core built from independent microservices, including a modular accounts engine spanning current, savings and deposit products. Its componentised design lets banks adopt individual modules incrementally, positioning it as a replacement for monolithic community-bank deposit cores in modernization programs.
