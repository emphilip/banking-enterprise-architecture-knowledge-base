# Napier AI

**Definition.** Napier AI is an AI-enhanced financial-crime platform from Napier AI whose Continuum product unifies transaction monitoring, transaction and client screening, and case management with explainable AI.
**Also known as:** Napier, Napier Continuum, Continuum, NapierAI.

## Relationships
- Transaction Monitoring Platform depends on Napier AI.
- Napier AI supersedes SAS Anti-Money Laundering.
- Napier AI supersedes LexisNexis Bridger Insight.

## Details
Napier AI Continuum combines configurable rules with machine learning and explainable AI across transaction monitoring, transaction and client screening, and case management, delivered as a cloud-native platform. Its AI-driven detection and explainability aim to cut false positives while improving coverage of novel money-laundering typologies, modernizing established rules-based monitoring and screening engines.
