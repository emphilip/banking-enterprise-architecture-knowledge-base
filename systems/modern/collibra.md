# Collibra

**Definition.** Collibra is a cloud data intelligence platform from Collibra that unifies data catalog, business glossary, lineage, data quality, and governance workflows.
**Also known as:** Collibra Data Intelligence Platform, Collibra Data Catalog, Collibra Data Governance.

## Relationships
- Data Governance depends on Collibra.
- Collibra supersedes Informatica Axon.

## Details
Collibra brings together a data catalog, business glossary, lineage, data quality, and stewardship workflows in a single cloud platform. Large banks adopt it to support regulatory data governance requirements such as BCBS 239, CCAR, and FRTB, where traceable lineage and accountable ownership of critical data elements are mandatory.
