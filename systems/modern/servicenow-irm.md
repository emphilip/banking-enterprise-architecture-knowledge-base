# ServiceNow IRM

**Definition.** ServiceNow IRM is a cloud Integrated Risk Management suite from ServiceNow covering Policy & Compliance, Risk Management, Audit, Vendor Risk, Business Continuity and Operational Resilience with guided RCSA workflows on the ServiceNow workflow platform.
**Also known as:** ServiceNow GRC, ServiceNow Integrated Risk Management.

## Relationships
- Governance Risk & Compliance Platform depends on ServiceNow IRM.
- ServiceNow IRM supersedes Archer IRM.

## Details
ServiceNow IRM delivers integrated risk management — Policy & Compliance, Risk Management, Audit Management, Vendor Risk Management, Business Continuity and Operational Resilience — natively on the ServiceNow Now Platform, with guided risk-and-control self-assessment (RCSA) workflows and continuous control monitoring. Its single cloud workflow engine connects risk to IT and operational data, displacing siloed legacy GRC deployments.
