# Envestnet

**Definition.** Envestnet is a wealth-tech platform from Envestnet for advisors and institutions providing goal-based planning, proposal generation, unified managed accounts and automated rebalancing and tax-loss harvesting.
**Also known as:** Envestnet Platform, Envestnet Wealth Management.

## Relationships
- Investment Advisory Platform depends on Envestnet.
- Envestnet supersedes Temenos WealthSuite.

## Details
Envestnet delivers goal-based planning and proposal generation alongside a turnkey asset management (TAMP) model with unified managed accounts, automated rebalancing and tax-loss harvesting. Its operating-system approach to modern wealth management supersedes standalone incumbent advisory suites by tying planning, proposals and managed accounts together.
