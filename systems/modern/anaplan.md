# Anaplan

**Definition.** Anaplan is a cloud connected-planning platform from Anaplan for financial planning, budgeting, forecasting and scenario modelling across finance and operations.
**Also known as:** Anaplan Platform, Connected Planning.

## Relationships
- Financial Consolidation Platform depends on Anaplan.
- Anaplan supersedes SAP BPC.

## Details
Anaplan provides driver-based financial planning, budgeting, rolling forecasts and scenario modelling on its in-memory Hyperblock calculation engine, connecting finance plans with workforce, supply-chain and sales planning for one source of plan and actual data. As a cloud connected-planning platform it modernizes legacy on-premise planning and consolidation tools.
