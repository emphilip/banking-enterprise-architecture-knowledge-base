# Workday Financials

**Definition.** Workday Financials is a cloud-based financial management and general ledger application from Workday that handles accounting, financial reporting, and consolidation.
**Also known as:** Workday Financial Management.

## Relationships
- General Ledger Engine depends on Workday Financials.
- Workday Financials supersedes SAP ECC.
- Workday Financials is related to Oracle Fusion ERP.

## Details
Workday Financials provides a unified cloud ERP for finance with an in-memory, object-based data model that combines transactions and analytics, delivered as multi-tenant SaaS. It supports continuous accounting, reporting, and configurable business processes. This modernizes legacy on-premises ERP by removing upgrade cycles and unifying financial data and reporting in the cloud.
