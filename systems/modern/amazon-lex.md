# Amazon Lex

**Definition.** Amazon Lex is a managed conversational-AI service with automatic speech recognition and natural-language understanding for building voice and text banking virtual assistants and automated self-service.
**Also known as:** AWS Lex, Lex V2.

## Relationships
- Conversational AI depends on Amazon Lex.
- Amazon Lex supersedes Legacy IVR System.
- Amazon Lex is related to Amazon Connect.

## Details
Amazon Lex (also known as AWS Lex and Lex V2) is Amazon Web Services' managed service for building conversational interfaces, combining automatic speech recognition with natural-language understanding to power chat and voice bots. Banks use it to deliver self-service assistants and automated call flows, often integrated with contact-centre services. As a cloud-native, pay-per-use conversational platform it supersedes rigid menu-driven legacy telephony systems.
