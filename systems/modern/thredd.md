# Thredd

**Definition.** Thredd is a cloud, API-first issuer-processing platform, formerly Global Processing Services and GPS Apex, for prepaid, debit, credit and virtual card programmes, with card controls, risk and fraud and wallet services.
**Also known as:** GPS, Global Processing Services, GPS Apex.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on Thredd.
- Thredd supersedes First Data Cards.
- Thredd supersedes Fiserv OmniPay.
- Thredd is related to Lithic.

## Details
Thredd provides API-first issuer processing for prepaid, debit, credit and virtual cards, handling ISO 8583 authorization, network tokenization and digital wallet provisioning with programmable card controls and real-time fraud screening. As a cloud platform it exposes developer-friendly APIs in place of batch-oriented legacy issuer stacks, and it is scheme-certified across Visa and Mastercard for global programmes.
