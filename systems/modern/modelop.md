# ModelOp

**Definition.** ModelOp is an AI and model governance platform (ModelOp Center) from ModelOp that ties model inventory, governance controls and production monitoring to model operations for ML-heavy estates.
**Also known as:** ModelOp Center.

## Relationships
- Model Risk Management Platform depends on ModelOp.
- ModelOp is related to ValidMind.

## Details
ModelOp Center provides enterprise AI and model governance, connecting a centralized model inventory and governance controls to model operations (ModelOps) and continuous production monitoring of model performance, drift and compliance. Aligned to SR 11-7 expectations for ongoing monitoring, it is positioned for organizations with large machine-learning model estates.
