# SimCorp One

**Definition.** SimCorp One is a modern end-to-end investment platform from SimCorp combining portfolio management, an accounting book-of-record and real-time risk, performance and attribution in one system.
**Also known as:** SimCorp Dimension, SimCorp.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management System depends on SimCorp One.
- SimCorp One supersedes FIS Global Plus.
- SimCorp One supersedes SunGard Asset Arena.

## Details
SimCorp One unifies the investment book-of-record with real-time performance and attribution analytics, replacing overnight-batch legacy accounting with a continuously valued, multi-asset platform. Its integrated front-to-back design consolidates portfolio management, accounting and risk that incumbents traditionally split across separate systems.
