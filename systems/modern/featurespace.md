# Featurespace

**Definition.** Featurespace is an AI-based fraud and financial crime prevention platform, part of Visa, whose ARIC engine uses adaptive behavioral analytics to detect anomalies in real time.
**Also known as:** ARIC.

## Relationships
- Fraud Analytics depends on Featurespace.
- Featurespace supersedes SAS Fraud Management.
- Featurespace is related to Feedzai.

## Details
Featurespace's ARIC platform applies adaptive behavioral analytics and machine learning to model individual customer behavior and flag deviations indicative of fraud or money laundering. It supports real-time scoring across payments and accounts. This modernizes legacy fraud engines by continuously learning normal behavior rather than relying solely on static rules.
