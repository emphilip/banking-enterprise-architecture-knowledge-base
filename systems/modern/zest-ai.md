# Zest AI

**Definition.** Zest AI is a machine-learning credit decisioning platform from Zest AI that builds and deploys explainable models to assess borrower credit risk.
**Also known as:** ZAML.

## Relationships
- Credit Decisioning Engine depends on Zest AI.
- Zest AI supersedes Legacy Credit Scoring.
- Zest AI is related to Upstart.

## Details
Zest AI helps lenders create machine-learning underwriting models with explainability and fairness tooling to support compliant credit decisions. Delivered as a cloud platform, it integrates with lending workflows for automated scoring. This modernizes legacy scorecard engines by using richer data and machine learning while maintaining model transparency.
