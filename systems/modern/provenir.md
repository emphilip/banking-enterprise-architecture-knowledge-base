# Provenir

**Definition.** Provenir is a cloud-native, no-code AI decisioning platform from Provenir that connects data, scoring and rules for instant credit decisions across the customer lifecycle.
**Also known as:** Provenir AI, Provenir Decisioning.

## Relationships
- Credit Decisioning Engine depends on Provenir.
- Provenir supersedes FICO Origination Manager.
- Provenir supersedes Experian PowerCurve.

## Details
Provenir unifies data access, AI/ML scoring and configurable decision rules in a single cloud-native, no-code platform, enabling instant credit decisions from application through to ongoing portfolio management. It positions itself as a modern alternative to component-heavy, vendor-anchored decision engines, letting risk teams orchestrate data and models without hand-coding each integration.
