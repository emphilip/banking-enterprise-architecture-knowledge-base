# Modern Treasury

**Definition.** Modern Treasury is a cloud-based payment operations platform from Modern Treasury that provides APIs to initiate, track, and reconcile bank payments and money movement.
**Also known as:** Modern Treasury.

## Relationships
- Payment Orchestration depends on Modern Treasury.
- Modern Treasury supersedes ACH Batch Processor.
- Modern Treasury is related to Stripe.

## Details
Modern Treasury offers API-driven payment operations software that connects directly to banks for ACH, wire, and RTP flows, with automated reconciliation and ledgering. Delivered as a cloud service, it gives companies programmatic control over money movement. This modernizes manual, batch-based ACH processing with real-time tracking and reconciliation.
