# MetricStream

**Definition.** MetricStream is a cloud, AI-first connected-GRC platform from MetricStream that unifies enterprise, operational and third-party risk, compliance, policy, audit, IT and cyber risk and case management with AI-driven risk quantification.
**Also known as:** MetricStream Enterprise GRC, Connected GRC.

## Relationships
- Governance Risk & Compliance Platform depends on MetricStream.
- MetricStream supersedes IBM OpenPages.

## Details
MetricStream's Connected GRC platform unifies enterprise and operational risk management, third-party risk, compliance, policy management, internal audit, IT and cyber risk and issue/case management on a single cloud system, with AI-driven risk quantification and continuous monitoring. Its connected data model and configurable workflows let banks consolidate fragmented legacy GRC tooling.
