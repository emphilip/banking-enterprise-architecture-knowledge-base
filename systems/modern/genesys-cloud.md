# Genesys Cloud

**Definition.** Genesys Cloud is a cloud-based contact center and customer experience platform from Genesys that provides omnichannel routing, IVR, and workforce engagement.
**Also known as:** Genesys Cloud CX.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Genesys Cloud.
- Genesys Cloud supersedes Cisco UCCE.
- Genesys Cloud is related to Amazon Connect.

## Details
Genesys Cloud CX is a multi-tenant, API-rich cloud contact center supporting voice, digital channels, routing, and AI-driven engagement and analytics. Delivered as SaaS, it scales on demand and updates continuously. This modernizes legacy on-premises contact center infrastructure with cloud-native omnichannel capabilities and embedded AI.
