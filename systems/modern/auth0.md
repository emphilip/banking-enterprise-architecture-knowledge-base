# Auth0

**Definition.** Auth0 is a developer-focused customer identity platform from Okta that provides authentication, authorization, and user management through APIs and SDKs.
**Also known as:** Auth0 by Okta.

## Relationships
- Customer Identity depends on Auth0.
- Auth0 supersedes IBM Tivoli Identity Manager.
- Auth0 is related to Ping Identity.

## Details
Auth0 delivers customer identity as a service with drop-in login, social and enterprise federation, multi-factor authentication, and extensible rules, all accessed through APIs and SDKs. Its developer-first model speeds integration of secure login into applications. This modernizes legacy identity systems with a cloud-native, standards-based CIAM offering.
