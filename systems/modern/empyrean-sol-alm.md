# Empyrean Sol ALM

**Definition.** Empyrean Sol ALM is a modern cloud ALM platform from Empyrean Solutions for IRRBB, liquidity stress testing, deposit/behavioural analytics, funds transfer pricing and balance-sheet planning.
**Also known as:** Sol ALM, Empyrean ALM, Empyrean Solutions.

## Relationships
- Asset Liability Management Engine depends on Empyrean Sol ALM.
- Empyrean Sol ALM supersedes QRM Balance Sheet Management.
- Empyrean Sol ALM supersedes SAS Asset And Liability Management.

## Details
Empyrean Sol ALM measures interest-rate-risk-in-the-banking-book (IRRBB) via cash-flow generation, EVE and NII sensitivity, models non-maturity-deposit and prepayment behaviour, runs liquidity stress testing, computes funds transfer pricing and projects the balance sheet for ALCO, delivered as a cloud platform. As a modern cloud ALM solution it supersedes legacy on-premise ALM/IRRBB systems.
