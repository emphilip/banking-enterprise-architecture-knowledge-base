# Quantexa

**Definition.** Quantexa is a cloud Decision Intelligence platform from Quantexa that uses entity resolution and network/contextual analytics to unify fragmented internal and external data for KYC, financial crime, fraud and risk decisioning.
**Also known as:** Quantexa DI Platform, Contextual Decision Intelligence.

## Relationships
- Risk Data Aggregation depends on Quantexa.
- Quantexa supersedes Moody's RiskAuthority.

## Details
Quantexa's Decision Intelligence platform applies entity resolution and graph/network analytics to connect fragmented internal and external data into an entity-resolved single view, providing context for KYC, financial crime, fraud and credit/risk decisioning. By unifying and aggregating risk-relevant data across silos, it serves as a modern, cloud-native risk data aggregation foundation.
