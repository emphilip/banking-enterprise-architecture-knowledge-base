# Q2

**Definition.** Q2 is a cloud-based digital banking platform from Q2 Holdings that provides online and mobile banking experiences for banks and credit unions.
**Also known as:** Q2 Digital.

## Relationships
- Digital Channel Platform depends on Q2.
- Q2 supersedes Legacy Online Banking.
- Q2 is related to Backbase.

## Details
Q2 delivers digital banking for retail and commercial customers through a cloud platform with online and mobile experiences, plus a developer ecosystem for extending functionality. It integrates with cores and fintech partners via APIs. This modernizes legacy internet banking by providing a unified, continuously updated digital channel platform.
