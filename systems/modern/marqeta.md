# Marqeta

**Definition.** Marqeta is a cloud-based, modern card issuing platform from Marqeta that provides APIs for issuing physical and virtual payment cards and processing card transactions.
**Also known as:** Marqeta.

## Relationships
- Card Processing depends on Marqeta.
- Marqeta supersedes TSYS TS2.
- Marqeta is related to Galileo.

## Details
Marqeta offers an open-API card issuing and processing platform with just-in-time funding and granular spend controls, delivered as a cloud service. Its programmable model lets fintechs and banks launch tailored card programs quickly. This modernizes legacy card processors by exposing real-time, developer-friendly APIs in place of batch-oriented, closed systems.
