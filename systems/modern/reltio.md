# Reltio

**Definition.** Reltio is a cloud-native, AI-driven multidomain master data management SaaS that provides real-time entity resolution, dynamic survivorship and stewardship for the golden party record.
**Also known as:** Reltio Data Cloud, Reltio MDM.

## Relationships
- Master Data Management depends on Reltio.
- Reltio supersedes Informatica MDM.
- Reltio is related to Snowflake.

## Details
Reltio (also known as Reltio Data Cloud and Reltio MDM) is a cloud-native, API-first master data management platform that unifies party and other domains with real-time, ML-assisted entity resolution, configurable survivorship and embedded stewardship. Delivered as continuously updated SaaS, it serves a trusted golden record to operational and analytical consumers and modernises on-premise master data hubs with elastic scale and real-time integration.
