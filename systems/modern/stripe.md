# Stripe

**Definition.** Stripe is a cloud-based payments platform from Stripe that provides payment acceptance, processing, and money-movement APIs for internet businesses.
**Also known as:** Stripe Payments.

## Relationships
- Payment Orchestration depends on Stripe.
- Stripe supersedes ACH Batch Processor.
- Stripe is related to Adyen.

## Details
Stripe offers a developer-first, API-driven payments stack delivered as a fully managed cloud service, covering card acquiring, ACH, and other payment methods. Its programmable model lets businesses orchestrate payments, billing, and payouts without managing payment infrastructure. This modernizes batch-oriented legacy payment processing with real-time, API-accessible money movement.
