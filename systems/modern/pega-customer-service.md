# Pega Customer Service

**Definition.** Pega Customer Service is a case-centric customer service and engagement application built on Pega's low-code platform, used for banking service, complaints and next-best-action.
**Also known as:** Pega CRM, Pega CS.

## Relationships
- CRM Platform depends on Pega Customer Service.
- Pega Customer Service supersedes Siebel CRM.
- Pega Customer Service is related to Microsoft Dynamics 365.

## Details
Pega Customer Service (also known as Pega CRM and Pega CS) is built on Pegasystems' low-code platform and centres servicing on dynamic case management, with embedded next-best-action decisioning and AI to guide interactions. Banks use it for service requests, complaints and omnichannel engagement with strong workflow automation. As a modern, continuously evolving engagement application it supersedes earlier on-premise CRM suites.
