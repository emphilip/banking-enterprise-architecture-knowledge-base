# Kasisto

**Definition.** Kasisto is a conversational AI platform from Kasisto, branded KAI, that provides virtual assistants and chatbots specialized for banking and finance.
**Also known as:** KAI.

## Relationships
- Conversational AI depends on Kasisto.
- Kasisto supersedes Legacy IVR System.
- Kasisto is related to Anthropic Claude.

## Details
Kasisto's KAI platform delivers digital assistants with banking-specific language understanding to handle customer inquiries, transactions, and servicing across digital channels. It is offered as a cloud platform that integrates with banking systems. This modernizes legacy IVR by enabling natural-language, self-service customer engagement.
