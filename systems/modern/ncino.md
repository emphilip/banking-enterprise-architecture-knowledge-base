# nCino

**Definition.** nCino is a cloud-based banking platform from nCino that provides loan origination and onboarding workflows, built on a cloud CRM foundation.
**Also known as:** nCino Cloud Banking.

## Relationships
- Loan Origination Platform depends on nCino.
- nCino supersedes Black Knight Empower.
- nCino is related to Blend.

## Details
nCino offers cloud-native loan origination, account opening, and portfolio management for commercial and retail banking, delivered as SaaS on the Salesforce platform. It digitizes lending workflows with a single system of engagement across the loan lifecycle. This modernizes legacy origination systems by unifying processes in a configurable cloud platform.
