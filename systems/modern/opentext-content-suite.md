# OpenText Content Suite

**Definition.** OpenText Content Suite is an enterprise content management platform from OpenText that governs the document lifecycle, records retention, and archiving, integrated with SAP, Microsoft 365, and Salesforce.
**Also known as:** OpenText Content Management, OpenText Extended ECM, Content Suite Platform.

## Relationships
- Document Management depends on OpenText Content Suite.
- OpenText Content Suite supersedes OpenText Documentum.

## Details
OpenText Content Suite, marketed as OpenText Content Management and Extended ECM, manages the full document lifecycle with classification, records management, retention, and archiving controls suited to regulated banking. It embeds content services into business applications such as SAP, Microsoft 365, and Salesforce, and is deployable in the cloud or on-premises.
