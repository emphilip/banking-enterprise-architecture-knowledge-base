# Workato

**Definition.** Workato is a cloud iPaaS from Workato for integration and workflow automation with an AI-assisted, low-code experience aimed at both IT and business citizen developers building automations called recipes.
**Also known as:** Workato iPaaS, Workato Recipes.

## Relationships
- Integration Platform depends on Workato.
- Workato supersedes IBM App Connect Enterprise.
- Workato is related to SnapLogic.

## Details
Workato combines application integration with workflow automation through reusable "recipes" and an AI-assisted, low-code builder targeting both IT and business users. It ships with connectors for common SaaS and enterprise systems. This modernizes on-premises integration middleware by enabling self-service, cloud-delivered automation.
