# FIS Modern Banking Platform

**Definition.** FIS Modern Banking Platform is a cloud-native, API-first, modular SaaS core from FIS covering deposits, lending, cards and customer management in real time, available on Microsoft Azure.
**Also known as:** MBP, FIS MBP, Modern Banking Platform.

## Relationships
- Core Banking Processing depends on FIS Modern Banking Platform.
- FIS Modern Banking Platform supersedes FIS Profile.
- FIS Modern Banking Platform supersedes FIS Horizon.

## Details
FIS Modern Banking Platform is FIS's "evergreen" replacement path for its own legacy cores, delivered as modular, real-time SaaS on Microsoft Azure. Its API-first, componentized architecture lets banks adopt deposits, lending and cards capabilities incrementally. Continuous "evergreen" updates aim to remove disruptive version migrations associated with older core systems.
