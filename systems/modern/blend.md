# Blend

**Definition.** Blend is a cloud-based digital lending and loan origination platform from Blend Labs that streamlines mortgage and consumer loan applications.
**Also known as:** Blend Platform.

## Relationships
- Loan Origination Platform depends on Blend.
- Blend supersedes Black Knight Empower.
- Blend is related to nCino.

## Details
Blend provides a consumer-facing, cloud-native origination platform for mortgages, home equity, and consumer loans, with automated data collection and workflow. It integrates with core and third-party services through APIs. This modernizes legacy origination systems by delivering a digital, self-service borrower experience and faster processing.
