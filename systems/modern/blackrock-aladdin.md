# BlackRock Aladdin

**Definition.** BlackRock Aladdin is a front-to-back investment platform from BlackRock that unifies portfolio management, risk analytics, performance attribution, accounting and compliance on a common data language with an API-first open architecture.
**Also known as:** Aladdin, Aladdin Wealth.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management System depends on BlackRock Aladdin.
- BlackRock Aladdin supersedes SS&C Advent Geneva.
- BlackRock Aladdin supersedes SunGard Asset Arena.

## Details
BlackRock Aladdin maintains the investment book-of-record and overlays whole-portfolio risk analytics, performance attribution and compliance across asset classes on a single common data language. Its API-first, open architecture lets firms replace siloed legacy accounting and risk systems with one front-to-back platform.
