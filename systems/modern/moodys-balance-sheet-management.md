# Moody's Balance Sheet Management

**Definition.** Moody's Balance Sheet Management is a cloud-enabled ALM and balance-sheet-management suite from Moody's Analytics for IRRBB, funds transfer pricing, behavioural modelling and earnings/EVE sensitivity, aligned to evolving regulatory standards.
**Also known as:** Moody's ALM, RiskConfidence.

## Relationships
- Asset Liability Management Engine depends on Moody's Balance Sheet Management.
- Moody's Balance Sheet Management supersedes QRM Balance Sheet Management.

## Details
Moody's Balance Sheet Management measures interest-rate-risk-in-the-banking-book (IRRBB) and net interest income, computes funds transfer pricing, models non-maturity-deposit and prepayment behaviour and runs earnings/EVE sensitivity and stress, with behavioural models embedded into stress testing. As a cloud-enabled ALM suite it supersedes legacy on-premise balance-sheet-management systems.
