# Regnology

**Definition.** Regnology is a regulatory reporting and compliance technology platform from Regnology that automates the production and submission of regulatory returns for financial institutions.
**Also known as:** Regnology Reporting.

## Relationships
- Regulatory Reporting Engine depends on Regnology.
- Regnology supersedes AxiomSL.
- Regnology is related to Suade.

## Details
Regnology provides regulatory reporting software covering data ingestion, calculation, and submission across jurisdictions, available with cloud deployment options. It maintains regulatory rule sets so institutions can keep pace with changing requirements. This modernizes legacy reporting engines by offering scalable, cloud-capable, continuously updated regulatory automation.
