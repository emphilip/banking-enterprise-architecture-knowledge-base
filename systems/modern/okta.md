# Okta

**Definition.** Okta is a cloud-based identity and access management platform from Okta that provides single sign-on, multi-factor authentication, and lifecycle management for workforce identities.
**Also known as:** Okta Identity Cloud.

## Relationships
- Identity Access Management depends on Okta.
- Okta supersedes CA SiteMinder.
- Okta is related to ForgeRock.

## Details
Okta delivers identity as a service, including single sign-on, adaptive multi-factor authentication, and automated user provisioning across thousands of integrated applications. Its cloud-native, standards-based design (SAML, OIDC, SCIM) simplifies access governance. This modernizes legacy on-premises access managers by centralizing identity in a managed, continuously updated cloud platform.
