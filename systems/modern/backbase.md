# Backbase

**Definition.** Backbase is a digital banking engagement platform from Backbase that provides web and mobile banking experiences layered over core and third-party systems.
**Also known as:** Engagement Banking.

## Relationships
- Digital Channel Platform depends on Backbase.
- Backbase supersedes Legacy Online Banking.
- Backbase is related to Q2.

## Details
Backbase offers an engagement banking platform with prebuilt digital banking journeys, an orchestration layer, and open APIs to unify channels across the customer lifecycle. It is cloud-deployable and integrates with multiple cores. This modernizes legacy online banking by delivering a composable, omnichannel digital experience layer.
