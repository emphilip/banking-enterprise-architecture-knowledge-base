# Addepar

**Definition.** Addepar is a modern cloud portfolio data-aggregation, performance and reporting platform from Addepar for RIAs, family offices and private banks, with an "Advent Converter" migration path from legacy Advent recordkeeping products (APX, Axys, PortfolioCenter).
**Also known as:** Addepar Platform.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management System depends on Addepar.
- Addepar supersedes Broadridge Portfolio Master.

## Details
Addepar aggregates multi-custodian holdings and alternatives into a single investment book-of-record, computing performance and producing client and advisor reporting in the cloud. Its "Advent Converter" migration path targets firms moving off legacy Advent recordkeeping products (APX, Axys, PortfolioCenter).
