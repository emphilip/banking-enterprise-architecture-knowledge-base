# ComplyAdvantage

**Definition.** ComplyAdvantage is an AI-driven financial crime detection platform from ComplyAdvantage that provides transaction monitoring, sanctions and watchlist screening, and risk data.
**Also known as:** ComplyAdvantage.

## Relationships
- Transaction Monitoring Platform depends on ComplyAdvantage.
- ComplyAdvantage supersedes NICE Actimize.
- ComplyAdvantage is related to Hawk AI.

## Details
ComplyAdvantage delivers cloud-based AML capabilities including real-time transaction monitoring, screening against continuously updated risk databases, and adverse media analysis, accessed through APIs. Its data and machine learning aim to reduce false positives and speed onboarding. This modernizes legacy monitoring suites with API-first, continuously refreshed risk intelligence.
