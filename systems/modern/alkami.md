# Alkami

**Definition.** Alkami is a cloud-based digital sales and service platform from Alkami Technology that delivers retail and business online and mobile banking, onboarding, data and marketing for U.S. banks and credit unions.
**Also known as:** Alkami Platform, Alkami Digital Banking, ORB.

## Relationships
- Digital Channel Platform depends on Alkami.
- Alkami supersedes Legacy Online Banking.

## Details
Alkami provides a unified, cloud-native digital banking platform spanning retail and business online and mobile banking, digital account opening, data analytics, and integrated marketing. Built for U.S. banks and credit unions, it modernizes legacy internet banking by delivering an extensible, API-driven omnichannel experience layer with frequent continuous delivery.
