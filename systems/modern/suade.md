# Suade

**Definition.** Suade is a cloud-based regulatory reporting platform from Suade Labs that transforms bank data into regulatory returns using a standardized data model.
**Also known as:** Suade Platform.

## Relationships
- Regulatory Reporting Engine depends on Suade.
- Suade supersedes Wolters Kluwer OneSumX.
- Suade is related to Regnology.

## Details
Suade uses an open, standardized data model to ingest bank data and produce regulatory reports and calculations, delivered as a cloud-native, API-driven platform. Its data-first approach decouples reporting logic from source systems. This modernizes legacy reporting engines by enabling automated, scalable, and adaptable regulatory output.
