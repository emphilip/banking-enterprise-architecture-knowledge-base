# Fenergo

**Definition.** Fenergo is an API-first SaaS client lifecycle management and KYC platform from Fenergo that covers onboarding, policy-driven due diligence, document AI, screening orchestration and perpetual KYC across jurisdictions.
**Also known as:** Fenergo CLM, Fenergo KYC, Fenergo Client Lifecycle Management.

## Relationships
- KYC Onboarding Platform depends on Fenergo.
- Fenergo is related to ComplyAdvantage.

## Details
Fenergo delivers client lifecycle management (CLM) and KYC as an API-first SaaS platform, orchestrating customer due diligence, identity and document verification, customer risk rating, screening and ongoing perpetual KYC / CLM review across multiple jurisdictions. Its rules-driven policy engine and out-of-the-box regulatory rule sets enable greenfield, cloud-native onboarding and continuous due diligence rather than transaction-centric monitoring.
