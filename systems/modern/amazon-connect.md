# Amazon Connect

**Definition.** Amazon Connect is a cloud-based contact center service from Amazon Web Services that provides telephony, routing, and AI-powered customer engagement.
**Also known as:** Connect.

## Relationships
- Contact Center Platform depends on Amazon Connect.
- Amazon Connect supersedes Avaya Aura.
- Amazon Connect is related to Genesys Cloud.

## Details
Amazon Connect delivers omnichannel contact center capabilities as a pay-as-you-go cloud service, with built-in routing, IVR, and AI features such as automated speech and sentiment analysis. It scales elastically without on-premises telephony hardware. This modernizes legacy contact center systems by removing infrastructure and adding cloud AI services.
