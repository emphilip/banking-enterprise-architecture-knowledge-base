# SAP S/4HANA Finance

**Definition.** SAP S/4HANA Finance is a modern in-memory ERP finance application from SAP built on the Universal Journal (ACDOCA), unifying financial and management accounting with a real-time general ledger, subledgers, allocations and the Financial Closing Cockpit.
**Also known as:** S/4HANA Finance, Simple Finance, S/4HANA Central Finance.

## Relationships
- General Ledger Engine depends on SAP S/4HANA Finance.
- SAP S/4HANA Finance supersedes SAP ECC.
- SAP S/4HANA Finance supersedes Oracle E-Business Suite.

## Details
SAP S/4HANA Finance stores every financial and management accounting line item in the single Universal Journal table (ACDOCA), removing redundant totals and reconciliation between GL, CO, AA and ML. It supports document splitting, parallel ledgers for multi-GAAP, real-time profitability and the Financial Closing Cockpit for period-end orchestration. As an in-memory cloud-ready ERP it modernizes legacy on-premise general ledgers by collapsing aggregates and enabling real-time reporting.
