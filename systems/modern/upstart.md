# Upstart

**Definition.** Upstart is an AI lending platform from Upstart that uses machine learning models to assess credit risk and automate loan decisioning for partner lenders.
**Also known as:** Upstart.

## Relationships
- Credit Decisioning Engine depends on Upstart.
- Upstart supersedes Legacy Credit Scoring.
- Upstart is related to Zest AI.

## Details
Upstart applies machine learning over a broad set of variables to predict creditworthiness and automate approvals, offered as a cloud platform to banks and credit unions. Its models aim to expand access while managing risk. This modernizes legacy scoring engines by replacing narrow rule-based scorecards with data-driven, automated decisioning.
