# Murex MX.3 Risk

**Definition.** Murex MX.3 Risk is the cloud-ready MX.3 Enterprise Risk suite from Murex that controls market, credit and liquidity risk with historical, parametric and Monte Carlo VaR, expected shortfall, stress testing, P&L explain, FRTB-SA/IMA, SA-CCR and XVA.
**Also known as:** MX.3 Enterprise Risk, Murex Enterprise Risk.

## Relationships
- Risk Analytics Engine depends on Murex MX.3 Risk.
- Murex MX.3 Risk supersedes FIS Adaptiv.

## Details
Murex MX.3 Risk computes market risk via historical, parametric and Monte Carlo VaR and expected shortfall, supports enterprise stress testing and P&L explain, and covers FRTB standardized approach (SA) and internal models approach (IMA) capital. It adds counterparty credit risk through SA-CCR exposure and XVA valuation adjustments on a cloud-ready, integrated trading-and-risk platform.
