# Temenos Origination

**Definition.** Temenos Origination is a cloud, omni-channel origination suite (Temenos Infinity) from Temenos with instant multi-factor decisioning that lifts auto-decision rates and removes paper processes.
**Also known as:** Temenos Infinity Origination, Infinity Origination.

## Relationships
- Loan Origination Platform depends on Temenos Origination.
- Temenos Origination supersedes Ellie Mae Encompass.
- Temenos Origination supersedes Finastra LaserPro.

## Details
Temenos Origination, delivered as part of the Temenos Infinity front-office suite, provides omni-channel, cloud-based loan and account origination with instant multi-factor decisioning. Temenos markets it to banks and credit unions as a way to raise straight-through auto-decision rates and eliminate paper-based steps, modernising document-centric and on-premise origination platforms.
