# Microsoft Dynamics 365

**Definition.** Microsoft Dynamics 365 is a cloud CRM and ERP suite whose Customer Service and Contact Center applications unify CRM, CCaaS and agentic AI for banking sales and service.
**Also known as:** Dynamics 365 Customer Service, D365.

## Relationships
- CRM Platform depends on Microsoft Dynamics 365.
- Microsoft Dynamics 365 supersedes SAP CRM.
- Microsoft Dynamics 365 is related to Pega Customer Service.

## Details
Microsoft Dynamics 365 (also known as Dynamics 365 Customer Service and D365) is Microsoft's cloud business-applications suite, delivering modular CRM and ERP capabilities with embedded Copilot and agentic AI. Its Customer Service and Contact Center apps combine case management, omnichannel engagement and AI assistance for banks. Delivered as continuously updated SaaS on Azure, it modernises on-premise CRM by replacing it with a cloud-native, AI-infused engagement platform.
