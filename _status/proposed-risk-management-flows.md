# Proposed Risk Management Process Flows

Multi-granularity process-flow decomposition for the **Risk Management** domain. The domain
currently defines only **2** processes (Fraud Detection, Fraud Investigation), so this proposal
adds **4 new processes** and decomposes all **6** processes into sub-processes, ordered steps and
supporting concepts. Grounded in APQC PCF risk-management processes (PCF 7.x "Manage Enterprise
Risk") plus the Basel framework, COSO ERM 2017, FSB Principles for an Effective Risk Appetite
Framework, BCBS 239 (risk data aggregation & reporting), model-risk governance (Fed SR 11-7 /
OCC 2011-12), stress testing (CCAR/DFAST, EBA), the Three Lines (of Defence) model, and fraud
operations (alert triage, SAR filing, loss recovery, model feedback).

Relationship verbs used (the 7): `depends_on`, `derived_from`, `supersedes`, `defined_in`,
`related_to`, `causes`, `mentions`.

Naming checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`. Collision-avoidance notes:
- Artifact **Fraud Alert** already exists (defined from Cards) — it is **reused** here (not
  re-declared) for Fraud Detection/Investigation. Listed in supporting concepts as REUSED.
- Artifact **Risk Profile** is reserved (Wealth & Investments investor artifact), so the
  enterprise risk picture uses **Enterprise Risk Profile** (NOT "Risk Profile").
- Role **Risk Analyst** is globally unique (distinct from "Performance Analyst", "KYC Analyst",
  "Reconciliation Analyst" etc. already in the registry).
- New process names are distinct from the Risk Management capabilities ("Enterprise Risk
  Management", "Credit/Market/Operational/Liquidity Risk Management", "Fraud Management") and from
  the existing processes "Fraud Detection"/"Fraud Investigation".
- Verb-form step names (e.g. "Set Risk Appetite", "Aggregate Risk Exposure", "Design Stress
  Scenario", "Triage Fraud Alert" is NOT reused — the existing Cards step "Triage Fraud Alert" is
  avoided; this domain uses "Screen Transaction Risk" / "Adjudicate Fraud Alert") are globally
  unique vs all existing Payments / Customer-Management / Lending / Deposits / Cards / Wealth
  steps, roles and artifacts.
