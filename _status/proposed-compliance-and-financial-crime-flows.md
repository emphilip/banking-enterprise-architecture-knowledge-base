# Proposed Compliance & Financial Crime Process Flows

Multi-granularity process-flow decomposition for the **Compliance & Financial Crime** domain. The
domain currently defines **3** processes (KYC Verification, Suspicious Activity Reporting,
Regulatory Filing), so this proposal adds **3 new processes** and decomposes all **6** processes
into sub-processes, ordered steps and supporting concepts. Grounded in APQC PCF category 11.0
("Manage Enterprise Risk, Compliance, Remediation, and Resiliency"), FATF Recommendations (esp.
Rec. 10 CDD, Rec. 12 PEPs, Rec. 20 STR/SAR reporting), Wolfsberg AML/sanctions program guidance,
FinCEN SAR (30-day filing) and CTR ($10,000 / 15-day) requirements, OFAC sanctions screening &
alert resolution (block + report within 10 business days, four-eyes), CDD/CIP/EDD onboarding, and
regulatory change/horizon-scanning practice.

Relationship verbs used (the 7): `depends_on`, `derived_from`, `supersedes`, `defined_in`,
`related_to`, `causes`, `mentions`.

Naming checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`. Collision-avoidance notes:
- Capability **Sanctions Screening** (L3, AML Monitoring) already exists, so the new PROCESS is
  named **Sanctions Screening Operations** (distinct from the capability), per the brief.
- Capability **Customer Due Diligence** (L3, Customer Management) exists; the new PROCESS is named
  **Customer Due Diligence Onboarding** (distinct from the capability). Process **Customer
  Onboarding** (Customer Management) also exists — the new domain process is intentionally the
  AML/CDD slice and is named to avoid that collision.
- Artifact **KYC Profile** already exists (Customer Management; alias "CDD Profile") — it is
  **REUSED** here (not re-declared) across KYC Verification and CDD Onboarding. Listed as REUSED.
- Role **Compliance Screening Officer** already exists (alias "Sanctions Officer") — **REUSED** for
  Sanctions Screening Operations (not re-declared). Role **KYC Analyst** already exists — **REUSED**
  for KYC Verification / CDD Onboarding.
- Existing steps avoided: "Screen Sanctions" (Payments), "Screen Customer" (Customer Onboarding),
  "Verify Identity" (Customer Onboarding), "File Suspicious Activity Report" & "Refer Suspicious
  Activity" (Risk/Cards fraud). New verb-form step names ("Resolve Sanctions Hit", "Triage AML
  Alert", "Lodge SAR Filing", "Scan Regulatory Change") are globally unique vs all prior domains'
  steps, roles and artifacts and are distinct from capability names.
- New role names "MLRO", "AML Compliance Officer", "Regulatory Change Manager" are globally unique
  (distinct from existing "Chief Risk Officer", "KYC Analyst", "Compliance Screening Officer").
