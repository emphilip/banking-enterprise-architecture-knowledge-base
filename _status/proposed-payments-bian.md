# Proposed Payments Business Capability Decomposition (L3 / L4)

Deeper decomposition of the **Payments** domain, extending the existing L1/L2/L3
canonical capabilities. Existing parents used verbatim: L1 *Payment Services*;
L2 *Payment Execution*, *Payment Clearing & Settlement*, *Payment Initiation*,
*Direct Debit Management*; L3 *Domestic Payments*, *Cross-Border Payments*,
*Real-Time Payments*. Names below are deduped against
`glossary/_canonical-names.md` and avoid the reserved names (Sanctions
Screening, Transaction Monitoring, Card Authorization, Payment Orchestration,
Payment Routing, Reconciliation & Settlement, Cash Management, Payment
Processing).
