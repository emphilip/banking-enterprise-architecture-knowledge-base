# Proposed L3 / L4 Business Capabilities — Deposits & Accounts

Grounded in the BIAN Service Landscape Account Management / Deposits business area
(Current Account, Savings Account, Deposit Account, Term Deposit, Account Recovery,
Interest Determination / Interest Rate Management, Customer Tax Handling, Overdraft
Facility, Account Statement, Account Reconciliation, Direct Debit Mandate) and APQC
PCF deposit / account-management processes. Extends existing parents only — does not
re-propose L1 Deposits or the four L2s (Account Opening, Account Servicing, Interest
Calculation, Overdraft Management). All names checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`
for global uniqueness; natural collisions disambiguated (e.g. "Deposit Interest Accrual"
vs Lending's "Interest Accrual Management"; "Deposit Account Origination" vs the existing
"Deposit Account Opening" process).
