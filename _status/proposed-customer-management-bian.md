# Proposed Customer Management Business Capability Decomposition (L3 / L4)

Deeper decomposition of the **Customer Management** domain, extending (not
duplicating) the existing L1/L2/L3 canonical capabilities. Existing parents used
verbatim: L1 *Customer Lifecycle Management*, *Marketing Management*; L2
*Customer Acquisition*, *Party Data Management*, *Customer Servicing*,
*Relationship Management*, *Campaign Management*, *Customer Segmentation*; L3
*Identity Verification*, *Customer Due Diligence*, *Customer Profile Management*,
*Case Management*.

Grounded in the BIAN Customer Management / Sales & Service / Party business areas
(service domains: Party Reference Data Directory, Customer Relationship
Management, Customer Case Management, Customer Behavior Insights, Customer
Agreement, Party Authentication, Customer Event History, Contact Dialogue,
Customer Campaign Management, Customer Profile, Customer Surveys, Lead/Opportunity
Management, Customer Offer, Party Lifecycle Management, Servicing Order, Contact
Routing) and APQC PCF 3.0 *Market and Sell Products and Services* and 6.0 *Manage
Customer Service*.

All names below are deduped against `glossary/_canonical-names.md` and avoid
reserved/collision names (KYC Management, Customer Onboarding [process],
Complaint Handling [process], Service Request Handling [process], Customer
Segmentation, Master Data Management [tech], CRM Platform [tech], Customer
Identity [tech], Identity Verification, Customer Due Diligence, Customer Profile
Management, Case Management). Where a natural name collides with an existing
process/tech entry, a disambiguated capability name is used.
