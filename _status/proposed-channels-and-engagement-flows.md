# Proposed Channels & Engagement Process Flows

Multi-granularity process-flow decomposition for the **Channels & Engagement** domain. The
domain currently defines only **1 process** (Branch Operations), so this proposal adds
**4 new processes** for adequate channel coverage and decomposes all **5** processes into
sub-processes, ordered steps, and supporting concepts.

Relationship verbs used (the 7): `depends_on`, `derived_from`, `supersedes`, `defined_in`,
`related_to`, `causes`, `mentions`.

Naming checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`. Collision-avoidance notes:
- Avoided the existing processes **Customer Onboarding** (Customer Management) and **Service Request Handling** (Customer Management); new process named **Digital Onboarding Journey** (digital account-opening front end, hands off to Customer Onboarding / KYC) and **Channel Servicing** (channel-side self-service, distinct from the back-office Service Request Handling).
- **Cash Management** already exists (Payments, corporate/treasury); the new branch/ATM process is named **ATM Cash Management** — distinct scope (ATM device cash supply chain).
- "Contact Center" / "Contact Center Platform" are a capability/tech-capability; the new process is the verb-shaped **Contact Centre Interaction** (UK spelling, globally unique).
- Roles deduped vs registry: avoided existing **Service Advisor**, **Onboarding Specialist**; new roles **Teller**, **Branch Manager**, **Contact Centre Agent**, **ATM Custodian**, **Digital Onboarding Specialist**, **Channel Operations Analyst**.
- All step names are verb-form and globally unique vs every prior domain's steps (e.g. "Authenticate Channel User" distinct from "Authenticate Requester"; "Replenish ATM Cash"; "Route Interaction").

Sources are cited per row (1-3 real URLs).
