# Proposed Finance & Treasury Business Capabilities

Deeper L3/L4 business capabilities extending the existing Finance & Treasury L1/L2
structure. Grounded in BIAN finance/treasury service domains (Financial Control,
Position Keeping, Financial Accounting, Management Accounting, Treasury, Asset &
Liability Management, Funding, Reservation Administration), IFRS (IFRS 9, IFRS 10,
IAS 1), Basel III capital framework (RWA, leverage, capital adequacy), ALM/FTP/IRRBB
practice, and APQC PCF 9.0 "Manage Financial Resources".

All names checked for global uniqueness against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`
(~414 capabilities plus processes, tech capabilities, systems and supporting
concepts across the 9 prior domains). Disambiguators applied where a natural name
collided (e.g. "Capital Adequacy Reporting" vs Risk-domain "Regulatory Capital
Adequacy"; "Funding Management" vs Risk-domain "Funding Risk Management";
"Intraday Liquidity Management" vs Risk-domain "Intraday Liquidity Monitoring").
