# Proposed Wealth & Investments Business Capabilities

Deeper L3 / L4 business capabilities extending the existing L1 (Wealth Management)
and L2 (Portfolio Management, Investment Advisory, Brokerage & Trading). All names
checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md` for global uniqueness; natural
collisions disambiguated (e.g. securities settlement named "Trade Settlement" to
avoid the Payments process "Reconciliation & Settlement"). Each row maps to a
named BIAN Investment Management / Wealth service domain, or, where wealth sits
outside BIAN's retail/commercial core, cites an alternative authority (CFA
Institute, MiFID II/ESMA, SEC Reg BI, FINRA, DTCC).
