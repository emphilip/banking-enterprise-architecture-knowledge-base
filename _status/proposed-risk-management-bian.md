# Proposed Risk Management Business Capabilities

Deeper L3 / L4 business capabilities extending the existing L1 (Enterprise Risk
Management) and the five L2s (Credit Risk Management, Market Risk Management,
Operational Risk Management, Liquidity Risk Management, Fraud Management). All
names checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md` for global uniqueness across
every section (capabilities, processes, tech, systems, supporting concepts).

Disambiguators applied to avoid collisions with prior domains:
- Risk capital sits under "Regulatory Capital Adequacy" / "Risk Capital
  Calculation" — distinct from the Finance & Treasury cap "Regulatory Capital
  Management".
- Liquidity risk caps named "Liquidity Risk Measurement", "LCR & NSFR
  Management", "Funding Risk Management" — distinct from Finance & Treasury
  "Liquidity Management".
- Fraud caps named "Fraud Risk Assessment", "Fraud Monitoring", "Fraud Loss
  Recovery" — distinct from the existing processes "Fraud Detection" /
  "Fraud Investigation".
- "Limit & Exposure Management" / "Trading Book Risk" do not collide with the
  Payments tech sub-cap "Liquidity & Limit Checker".

Each row maps to a named BIAN risk service domain (Credit Risk Models, Market
Risk Models, Operational Risk Models, Liquidity Risk Models, Fraud Model,
Position Keeping, Collateral Asset Administration, plus the Credit/Market Risk
Operating Model domains) or to a named Basel/regulatory authority (BCBS Basel
III/IV, FRTB, SA-CCR, IRB, IFRS 9, ICAAP/ILAAP, ECB/EBA, Fed SR 11-7, COSO ERM,
ORX). Relationships use only the 7 verbs and prefer existing registry tech names
(Analytics Platform, Machine Learning Platform, Fraud Analytics, Threat
Detection, Data Warehousing).
