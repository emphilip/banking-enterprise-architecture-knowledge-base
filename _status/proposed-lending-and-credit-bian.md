# Proposed Lending & Credit Business Capabilities (L3 / L4)

Deeper L3 and L4 business capabilities for the **Lending & Credit** domain, grounded in
the BIAN Lending / Loans business area service domains (Consumer Loan, Corporate Loan,
Mortgage Loan, Credit Facility, Credit Management, Collateral Administration / Collateral
Asset Administration, Customer Credit Rating, Disbursement, Repayment, Delinquent Account
Handling, Collection/Recovery, Loan Syndication, Guarantee, Leasing, Credit Risk Models)
and APQC PCF lending/credit processes.

Extends (does NOT duplicate) the existing hierarchy:
- L1 Lending → L2 Loan Origination, Loan Servicing, Collateral Management, Collections & Recovery
- Existing L3: Credit Decisioning (under Loan Origination)

All names checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md` for global uniqueness. Disambiguators
appended where a natural name collides (e.g. "Loan Application Management" avoids "Application
Processing"/"Application Management"; "Credit Assessment" already an alias of Credit Decisioning,
so "Affordability Assessment" is used; "Loan Pricing" distinct from existing names).

NOTE: bian.org service-domain pages return HTTP 403 to automated fetch and the V9 Service
Landscape is delivered as an index-level value-chain PDF; service-domain names below are
corroborated via OpenRiskManual's BIAN Service Domain catalog and BIAN semantic-API listings.
