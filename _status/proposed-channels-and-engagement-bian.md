# Proposed Channels & Engagement Business Capabilities (L3 / L4)

Extends existing Channels & Engagement capabilities without duplicating them. Parents already in the registry (do NOT re-propose): L1 Channel Management; L2 Digital Banking, Branch Banking, Contact Center, ATM Management; L3 (under Digital Banking) Mobile Banking, Online Banking.

Grounded in BIAN's channel service domains across the Servicing, Cross Channel and Distribution areas of the Service Landscape (e.g. Contact Center Operations, Contact Routing, Servicing Position Management, Customer Workbench, Point of Service, Party Authentication, Session Dialogue, Customer Access Entitlement, Channel Activity History, eBranch Operations, Branch Location Management, Branch Currency Management, ATM Network Operations, Card Terminal Operations). Where a capability sits outside BIAN's core (e.g. conversational/AI engagement, omnichannel personalisation, accessibility) an APQC PCF or digital-banking practice authority is cited.

All names checked for global uniqueness against `glossary/_canonical-names.md` (capabilities, processes, tech, systems, sub-capabilities, glossary terms). Disambiguators appended where a natural name collided: "Digital Onboarding" (vs Customer Onboarding process and Customer Acquisition cap); "Interaction Routing" was already taken as an alias so "Channel Interaction Routing" is used; "Contact Dialogue Management" exists in Customer Management so contact-center work uses "Inbound Contact Servicing"; tech sub-caps Self-Service Virtual Assistant / Agent Assist are avoided by naming business caps "Conversational Banking" and "Agent Desktop Servicing".
