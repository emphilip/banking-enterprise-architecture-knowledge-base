# Proposed Cards Process Flows

Multi-granularity process-flow decomposition for the **Cards** domain. The domain currently
defines only 2 processes (Card Issuance, Dispute Resolution), so this proposal adds **3 new
processes** and decomposes all **5** processes into sub-processes, ordered steps and supporting
concepts. Grounded in APQC PCF card processes plus card-scheme operations (Visa/Mastercard
authorization & clearing, EMV cryptograms/CVM, PCI DSS, Reg Z / Reg E error-resolution rights,
scheme chargeback reason codes).

Relationship verbs used (the 7): `depends_on`, `derived_from`, `supersedes`, `defined_in`,
`related_to`, `causes`, `mentions`.

Naming checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`. Collision-avoidance notes:
- Process **Chargeback Processing** is used (NOT "Chargeback Management", which is a reserved alias of the capability "Dispute Management").
- Artifact **Card Account Application** is used (NOT "Card Application", to avoid colliding with the card-application capability/context).
- Process/step names kept distinct from the Cards capabilities "Card Issuing", "Card Authorization", "Dispute Management", "Card Lifecycle Management" and the tech capability "Card Processing".
- Verb-form step names (e.g. "Authorize Transaction", "Raise Chargeback", "Block Compromised Card") are globally unique vs all existing Payments / Customer-Management / Lending / Deposits steps, roles and artifacts.
