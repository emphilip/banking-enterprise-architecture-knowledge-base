# Proposed Finance & Treasury Process Flows

Multi-granularity process-flow decomposition for the **Finance & Treasury** domain. The domain
currently defines **0 processes**, so this proposal adds **5 new processes** and decomposes each
into sub-processes, ordered steps and supporting concepts. Grounded in APQC PCF **"Manage
Financial Resources"** (record-to-report / general accounting / financial close, planning &
management reporting), IFRS consolidation (IFRS 10 / IAS 27 / IAS 28, intercompany elimination),
Basel capital reporting (COREP / FINREP under CRR, FR Y-9C / FR Y-14 and CCAR capital plan
submission), and treasury / ALM / FTP operations (intraday liquidity, LCR / NSFR, nostro
reconciliation, matched-maturity FTP curve, ALCO governance).

Relationship verbs used (the 7): `depends_on`, `derived_from`, `supersedes`, `defined_in`,
`related_to`, `causes`, `mentions`.

Naming checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`. Collision-avoidance notes:
- Process **"Cash Management"** already exists (Payments domain) — this domain uses **"Cash &
  Liquidity Management"** as instructed.
- Process **"Performance Reporting"** is a Wealth & Investments process — this domain uses
  **"Management Reporting"** for internal FP&A / board MI (distinct name).
- Process **"Regulatory Filing"** exists (Compliance & Financial Crime) — this domain uses
  **"Regulatory Capital Reporting"** as instructed (prudential COREP/FINREP/CCAR, not AML/SAR).
- Existing **steps** avoided / not reused: "Compile Regulatory Return", "Submit Regulatory
  Return", "Compile Risk Report", "Forecast Cash Flow", "Monitor Liquidity Position", "Determine
  Cash Position", "Report Cash Status", "Obtain Board Approval", "Submit Capital Plan", "Project
  Capital Impact", "Assess Capital Adequacy", "Reconcile Positions", "Sign Off Reconciliation",
  "Post Adjustment". This domain mints fresh verb-form names ("Post Closing Journals",
  "Run Consolidation", "Calculate RWA", "Build FTP Curve", "Project Liquidity Position", etc.).
- Existing **roles** avoided / not reused: "Treasury Cash Manager" (Payments), "Reconciliation
  Analyst" (Payments), "Regulatory Reporting Analyst" (Compliance). This domain mints
  "Financial Controller", "Treasury Analyst", "Regulatory Reporting Specialist (Capital)" etc.
- Existing **artifacts** avoided / not reused: "Cash Forecast" (Payments), "Reconciliation
  Report" (Payments), "Risk Profile" (Wealth), "Risk Report" (Risk). This domain mints
  "Trial Balance", "Journal Entry", "Consolidated Financial Statements", etc.
- New process names are distinct from the Finance & Treasury **capabilities** ("Finance
  Management", "General Ledger Accounting", "Financial Reporting", "Regulatory Capital
  Management", "Treasury Management", "Liquidity Management", "Asset Liability Management").
