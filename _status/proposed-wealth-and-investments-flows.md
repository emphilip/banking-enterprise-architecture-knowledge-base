# Proposed Wealth & Investments Process Flows

Multi-granularity process-flow decomposition for the **Wealth & Investments** domain. The domain
currently defines only **1 process** (Wealth Onboarding), so this proposal adds **4 new processes**
and decomposes all **5** processes into sub-processes, ordered steps and supporting concepts.
Grounded in APQC PCF wealth/investment processes plus industry practice: MiFID II / ESMA suitability
and Reg BI care obligation, KYC for investors, the front-to-back securities trade lifecycle
(OMS/EMS, best execution, allocation), T+1 settlement, corporate-actions processing, and GIPS
performance reporting (time-weighted return, benchmark, attribution).

Relationship verbs used (the 7): `depends_on`, `derived_from`, `supersedes`, `defined_in`,
`related_to`, `causes`, `mentions`.

Naming checked against `glossary/_canonical-names.md`. Collision-avoidance notes:
- Process **Trade Order Management** is used (NOT "Trade Execution", which is a reserved alias of the L2 capability **Brokerage & Trading**). Step verb is **Execute Order** (distinct from the capability alias).
- Securities settlement step is **Settle Trade** — NOT "Confirm Settlement" (existing Payments step) and NOT "Settle Trade Payment" (existing Trade Finance step).
- Event **Trade Executed Event** is used (NOT "Trade Settlement Event", which already exists in Trade Finance Processing).
- Process/step/role/artifact names kept distinct from the Wealth capabilities "Portfolio Management", "Investment Advisory", "Brokerage & Trading" and from all existing Payments / Customer-Management / Lending / Deposits / Cards concepts. Existing "Wealth Onboarding" parent is reused verbatim.
- Verb-form step names (e.g. "Capture Risk Profile", "Generate Rebalance Proposal", "Execute Order", "Settle Trade", "Calculate Returns") are globally unique vs the registry.
