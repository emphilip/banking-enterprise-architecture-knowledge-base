# Proposed Cards Business Capabilities (L3 / L4)

Extends existing Cards capabilities without duplicating them. Parents already in the registry (do NOT re-propose): L1 Cards Management; L2 Card Issuing, Card Authorization, Dispute Management, Card Lifecycle Management. All names checked for global uniqueness against `glossary/_canonical-names.md` (capabilities, processes, tech, systems, sub-capabilities). Disambiguators appended where a natural name collided (e.g. Card Clearing & Settlement vs Payments' Payment Clearing & Settlement; Card Fraud Authorization Screening to avoid Fraud Management collision).
