# Contact Center Platform

**Definition.** Contact Center Platform is the technology capability that routes and manages inbound and outbound voice and digital customer interactions, providing IVR, queuing, agent desktop, and analytics for service operations.
**Also known as:** CCaaS.

- Contact Center Platform is related to CRM Platform.

## Details
Contact Center Platform handles omnichannel routing, agent assist, and interaction analytics for the contact center. Legacy on-premise systems such as Avaya Aura and Cisco UCCE are hardware-bound and costly to scale, while cloud CCaaS like Amazon Connect and Genesys Cloud provide elastic, AI-enabled routing and analytics. It depends on the Conversational AI capability and integrates with the CRM Platform for context.
