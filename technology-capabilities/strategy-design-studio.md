# Strategy Design Studio

**Definition.** Strategy Design Studio is the technology capability that provides no-code authoring, simulation and champion/challenger testing of credit strategies and rules before deployment to the decision flow.
**Also known as:** Strategy Authoring Studio, Champion-Challenger Workbench.

## Relationships
- Strategy Design Studio is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Strategy Design Studio is derived from Decision Rules Engine.
- Strategy Design Studio is related to Analytics Platform.

## Details
Strategy Design Studio gives credit risk teams a visual, no-code environment to author decision strategies, simulate their impact and run champion/challenger tests before promoting them to production. Experian PowerCurve Strategy Management exemplifies this pattern, letting analysts design and test strategies safely so that only validated rule changes are deployed to the live decision flow.
