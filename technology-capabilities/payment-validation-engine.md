# Payment Validation Engine

**Definition.** Payment Validation Engine is the technology capability that performs schema, syntactic, business-rule and scheme-format validation of payment instructions to maximise straight-through processing and reject malformed payments early.
**Also known as:** Validation Engine, STP Validation.

## Relationships
- Payment Validation Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Payment Validation Engine is derived from Payment Orchestration.
- Payment Validation Engine is related to Payment Message Transformer.
- Payment Validation Engine is related to Payment Repair Workbench.

## Details
Payment Validation Engine applies layered checks (schema, syntax, business rules and scheme-specific formats) so that only well-formed, compliant payments proceed to routing and settlement. Early rejection of malformed payments raises straight-through processing rates, a design seen in FIS Open Payment Framework and Finastra Global PAYplus.
