# Identity Proofing Service

**Definition.** Identity Proofing Service is the customer identity technology sub-capability that validates document, biometric and data signals to establish a customer's identity at onboarding and high-risk events.
**Also known as:** Identity Verification Service, ID Proofing.

## Relationships
- Identity Proofing Service is defined in the Security & Identity domain.
- Identity Proofing Service is derived from Customer Identity.
- Identity Proofing Service depends on Intelligent Document Processing.
- Identity Proofing Service is related to Identity Verification.

## Details
Identity Proofing Service orchestrates document capture and authentication, biometric liveness checks and data-source verification to confirm a customer is who they claim to be. It is invoked at digital onboarding and step-up moments, drawing on document-processing technology to extract and validate identity evidence, and feeds verified identities into customer records and risk decisions.
