# Customer 360 View

**Definition.** Customer 360 View is the CRM technology sub-capability that assembles holdings, transactions, interactions and life events into a single, aggregated and actionable profile for advisors and service agents.
**Also known as:** Single Customer View, C360.

## Relationships
- Customer 360 View is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Customer 360 View is derived from CRM Platform.
- Customer 360 View depends on Master Data Management.
- Customer 360 View is related to Contact & Account Management.

## Details
Customer 360 View aggregates data from core banking, transaction systems, CRM and external sources into a consolidated profile, giving frontline staff a complete picture of relationships, products and recent activity. It relies on master data for an authoritative party identity and feeds downstream personalisation, next-best-action and campaign processes in banking CRM deployments.
