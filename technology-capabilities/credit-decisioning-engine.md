# Credit Decisioning Engine

**Definition.** Credit Decisioning Engine is the technology capability that executes scoring models, policy rules, and affordability checks to approve, decline, or price credit applications in real time.
**Also known as:** Decision Engine.

## Relationships
- Credit Decisioning Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.

- Credit Decisioning Engine mentions Zest AI.
- Credit Decisioning Engine mentions Upstart.
- Credit Decisioning Engine is related to Loan Origination Platform.

## Details
Credit Decisioning Engine combines deterministic policy rules with statistical and machine-learning scorecards to render underwriting decisions. Legacy scorecard engines are rules-only and slow to retrain, while modern providers such as Zest AI and Upstart use explainable ML to widen approvals and reduce risk. It is invoked by the Loan Origination Platform and integrates with bureau and alternative data sources.
