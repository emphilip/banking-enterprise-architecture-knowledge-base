# GIPS Composite Service

**Definition.** GIPS Composite Service maintains composites and benchmarks and produces GIPS-compliant performance reports and disclosures from measured returns.
**Also known as:** GIPS Reporting Service, Composite Management Service, Performance Composite Engine.

## Relationships
- GIPS Composite Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- GIPS Composite Service is derived from Performance & Attribution Engine.

## Details
GIPS Composite Service groups discretionary portfolios that share a common investment mandate, objective or strategy into composites, the unit of presentation required by the Global Investment Performance Standards. It applies composite-inclusion and exclusion rules, computes asset-weighted composite returns, tracks the number of portfolios and dispersion, and assigns the appropriate benchmark. From these it generates the GIPS-compliant presentation and required disclosures so a firm can claim compliance and present a fair, comparable track record to prospective clients.
