# Governance Risk & Compliance Platform

**Definition.** Governance Risk & Compliance Platform is the Integrated Risk Management (IRM/GRC) technology capability that unifies the operational-risk register, risk-and-control self-assessment, loss-event capture, policy and control management, issues and actions and audit across the enterprise on a single workflow-driven system.
**Also known as:** GRC Platform, Integrated Risk Management Platform, IRM Platform.

## Details
Governance Risk & Compliance Platform drives risk-and-control self-assessment (RCSA) workflows — inherent risk, control effectiveness, residual and target risk, KRIs and scenario analysis — together with loss-event databases, policy and control libraries, regulatory-obligation mapping and issue tracking. Legacy suites such as IBM OpenPages and Archer IRM are highly configurable but implementation-heavy, while cloud platforms ServiceNow IRM and MetricStream deliver guided RCSA, connected-GRC and AI-driven risk quantification on a unified workflow engine spanning operational, third-party, IT and compliance risk.
