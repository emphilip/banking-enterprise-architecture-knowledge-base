# IRRBB Analytics Engine

**Definition.** IRRBB Analytics Engine is the technology capability that measures interest-rate risk in the banking book: cash-flow generation, repricing and gap analysis, economic value of equity (EVE) and net interest income (NII) sensitivity under prescribed and internal rate-shock scenarios.
**Also known as:** Interest Rate Risk Engine, EVE/NII Engine, Banking Book Rate Risk Engine.

## Relationships
- IRRBB Analytics Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- IRRBB Analytics Engine is derived from Asset Liability Management Engine.
- IRRBB Analytics Engine is related to Behavioural Modelling Service.

## Details
IRRBB Analytics Engine generates principal and interest cash flows for every banking-book instrument, builds repricing gaps, and computes EVE under the six standardised rate shocks of the Basel IRRBB framework plus internal scenarios, alongside NII sensitivity over a 12-month-plus horizon. It separates the supervisory outlier test from internal earnings analysis and consumes deposit decay and prepayment assumptions from the Behavioural Modelling Service.
