# Product & Pricing Engine

**Definition.** Product & Pricing Engine is the technology capability that configures deposit products and their terms, tiers, fee schedules and pricing rules without code, enabling rapid product manufacture and parameter-driven product behaviour.
**Also known as:** Deposit Product Engine, Product Configurator (Deposits), Universal Product Engine.

## Relationships
- Product & Pricing Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Product & Pricing Engine is derived from Core Banking Processing.
- Product & Pricing Engine is related to Deposits.

## Details
Product & Pricing Engine lets institutions define deposit products, tiers, fee schedules and pricing rules as configuration rather than code. Thought Machine Vault Core uses a Universal Product Engine in which products are expressed as smart contracts, and SaaScada exposes product configuration over real-time data so new current and savings products can be assembled and re-priced without bespoke development.
