# Payment Sanctions Filter

**Definition.** Payment Sanctions Filter is the technology capability providing in-flight, sub-second sanctions/watchlist and fraud-control hooks embedded in the payment flow that hold, release or reject payments before settlement.
**Also known as:** Inline Sanctions Filter, In-Flight Screening.

## Relationships
- Payment Sanctions Filter is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Payment Sanctions Filter is derived from Payment Orchestration.
- Payment Sanctions Filter depends on Transaction Monitoring Platform.
- Payment Sanctions Filter is related to Fraud Analytics.
- Payment Sanctions Filter mentions Sanctions Screening.

## Details
Payment Sanctions Filter embeds sub-second screening and fraud-control hooks directly in the payment flow, holding, releasing or rejecting payments before settlement to meet real-time scheme deadlines. It calls watchlist and screening engines inline, a pattern required by instant-payment sanctions risk controls and supported by transaction-screening platforms.
