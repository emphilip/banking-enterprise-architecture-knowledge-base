# iPaaS Connector Hub

**Definition.** iPaaS Connector Hub is the cloud-native integration layer offering pre-built application and data connectors and low-code flow design to integrate SaaS, on-prem, and data sources.
**Also known as:** Integration Platform as a Service, Cloud Integration Hub, Connector Catalog.

## Relationships
- iPaaS Connector Hub is defined in the Integration & APIs domain.
- iPaaS Connector Hub is derived from Integration Platform.
- iPaaS Connector Hub is related to Adapter Framework.

## Details
iPaaS Connector Hub delivers integration as a managed cloud service: a library of pre-built connectors for SaaS apps (Salesforce, Workday, NetSuite), databases, and messaging, combined with a low-code flow designer, scheduling, and managed runtime so teams build integrations without managing servers. Celigo and IBM App Connect provide connector catalogs, recipe templates, and on-prem agents for hybrid connectivity. The hub handles authentication, pagination, retry, and rate-limit handling per connector so flow authors focus on mapping and orchestration logic.
