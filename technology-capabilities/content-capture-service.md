# Content Capture Service

**Definition.** Content Capture Service scans, ingests, and digitises paper and electronic documents into searchable, classified content with extracted metadata.
**Also known as:** Capture Service, Document Imaging, Content Capture.

## Relationships
- Content Capture Service is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Content Capture Service is derived from Document Management.
- Content Capture Service depends on Content Repository.
- Content Capture Service is related to Intelligent Document Recognition.

## Details
Content Capture Service is the on-ramp into an ECM platform, converting inbound documents from scanners, email, fax, and upload into digital objects. Capture applies imaging clean-up, optical character recognition, indexing, and document classification so each item arrives in the repository already searchable and tagged with key metadata. High-volume banking processes such as account opening, loan applications, and KYC document collection depend on capture to remove manual data entry. Once captured and indexed, documents are filed in the content repository and can trigger downstream content workflows. Capture is where intelligent recognition adds machine-learning classification and field extraction.
