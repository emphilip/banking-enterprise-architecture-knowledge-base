# Document Management

**Definition.** Document Management is the technology capability that captures, stores, indexes, retains, and retrieves enterprise documents and content with version control, records retention, and access governance.
**Also known as:** ECM, Content Services.

## Relationships
- Document Management is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Document Management is related to Intelligent Document Processing.
- Document Management mentions IBM FileNet.
- Document Management mentions OpenText Documentum.

## Details
Document Management provides the content repository for KYC packs, loan files, statements, and correspondence, enforcing retention and legal hold policies. Legacy enterprise content management suites such as IBM FileNet and OpenText Documentum are on-premise and integration-heavy, while modern content services are API-first and cloud-native. It pairs with Intelligent Document Processing to extract data from stored content.
