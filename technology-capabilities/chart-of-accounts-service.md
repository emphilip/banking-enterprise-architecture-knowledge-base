# Chart Of Accounts Service

**Definition.** Chart Of Accounts Service is the technology capability that manages the accounting structures and master data of the ledger: the chart of accounts, ledgers (leading and parallel for multi-GAAP), accounting dimensions and segments (cost centre, profit centre, segment) and account hierarchies.
**Also known as:** COA Service, Accounting Structure Service, Ledger & Segment Master, Account Master Service.

## Relationships
- Chart Of Accounts Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Chart Of Accounts Service is derived from General Ledger Engine.
- Chart Of Accounts Service is related to Allocation Engine.

## Details
Chart Of Accounts Service governs the account master and the dimensional model that every posting references. Leading and parallel ledgers let the bank maintain local GAAP and IFRS valuations side by side, while accounting segments (profit centre, cost centre, business area) enable management reporting and document splitting. Hierarchies roll detailed accounts up to financial-statement nodes for trial balance and reporting.
