# BI & Reporting Service

**Definition.** BI & Reporting Service produces governed formatted and interactive reports and ad-hoc analyses over curated data for business and compliance reporting.
**Also known as:** BI Reporting Service, Reporting Service, Operational Reporting.

## Relationships
- BI & Reporting Service is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- BI & Reporting Service is derived from Analytics Platform.
- BI & Reporting Service depends on Semantic Layer Service.
- BI & Reporting Service is related to Dashboarding Service.

## Details
BI & Reporting Service is the consumption tier of a business-intelligence platform, generating pixel-perfect operational reports, scheduled distributions, and exploratory analyses over governed data. Modern BI platforms connect to the warehouse or semantic model, push queries down to the underlying engine, and add caching, row-level security, and certified content so figures are consistent and auditable. In banking this supports regulatory and management reporting where lineage and reproducibility matter. By consuming a shared semantic layer, reports reuse a single set of metric definitions rather than re-encoding business logic in each report.
