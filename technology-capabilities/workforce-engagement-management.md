# Workforce Engagement Management

**Definition.** Workforce Engagement Management is the technology sub-capability providing forecasting, scheduling, quality management, recording and performance and coaching tooling for contact-centre staff.
**Also known as:** WEM, Workforce Optimization, WFO.

## Relationships
- Workforce Engagement Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Workforce Engagement Management is derived from Contact Center Platform.
- Workforce Engagement Management is related to Interaction Analytics.

## Details
Workforce Engagement Management forecasts contact volumes and handle times (often using Erlang C and now ML models), builds skills-aware schedules and shifts, manages intraday adherence, and runs quality evaluation, recording, coaching and gamification. It blends classic workforce management (WFM) with quality and performance tooling into the modern WEM stack. Verint and Genesys position WEM as the staffing and quality engine that aligns the right agents to forecast demand and continuously improves performance.
