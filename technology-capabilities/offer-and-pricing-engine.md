# Offer & Pricing Engine

**Definition.** Offer & Pricing Engine is the technology capability that generates risk-based pricing, rates and structured offers for an application, applying pricing strategies and producing presentable loan offers.
**Also known as:** Loan Pricing Engine, Offer Generation Service.

## Relationships
- Offer & Pricing Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Offer & Pricing Engine is derived from Loan Origination Platform.
- Offer & Pricing Engine depends on Credit Scoring Service.

## Details
Offer & Pricing Engine applies risk-based pricing strategies to a scored application, computing rates, fees and term structures and assembling presentable loan offers. Experian PowerCurve Originations couples decisioning with value-based offer generation, illustrating how pricing and offer logic consume credit scores to deliver consistent, compliant offers within the origination flow.
