# Income Verification Service

**Definition.** Income Verification Service is the technology capability that verifies borrower income and employment from documents, payroll, open-banking or bureau sources to feed affordability and DTI calculations.
**Also known as:** Income & Employment Verification, Payroll Verification Service.

## Relationships
- Income Verification Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Income Verification Service is derived from Affordability Assessment Engine.
- Income Verification Service depends on Bureau Data Integration.

## Details
Income Verification Service confirms a borrower's income and employment by drawing on payroll feeds, bank-statement and open-banking data, uploaded documents and bureau-linked sources. Underwriting tools such as Inscribe extract and validate income from these inputs so that verified figures flow into affordability and debt-to-income calculations, reducing manual document review during origination.
