# Disclosure Management Service

**Definition.** Disclosure Management Service is the technology capability that produces narrative and statutory disclosures and board packages from consolidated data, with collaborative authoring, audit trail and tagged regulatory output (XBRL/iXBRL, ESEF).
**Also known as:** Narrative Reporting Service, XBRL Tagging Service, Statutory Reporting Service.

## Relationships
- Disclosure Management Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Disclosure Management Service is derived from Financial Consolidation Platform.
- Disclosure Management Service is related to Consolidation Engine.
- Disclosure Management Service is related to Regulatory Reporting Engine.

## Details
Disclosure Management Service assembles annual reports, board packs and statutory accounts by embedding live figures from the Consolidation Engine into managed documents, so a single number change reflows every dependent table and narrative. Multi-author workflow, review/sign-off and full audit trail control the production cycle, and the service applies iXBRL/ESEF tagging to generate machine-readable filings for regulators and exchanges.
