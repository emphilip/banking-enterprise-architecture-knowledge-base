# Financial Consolidation Platform

**Definition.** Financial Consolidation Platform is the corporate performance and financial-close technology capability that automates the group financial close, multi-entity and multi-GAAP consolidation (intercompany elimination, currency translation, minority interest), planning and budgeting, management and statutory reporting, and regulatory disclosure (XBRL/ESEF).
**Also known as:** CPM Platform, EPM Platform, Consolidation & Close Platform, Corporate Performance Management.

## Details
Financial Consolidation Platform collects trial balances from entities, applies currency translation, intercompany elimination and ownership/minority-interest calculations, and produces consolidated multi-GAAP and management views per IFRS 10. Built-in financial data quality validates and certifies source data with drill-back. Disclosure management produces tagged XBRL/iXBRL and ESEF statutory output. Modern unified cloud platforms OneStream and Oracle EPM Cloud supersede legacy on-premise products Oracle Hyperion HFM and SAP BPC, while Anaplan adds connected planning, budgeting and scenario modelling integrated with the consolidation model.
