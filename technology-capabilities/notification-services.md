# Notification Services

**Definition.** Notification Services is the technology capability that composes and delivers transactional and marketing communications to customers across SMS, email, push, and in-app channels with templating and delivery tracking.
**Also known as:** Messaging, Notifications.

## Relationships
- Notification Services is defined in the Integration & APIs domain.
- Notification Services depends on the Integration Platform capability.
- Notification Services depends on the Data Streaming capability.
- Notification Services is related to Digital Channel Platform.

## Details
Notification Services centralizes event-triggered alerts such as payment confirmations, fraud warnings, and statement availability. It subscribes to events from the Data Streaming capability and renders multi-channel messages with consent and preference enforcement. Modern realizations are cloud-native communication APIs offering high deliverability and real-time status webhooks. It is a shared service across channels and operations.
