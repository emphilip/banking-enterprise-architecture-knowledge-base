# Onboarding Journey Orchestrator

**Definition.** Onboarding Journey Orchestrator is the technology sub-capability delivering configurable account-opening and origination journeys through digital channels, with form orchestration, document upload and progress save and resume.
**Also known as:** Onboarding Journey Engine, Origination Front-End, Digital Onboarding Module.

## Relationships
- Onboarding Journey Orchestrator is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Onboarding Journey Orchestrator is derived from Digital Channel Platform.
- Onboarding Journey Orchestrator depends on KYC Onboarding Platform.
- Onboarding Journey Orchestrator is related to Loan Origination Platform.

## Details
Onboarding Journey Orchestrator drives multi-step, branching origination flows with dynamic forms, eligibility gating, document capture and save-and-resume so customers can pause and continue across devices. It invokes identity and KYC checks during the flow and hands off to product origination back ends to instantiate accounts or loans. Digital banking suites such as Temenos Infinity and Alkami expose low-code journey builders so product teams configure onboarding without releasing app code.
