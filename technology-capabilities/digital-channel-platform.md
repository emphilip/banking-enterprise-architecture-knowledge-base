# Digital Channel Platform

**Definition.** Digital Channel Platform is the technology capability that delivers customer-facing web and mobile banking experiences, orchestrating journeys, presentation, and engagement across digital touchpoints.
**Also known as:** Digital Banking Platform.

## Relationships
- Digital Channel Platform is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Channel Platform depends on the API Management capability.
- Digital Channel Platform depends on the Customer Identity capability.
- Digital Channel Platform mentions Legacy Online Banking.
- Digital Channel Platform mentions Backbase.
- Digital Channel Platform mentions Q2.
- Digital Channel Platform is related to Contact Center Platform.

## Details
Digital Channel Platform provides the engagement layer for mobile and online banking, composing micro-frontends and journeys over backend APIs. Legacy online banking is monolithic and difficult to evolve, while modern platforms like Backbase and Q2 offer composable, API-led engagement banking with rapid release cycles. It depends on the API Management and Customer Identity capabilities and consumes Notification Services.
