# Goal-Based Planning Engine

**Definition.** Goal-Based Planning Engine is the technology capability that lets investors define financial goals such as retirement, home purchase and education, runs cashflow and Monte-Carlo style projections and simulations, and converts plans into funding and investment recommendations.
**Also known as:** Financial Planning Engine, Goals-Based Planning Service, Cashflow Planning Engine.

## Relationships
- Goal-Based Planning Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Goal-Based Planning Engine is derived from Investment Advisory Platform.
- Goal-Based Planning Engine depends on Risk Profiling Engine.
- Goal-Based Planning Engine is related to Investment Advisory.

## Details
Goal-Based Planning Engine models discrete investor goals (retirement, home purchase, education) with funding amounts, time horizons and priorities, then runs deterministic cashflow and Monte-Carlo simulations to estimate probability of success and required savings. It consumes the investor's risk profile from the Risk Profiling Engine to bound feasible allocations and converts plans into funding and product recommendations. Platforms such as InvestCloud NaviPlan and Temenos WealthSuite realize cashflow and goals-based planning across retirement, decumulation and multi-goal scenarios.
