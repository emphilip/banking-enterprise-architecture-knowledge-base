# Card Rewards Engine

**Definition.** Card Rewards Engine is the technology capability that calculates and accrues card rewards, points, cashback and loyalty benefits against qualifying transactions, applying earn rules, tiers, multipliers and redemption against the cardholder account.
**Also known as:** Loyalty & Rewards Engine, Points Engine, Cashback Engine.

## Relationships
- Card Rewards Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Card Rewards Engine is derived from Card Processing.
- Card Rewards Engine depends on Card Account Ledger.
- Card Rewards Engine is related to Card Issuing.

## Details
Card Rewards Engine evaluates each cleared transaction against earn rules, merchant-category multipliers and tier qualifications to accrue points or cashback, then posts redemptions and statement credits back to the cardholder account ledger. It supports promotional offers and threshold bonuses for issuer card programs. Marqeta's program management tooling and Fiserv Credit Select describe loyalty and rewards configuration for issuer-managed card products.
