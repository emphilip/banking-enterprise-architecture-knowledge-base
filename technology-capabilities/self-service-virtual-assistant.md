# Self-Service Virtual Assistant

**Definition.** Self-Service Virtual Assistant is the conversational AI technology sub-capability that provides a customer-facing chat or voice agent using NLU and NLG to answer queries, surface data and execute self-service banking tasks with human handoff.
**Also known as:** Digital Assistant, Customer Chatbot.

## Relationships
- Self-Service Virtual Assistant is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Self-Service Virtual Assistant is derived from Conversational AI.
- Self-Service Virtual Assistant depends on Generative AI Platform.
- Self-Service Virtual Assistant is related to Customer Servicing.

## Details
Self-Service Virtual Assistant lets customers interact in natural language across chat and voice channels to check balances, make payments, resolve queries and complete servicing tasks, escalating to a human agent when needed. It combines natural-language understanding and generation with secure access to banking data and APIs, and is delivered on platforms such as Kasisto KAI and conversational services like Amazon Lex.
