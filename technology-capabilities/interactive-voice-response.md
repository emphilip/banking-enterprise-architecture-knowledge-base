# Interactive Voice Response

**Definition.** Interactive Voice Response is the technology sub-capability providing the self-service voice front door using DTMF and conversational prompts, menu and flow design, text-to-speech and call data collection before routing.
**Also known as:** IVR Engine, Voice Self-Service Engine, Conversational IVR.

## Relationships
- Interactive Voice Response is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Interactive Voice Response is derived from Contact Center Platform.
- Interactive Voice Response is related to Conversational AI.

## Details
Interactive Voice Response presents callers with DTMF (touch-tone) and natural-language prompts driven by a visual flow designer, using automatic speech recognition and text-to-speech to capture intent and account data, authenticate, and resolve simple requests before any agent is engaged. Modern conversational IVR replaces rigid menus with open-ended speech understanding and connects to back-end APIs for balances, payments and status. Genesys and Amazon Connect describe IVR as the programmable self-service tier feeding the routing engine with collected context.
