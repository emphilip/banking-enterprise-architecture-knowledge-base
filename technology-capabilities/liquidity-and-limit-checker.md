# Liquidity & Limit Checker

**Definition.** Liquidity & Limit Checker is the technology capability that performs real-time funds availability, liquidity buffer and limit / debit-cap checks before a payment is released for settlement.
**Also known as:** Limit Check, Funds Control, Liquidity Check.

## Relationships
- Liquidity & Limit Checker is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Liquidity & Limit Checker is derived from Payment Orchestration.
- Liquidity & Limit Checker is related to Settlement Instruction Engine.
- Liquidity & Limit Checker is related to Liquidity Management.

## Details
Liquidity & Limit Checker verifies funds availability, liquidity buffers and debit caps in real time so that payments are only released when within limits, a control made mandatory for instant-payment schemes. It supports straight-through release decisions in hubs such as ACI Enterprise Payments Platform and Volante VolPay Hub.
