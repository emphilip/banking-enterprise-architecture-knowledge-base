# Risk Profiling Engine

**Definition.** Risk Profiling Engine is the technology capability that captures and scores an investor's risk tolerance and risk capacity via configurable questionnaires and behavioural inputs, producing the risk profile that drives suitable allocation and advice.
**Also known as:** Investor Risk Profiler, Risk Tolerance Engine, Suitability Profiling Service.

## Relationships
- Risk Profiling Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Risk Profiling Engine is derived from Investment Advisory Platform.
- Risk Profiling Engine is related to Investment Advisory.

## Details
Risk Profiling Engine separates and scores risk tolerance (an investor's willingness to take risk) from risk capacity (their financial ability to absorb loss), using configurable questionnaires, scoring weights and behavioural signals. The resulting risk profile becomes the constraint that drives suitable asset allocation and advice downstream. Robo-advisory platforms such as Temenos WealthSuite, Comarch Wealth Management and InvestSuite realize profiling as the first step of the digital advice journey, retaining the score as evidence for suitability and best-interest obligations.
