# Affordability Assessment Engine

**Definition.** Affordability Assessment Engine is the technology capability that assesses borrower affordability and repayment capacity (income, expenditure, debt-to-income, stress tests) to support responsible-lending decisions.
**Also known as:** Affordability Engine, Repayment Capacity Service.

## Relationships
- Affordability Assessment Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Affordability Assessment Engine is derived from Credit Decisioning Engine.
- Affordability Assessment Engine is related to Credit Risk Management.

## Details
Affordability Assessment Engine evaluates a borrower's income, expenditure, debt-to-income ratio and stressed repayment capacity to support responsible-lending obligations. Underwriting automation vendors such as Inscribe analyse income and bank-statement data to derive affordability signals, which the decision flow combines with credit scores so that approvals reflect a borrower's genuine capacity to repay.
