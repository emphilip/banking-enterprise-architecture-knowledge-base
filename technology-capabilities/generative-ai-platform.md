# Generative AI Platform

**Definition.** Generative AI Platform is the technology capability that hosts and governs large language models for content generation, summarization, retrieval-augmented assistance, and agentic workflows across the bank.
**Also known as:** GenAI, LLM Platform.

## Relationships
- Generative AI Platform is defined in the AI & Automation domain.

- Generative AI Platform depends on the Data Governance capability.
- Generative AI Platform mentions Legacy IVR System.
- Generative AI Platform mentions Anthropic Claude.
- Generative AI Platform is related to Conversational AI.

## Details
Generative AI Platform provides model access, prompt orchestration, retrieval-augmented generation, guardrails, and evaluation for LLM use cases such as customer servicing, advisory drafting, and code assistance. Modern realizations such as Anthropic Claude are accessed via secure APIs with grounding on governed enterprise data. It depends on the Machine Learning Platform and Data Governance capabilities and powers the Conversational AI capability.
