# Probabilistic Match Engine

**Definition.** Probabilistic Match Engine is the master data technology sub-capability providing ML-augmented fuzzy matching with weighted, relevance-based scoring to resolve party records beyond exact deterministic rules.
**Also known as:** Fuzzy Match Engine, AI Matching.

## Relationships
- Probabilistic Match Engine is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Probabilistic Match Engine is derived from Party Matching & Merge.
- Probabilistic Match Engine depends on Machine Learning Platform.

## Details
Probabilistic Match Engine scores candidate record pairs using weighted attribute comparisons and machine-learning models, capturing matches that deterministic keys miss due to typos, abbreviations and missing fields. It produces ranked, confidence-scored clusters that drive auto-merge thresholds and steward review queues, improving recall and precision in entity resolution as part of party matching.
