# Golden Record Management

**Definition.** Golden Record Management is the master data technology sub-capability that constructs and maintains the trusted, best-version-of-truth golden party record with crosswalks and lineage through survivorship.
**Also known as:** Survivorship Engine, Best Record Management.

## Relationships
- Golden Record Management is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Golden Record Management is derived from Master Data Management.
- Golden Record Management depends on Data Governance.
- Golden Record Management is related to Party Matching & Merge.

## Details
Golden Record Management takes matched and merged record clusters and applies survivorship logic to select the prevailing value for each attribute, producing a single authoritative party record with crosswalks back to every contributing source. It maintains lineage and history for auditability and is the master data feature that supplies a consistent customer identity to CRM, analytics and servicing systems.
