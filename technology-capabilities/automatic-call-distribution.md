# Automatic Call Distribution

**Definition.** Automatic Call Distribution is the technology sub-capability providing the queueing and distribution engine that places, prioritises and matches inbound contacts to available agents.
**Also known as:** ACD Engine, Call Distribution Engine.

## Relationships
- Automatic Call Distribution is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Automatic Call Distribution is derived from Contact Center Platform.

## Details
Automatic Call Distribution maintains virtual queues, applies priority and overflow rules, tracks agent presence and state, and selects the next contact and the best available agent according to configured distribution strategies (longest-idle, most-skilled, priority). It exposes real-time queue metrics (service level, average speed of answer, abandonment) that drive announcements and callbacks. As the inbound matchmaking core of CCaaS platforms such as Genesys Cloud and Amazon Connect, ACD is the foundation that skills-based and omnichannel routing extend.
