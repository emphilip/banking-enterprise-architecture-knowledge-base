# Customer Identity

**Definition.** Customer Identity is the technology capability that manages external customer registration, authentication, consent, and progressive profiling for digital channels at consumer scale.
**Also known as:** CIAM.

## Relationships
- Customer Identity is defined in the Security & Identity domain.
- Customer Identity depends on the Identity Access Management capability.
- Customer Identity mentions IBM Tivoli Identity Manager.
- Customer Identity mentions Ping Identity.
- Customer Identity mentions Auth0.
- Customer Identity is related to Digital Channel Platform.

## Details
Customer Identity (CIAM) handles high-volume consumer login, social and passwordless authentication, MFA, and consent management for online and mobile banking. Legacy systems such as IBM Tivoli Identity Manager were workforce-oriented and ill-suited to consumer scale, while modern platforms like Ping Identity and Auth0 deliver elastic, developer-friendly CIAM with fraud signals and adaptive risk. It enables the Digital Channel Platform capability.
