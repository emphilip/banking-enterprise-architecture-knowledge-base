# Data Quality Engine

**Definition.** Data Quality Engine is the technology sub-capability that profiles, monitors and remediates data quality, detecting anomalies against statistical baselines and raising issues for stewards.
**Also known as:** Data Quality Service, DQ Engine, Data Profiling Service, Quality Monitoring.

## Relationships
- Data Quality Engine is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Data Quality Engine is derived from Data Governance.
- Data Quality Engine depends on Metadata Catalog.
- Data Quality Engine is related to Party Data Quality.

## Details
Data Quality Engine, as found in Collibra-class governance platforms, profiles datasets to learn distributions and then runs dimension-based rules (completeness, validity, uniqueness, consistency, timeliness) and adaptive anomaly detection against those baselines. Failed checks become data-quality issues routed to stewards through governance workflow, with quality scores published against catalogued assets so consumers can judge fitness for use. It underpins trustworthy regulatory reporting and party data, where poor quality directly drives report errors and remediation cost.
