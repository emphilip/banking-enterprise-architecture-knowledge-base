# Funds Transfer Pricing Engine

**Definition.** Funds Transfer Pricing Engine is the technology capability that allocates the cost and value of funds and liquidity premia across products, business units and the balance sheet to isolate net interest margin and support profitability and ALCO decisions.
**Also known as:** FTP Engine, Transfer Pricing Service, Funds Transfer Pricing Service.

## Relationships
- Funds Transfer Pricing Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Funds Transfer Pricing Engine is derived from Asset Liability Management Engine.
- Funds Transfer Pricing Engine is related to IRRBB Analytics Engine.

## Details
Funds Transfer Pricing Engine assigns each asset a funding charge and each liability a funding credit using matched-maturity transfer curves built from market and internal rates, plus add-ons for liquidity premium, basis and optionality. The result splits net interest income into asset spread, liability spread and a residual rate-risk margin held by treasury, letting the bank measure true product, customer and business-unit profitability for ALCO.
