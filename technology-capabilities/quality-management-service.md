# Quality Management Service

**Definition.** Quality Management Service is the technology sub-capability providing interaction recording, evaluation, scorecards and automated quality scoring for agent coaching and compliance.
**Also known as:** Quality Management, QM, Automated QA.

## Relationships
- Quality Management Service is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Quality Management Service is derived from Workforce Engagement Management.
- Quality Management Service is related to Interaction Analytics.

## Details
Quality Management Service records voice and screen, samples or auto-selects interactions, and applies evaluation forms and scorecards either by human evaluators or by automated scoring that uses speech and text analytics to grade 100% of contacts against criteria (greeting, compliance disclosures, resolution). It links findings to targeted coaching and calibration workflows. Verint and Genesys position automated QM as the quality and compliance pillar of workforce engagement, moving banks beyond small manual sampling.
