# Investment Advisory Platform

**Definition.** Investment Advisory Platform is the digital advisory and financial-planning technology capability that profiles investor risk tolerance and capacity, builds goal-based and cashflow plans, generates suitable proposals and recommendations, and powers automated and hybrid robo-advisory journeys including asset allocation and proposal-to-portfolio conversion.
**Also known as:** Digital Advice Platform, Financial Planning Platform, Wealth Advisory Suite.

## Details
Investment Advisory Platform powers digital, hybrid and fully-advised wealth journeys. It captures risk tolerance and capacity through configurable questionnaires, runs goal-based and Monte-Carlo cashflow projections, and converts plans into suitable proposals with documented suitability rationale. Incumbent suites such as Temenos WealthSuite package client profiling, asset allocation, rebalancing and goal-based investing, while platforms like InvestCloud (NaviPlan) and Envestnet add cashflow, tax and estate planning, unified managed accounts and automated rebalancing and tax-loss harvesting. Generative AI increasingly drafts client-ready advice narratives and explains recommendations under best-interest and suitability obligations.
