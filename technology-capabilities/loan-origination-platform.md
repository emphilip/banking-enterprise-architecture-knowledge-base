# Loan Origination Platform

**Definition.** Loan Origination Platform is the technology capability that captures loan applications, orchestrates underwriting and document collection, and books approved facilities into core systems across consumer, mortgage, and commercial lending.
**Also known as:** LOS.

## Relationships

- Loan Origination Platform mentions Black Knight Empower.
- Loan Origination Platform mentions Blend.
- Loan Origination Platform mentions nCino.

## Details
Loan Origination Platform coordinates the application-to-funding journey, integrating credit decisioning, document processing, and core booking. Legacy systems such as Black Knight Empower are monolithic and product-specific, while modern cloud platforms like Blend and nCino offer configurable, omnichannel origination built on cloud-native services such as Salesforce. It sits between channels and the core, automating the Loan Origination Workflow.
