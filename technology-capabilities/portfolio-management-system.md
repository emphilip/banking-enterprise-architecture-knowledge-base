# Portfolio Management System

**Definition.** Portfolio Management System is the front-to-back investment book-of-record technology capability that maintains portfolio positions, holdings and cash, computes portfolio accounting and valuations, runs performance and attribution analytics, and manages model portfolios and rebalancing across multiple asset classes and currencies.
**Also known as:** PMS, Investment Book of Record, IBOR, Portfolio & Investment Management Platform.

## Details
Portfolio Management System is the investment book-of-record (IBOR) for wealth and asset-management operations. It unifies a real-time position and cash ledger with multi-currency portfolio accounting, NAV and valuation, and performance and attribution analytics computed at holding level. Legacy realizations such as FIS Global Plus and SunGard Asset Arena run batch trust/portfolio accounting, while modern front-to-back platforms (BlackRock Aladdin, SimCorp One) and aggregation tools (Addepar) deliver a common data language, real-time IBOR and API-first open architecture. Increasingly the IBOR moves from overnight-batch reconciliation to an intraday, event-driven book that feeds rebalancing and trade-order generation directly.
