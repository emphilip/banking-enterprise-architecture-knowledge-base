# Payment Repair Workbench

**Definition.** Payment Repair Workbench is the technology capability providing automated and manual repair, exception handling and investigations (including ISO 20022 exceptions and investigations) of failed or non-STP payments, restoring them to a processable state.
**Also known as:** Auto-Repair, Exceptions and Investigations, STP Repair.

## Relationships
- Payment Repair Workbench is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Payment Repair Workbench is derived from Payment Orchestration.
- Payment Repair Workbench depends on Payment Validation Engine.
- Payment Repair Workbench is related to Workflow Orchestration.

## Details
Payment Repair Workbench combines auto-repair rules with operator queues to fix payments that fail validation or processing, and supports ISO 20022 exceptions and investigations flows such as recalls and status requests. It restores non-STP payments to a processable state, a function embedded in hubs like Finastra Global PAYplus.
