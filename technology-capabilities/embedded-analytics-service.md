# Embedded Analytics Service

**Definition.** Embedded Analytics Service integrates reports, dashboards, and visualisations directly into business and customer-facing applications via APIs and SDKs.
**Also known as:** Embedded Analytics, Embedded BI, White-Label Analytics.

## Relationships
- Embedded Analytics Service is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Embedded Analytics Service is derived from Analytics Platform.
- Embedded Analytics Service is related to Dashboarding Service.
- Embedded Analytics Service is related to Data Visualisation Library.

## Details
Embedded Analytics Service surfaces analytics inside the workflow of another application rather than in a standalone BI portal, so users see relevant charts and KPIs in context. It exposes APIs and SDKs to embed dashboards, pass row-level security tokens for the signed-in user, and white-label the look and feel to match the host product. This lets a bank offer customers and relationship managers in-product insights, such as spending breakdowns or portfolio analytics, without sending them to a separate tool. The service reuses the same governed datasets and visualisation components as the central analytics platform while delegating identity and theming to the embedding application.
