# Disbursement Engine

**Definition.** Disbursement Engine is the technology capability that books the approved loan and executes funding/disbursement, coordinating account creation, fee handling and hand-off to servicing and the ledger.
**Also known as:** Loan Booking & Funding, Funding Engine.

## Relationships
- Disbursement Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Disbursement Engine is derived from Loan Origination Platform.
- Disbursement Engine depends on Core Banking Processing.

## Details
Disbursement Engine completes origination by booking the approved facility, executing fund disbursement, applying fees and handing the loan to servicing and the general ledger. nCino and Finastra origination platforms coordinate account creation with the core banking system so that, once conditions precedent are cleared, funds are released and the loan account is established for ongoing servicing.
