# Policy & Control Management

**Definition.** Policy & Control Management is the technology capability that manages the policy and control library, regulatory-obligation mapping, control testing, attestations and issue and action tracking across the enterprise.
**Also known as:** Policy Management, Control Library, Compliance Management Service.

## Relationships
- Policy & Control Management is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Policy & Control Management is derived from Governance Risk & Compliance Platform.
- Policy & Control Management depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Policy & Control Management is related to the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.

## Details
Policy & Control Management maintains a versioned library of policies, standards and controls, maps them to regulatory obligations and risks, and schedules control testing, attestations and certifications through workflow. It tracks issues, findings and remediation actions to closure and provides the control evidence consumed by compliance reporting, as delivered by ServiceNow IRM and MetricStream.
