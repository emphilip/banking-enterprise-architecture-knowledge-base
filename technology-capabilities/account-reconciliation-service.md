# Account Reconciliation Service

**Definition.** Account Reconciliation Service is the technology capability that automates general-ledger-to-subledger and balance-sheet account reconciliation: high-volume transaction matching, reconciliation certification and exception/break management feeding the close.
**Also known as:** Reconciliation Engine, Transaction Matching Service, Balance Sheet Reconciliation.

## Relationships
- Account Reconciliation Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Account Reconciliation Service is derived from General Ledger Engine.
- Account Reconciliation Service is related to Period Close Manager.

## Details
Account Reconciliation Service ingests GL balances and supporting subledger or bank data, runs rule-based and AI-assisted matching to clear items, and routes unmatched breaks for investigation. Balance-sheet account substantiation requires preparers and reviewers to certify each reconciliation, with risk-based auto-certification of low-risk accounts. Status feeds the close calendar so that unreconciled accounts block sign-off.
