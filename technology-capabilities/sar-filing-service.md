# SAR Filing Service

**Definition.** SAR Filing Service is the technology sub-capability for generation, e-filing and tracking of suspicious activity and transaction reports (SAR/STR) to financial intelligence units from investigated cases.
**Also known as:** STR Filing Service, Regulatory Report Filing (AML), goAML Connector, SAR Generator.

## Relationships
- SAR Filing Service is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- SAR Filing Service is derived from Financial Crime Case Manager.
- SAR Filing Service is related to Suspicious Activity Reporting.

## Details
SAR Filing Service turns a dispositioned investigation into a regulator-ready filing: it maps case data to the required schema (FinCEN BSA E-Filing, UNODC goAML, national FIU formats), validates mandatory fields, submits electronically and tracks acknowledgements and rejections. It maintains filing deadlines, supports continuing-activity reports and enforces confidentiality / tipping-off controls around filed reports. As a child of case management it draws narrative and entity detail directly from the case, providing an end-to-end audit trail from alert to filed report.
