# Personalisation Engine

**Definition.** Personalisation Engine is the technology sub-capability providing real-time content, offer and next-best-action targeting that tailors digital experiences using customer context and behavioural signals.
**Also known as:** Personalization Engine, Offer Targeting Engine, Next-Best-Experience Engine.

## Relationships
- Personalisation Engine is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Personalisation Engine is derived from Digital Channel Platform.
- Personalisation Engine depends on Analytics Platform.
- Personalisation Engine is related to Campaign Automation.

## Details
Personalisation Engine evaluates real-time customer context, eligibility rules and propensity/next-best-action models to decide which content, offer or prompt to render in each channel slot, then returns ranked decisions to the front end with millisecond latency. It supports A/B and holdout measurement and feeds outcomes back to improve targeting. It consumes features and scores from the enterprise analytics and ML estate, and coordinates with marketing campaign automation so in-session decisions stay consistent with orchestrated journeys.
