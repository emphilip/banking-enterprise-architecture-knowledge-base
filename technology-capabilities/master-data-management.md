# Master Data Management

**Definition.** Master Data Management is the technology capability that creates and maintains a single, golden record for key entities such as customers, products, and counterparties by matching, merging, and deduplicating data across systems.
**Also known as:** MDM.

## Relationships
- Master Data Management is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Master Data Management depends on the Data Governance capability.
- Master Data Management is related to Data Warehousing.

## Details
Master Data Management resolves entity identity across fragmented source systems to support party data management and a 360-degree customer view. It applies probabilistic and deterministic matching to build golden records that feed CRM, compliance, and analytics. Modern MDM is increasingly graph-based and cloud-native, integrating with the Data Governance capability to enforce stewardship and quality rules. It is a foundational data domain service.
