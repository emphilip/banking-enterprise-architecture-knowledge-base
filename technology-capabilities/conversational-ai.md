# Conversational AI

**Definition.** Conversational AI is the technology capability that enables natural-language dialogue with customers and agents through chatbots and voice assistants, interpreting intent and orchestrating banking actions.
**Also known as:** Chatbot, Virtual Assistant.

## Relationships
- Conversational AI is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Conversational AI depends on the Generative AI Platform capability.
- Conversational AI mentions Legacy IVR System.
- Conversational AI mentions Kasisto.
- Conversational AI is related to Contact Center Platform.

## Details
Conversational AI handles customer servicing and contact-center deflection via text and voice, resolving intents and invoking core services. Legacy IVR systems use rigid menu trees and keyword recognition, while modern assistants such as Kasisto and LLM-backed agents understand free-form language and context. It increasingly depends on the Generative AI Platform capability for fluent, grounded responses and integrates with the Contact Center Platform.
