# In-App Messaging

**Definition.** In-App Messaging is the technology sub-capability providing secure two-way in-session messaging, alerts and contextual prompts rendered inside digital channels, distinct from cross-channel delivery.
**Also known as:** In-Session Messaging, Secure Inbox, Contextual Messaging.

## Relationships
- In-App Messaging is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- In-App Messaging is derived from Digital Channel Platform.
- In-App Messaging depends on Notification Services.
- In-App Messaging is related to Conversational AI.

## Details
In-App Messaging renders a secure inbox and inline conversational threads within the authenticated app, supporting agent-to-customer and system-to-customer messages, read receipts and contextual nudges anchored to the screen the user is on. Unlike outbound push or SMS, messages stay inside the encrypted session and are tied to customer context. It relies on the cross-channel notification fabric for delivery and event triggers, and can escalate threads into assisted or bot-driven conversations.
