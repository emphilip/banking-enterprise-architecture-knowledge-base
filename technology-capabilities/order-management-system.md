# Order Management System

**Definition.** Order Management System is the buy-side order and execution management technology capability that raises orders from model and benchmark drift and manual instructions, enforces pre-trade compliance, routes and executes orders across brokers, venues and EMS connectivity, then allocates and produces settlement instructions for the trade lifecycle.
**Also known as:** OMS, Order & Execution Management Platform.

## Details
Order Management System covers the buy-side OMS/EMS lifecycle: order raise from model or benchmark drift, pre-trade compliance, route, execute, allocate and settle, with post-trade compliance and standing settlement instructions (SSIs). Orders are routed and executed over the FIX protocol to brokers, trading venues and execution providers, with smart-order routing and best-execution monitoring. Legacy desks ran SS&C Advent Moxy for order generation and trade workflow, while integrated platforms such as Charles River IMS and Bloomberg AIM combine OMS/EMS, order modelling, rebalancing-driven orders and automated pre/post-trade compliance across multi-asset workflows. Shorter settlement cycles (T+1) increase the need for same-day affirmation and straight-through allocation.
