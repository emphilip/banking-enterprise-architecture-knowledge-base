# Hedge Accounting Service

**Definition.** Hedge Accounting Service designates hedge relationships, performs effectiveness testing and generates the accounting entries required for cash-flow and fair-value hedges under IFRS 9.
**Also known as:** Hedge Accounting Engine, Hedge Designation Service, IFRS 9 Hedge Service.

## Relationships
- Hedge Accounting Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Hedge Accounting Service is derived from Treasury Risk Management Service.

## Details
Hedge Accounting Service records the formal designation that links a hedging instrument to a hedged item, captures the risk-management objective and the hedge type, and runs prospective and retrospective effectiveness assessment. Under IFRS 9 it applies the economic-relationship test rather than the old 80–125% bright line. For a cash-flow hedge it books the effective portion of fair-value change to the cash-flow hedge reserve in other comprehensive income and any ineffectiveness to profit or loss; for a fair-value hedge it adjusts the carrying amount of the hedged item. It maintains the hedge documentation that auditors require to support continued hedge accounting.
