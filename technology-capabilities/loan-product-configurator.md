# Loan Product Configurator

**Definition.** Loan Product Configurator is the technology capability that defines loan products, terms, eligibility parameters and workflow rules without code, enabling rapid product setup and multi-product origination.
**Also known as:** Product Configurator, Lending Product Designer.

## Relationships
- Loan Product Configurator is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Loan Product Configurator is derived from Loan Origination Platform.
- Loan Product Configurator is related to Application Intake Engine.

## Details
Loan Product Configurator lets lending product teams model products, fees, eligibility criteria and origination workflow rules through configuration rather than code. Temenos and nCino origination suites use such configurators to launch new consumer and commercial lending products quickly and to drive multi-product origination from a single platform, reducing time-to-market for new loan offerings.
