# Knowledge Base Service

**Definition.** Knowledge Base Service is the technology sub-capability providing centralised, searchable knowledge articles and guided answers surfaced to agents and self-service to drive consistent resolution.
**Also known as:** Knowledge Management, Service Knowledge Base, Answer Library.

## Relationships
- Knowledge Base Service is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Knowledge Base Service is derived from CRM Platform.
- Knowledge Base Service is related to Case Management Workbench.

## Details
Knowledge Base Service manages an authored and versioned article library with role-based publishing, search and increasingly generative-AI answer retrieval, surfacing the right answer in the agent desktop and in self-service channels for a single source of truth. It supports article suggestions tied to case context, feedback loops on article quality, and decay/review workflows. Genesys Knowledge and Glia digital customer service position the knowledge base as the resolution backbone shared by assisted and self-service experiences, reducing handle time and inconsistent answers.
