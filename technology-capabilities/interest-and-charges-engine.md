# Interest & Charges Engine

**Definition.** Interest & Charges Engine is the technology capability that calculates daily interest accruals, capitalisation and payment, tiered and bonus rates, service charges, fees and withholding tax for interest-bearing and fee-bearing deposit accounts.
**Also known as:** Accrual Engine, Interest & Fee Engine, Charges Engine.

## Relationships
- Interest & Charges Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Interest & Charges Engine is derived from Core Banking Processing.
- Interest & Charges Engine is related to Interest Calculation.

## Details
Interest & Charges Engine computes daily accruals, capitalisation, tiered and bonus rates, service charges and withholding tax across deposit products. Mambu documents deposit-account interest application and withholding within the account lifecycle, and core-banking data-model references describe accrual, capitalisation and service-charge calculation as discrete ledger functions, which this engine consolidates for interest-bearing and fee-bearing accounts.
