# Contact & Account Management

**Definition.** Contact & Account Management is the CRM technology sub-capability that maintains person, household and legal-entity records, their relationships and interaction history as the system-of-engagement view of the customer.
**Also known as:** Account & Contact Management, Party Record Management.

## Relationships
- Contact & Account Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Contact & Account Management is derived from CRM Platform.
- Contact & Account Management depends on Master Data Management.

## Details
Contact & Account Management provides the CRM data foundation for relationship managers and service agents, holding contact details, account hierarchies, household groupings and a running log of interactions. It is delivered in suites such as Salesforce Financial Services Cloud, where it complements the authoritative golden record maintained in master data platforms by surfacing an engagement-oriented view at the point of customer contact.
