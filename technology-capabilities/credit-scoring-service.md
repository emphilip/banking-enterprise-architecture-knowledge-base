# Credit Scoring Service

**Definition.** Credit Scoring Service is the technology capability that computes application and behavioural credit scores using scorecards and ML models, returning scores and contributing factors to the decision flow.
**Also known as:** Scorecard Service, Credit Score Engine.

## Relationships
- Credit Scoring Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Credit Scoring Service is derived from Credit Decisioning Engine.
- Credit Scoring Service depends on Machine Learning Platform.
- Credit Scoring Service is related to Credit Decisioning.

## Details
Credit Scoring Service executes statistical scorecards and machine-learning models against application and bureau data, returning scores plus the contributing factors used by downstream rules and pricing. Cloud decisioning platforms such as Provenir embed scoring alongside data and rules, while AI scoring approaches add explainable model features so the resulting scores feed the credit decision flow consistently.
