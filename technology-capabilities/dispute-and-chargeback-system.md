# Dispute & Chargeback System

**Definition.** Dispute & Chargeback System is the technology capability that manages the issuer side of the scheme dispute lifecycle, covering chargeback initiation and reason-code assignment, representment, pre-arbitration and arbitration and provisional credits, integrating with Visa VROL and VCR and Mastercard Mastercom.
**Also known as:** Chargeback Engine, Dispute Management System, Chargeback Processor.

## Relationships
- Dispute & Chargeback System is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Dispute & Chargeback System is derived from Card Processing.
- Dispute & Chargeback System is related to Dispute Management.

## Details
Dispute & Chargeback System drives the issuer dispute lifecycle through the scheme rule windows, assigning Visa and Mastercard reason codes, raising chargebacks, handling representments and escalating to pre-arbitration and arbitration. It posts provisional credits to the cardholder while a case is investigated and exchanges case data with Visa Resolve Online (VROL) and Mastercard Mastercom. Reason-code accuracy and deadline tracking determine whether a chargeback is upheld.
