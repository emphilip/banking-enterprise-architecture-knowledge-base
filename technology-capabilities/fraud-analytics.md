# Fraud Analytics

**Definition.** Fraud Analytics is the technology capability that scores transactions and behaviours in real time using models and rules to detect and prevent payment, card, and application fraud.
**Also known as:** Fraud Detection Platform.

## Relationships
- Fraud Analytics is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Fraud Analytics depends on the Data Streaming capability.
- Fraud Analytics depends on the Machine Learning Platform capability.
- Fraud Analytics mentions FICO Falcon.
- Fraud Analytics mentions SAS Fraud Management.
- Fraud Analytics mentions Feedzai.
- Fraud Analytics mentions Featurespace.

## Details
Fraud Analytics evaluates events against behavioural profiles and machine-learning models to block or challenge suspicious activity with sub-second latency. Legacy engines such as FICO Falcon and SAS Fraud Management are powerful but rule-heavy and slow to retrain, while modern platforms like Feedzai and Featurespace use adaptive behavioural analytics and real-time streaming. It depends on the Data Streaming and Machine Learning Platform capabilities.
