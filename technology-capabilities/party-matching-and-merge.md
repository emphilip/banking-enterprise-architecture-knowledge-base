# Party Matching & Merge

**Definition.** Party Matching & Merge is the master data technology sub-capability that applies deterministic and probabilistic match rules plus merge and unmerge to deduplicate party records across source systems.
**Also known as:** Entity Resolution, Match & Merge.

## Relationships
- Party Matching & Merge is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Party Matching & Merge is derived from Master Data Management.
- Party Matching & Merge depends on Machine Learning Platform.
- Party Matching & Merge is related to Golden Record Management.

## Details
Party Matching & Merge is the entity-resolution engine within master data management that compares incoming and existing records, scores their similarity and consolidates duplicates into a single party. It combines deterministic keys with probabilistic, ML-assisted matching to handle real-world variation in names, addresses and identifiers, and hands resolved clusters to survivorship for golden-record construction.
