# Business Glossary Manager

**Definition.** Business Glossary Manager is the technology sub-capability that manages business terms, definitions, classifications and their links to data assets so data is interpreted consistently across the enterprise.
**Also known as:** Business Glossary, Term Management, Semantic Glossary, Data Dictionary.

## Relationships
- Business Glossary Manager is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Business Glossary Manager is derived from Metadata Catalog.
- Business Glossary Manager is related to Data Governance.

## Details
Business Glossary Manager holds approved business terms with definitions, synonyms, classifications and accountable owners, governed through a review and approval workflow. Terms are linked to the technical assets in the metadata catalogue that physically represent them, so a concept like customer or exposure has one agreed meaning mapped to its columns and reports. This semantic layer reduces ambiguity in regulatory and management reporting and gives stewards the controlled vocabulary that policies, quality rules and lineage reference.
