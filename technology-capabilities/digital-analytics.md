# Digital Analytics

**Definition.** Digital Analytics is the technology sub-capability capturing and analysing digital behavioural events, journey funnels and conversion and engagement metrics across web and mobile channels.
**Also known as:** Digital Behavioural Analytics, Journey Analytics, Product Analytics.

## Relationships
- Digital Analytics is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Analytics is derived from Digital Channel Platform.
- Digital Analytics depends on Analytics Platform.

## Details
Digital Analytics instruments client and server events (page/screen views, taps, form steps, errors), stitches them into sessions and customer journeys, and computes funnel conversion, drop-off, retention and feature adoption. It supports cohorting, path analysis and event-stream export to the enterprise analytics estate for joined-up reporting and model training. Digital banking platforms such as Temenos Infinity and Alkami embed event tracking and dashboards so product teams optimise journeys with behavioural evidence.
