# Model Inventory Registry

**Definition.** Model Inventory Registry is the technology capability that maintains a centralized repository of all models and AI systems with metadata, risk tiering, ownership, dependencies, approvals and lifecycle status — the SR 11-7 model inventory of record.
**Also known as:** Model Inventory, Model Catalog, AI Inventory.

## Relationships
- Model Inventory Registry is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Model Inventory Registry is derived from Model Risk Management Platform.
- Model Inventory Registry is related to Model Validation Workbench.
- Model Inventory Registry mentions the Machine Learning Platform capability.

## Details
Model Inventory Registry is the authoritative SR 11-7 inventory: it records every model and AI system with its purpose, owner, developer, validator, risk tier, upstream and downstream dependencies, approval state and lifecycle stage. Risk-tiering drives the depth and frequency of validation and monitoring, and the inventory underpins enterprise model-risk reporting, as delivered by ValidMind.
