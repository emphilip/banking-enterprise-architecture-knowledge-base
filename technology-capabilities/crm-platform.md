# CRM Platform

**Definition.** CRM Platform is the technology capability that manages customer relationships, interactions, opportunities, and campaigns, providing a unified view of the customer for relationship managers and marketing teams.
**Also known as:** Customer Relationship Management.

## Relationships
- CRM Platform is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- CRM Platform depends on the Master Data Management capability.
- CRM Platform mentions Siebel CRM.
- CRM Platform mentions Salesforce Financial Services Cloud.
- CRM Platform is related to Contact Center Platform.

## Details
CRM Platform consolidates relationship, servicing, and marketing data to support relationship management and campaign execution. Legacy systems such as Siebel CRM are heavily customized and on-premise, while modern cloud CRM like Salesforce Financial Services Cloud offers industry data models, automation, and AI insights. It depends on the Master Data Management capability for golden customer records and integrates with the Contact Center Platform.
