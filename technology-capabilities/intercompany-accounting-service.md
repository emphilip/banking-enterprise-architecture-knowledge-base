# Intercompany Accounting Service

**Definition.** Intercompany Accounting Service is the technology capability that generates and matches intercompany transactions and balances across legal entities, supporting netting and elimination preparation ahead of consolidation.
**Also known as:** Intercompany Engine, ICO Matching Service, Intercompany Reconciliation.

## Relationships
- Intercompany Accounting Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Intercompany Accounting Service is derived from Subledger Accounting.
- Intercompany Accounting Service is related to Consolidation Engine.

## Details
Intercompany Accounting Service books the two-sided entries of intra-group sales, loans and recharges, then matches the trading-partner balances between entities to surface and resolve mismatches before close. It supports settlement netting and produces reconciled intercompany positions tagged by trading partner, which the Consolidation Engine uses to perform intercompany elimination during group consolidation.
