# Planning & Budgeting Engine

**Definition.** Planning & Budgeting Engine is the technology capability that performs driver-based planning, budgeting, rolling forecasts and scenario modelling (workforce, capex, revenue) integrated with the consolidation model so that plan and actual data share one source.
**Also known as:** Planning Engine, FP&A Engine, Budgeting & Forecasting Service, EPM Planning.

## Relationships
- Planning & Budgeting Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Planning & Budgeting Engine is derived from Financial Consolidation Platform.
- Planning & Budgeting Engine is related to Consolidation Engine.

## Details
Planning & Budgeting Engine models financial and operational plans on a multidimensional calculation engine, linking driver assumptions (volumes, rates, headcount) to financial outputs for budgets, rolling forecasts and long-range plans. Connected workforce, capex and revenue plans drive the P&L and balance sheet, while versioning and scenarios support what-if analysis. Sharing dimensions with the Consolidation Engine gives one model for plan and actuals and seamless variance reporting.
