# Consent Management Service

**Definition.** Consent Management Service is the customer identity technology sub-capability that captures, enforces and audits customer consent, privacy preferences and communication opt-ins and opt-outs for regulatory compliance.
**Also known as:** Privacy Management, Consent Service.

## Relationships
- Consent Management Service is defined in the Security & Identity domain.
- Consent Management Service is derived from Customer Identity.
- Consent Management Service depends on Data Governance.
- Consent Management Service is related to Regulatory Compliance.

## Details
Consent Management Service records granular customer consents and privacy preferences, versions them over time and enforces them at the point of data use and communication. It provides auditable evidence for regulations such as GDPR and open-banking consent regimes, exposing consent state to channels and downstream systems as a shared service within consumer identity and access management.
