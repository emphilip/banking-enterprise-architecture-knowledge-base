# Document Capture & Verification

**Definition.** Document Capture & Verification is the technology capability that collects, classifies and verifies borrower documents (ID, financials, statements) supporting the origination file, integrating OCR/IDP and e-signature.
**Also known as:** Document Capture Service, Doc Verification Engine.

## Relationships
- Document Capture & Verification is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Document Capture & Verification is derived from Loan Origination Platform.
- Document Capture & Verification depends on Intelligent Document Processing.

## Details
Document Capture & Verification ingests borrower-supplied documents, classifies them, extracts data through OCR and intelligent document processing, and verifies completeness against origination checklists. Finastra origination and nCino link this capability to e-signature and downstream underwriting so that a complete, verified document file is assembled before a credit decision is rendered.
