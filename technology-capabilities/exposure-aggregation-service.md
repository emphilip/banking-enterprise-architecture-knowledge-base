# Exposure Aggregation Service

**Definition.** Exposure Aggregation Service is the technology capability that aggregates and nets exposures across products, legal entities, counterparties and risk types into consolidated, drill-downable risk positions for limits, capital and reporting.
**Also known as:** Risk Aggregation Service, Exposure Consolidation.

## Relationships
- Exposure Aggregation Service is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Exposure Aggregation Service is derived from Risk Data Aggregation.
- Exposure Aggregation Service is related to Risk Data Warehouse.
- Exposure Aggregation Service is related to the Risk Analytics Engine capability.

## Details
Exposure Aggregation Service rolls up gross exposures, applies netting and collateral offsets, and consolidates positions across legal-entity and counterparty hierarchies and across credit, market and liquidity risk types. It produces the consolidated, drill-downable exposure views that feed limit monitoring, regulatory and economic capital and board risk reporting, supporting the BCBS 239 principle of completeness with on-demand aggregation at the group level.
