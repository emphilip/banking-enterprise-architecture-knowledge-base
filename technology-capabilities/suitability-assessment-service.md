# Suitability Assessment Service

**Definition.** Suitability Assessment Service is the technology capability that tests recommended products and portfolios against the investor's profile, objectives and constraints to evidence suitability and appropriateness and capture the rationale for advice given.
**Also known as:** Suitability Engine, Appropriateness Service, Advice Suitability Check.

## Relationships
- Suitability Assessment Service is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Suitability Assessment Service is derived from Risk Profiling Engine.
- Suitability Assessment Service is related to Investment Advisory.

## Details
Suitability Assessment Service checks each recommendation against the investor's risk profile, objectives, knowledge and experience, and constraints, producing a suitability or appropriateness determination and a documented rationale. It supports MiFID II appropriateness, US Reg BI care obligations and product-governance target-market checks, generating the suitability report that accompanies advice. Platforms such as Temenos WealthSuite, Comarch Wealth Management and InvestCloud realize automated suitability testing as a control gate on advice generation and product recommendation.
