# Dashboarding Service

**Definition.** Dashboarding Service composes interactive dashboards from charts, KPIs, and filters to monitor metrics and tell visual data narratives.
**Also known as:** Dashboard Service, Interactive Dashboards, KPI Dashboards.

## Relationships
- Dashboarding Service is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Dashboarding Service is derived from Analytics Platform.
- Dashboarding Service depends on Data Visualisation Library.
- Dashboarding Service is related to BI & Reporting Service.

## Details
Dashboarding Service lets authors arrange visual components, KPI tiles, and cross-filters onto a canvas that refreshes against live queries, giving users a single monitoring surface for metrics. Interactivity such as drill-through, parameterised filters, and linked selections lets viewers investigate without writing queries, while scheduled refresh and alerting flag threshold breaches. Dashboards render through an underlying visualisation component library and typically read from governed semantic models so the displayed numbers stay consistent with reports. In banking this powers operational control rooms, executive scorecards, and risk and liquidity monitoring views.
