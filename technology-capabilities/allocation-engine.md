# Allocation Engine

**Definition.** Allocation Engine is the technology capability that performs periodic and on-demand cost and revenue allocations, distributions, assessments and apportionments across cost centres, profit centres and segments to support management accounting and profitability.
**Also known as:** Cost Allocation Engine, Distribution & Assessment Engine, Profitability Allocation Service.

## Relationships
- Allocation Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Allocation Engine is derived from General Ledger Engine.
- Allocation Engine is related to Chart Of Accounts Service.

## Details
Allocation Engine executes distribution cycles (which retain the original cost element) and assessment cycles (which use secondary allocation accounts) to move balances from collecting cost centres to receivers by fixed shares, percentages or statistical drivers. Run as part of the period close, allocations produce fully-costed profit-centre and segment results that feed management reporting and product/customer profitability analysis.
