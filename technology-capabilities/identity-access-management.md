# Identity Access Management

**Definition.** Identity Access Management is the technology capability that governs workforce identities, authentication, single sign-on, privileged access, and entitlement lifecycle across the bank's internal systems.
**Also known as:** IAM.

## Details
Identity Access Management secures employee and service access through SSO, MFA, role-based access, and access certification. Legacy products such as CA SiteMinder and Oracle Identity Manager are on-premise and complex to operate, while modern cloud identity platforms like Okta and ForgeRock provide zero-trust, standards-based (OIDC, SAML, SCIM) access governance. It underpins the API Management and Threat Detection capabilities.
