# Settlement Instruction Engine

**Definition.** Settlement Instruction Engine is the technology capability that handles generation, sequencing and management of settlement instructions, nostro/vostro position updates and settlement-cycle handling against clearing infrastructures.
**Also known as:** Settlement Engine, Settlement Instruction.

## Relationships
- Settlement Instruction Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Settlement Instruction Engine is derived from Payment Orchestration.
- Settlement Instruction Engine is related to Liquidity & Limit Checker.
- Settlement Instruction Engine is related to Scheme Connectivity Gateway.

## Details
Settlement Instruction Engine creates and sequences settlement instructions, updates nostro and vostro positions and manages settlement cycles against RTGS, net and instant settlement infrastructures. It coordinates with liquidity controls and scheme gateways, as exemplified by ACI Enterprise Payments Platform, Volante VolPay Hub and Finastra Global PAYplus.
