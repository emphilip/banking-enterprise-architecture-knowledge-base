# Standing Order & Sweep Engine

**Definition.** Standing Order & Sweep Engine is the technology capability that executes scheduled and rule-based account movements: standing orders, recurring internal transfers, zero and target-balance sweeps and cash-concentration or pooling between deposit accounts.
**Also known as:** Sweep Engine, Scheduled Transfer Engine, Cash Concentration Engine.

## Relationships
- Standing Order & Sweep Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Standing Order & Sweep Engine is derived from Core Banking Processing.
- Standing Order & Sweep Engine is related to Standing Order Management.

## Details
Standing Order & Sweep Engine runs scheduled and rule-based movements such as standing orders, recurring internal transfers and zero or target-balance sweeps. Core-banking component models include scheduled-transfer and cash-management functions, and Tuum's accounts engine supports configurable account movements, so cash-concentration and pooling between deposit accounts execute as parameter-driven runs inside the core.
