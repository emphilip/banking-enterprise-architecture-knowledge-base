# Data Policy & Stewardship Service

**Definition.** Data Policy & Stewardship Service is the technology sub-capability that manages data policies, ownership models, stewardship workflows and audit-ready controls that operationalise governance across the enterprise.
**Also known as:** Stewardship Workflow, Policy Management (Data), Data Governance Workflow, Data Ownership Service.

## Relationships
- Data Policy & Stewardship Service is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Data Policy & Stewardship Service is derived from Data Governance.
- Data Policy & Stewardship Service depends on Metadata Catalog.
- Data Policy & Stewardship Service is related to Data Quality Engine.

## Details
Data Policy & Stewardship Service codifies data policies and standards, assigns domain owners and data stewards, and drives the workflows — issue resolution, term approval, access requests, attestations — that make governance operational rather than documentary. Tasks, approvals and decisions are tracked on an audit trail that demonstrates control to regulators and internal audit. It binds policy to the catalogued assets and feeds quality and lineage activities, turning the governance operating model into enforceable, accountable day-to-day work.
