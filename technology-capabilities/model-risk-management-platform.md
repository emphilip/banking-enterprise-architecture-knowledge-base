# Model Risk Management Platform

**Definition.** Model Risk Management Platform is the SR 11-7 / SR 26-2-aligned technology capability that governs the full model lifecycle — centralized model inventory, independent validation, documentation, ongoing performance monitoring, approvals and risk tiering — for quantitative and AI/ML models used in bank decision-making.
**Also known as:** MRM Platform, Model Governance Platform, Model Inventory & Validation Platform.

## Details
Model Risk Management Platform maintains the SR 11-7 model inventory of record — every model and AI system with metadata, risk tiering, ownership, dependencies, approvals and lifecycle status — and automates independent validation: testing, benchmarking, documentation and ongoing performance monitoring producing audit-ready validation reports. Cloud, AI-first platforms such as ValidMind and ModelOp tie model inventory, governance controls and production monitoring to model operations across ML-heavy estates, addressing the modernization gap between SR 11-7 and SR 26-2 for traditional and generative-AI models.
