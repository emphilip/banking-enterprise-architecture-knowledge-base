# Adverse Action Generator

**Definition.** Adverse Action Generator is the technology capability that produces compliant adverse-action reason codes and decline notices reflecting actual model and rule drivers, supporting explainability and regulatory obligations.
**Also known as:** Adverse Action Service, Reason Code Generator.

## Relationships
- Adverse Action Generator is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Adverse Action Generator is derived from Credit Decisioning Engine.
- Adverse Action Generator is related to Regulatory Compliance.

## Details
Adverse Action Generator derives the principal reasons behind a decline or counter-offer from the actual model features and rule outcomes, generating compliant adverse-action reason codes and notices. For AI-driven scoring this requires explainability over model drivers so that adverse-action notices meet fair-lending and disclosure requirements while accurately reflecting why a decision was made.
