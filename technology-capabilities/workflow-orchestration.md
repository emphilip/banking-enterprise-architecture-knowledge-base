# Workflow Orchestration

**Definition.** Workflow Orchestration is the technology capability that models, automates, and coordinates long-running business processes and human tasks, managing state, routing, and SLAs across distributed services.
**Also known as:** BPM, Process Orchestration.

- Workflow Orchestration is related to Robotic Process Automation.

## Details
Workflow Orchestration drives onboarding, servicing, and loan origination journeys that span systems and people. Legacy BPM suites such as Pega BPM are model-driven and monolithic, while modern engines like Camunda and Temporal provide developer-centric, code-first orchestration with durable execution and microservice choreography. It coordinates services exposed through the Integration Platform and API Management capabilities.
