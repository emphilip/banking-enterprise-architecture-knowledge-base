# Case Management Workbench

**Definition.** Case Management Workbench is the CRM technology sub-capability providing an agent workspace for logging, routing, prioritising and resolving service cases, complaints and requests with SLA tracking and automated assignment.
**Also known as:** Service Case Workbench, Service Console.

## Relationships
- Case Management Workbench is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Case Management Workbench is derived from CRM Platform.
- Case Management Workbench depends on Workflow Orchestration.
- Case Management Workbench is related to Contact & Account Management.

## Details
Case Management Workbench is the agent-facing console in modern CRM suites for handling service interactions end to end, with queues, prioritisation, SLA timers and automated assignment rules. It typically embeds knowledge articles and AI assistance to speed resolution, and orchestrates multi-step servicing through a workflow engine while drawing customer context from the engagement record.
