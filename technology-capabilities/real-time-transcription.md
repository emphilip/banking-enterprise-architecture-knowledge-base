# Real-Time Transcription

**Definition.** Real-Time Transcription is the technology sub-capability providing live speech-to-text transcription of interactions that feeds agent assist, real-time alerting and downstream analytics.
**Also known as:** Live Transcription, Real-Time Speech-to-Text.

## Relationships
- Real-Time Transcription is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Real-Time Transcription is derived from Interaction Analytics.
- Real-Time Transcription is related to Agent Assist.

## Details
Real-Time Transcription streams automatic speech recognition during a live call, producing a low-latency, speaker-separated transcript that powers in-the-moment capabilities: keyword and sentiment alerts to supervisors, compliance-phrase detection, and prompts surfaced to agents. The transcript also seeds knowledge lookups and call summaries. Amazon Connect (Contact Lens real-time) and Genesys provide live transcription as the streaming front end to interaction analytics and AI agent assist.
