# Outbound Dialer

**Definition.** Outbound Dialer is the technology sub-capability providing predictive, progressive and preview outbound calling and a campaign engine with compliance pacing for collections and proactive contact.
**Also known as:** Predictive Dialer, Campaign Dialer, Proactive Outbound.

## Relationships
- Outbound Dialer is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Outbound Dialer is derived from Contact Center Platform.
- Outbound Dialer is related to Campaign Automation.

## Details
Outbound Dialer runs contact lists through predictive (statistical over-dialling), progressive (one-to-one) and preview modes, detects answering machines, and paces calls to balance agent occupancy against abandonment limits. It enforces compliance controls such as time-of-day windows, do-not-call and consent suppression, and supports blended inbound/outbound agents. Five9 and Genesys position the dialer as the proactive engine for collections, retention and reminders, executing the audiences and treatments defined by marketing campaign automation.
