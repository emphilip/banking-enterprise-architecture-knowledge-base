# Payment Routing Engine

**Definition.** Payment Routing Engine is the technology capability that performs rules-driven selection of the optimal clearing rail, scheme and processing path for each payment based on type, cost, speed, cut-off and availability.
**Also known as:** Smart Routing, Least-Cost Routing.

## Relationships
- Payment Routing Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Payment Routing Engine is derived from Payment Orchestration.
- Payment Routing Engine is related to Scheme Connectivity Gateway.

## Details
Payment Routing Engine evaluates each payment instruction against routing rules covering rail type, cost, speed, scheme cut-off times and availability to choose the optimal clearing and settlement path. It underpins multi-rail payment hubs such as Volante VolPay Hub, FIS Open Payment Framework and ACI Enterprise Payments Platform, and feeds selected payments to the appropriate scheme adapters.
