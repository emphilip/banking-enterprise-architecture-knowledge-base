# Interaction Analytics

**Definition.** Interaction Analytics is the technology sub-capability providing speech and text analytics over recorded and live interactions for sentiment, topic, compliance and coaching insight, distinct from the enterprise analytics estate.
**Also known as:** Speech Analytics, Conversation Analytics, Interaction Insights.

## Relationships
- Interaction Analytics is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Interaction Analytics is derived from Contact Center Platform.
- Interaction Analytics is related to Workforce Engagement Management.

## Details
Interaction Analytics applies automatic speech recognition, natural-language processing and acoustic analysis across 100% of voice and digital interactions to detect topics, intents, sentiment, silence, talk-over and compliance phrases (e.g. mandatory disclosures), producing searchable transcripts and trends. Findings feed quality scoring, agent coaching and contact-driver analysis. Genesys and Amazon Connect (Contact Lens) provide this conversation-analytics layer on top of recording, separate from the bank-wide analytics platform.
