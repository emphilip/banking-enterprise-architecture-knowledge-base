# Robo-Advisory Engine

**Definition.** Robo-Advisory Engine is the automated and hybrid advice technology capability that takes risk profile and goals, applies allocation algorithms to construct or adjust a portfolio, and orchestrates onboarding-to-portfolio and ongoing automated management with minimal advisor intervention.
**Also known as:** Robo-Advisor, Automated Advice Engine, Digital Advice Engine.

## Relationships
- Robo-Advisory Engine is defined in the Core Processing domain.
- Robo-Advisory Engine is derived from Investment Advisory Platform.
- Robo-Advisory Engine depends on Goal-Based Planning Engine.
- Robo-Advisory Engine is related to Rebalancing Engine.

## Details
Robo-Advisory Engine orchestrates the end-to-end digital advice journey: it ingests the investor's risk profile and goal plans, applies allocation algorithms to construct or adjust a model-aligned portfolio, and automates onboarding-to-portfolio conversion and ongoing monitoring with minimal advisor touch. It hands target weights to the Rebalancing Engine for ongoing realignment. Platforms such as Temenos WealthSuite and Comarch Wealth Management realize fully-automated and hybrid robo journeys, blending algorithmic allocation with optional human advisor escalation.
