# Campaign Automation

**Definition.** Campaign Automation is the CRM technology sub-capability that designs, triggers and measures multi-channel, behaviour-driven outreach campaigns and journeys from the CRM.
**Also known as:** Marketing Automation, Journey Orchestration.

## Relationships
- Campaign Automation is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Campaign Automation is derived from CRM Platform.
- Campaign Automation depends on Customer 360 View.

## Details
Campaign Automation builds and executes segmented, event-triggered marketing journeys across email, mobile, web and contact channels, with A/B testing and attribution reporting. It consumes the unified profile assembled by the customer view to target audiences and personalise offers, and is delivered as part of marketing-automation modules in enterprise CRM platforms.
