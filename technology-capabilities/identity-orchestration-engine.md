# Identity Orchestration Engine

**Definition.** Identity Orchestration Engine is the customer identity technology sub-capability providing a visual workflow engine that sequences anti-fraud, document and biometric proofing services into consistent identity journeys.
**Also known as:** Identity Orchestration, Proofing Orchestration.

## Relationships
- Identity Orchestration Engine is defined in the Security & Identity domain.
- Identity Orchestration Engine is derived from Identity Proofing Service.
- Identity Orchestration Engine is related to Workflow Orchestration.

## Details
Identity Orchestration Engine lets architects compose identity journeys from reusable steps, calling anti-fraud checks, document verification, biometric proofing and data sources in sequence or in parallel based on risk. It abstracts individual vendors behind a no-code flow, enabling consistent, adaptable proofing across channels as the coordination layer within the identity proofing service.
