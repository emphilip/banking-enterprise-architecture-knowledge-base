# Analytics Platform

**Definition.** Analytics Platform is the technology capability that delivers business intelligence, dashboards, statistical analysis, and self-service reporting over governed data for decision-making across the bank.
**Also known as:** BI Platform, Analytics and BI.

## Relationships
- Analytics Platform is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Analytics Platform depends on the Data Warehousing capability.
- Analytics Platform mentions IBM Cognos.
- Analytics Platform mentions SAS Platform.
- Analytics Platform is related to Machine Learning Platform.

## Details
Analytics Platform turns warehoused data into reports, visualizations, and ad hoc analysis for finance, risk, and marketing teams. Legacy tools such as IBM Cognos and the SAS Platform are server-bound and licensed per seat, while modern cloud BI integrates directly with lakehouse engines for elastic, governed self-service. It consumes the Data Warehousing capability and feeds segmentation and risk reporting use cases.
