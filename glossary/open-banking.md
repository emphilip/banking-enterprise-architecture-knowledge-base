# Open Banking

**Definition.** Open Banking is a model in which banks expose customer account and payment data through secure, standardized APIs to authorized third parties with customer consent.
**Also known as:** Open Banking APIs.

## Relationships
- Open Banking is related to the Integration & APIs domain.
- Open Banking is related to the API Management capability.
- Open Banking mentions the ISO 20022 concept.

## Details
Driven by regulations such as the EU's PSD2 and the UK Open Banking standard, Open Banking enables account information and payment initiation services. It depends on robust API management, customer identity, and consent frameworks, and is a foundation for embedded finance.
