# Technology Capability

**Definition.** Technology Capability is a description of an IT-enabled ability, such as payment orchestration or data warehousing, that realizes and supports one or more business capabilities.
**Also known as:** Tech Capability.

## Relationships
- Technology Capability is related to the Business Capability concept.
- Technology Capability is related to the Cloud-Native concept.
- Technology Capability mentions the Core Banking concept.

## Details
Technology capabilities sit in technology domains such as Core Processing, Data & Analytics, and Integration & APIs, and are realized by specific systems (legacy or modern). Business capabilities depend on technology capabilities, which in turn depend on the systems that implement them.
