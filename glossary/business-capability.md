# Business Capability

**Definition.** Business Capability is a stable, outcome-oriented description of what a business does, independent of how, where, or by whom it is performed.
**Also known as:** Capability.

## Relationships
- Business Capability is related to the Value Stream concept.
- Business Capability is related to the Technology Capability concept.
- Business Capability is related to the BIAN concept.

## Details
Business capabilities are organized into leveled hierarchies (L1, L2, L3) and form the backbone of capability-based planning in TOGAF and BIZBOK. They are realized by people, process, and technology, and depend on technology capabilities for enablement.
