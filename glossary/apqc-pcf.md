# APQC PCF

**Definition.** APQC PCF is a taxonomy of cross-industry and industry-specific business processes published by APQC, used to define, benchmark, and improve enterprise process performance.
**Also known as:** Process Classification Framework.

## Relationships
- APQC PCF is related to the Value Stream concept.
- APQC PCF is related to the Business Capability concept.
- APQC PCF mentions the BIAN concept.

## Details
The APQC Process Classification Framework organizes work into operating and management/support process categories, groups, and activities, enabling consistent process benchmarking. APQC publishes a banking-specific PCF that complements capability models such as BIAN.
