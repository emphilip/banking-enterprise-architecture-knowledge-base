# ISO 20022

**Definition.** ISO 20022 is an international standard for electronic data interchange between financial institutions, defining a common XML/JSON-based messaging syntax and rich, structured financial data model.
**Also known as:** ISO20022.

## Relationships
- ISO 20022 is related to the Payments domain.
- ISO 20022 is related to the Payment Orchestration capability.
- ISO 20022 mentions the Open Banking concept.

## Details
ISO 20022 underpins modern payment rails including SWIFT cross-border, SEPA, and many real-time schemes, replacing legacy formats like MT messages. Its richer structured data improves straight-through processing, reconciliation, and financial-crime screening.
