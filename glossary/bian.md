# BIAN

**Definition.** BIAN is a not-for-profit industry consortium that defines a standardized service-oriented reference architecture for banking, organized as a Service Landscape of service domains.
**Also known as:** Banking Industry Architecture Network.

## Relationships
- BIAN is related to the Business Capability concept.
- BIAN is related to the Technology Capability concept.
- BIAN mentions the Value Stream concept.

## Details
BIAN's Service Landscape decomposes the bank into business areas, business domains, and service domains, giving institutions a common vocabulary for capability mapping, API design, and vendor evaluation. It is widely used as a reference model for enterprise architecture in financial services.
