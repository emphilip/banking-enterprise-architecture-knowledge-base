# Zettelkasten

**Definition.** Zettelkasten is a note-taking and knowledge-management method in which each note captures a single atomic idea and is densely linked to other notes to form an emergent network of knowledge.
**Also known as:** Atomic Notes.

## Relationships
- Zettelkasten is related to the Business Capability concept.
- Zettelkasten mentions the BIAN concept.

## Details
Popularized by sociologist Niklas Luhmann, the Zettelkasten method underpins this knowledge base's one-concept-per-file convention, where atomic notes carry explicit, named relationships. The linking discipline enables a knowledge graph to be extracted from the notes.
