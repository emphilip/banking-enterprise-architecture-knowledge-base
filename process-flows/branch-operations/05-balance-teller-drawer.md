# Balance Teller Drawer

**Definition.** Balance Teller Drawer counts drawer cash at end of day and reconciles it to the system balance, resolving differences.
**Also known as:** Drawer Balancing, Teller Recon.

## Relationships
- Balance Teller Drawer is defined in the Branch Operations process.
- Balance Teller Drawer causes Reconcile Branch Cash.
- Balance Teller Drawer depends on the Branch Banking capability.
- Balance Teller Drawer mentions Teller.

## Details
The Teller balances the drawer. Inputs are drawer cash and the system balance; outputs are a reconciled drawer. At the decision point, if a variance exists it is investigated. Controls include end-of-day branch balancing and variance investigation.
