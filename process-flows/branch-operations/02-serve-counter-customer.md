# Serve Counter Customer

**Definition.** Serve Counter Customer identifies the customer at the counter and captures the requested transaction or servicing.
**Also known as:** Counter Service, Customer Identification.

## Relationships
- Serve Counter Customer is defined in the Branch Operations process.
- Serve Counter Customer causes Process Teller Transaction.
- Serve Counter Customer depends on the Branch Banking capability.
- Serve Counter Customer mentions Teller.

## Details
The Teller serves the customer. Inputs are the customer, identity documents and request; outputs are a captured transaction. Controls include identity verification at the counter and accessibility provision.
