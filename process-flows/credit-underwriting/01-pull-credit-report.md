# Pull Credit Report

**Definition.** Pull Credit Report obtains bureau credit reports and scores for the borrower under a permissible purpose.
**Also known as:** Order Bureau Data, Credit Pull Step.

## Relationships
- Pull Credit Report is defined in the Credit Underwriting process.
- Pull Credit Report causes Score Credit Risk.
- Pull Credit Report depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.
- Pull Credit Report mentions Credit Underwriter.
- Pull Credit Report mentions Credit Report.

## Details
The Credit Underwriter orders the bureau data. Inputs are the decision-ready file; outputs are a Credit Report. Controls include FCRA permissible purpose, hard-pull consent and a bureau audit trail.
