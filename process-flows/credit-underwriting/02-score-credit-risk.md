# Score Credit Risk

**Definition.** Score Credit Risk applies scorecards and models to character and capital signals to derive a risk grade.
**Also known as:** Risk Grading Step, Credit Scoring.

## Relationships
- Score Credit Risk is defined in the Credit Underwriting process.
- Score Credit Risk causes Assess Affordability.
- Score Credit Risk depends on the Machine Learning Platform capability.
- Score Credit Risk mentions Credit Underwriter.

## Details
The Credit Underwriter reviews the model output. Inputs are the Credit Report; outputs are a risk grade. Controls include model governance, scorecard validation and fair-lending checks.
