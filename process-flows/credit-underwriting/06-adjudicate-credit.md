# Adjudicate Credit

**Definition.** Adjudicate Credit renders the approve, conditionally approve, decline or refer decision.
**Also known as:** Make Credit Decision, Adjudication Step.

## Relationships
- Adjudicate Credit is defined in the Credit Underwriting process.
- Adjudicate Credit depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Adjudicate Credit mentions Credit Underwriter.
- Adjudicate Credit mentions Credit Decision.

## Details
The Credit Underwriter renders the decision. Inputs are the policy-checked case; outputs are a Credit Decision. Controls include delegated authority, four-eyes on overrides and a decision audit. The decision branches on approve, conditionally approve, decline or refer, and this terminal step emits the Credit Decision Issued Event.
