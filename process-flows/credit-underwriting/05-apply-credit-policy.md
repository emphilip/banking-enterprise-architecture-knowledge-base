# Apply Credit Policy

**Definition.** Apply Credit Policy tests the consolidated assessment against credit policy rules and conditions.
**Also known as:** Policy Check Step, Conditions Setting.

## Relationships
- Apply Credit Policy is defined in the Credit Underwriting process.
- Apply Credit Policy causes Adjudicate Credit.
- Apply Credit Policy depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.
- Apply Credit Policy mentions Credit Underwriter.

## Details
The Credit Underwriter applies the ruleset. Inputs are the collateral assessment; outputs are a policy-checked case. Controls include the credit policy ruleset, exception logging and a conditions matrix.
