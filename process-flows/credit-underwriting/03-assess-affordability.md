# Assess Affordability

**Definition.** Assess Affordability tests repayment capacity by weighing income against obligations using DTI/DSR measures.
**Also known as:** Capacity Test, Affordability Step.

## Relationships
- Assess Affordability is defined in the Credit Underwriting process.
- Assess Affordability causes Evaluate Collateral.
- Assess Affordability depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.
- Assess Affordability mentions Credit Underwriter.

## Details
The Credit Underwriter tests capacity. Inputs are the risk grade; outputs are an affordability result. Controls include affordability policy, DTI/DSR limits and stress sensitivity testing.
