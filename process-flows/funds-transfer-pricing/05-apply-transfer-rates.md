# Apply Transfer Rates

**Definition.** Apply Transfer Rates applies approved transfer rates to assets and liabilities to assign FTP charges and credits.
**Also known as:** Apply FTP Rates, Assign Transfer Rates.

## Relationships
- Apply Transfer Rates is defined in the Funds Transfer Pricing process.
- Apply Transfer Rates causes Attribute Net Margin.
- Apply Transfer Rates depends on the Asset Liability Management capability.
- Apply Transfer Rates mentions ALM Analyst.
- Apply Transfer Rates mentions FTP Curve.

## Details
The ALM Analyst applies the approved FTP Curve transfer rates to assets and liabilities to assign FTP charges and credits. Inputs are the approved FTP Curve and balance-sheet positions; outputs are transfer-priced balances. Controls include rate-application reconciliation and a completeness check.
