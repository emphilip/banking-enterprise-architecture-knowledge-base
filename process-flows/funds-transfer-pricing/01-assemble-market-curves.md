# Assemble Market Curves

**Definition.** Assemble Market Curves assembles market funding curves (senior unsecured debt yields, swap-plus-spread) as inputs to the transfer curve.

## Relationships
- Assemble Market Curves is defined in the Funds Transfer Pricing process.
- Assemble Market Curves causes Build FTP Curve.
- Assemble Market Curves depends on the Asset Liability Management capability.
- Assemble Market Curves mentions ALM Analyst.

## Details
The ALM Analyst assembles market funding curves from senior unsecured debt yields and swap-plus-spread inputs. Inputs are market yields, swap curves and credit spreads; outputs are curated market curves. Controls include data-source governance and curve input validation.
