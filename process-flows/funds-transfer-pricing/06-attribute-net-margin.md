# Attribute Net Margin

**Definition.** Attribute Net Margin attributes net interest margin to business units, splitting customer spread from funding and liquidity cost.
**Also known as:** Attribute NIM, Allocate Net Margin.

## Relationships
- Attribute Net Margin is defined in the Funds Transfer Pricing process.
- Attribute Net Margin causes Report FTP Outcome.
- Attribute Net Margin depends on the Asset Liability Management capability.
- Attribute Net Margin mentions ALM Analyst.

## Details
The ALM Analyst attributes net interest margin to business units, splitting customer spread from funding and liquidity cost. Inputs are the transfer-priced balances; outputs are the NIM attribution. Controls include margin-attribution reconciliation and allocation-rule governance.
