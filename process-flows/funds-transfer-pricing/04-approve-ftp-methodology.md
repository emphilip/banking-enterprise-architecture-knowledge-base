# Approve FTP Methodology

**Definition.** Approve FTP Methodology presents the methodology, curve and assumptions to ALCO for approval before application.

## Relationships
- Approve FTP Methodology is defined in the Funds Transfer Pricing process.
- Approve FTP Methodology causes Apply Transfer Rates.
- Approve FTP Methodology depends on the Asset Liability Management capability.
- Approve FTP Methodology mentions ALM Analyst.
- Approve FTP Methodology mentions FTP Curve.

## Details
The ALCO Chair approves the methodology, draft FTP Curve and assumption set presented by the ALM Analyst, raising the FTP Curve Approved Event. Inputs are the draft FTP Curve and assumption set; outputs are the approved FTP Curve and methodology approval. Controls include FTP curve governance with ALCO approval and board-delegated ownership.
