# Compare Against Limits

**Definition.** Compare Against Limits compares exposures to approved limits and appetite and detects breaches.
**Also known as:** Check Limit Utilisation, Limit Comparison Step.

## Relationships
- Compare Against Limits is defined in the Risk Reporting process.
- Compare Against Limits causes Escalate Limit Breach.
- Compare Against Limits depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Compare Against Limits mentions Risk Reporting Manager.

## Details
The Risk Reporting Manager compares the reconciled risk dataset to risk limits into breach findings. Controls include limit-comparison rules, appetite measurement and tolerance bands.
