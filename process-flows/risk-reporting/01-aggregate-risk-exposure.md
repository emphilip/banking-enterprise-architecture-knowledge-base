# Aggregate Risk Exposure

**Definition.** Aggregate Risk Exposure gathers and aggregates risk data across systems and risk types for the reporting period.
**Also known as:** Aggregate Exposure, Risk Aggregation Step.

## Relationships
- Aggregate Risk Exposure is defined in the Risk Reporting process.
- Aggregate Risk Exposure causes Reconcile Risk Data.
- Aggregate Risk Exposure depends on the Data Warehousing capability.
- Aggregate Risk Exposure mentions Risk Reporting Manager.
- Aggregate Risk Exposure mentions Risk Register.

## Details
The Risk Reporting Manager aggregates risk data and the Risk Register into an aggregated risk dataset. Controls include BCBS 239 completeness, a single source and documented aggregation rules.
