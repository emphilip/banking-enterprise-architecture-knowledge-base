# Reconcile Risk Data

**Definition.** Reconcile Risk Data reconciles aggregated risk data to finance and source systems and validates quality and lineage.
**Also known as:** Validate Risk Data, Data Reconciliation Step.

## Relationships
- Reconcile Risk Data is defined in the Risk Reporting process.
- Reconcile Risk Data causes Compare Against Limits.
- Reconcile Risk Data depends on the Data Governance capability.
- Reconcile Risk Data mentions Risk Reporting Manager.

## Details
The Risk Reporting Manager reconciles the aggregated risk dataset into a reconciled dataset. Controls include BCBS 239 accuracy and adaptability, data lineage and reconciliation to the general ledger.
