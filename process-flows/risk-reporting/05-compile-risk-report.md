# Compile Risk Report

**Definition.** Compile Risk Report compiles the Risk Report covering all material risk types and the profile versus appetite.
**Also known as:** Produce Risk Report, Report Compilation Step.

## Relationships
- Compile Risk Report is defined in the Risk Reporting process.
- Compile Risk Report causes Distribute Risk Report.
- Compile Risk Report depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Compile Risk Report mentions Risk Reporting Manager.
- Compile Risk Report mentions Risk Report.
- Compile Risk Report mentions Stress Test Result.

## Details
The Risk Reporting Manager compiles the Risk Report from the reconciled risk dataset, breach findings and the Stress Test Result. Controls include BCBS 239 Principle 8 comprehensiveness, clarity and no material gaps.
