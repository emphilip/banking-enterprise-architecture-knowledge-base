# Distribute Risk Report

**Definition.** Distribute Risk Report distributes reports to the board and committees and submits regulatory returns, emitting the Limit Breach Event where applicable.
**Also known as:** Deliver Risk Report, Report Distribution Step.

## Relationships
- Distribute Risk Report is defined in the Risk Reporting process.
- Distribute Risk Report depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.
- Distribute Risk Report mentions Risk Reporting Manager.
- Distribute Risk Report mentions Risk Report.
- Distribute Risk Report mentions Limit Breach Event.

## Details
The Risk Reporting Manager distributes the Risk Report and limit-breach escalation to the board and committees and submits regulatory returns. Controls include board and committee distribution, regulatory submission and timeliness. This is the final step of Risk Reporting.
