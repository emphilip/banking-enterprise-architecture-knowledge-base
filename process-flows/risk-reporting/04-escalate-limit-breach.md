# Escalate Limit Breach

**Definition.** Escalate Limit Breach is the decision point that escalates detected breaches through governance per severity.
**Also known as:** Raise Breach, Breach Escalation Step.

## Relationships
- Escalate Limit Breach is defined in the Risk Reporting process.
- Escalate Limit Breach causes Compile Risk Report.
- Escalate Limit Breach depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Escalate Limit Breach mentions Risk Reporting Manager.
- Escalate Limit Breach mentions Limit Breach Event.

## Details
The Risk Reporting Manager turns breach findings into a limit-breach escalation and raises the Limit Breach Event. Controls include escalation thresholds, branching on breach severity and an audit trail.
