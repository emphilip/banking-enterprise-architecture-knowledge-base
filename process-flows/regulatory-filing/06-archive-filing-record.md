# Archive Filing Record

**Definition.** Archive Filing Record captures the acknowledgement and retains the filing record for audit, and emits the Regulatory Filing Submitted Event.
**Also known as:** Retain Filing, Filing Archival Step.

## Relationships
- Archive Filing Record is defined in the Regulatory Filing process.
- Archive Filing Record depends on the Document Management capability.
- Archive Filing Record mentions Regulatory Reporting Analyst.
- Archive Filing Record mentions Regulatory Filing Submitted Event.

## Details
The Regulatory Reporting Analyst captures the regulator acknowledgement and retains the record. Controls include acknowledgement capture, 5-year retention and audit trail, with completion signalled by the Regulatory Filing Submitted Event. This is the final step of Regulatory Filing.
