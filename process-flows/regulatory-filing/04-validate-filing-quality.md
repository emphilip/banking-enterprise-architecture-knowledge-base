# Validate Filing Quality

**Definition.** Validate Filing Quality is the decision point that validates the return and reviews it under four-eyes, returning errors for correction.
**Also known as:** Review Return, Filing Validation Step.

## Relationships
- Validate Filing Quality is defined in the Regulatory Filing process.
- Validate Filing Quality causes Submit Regulatory Return.
- Validate Filing Quality depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.
- Validate Filing Quality mentions Regulatory Reporting Analyst.
- Validate Filing Quality mentions CTR Filing.

## Details
The Regulatory Reporting Analyst validates the compiled return to produce a validated return and exceptions. Controls include validation rules, four-eyes review and a branch on pass versus rework before submission.
