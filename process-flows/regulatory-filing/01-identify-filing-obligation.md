# Identify Filing Obligation

**Definition.** Identify Filing Obligation identifies which return is due, such as a CTR or prudential return, the regulator and the deadline.
**Also known as:** Determine Filing, Obligation Identification Step.

## Relationships
- Identify Filing Obligation is defined in the Regulatory Filing process.
- Identify Filing Obligation causes Aggregate Filing Data.
- Identify Filing Obligation depends on the Regulatory Compliance capability.
- Identify Filing Obligation mentions Regulatory Reporting Analyst.
- Identify Filing Obligation mentions Regulatory Obligation.

## Details
The Regulatory Reporting Analyst maps the applicable Regulatory Obligation and filing calendar to a filing requirement. Controls include obligation mapping, deadline determination and threshold rules such as the CTR $10,000 reporting threshold.
