# Determine Outcome

**Definition.** Determine Outcome decides whether the complaint is upheld and what redress applies and authorises compensation within delegated limits.
**Also known as:** Decide Redress, Outcome Decision Step.

## Relationships
- Determine Outcome is defined in the Complaint Handling process.
- Determine Outcome causes Issue Final Response.
- Determine Outcome depends on the Case Management capability.
- Determine Outcome mentions Complaints Officer.
- Determine Outcome mentions Complaint Record.

## Details
The Complaints Officer decides the outcome. Inputs are the investigation findings; outputs are a redress decision. The redress authorisation and delegated-limit control branch across uphold, reject and partial.
