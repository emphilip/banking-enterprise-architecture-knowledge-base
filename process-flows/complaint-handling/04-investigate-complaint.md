# Investigate Complaint

**Definition.** Investigate Complaint investigates competently, diligently and impartially, gathering evidence and assessing merits against the facts.
**Also known as:** Examine Complaint, Complaint Investigation Step.

## Relationships
- Investigate Complaint is defined in the Complaint Handling process.
- Investigate Complaint causes Determine Outcome.
- Investigate Complaint depends on the Case Management capability.
- Investigate Complaint mentions Complaints Officer.
- Investigate Complaint mentions Complaint Record.

## Details
The Complaints Officer investigates the complaint. Inputs are the triaged complaint; outputs are investigation findings recorded in the Complaint Record. Controls include the impartial-investigation control and an evidence trail.
