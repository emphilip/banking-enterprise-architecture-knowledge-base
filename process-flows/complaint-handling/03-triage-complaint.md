# Triage Complaint

**Definition.** Triage Complaint classifies the category, checks eligibility and jurisdiction and prioritises, routing to the right handler or specialist team.
**Also known as:** Classify Complaint, Complaint Triage Step.

## Relationships
- Triage Complaint is defined in the Complaint Handling process.
- Triage Complaint causes Investigate Complaint.
- Triage Complaint depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Triage Complaint mentions Complaints Officer.
- Triage Complaint mentions Complaint Record.

## Details
The Complaints Officer triages the complaint. Inputs are the acknowledged complaint; outputs are a triaged complaint. The eligibility/jurisdiction check and priority assignment branch across route versus reject.
