# Close Complaint

**Definition.** Close Complaint confirms resolution with the customer, captures satisfaction feedback and closes and retains the complaint record.
**Also known as:** Resolve & Close, Complaint Closure Step.

## Relationships
- Close Complaint is defined in the Complaint Handling process.
- Close Complaint depends on the Case Management capability.
- Close Complaint mentions Complaints Officer.
- Close Complaint mentions Complaint Record.

## Details
The Complaints Officer confirms resolution and closes the Complaint Record. Inputs are the root cause report; outputs are a closed complaint. Controls include closure confirmation, feedback capture and 3-year record retention. This is the final step of the Complaint Handling flow.
