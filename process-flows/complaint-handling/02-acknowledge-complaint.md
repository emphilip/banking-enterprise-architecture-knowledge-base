# Acknowledge Complaint

**Definition.** Acknowledge Complaint sends a prompt acknowledgement with the firm's complaints procedure and expected timeframes and attempts summary resolution.
**Also known as:** Send Acknowledgement, Complaint Acknowledgement Step.

## Relationships
- Acknowledge Complaint is defined in the Complaint Handling process.
- Acknowledge Complaint causes Triage Complaint.
- Acknowledge Complaint depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Acknowledge Complaint mentions Complaints Officer.
- Acknowledge Complaint mentions Complaint Record.

## Details
The Complaints Officer sends the acknowledgement. Inputs are the logged complaint; outputs are an acknowledged complaint. The acknowledgement SLA control and 3-day summary-resolution option branch on whether summary resolution succeeds.
