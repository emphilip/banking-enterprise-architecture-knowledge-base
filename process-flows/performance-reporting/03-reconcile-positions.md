# Reconcile Positions

**Definition.** Reconcile Positions reconciles internal positions and cash against custodian records, branching matched or break.
**Also known as:** Position Reconciliation Step, Book Recon Step.

## Relationships
- Reconcile Positions is defined in the Performance Reporting process.
- Reconcile Positions causes Calculate Returns.
- Reconcile Positions depends on the Portfolio Management capability.
- Reconcile Positions mentions Performance Analyst.

## Details
The Performance Analyst reconciles adjusted positions and cash against custody records, branching matched or break. Controls include position/cash reconciliation and break investigation; a break is investigated before returns are calculated.
