# Process Corporate Actions

**Definition.** Process Corporate Actions captures issuer corporate-action events, calculates entitlements and adjusts positions and cash.
**Also known as:** Corporate Actions Step, Entitlement Processing Step.

## Relationships
- Process Corporate Actions is defined in the Performance Reporting process.
- Process Corporate Actions causes Reconcile Positions.
- Process Corporate Actions depends on the Portfolio Management capability.
- Process Corporate Actions mentions Performance Analyst.
- Process Corporate Actions mentions Corporate Action Announced Event.

## Details
The Performance Analyst processes the Corporate Action Announced Event, calculates entitlements and adjusts positions. Controls include corporate-action capture, entitlement calculation, and election cut-offs.
