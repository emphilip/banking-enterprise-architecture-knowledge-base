# Open Channel Session

**Definition.** Open Channel Session initiates a customer Channel Session and identifies the entry channel and intent.
**Also known as:** Start Channel Session, Session Init.

## Relationships
- Open Channel Session is defined in the Channel Servicing process.
- Open Channel Session causes Authenticate Channel User.
- Open Channel Session depends on the Channel Management capability.
- Open Channel Session mentions Channel Operations Analyst.
- Open Channel Session mentions Channel Session.

## Details
The Channel Operations Analyst opens the session. Inputs are the inbound channel interaction; outputs are a Channel Session. Controls include session integrity and accessibility; authentication is performed in the next step.
