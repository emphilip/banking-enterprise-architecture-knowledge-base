# Escalate To Assisted Servicing

**Definition.** Escalate To Assisted Servicing escalates unresolved requests to assisted servicing with full context preserved.
**Also known as:** Assisted Escalation, Warm Transfer.

## Relationships
- Escalate To Assisted Servicing is defined in the Channel Servicing process.
- Escalate To Assisted Servicing causes Record Channel Interaction.
- Escalate To Assisted Servicing depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Escalate To Assisted Servicing mentions Channel Operations Analyst.

## Details
The Channel Operations Analyst performs the warm handoff. Inputs are the unresolved request and context; outputs are a warm handoff. Controls include context-preserving handoff and no re-authentication loss.
