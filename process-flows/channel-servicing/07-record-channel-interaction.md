# Record Channel Interaction

**Definition.** Record Channel Interaction persists the Interaction Record and outcome to interaction history across channels.
**Also known as:** Log Interaction, Capture Channel Activity.

## Relationships
- Record Channel Interaction is defined in the Channel Servicing process.
- Record Channel Interaction depends on the CRM Platform capability.
- Record Channel Interaction mentions Channel Operations Analyst.
- Record Channel Interaction mentions Interaction Record.

## Details
The Channel Operations Analyst records the interaction. Inputs are the resolved request or handoff; outputs are an Interaction Record. Controls include the audit trail and data protection.
