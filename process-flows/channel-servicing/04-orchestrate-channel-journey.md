# Orchestrate Channel Journey

**Definition.** Orchestrate Channel Journey orchestrates the journey across channels, routing to self-service or assisted servicing.
**Also known as:** Channel Routing, Cross-Channel Orchestration.

## Relationships
- Orchestrate Channel Journey is defined in the Channel Servicing process.
- Orchestrate Channel Journey causes Resolve Self-Service Request.
- Orchestrate Channel Journey depends on the Integration Platform capability.
- Orchestrate Channel Journey mentions Channel Operations Analyst.

## Details
The Channel Operations Analyst orchestrates the journey. Inputs are the classified intent; outputs are an orchestrated journey. At the decision point, if the request is not self-serviceable it escalates. Controls include routing rules and escalation handoff control that preserves authenticated context.
