# Identify Service Intent

**Definition.** Identify Service Intent determines the customer's servicing intent and assembles cross-channel context.
**Also known as:** Intent Detection, Need Identification.

## Relationships
- Identify Service Intent is defined in the Channel Servicing process.
- Identify Service Intent causes Orchestrate Channel Journey.
- Identify Service Intent depends on the Conversational AI capability.
- Identify Service Intent mentions Channel Operations Analyst.

## Details
The Channel Operations Analyst classifies intent. Inputs are the authenticated session; outputs are a classified intent. Controls include cross-channel context integrity so that re-authentication and context are not lost on escalation.
