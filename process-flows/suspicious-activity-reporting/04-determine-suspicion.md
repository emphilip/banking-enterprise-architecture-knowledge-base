# Determine Suspicion

**Definition.** Determine Suspicion is the decision point that decides whether reasonable grounds for suspicion exist and escalates to the MLRO.
**Also known as:** Decide Suspicion, Suspicion Decision Step.

## Relationships
- Determine Suspicion is defined in the Suspicious Activity Reporting process.
- Determine Suspicion causes Lodge SAR Filing.
- Determine Suspicion depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.
- Determine Suspicion mentions AML Investigator.
- Determine Suspicion mentions MLRO.

## Details
The AML Investigator weighs the investigation findings to produce a suspicion determination. Controls include the reasonable-grounds standard, a branch on file versus close, and escalation to the MLRO for the filing decision.
