# Triage AML Alert

**Definition.** Triage AML Alert triages transaction-monitoring alerts, dedupes and prioritises them, and decides which warrant investigation.
**Also known as:** Screen AML Alert, Alert Triage Step.

## Relationships
- Triage AML Alert is defined in the Suspicious Activity Reporting process.
- Triage AML Alert causes Open Investigation Case.
- Triage AML Alert depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.
- Triage AML Alert mentions AML Investigator.
- Triage AML Alert mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The AML Investigator triages AML alerts into triaged alerts. Controls include alert triage, prioritisation, false-positive filtering and SLA, deciding which alerts proceed to a case.
