# Lodge SAR Filing

**Definition.** Lodge SAR Filing, on MLRO sign-off, prepares and files the SAR with the FIU within the filing deadline.
**Also known as:** Submit SAR, SAR Filing Step.

## Relationships
- Lodge SAR Filing is defined in the Suspicious Activity Reporting process.
- Lodge SAR Filing causes Notify Financial Intelligence.
- Lodge SAR Filing depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.
- Lodge SAR Filing mentions MLRO.
- Lodge SAR Filing mentions SAR Filing.

## Details
The MLRO prepares and files the SAR Filing from the suspicion determination and investigation findings. Controls include MLRO sign-off, the FinCEN 30-day (up to 60) deadline, narrative quality and BSA E-Filing.
