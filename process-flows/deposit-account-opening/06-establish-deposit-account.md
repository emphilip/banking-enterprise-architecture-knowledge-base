# Establish Deposit Account

**Definition.** Establish Deposit Account creates the account on the core ledger with product, mandate and limits.
**Also known as:** Create Account, Account Setup Step.

## Relationships
- Establish Deposit Account is defined in the Deposit Account Opening process.
- Establish Deposit Account causes Fund New Account.
- Establish Deposit Account depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Establish Deposit Account mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.
- Establish Deposit Account mentions Signature Card.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk creates the account record from accepted disclosures and the Signature Card. Controls include account setup controls and dual control on creation.
