# Capture Account Application

**Definition.** Capture Account Application captures applicant details and product selection on the Account Opening Form.
**Also known as:** Take Account Application, Opening Intake Step.

## Relationships
- Capture Account Application is defined in the Deposit Account Opening process.
- Capture Account Application causes Collect CIP Information.
- Capture Account Application depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.
- Capture Account Application mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Capture Account Application mentions Account Opening Form.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist captures the application; inputs are the Account Opening Form and outputs are an application record. Controls include data completeness and product eligibility checks at intake.
