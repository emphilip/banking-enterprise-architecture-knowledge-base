# Disclose Account Terms

**Definition.** Disclose Account Terms presents Truth-in-Savings (Reg DD) fee and rate disclosures and captures acceptance.
**Also known as:** Provide TIS Disclosure, Terms Disclosure Step.

## Relationships
- Disclose Account Terms is defined in the Deposit Account Opening process.
- Disclose Account Terms causes Establish Deposit Account.
- Disclose Account Terms depends on the Document Management capability.
- Disclose Account Terms mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Disclose Account Terms mentions Account Opening Form.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist presents product configuration and captures accepted disclosures. Controls require Reg DD fee and rate (Truth-in-Savings) disclosure prior to account establishment.
