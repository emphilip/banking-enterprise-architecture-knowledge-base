# Collect CIP Information

**Definition.** Collect CIP Information collects name, date of birth, address and TIN required under the Customer Identification Program.
**Also known as:** Gather CIP Data, CIP Collection Step.

## Relationships
- Collect CIP Information is defined in the Deposit Account Opening process.
- Collect CIP Information causes Verify Account Applicant.
- Collect CIP Information depends on the Intelligent Document Processing capability.
- Collect CIP Information mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Collect CIP Information mentions Account Opening Form.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist collects the CIP dataset from the application record and identity documents. Controls include CIP and KYC requirements and mandatory TIN collection per 31 CFR 1020.220.
