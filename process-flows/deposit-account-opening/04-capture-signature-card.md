# Capture Signature Card

**Definition.** Capture Signature Card records authorized signatories and the mandate on the Signature Card.
**Also known as:** Record Signatories, Signature Capture Step.

## Relationships
- Capture Signature Card is defined in the Deposit Account Opening process.
- Capture Signature Card causes Disclose Account Terms.
- Capture Signature Card depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Capture Signature Card mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.
- Capture Signature Card mentions Signature Card.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk records signatories from the verification result onto the Signature Card. Controls include signatory verification and operating mandate setup.
