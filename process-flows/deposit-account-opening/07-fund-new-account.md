# Fund New Account

**Definition.** Fund New Account applies the initial deposit and activates the account, emitting the Account Opened Event.
**Also known as:** Activate Account, Initial Funding Step.

## Relationships
- Fund New Account is defined in the Deposit Account Opening process.
- Fund New Account depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Fund New Account mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.
- Fund New Account mentions Account Opened Event.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk applies the initial deposit to the account record and activates the account. Controls include funding verification and activation; the Account Opened Event is emitted on completion.
