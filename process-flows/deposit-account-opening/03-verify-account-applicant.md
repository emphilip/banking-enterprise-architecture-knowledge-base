# Verify Account Applicant

**Definition.** Verify Account Applicant verifies identity via documentary and non-documentary methods, including ChexSystems checks.
**Also known as:** Run CIP Verification, Applicant Verification Step.

## Relationships
- Verify Account Applicant is defined in the Deposit Account Opening process.
- Verify Account Applicant causes Capture Signature Card.
- Verify Account Applicant depends on the Identity Verification capability.
- Verify Account Applicant mentions KYC Analyst.
- Verify Account Applicant mentions Account Opening Form.

## Details
The KYC Analyst verifies the CIP dataset and produces a verification result. Controls include CIP verification and watchlist screening before account setup proceeds.
