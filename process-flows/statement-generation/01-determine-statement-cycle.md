# Determine Statement Cycle

**Definition.** Determine Statement Cycle identifies accounts due in the cycle and locks the cycle period.
**Also known as:** Identify Cycle, Cycle Determination Step.

## Relationships
- Determine Statement Cycle is defined in the Statement Generation process.
- Determine Statement Cycle causes Extract Account Activity.
- Determine Statement Cycle depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Determine Statement Cycle mentions Statement Production Officer.

## Details
The Statement Production Officer identifies the cycle population from the cycle calendar and account list. Controls require cycle consistency within four days under Reg DD periodic statement rules.
