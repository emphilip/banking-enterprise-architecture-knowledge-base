# Assemble Statement Document

**Definition.** Assemble Statement Document composes statement content with the required Reg E and Reg DD disclosures.
**Also known as:** Compose Statement, Statement Assembly Step.

## Relationships
- Assemble Statement Document is defined in the Statement Generation process.
- Assemble Statement Document causes Render Statement Output.
- Assemble Statement Document depends on the Document Management capability.
- Assemble Statement Document mentions Statement Production Officer.

## Details
The Statement Production Officer composes the assembled statement from the reconciled dataset. Controls require Reg E and Reg DD periodic content in the statement.
