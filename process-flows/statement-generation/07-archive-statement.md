# Archive Statement

**Definition.** Archive Statement archives the statement for retention and self-service retrieval.
**Also known as:** Store Statement, Statement Archival Step.

## Relationships
- Archive Statement is defined in the Statement Generation process.
- Archive Statement depends on the Document Management capability.
- Archive Statement mentions Statement Production Officer.
- Archive Statement mentions Bank Account Statement.

## Details
The Statement Production Officer archives the delivered Bank Account Statement. Controls include record retention and secure self-service retrieval.
