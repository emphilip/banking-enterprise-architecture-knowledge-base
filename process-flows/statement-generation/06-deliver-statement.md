# Deliver Statement

**Definition.** Deliver Statement delivers via mail or electronic channel and emits the Statement Issued Event.
**Also known as:** Distribute Statement, Statement Delivery Step.

## Relationships
- Deliver Statement is defined in the Statement Generation process.
- Deliver Statement causes Archive Statement.
- Deliver Statement depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Deliver Statement mentions Statement Production Officer.
- Deliver Statement mentions Bank Account Statement.
- Deliver Statement mentions Statement Issued Event.

## Details
The Statement Production Officer delivers the rendered Bank Account Statement to the customer. Controls include delivery confirmation and e-consent; the Statement Issued Event is emitted on delivery.
