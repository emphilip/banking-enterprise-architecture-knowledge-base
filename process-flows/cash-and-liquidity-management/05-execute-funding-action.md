# Execute Funding Action

**Definition.** Execute Funding Action executes the agreed funding and placement transactions and updates the live position.
**Also known as:** Execute Funding, Place Funds.

## Relationships
- Execute Funding Action is defined in the Cash & Liquidity Management process.
- Execute Funding Action causes Reconcile End-Of-Day Cash.
- Execute Funding Action depends on the Liquidity Management capability.
- Execute Funding Action mentions Treasury Analyst.

## Details
The Treasury Analyst executes the agreed funding and placement transactions and updates the live position. Inputs are the funding decision; outputs are the executed funding and updated position. Controls include dual-authorisation of dealing and settlement confirmation.
