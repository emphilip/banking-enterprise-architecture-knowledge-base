# Assess Liquidity Buffers

**Definition.** Assess Liquidity Buffers assesses the projected position against LCR/NSFR buffers and internal limits, flagging breaches.
**Also known as:** Check Liquidity Limits, Assess LCR Buffer.

## Relationships
- Assess Liquidity Buffers is defined in the Cash & Liquidity Management process.
- Assess Liquidity Buffers causes Decide Funding Action.
- Assess Liquidity Buffers depends on the Liquidity Management capability.
- Assess Liquidity Buffers mentions Treasury Analyst.
- Assess Liquidity Buffers mentions Liquidity Position.

## Details
The Treasury Analyst assesses the projected Liquidity Position against LCR/NSFR buffers and internal limits. Inputs are the Liquidity Position and regulatory limits; outputs are the buffer assessment and breach flags. Controls include LCR/NSFR limit governance and limit-breach flagging; a breach raises the Liquidity Limit Breach Event and causes Decide Funding Action to escalate.
