# Report Liquidity Position

**Definition.** Report Liquidity Position produces the intraday and daily liquidity report for ALCO and the treasurer and obtains end-of-day sign-off.

## Relationships
- Report Liquidity Position is defined in the Cash & Liquidity Management process.
- Report Liquidity Position depends on the Liquidity Management capability.
- Report Liquidity Position mentions Treasury Analyst.
- Report Liquidity Position mentions Liquidity Position.

## Details
The Treasury Analyst produces the intraday and daily liquidity report from the reconciled cash and Liquidity Position for ALCO and the treasurer. Inputs are the reconciled cash and Liquidity Position; outputs are the liquidity report and sign-off. Controls include end-of-day cash sign-off and report distribution control.
