# Decide Funding Action

**Definition.** Decide Funding Action decides funding, placement or buffer actions to keep the position within limits and escalates breaches to the treasurer.

## Relationships
- Decide Funding Action is defined in the Cash & Liquidity Management process.
- Decide Funding Action causes Execute Funding Action.
- Decide Funding Action depends on the Liquidity Management capability.
- Decide Funding Action mentions Treasury Analyst.

## Details
The Treasury Analyst decides funding, placement or buffer actions to keep the position within limits and escalates breaches to the treasurer. Inputs are the buffer assessment and breach flags; outputs are the funding decision. Controls include funding-authority limits and treasurer escalation.
