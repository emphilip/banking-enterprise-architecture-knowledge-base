# Project Liquidity Position

**Definition.** Project Liquidity Position projects the intraday and short-term liquidity position from known and forecast payment flows.
**Also known as:** Forecast Liquidity, Project Cash Position.

## Relationships
- Project Liquidity Position is defined in the Cash & Liquidity Management process.
- Project Liquidity Position causes Assess Liquidity Buffers.
- Project Liquidity Position depends on the Liquidity Management capability.
- Project Liquidity Position mentions Treasury Analyst.
- Project Liquidity Position mentions Liquidity Position.

## Details
The Treasury Analyst projects the intraday and short-term Liquidity Position from known and forecast payment flows. Inputs are the aggregated cash position and payment flows; outputs are the Liquidity Position. Controls include intraday liquidity monitoring and flow-forecast validation.
