# Fund Digital Account

**Definition.** Fund Digital Account captures and processes initial account funding within the digital onboarding journey.
**Also known as:** Fund Online Account, First Deposit Capture.

## Relationships
- Fund Digital Account is defined in the Digital Onboarding Journey process.
- Fund Digital Account causes Activate Digital Channel.
- Fund Digital Account depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.
- Fund Digital Account mentions Digital Onboarding Specialist.

## Details
The Digital Onboarding Specialist oversees funding. Inputs are the verified identity and a funding source; outputs are a funded account. At the decision point, if funding is not completed the account activates with a zero balance and a nudge follows. Controls include funding-source validation and AML on the first deposit.
