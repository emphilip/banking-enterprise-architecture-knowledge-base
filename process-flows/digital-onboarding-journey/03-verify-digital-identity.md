# Verify Digital Identity

**Definition.** Verify Digital Identity runs electronic identity verification (eKYC), performing ID document and biometric / liveness checks.
**Also known as:** Run eKYC, Digital ID Proofing.

## Relationships
- Verify Digital Identity is defined in the Digital Onboarding Journey process.
- Verify Digital Identity causes Enrol Strong Authentication.
- Verify Digital Identity depends on the Identity Proofing Service capability.
- Verify Digital Identity mentions Digital Onboarding Specialist.

## Details
The Digital Onboarding Specialist oversees the eKYC handoff. Inputs are captured applicant data and ID documents; outputs are a verified identity. At the decision point, if verification fails the case routes to manual review. Controls include the eKYC / digital-identity handoff, document authenticity and liveness, and fraud screening.
