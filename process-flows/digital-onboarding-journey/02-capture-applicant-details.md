# Capture Applicant Details

**Definition.** Capture Applicant Details captures applicant personal and contact data and consents, with pre-fill where available.
**Also known as:** Capture Application Data, Applicant Capture.

## Relationships
- Capture Applicant Details is defined in the Digital Onboarding Journey process.
- Capture Applicant Details causes Verify Digital Identity.
- Capture Applicant Details depends on the Digital Banking capability.
- Capture Applicant Details mentions Digital Onboarding Specialist.
- Capture Applicant Details mentions Digital Application.

## Details
The Digital Onboarding Specialist captures applicant data. Inputs are the Digital Application; outputs are captured applicant data. Controls include consent management, data minimisation and accessibility, preparing the record for eKYC handoff.
