# Activate Digital Channel

**Definition.** Activate Digital Channel provisions digital banking access, delivers the welcome and hands off to back-office Customer Onboarding and KYC.
**Also known as:** Channel Activation, Welcome Enablement.

## Relationships
- Activate Digital Channel is defined in the Digital Onboarding Journey process.
- Activate Digital Channel depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Activate Digital Channel mentions Digital Onboarding Specialist.

## Details
The Digital Onboarding Specialist completes activation. Inputs are the funded account and SCA credentials; outputs are an activated digital customer. Controls include welcome controls and the KYC back-office handoff that completes the eKYC chain.
