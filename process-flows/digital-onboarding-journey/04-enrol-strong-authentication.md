# Enrol Strong Authentication

**Definition.** Enrol Strong Authentication enrols the customer in strong customer authentication (passkey or MFA) credentials for ongoing access.
**Also known as:** Enrol SCA, Set Up MFA.

## Relationships
- Enrol Strong Authentication is defined in the Digital Onboarding Journey process.
- Enrol Strong Authentication causes Fund Digital Account.
- Enrol Strong Authentication depends on the Customer Authentication capability.
- Enrol Strong Authentication mentions Digital Onboarding Specialist.

## Details
The Digital Onboarding Specialist drives SCA enrolment after the eKYC handoff. Inputs are the verified identity; outputs are SCA credentials. Controls include SCA enrolment and possession / inherence factor binding.
