# Assign Risk Treatment

**Definition.** Assign Risk Treatment decides treatment (accept, mitigate, transfer or avoid), assigns an owner and sets a target date.
**Also known as:** Decide Risk Response, Treatment Assignment Step.

## Relationships
- Assign Risk Treatment is defined in the Risk Identification & Assessment process.
- Assign Risk Treatment causes Track Risk Remediation.
- Assign Risk Treatment depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Assign Risk Treatment mentions Risk Analyst.

## Details
The Risk Analyst turns the residual rating and appetite gap into a treatment plan with an assigned owner. Controls include treatment-decision governance, owner accountability and escalation of out-of-appetite risks.
