# Evaluate Residual Risk

**Definition.** Evaluate Residual Risk assesses control effectiveness, derives residual risk and compares it against risk appetite.
**Also known as:** Assess Residual Risk, Residual Evaluation Step.

## Relationships
- Evaluate Residual Risk is defined in the Risk Identification & Assessment process.
- Evaluate Residual Risk causes Assign Risk Treatment.
- Evaluate Residual Risk depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Evaluate Residual Risk mentions Risk Analyst.
- Evaluate Residual Risk mentions Risk Appetite Statement.

## Details
The Risk Analyst derives a residual risk rating and appetite gap from the inherent rating, control inventory and the Risk Appetite Statement. Controls include control-effectiveness assessment and residual-versus-appetite evaluation.
