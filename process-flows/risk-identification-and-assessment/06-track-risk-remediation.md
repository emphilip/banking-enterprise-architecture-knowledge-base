# Track Risk Remediation

**Definition.** Track Risk Remediation tracks remediation actions to closure, refreshes the register and emits the Risk Identified Event closure.
**Also known as:** Monitor Remediation, Remediation Tracking Step.

## Relationships
- Track Risk Remediation is defined in the Risk Identification & Assessment process.
- Track Risk Remediation depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Track Risk Remediation mentions Risk Analyst.
- Track Risk Remediation mentions Risk Register.
- Track Risk Remediation mentions Risk Identified Event.

## Details
The Risk Analyst drives the treatment plan to closure, refreshes the Risk Register and closes the Risk Identified Event lifecycle. Controls include remediation tracking, a next-review-date control and a re-assessment trigger. This is the final step of Risk Identification & Assessment.
