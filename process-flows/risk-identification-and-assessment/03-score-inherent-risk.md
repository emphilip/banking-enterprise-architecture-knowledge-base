# Score Inherent Risk

**Definition.** Score Inherent Risk scores inherent risk before controls using likelihood and impact scales.
**Also known as:** Rate Inherent Risk, Inherent Scoring Step.

## Relationships
- Score Inherent Risk is defined in the Risk Identification & Assessment process.
- Score Inherent Risk causes Evaluate Residual Risk.
- Score Inherent Risk depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Score Inherent Risk mentions Risk Analyst.
- Score Inherent Risk mentions Risk Register.

## Details
The Risk Analyst scores each Risk Register entry into an inherent risk rating. Controls include standardized 1-5 scales, scoring consistency and the inherent-before-controls rule.
