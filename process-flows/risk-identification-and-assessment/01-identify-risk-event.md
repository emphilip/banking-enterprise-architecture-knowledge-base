# Identify Risk Event

**Definition.** Identify Risk Event identifies risks across categories from activities, loss events and emerging-risk scans.
**Also known as:** Spot Risk, Risk Identification Step.

## Relationships
- Identify Risk Event is defined in the Risk Identification & Assessment process.
- Identify Risk Event causes Register Risk Item.
- Identify Risk Event depends on the Operational Risk Management capability.
- Identify Risk Event mentions Risk Analyst.

## Details
The Risk Analyst surfaces identified risk items from business activities and loss events. Controls include COSO identification, taxonomy completeness and emerging-risk scanning so the picture stays current.
