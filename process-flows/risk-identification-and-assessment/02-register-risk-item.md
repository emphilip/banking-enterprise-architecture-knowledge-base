# Register Risk Item

**Definition.** Register Risk Item records each risk in the Risk Register with description, category and provisional owner.
**Also known as:** Log Risk, Risk Registration Step.

## Relationships
- Register Risk Item is defined in the Risk Identification & Assessment process.
- Register Risk Item causes Score Inherent Risk.
- Register Risk Item depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Register Risk Item mentions Risk Analyst.
- Register Risk Item mentions Risk Register.

## Details
The Risk Analyst records each identified item as a Risk Register entry. Controls include register completeness, mandatory fields and stable ID assignment for traceability.
