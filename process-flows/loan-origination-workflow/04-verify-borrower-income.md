# Verify Borrower Income

**Definition.** Verify Borrower Income verifies income, employment and assets against the submitted documentation.
**Also known as:** Income Verification Step, Verify Affordability Inputs.

## Relationships
- Verify Borrower Income is defined in the Loan Origination Workflow process.
- Verify Borrower Income causes Render Loan Decision.
- Verify Borrower Income depends on the Document Management capability.
- Verify Borrower Income mentions Loan Processor.

## Details
The Loan Processor verifies income, employment and assets. Inputs are the document set; outputs are a verified loan file. Controls include income and employment verification, four-eyes on overrides and AML screening.
