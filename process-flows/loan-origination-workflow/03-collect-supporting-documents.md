# Collect Supporting Documents

**Definition.** Collect Supporting Documents gathers and classifies income, identity and asset documents needed to complete the file.
**Also known as:** Gather Loan Documents, Document Intake Step.

## Relationships
- Collect Supporting Documents is defined in the Loan Origination Workflow process.
- Collect Supporting Documents causes Verify Borrower Income.
- Collect Supporting Documents depends on the Intelligent Document Processing capability.
- Collect Supporting Documents mentions Loan Processor.

## Details
The Loan Processor gathers and classifies documents. Inputs are the pre-qualified application; outputs are a document set. Controls include the document checklist, retention rules and a completeness control before verification.
