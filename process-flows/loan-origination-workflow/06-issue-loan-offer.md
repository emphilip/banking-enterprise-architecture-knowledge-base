# Issue Loan Offer

**Definition.** Issue Loan Offer generates and presents the credit offer with terms, pricing and regulatory disclosures.
**Also known as:** Present Loan Offer, Offer Issuance Step.

## Relationships
- Issue Loan Offer is defined in the Loan Origination Workflow process.
- Issue Loan Offer causes Disburse Loan Funds.
- Issue Loan Offer depends on the Loan Origination Platform capability.
- Issue Loan Offer mentions Loan Officer.

## Details
The Loan Officer presents the offer. Inputs are the Credit Decision; outputs are a loan offer. Controls include regulatory disclosures, pricing governance and an offer-validity control.
