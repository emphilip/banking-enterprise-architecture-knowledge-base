# Verify Account Holder

**Definition.** Verify Account Holder verifies requester identity and signatory authority against the mandate.
**Also known as:** Authenticate Account Holder, Holder Verification Step.

## Relationships
- Verify Account Holder is defined in the Account Maintenance process.
- Verify Account Holder causes Assess Change Impact.
- Verify Account Holder depends on the Identity Access Management capability.
- Verify Account Holder mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.
- Verify Account Holder mentions Signature Card.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk authenticates the requester against the Signature Card mandate. Controls include signatory verification and customer authentication before any change.
