# Apply Account Change

**Definition.** Apply Account Change executes the change in the core system under dual control.
**Also known as:** Execute Account Change, Change Execution Step.

## Relationships
- Apply Account Change is defined in the Account Maintenance process.
- Apply Account Change causes Post Account Interest.
- Apply Account Change depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Apply Account Change mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.
- Apply Account Change mentions Service Maintenance Request.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk applies the change assessment to produce an updated account record. Controls include dual control on static-data changes.
