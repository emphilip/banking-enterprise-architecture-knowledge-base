# Assess Change Impact

**Definition.** Assess Change Impact determines downstream impacts and whether a change-in-terms notice is required.
**Also known as:** Evaluate Change, Impact Assessment Step.

## Relationships
- Assess Change Impact is defined in the Account Maintenance process.
- Assess Change Impact causes Apply Account Change.
- Assess Change Impact depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Assess Change Impact mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.
- Assess Change Impact mentions Service Maintenance Request.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk evaluates the verified request for downstream impacts. Controls include the Reg DD change-in-terms assessment to determine notice obligations.
