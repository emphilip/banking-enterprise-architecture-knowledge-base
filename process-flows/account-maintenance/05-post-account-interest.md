# Post Account Interest

**Definition.** Post Account Interest calculates and posts accrued interest and any fee changes to the account.
**Also known as:** Apply Interest Posting, Interest Posting Step.

## Relationships
- Post Account Interest is defined in the Account Maintenance process.
- Post Account Interest causes Notify Account Change.
- Post Account Interest depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Post Account Interest mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk posts accrued interest and fee changes to the updated account record. Controls include interest accrual accuracy and Reg DD fee disclosure.
