# Notify Account Change

**Definition.** Notify Account Change issues confirmation and any required change-in-terms notice to the customer.
**Also known as:** Confirm Account Change, Change Notification Step.

## Relationships
- Notify Account Change is defined in the Account Maintenance process.
- Notify Account Change depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Notify Account Change mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk issues a change confirmation from the updated account record. Controls include the Reg DD change-in-terms notice and delivery confirmation.
