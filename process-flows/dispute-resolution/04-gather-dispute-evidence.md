# Gather Dispute Evidence

**Definition.** Gather Dispute Evidence collects transaction records and cardholder statements to support the chargeback.

## Relationships
- Gather Dispute Evidence is defined in the Dispute Resolution process.
- Gather Dispute Evidence causes Adjudicate Dispute Outcome.
- Gather Dispute Evidence depends on the Document Management capability.
- Gather Dispute Evidence mentions Chargeback Case.
- Gather Dispute Evidence mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Gather Dispute Evidence assembles transaction records and cardholder statements into an evidence package, with the burden of proof resting on the issuer.
