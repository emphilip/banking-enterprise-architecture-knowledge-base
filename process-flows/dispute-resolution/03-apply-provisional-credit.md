# Apply Provisional Credit

**Definition.** Apply Provisional Credit issues provisional credit to the cardholder within regulatory timeframes.

## Relationships
- Apply Provisional Credit is defined in the Dispute Resolution process.
- Apply Provisional Credit causes Gather Dispute Evidence.
- Apply Provisional Credit depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Apply Provisional Credit mentions Chargeback Case.
- Apply Provisional Credit mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Apply Provisional Credit posts provisional credit to the cardholder account under the Reg E 10-business-day provisional-credit rule pending investigation.
