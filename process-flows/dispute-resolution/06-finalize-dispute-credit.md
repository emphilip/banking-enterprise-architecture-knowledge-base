# Finalize Dispute Credit

**Definition.** Finalize Dispute Credit finalizes provisional credit (make permanent or reverse) and notifies the cardholder, emitting the Dispute Resolved Event.

## Relationships
- Finalize Dispute Credit is defined in the Dispute Resolution process.
- Finalize Dispute Credit depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Finalize Dispute Credit mentions Dispute Resolved Event.
- Finalize Dispute Credit mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Finalize Dispute Credit makes the provisional credit permanent or reverses it, issues a notice of correction or denial, and raises the Dispute Resolved Event. This is the final step of Dispute Resolution.
