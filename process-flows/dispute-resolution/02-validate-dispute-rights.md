# Validate Dispute Rights

**Definition.** Validate Dispute Rights verifies the claim falls within Reg Z / Reg E error-resolution rights and timelines.

## Relationships
- Validate Dispute Rights is defined in the Dispute Resolution process.
- Validate Dispute Rights causes Apply Provisional Credit.
- Validate Dispute Rights depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Validate Dispute Rights mentions Chargeback Case.
- Validate Dispute Rights mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Validate Dispute Rights confirms the claim is within Reg Z 60-day billing-error and Reg E error-resolution timelines and commits the issuer to a reasonable investigation.
