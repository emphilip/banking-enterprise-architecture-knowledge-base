# Register Dispute Claim

**Definition.** Register Dispute Claim registers a cardholder dispute claim against a posted transaction and opens the Chargeback Case.

## Relationships
- Register Dispute Claim is defined in the Dispute Resolution process.
- Register Dispute Claim causes Validate Dispute Rights.
- Register Dispute Claim depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Register Dispute Claim mentions Chargeback Case.
- Register Dispute Claim mentions Card Statement.
- Register Dispute Claim mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Register Dispute Claim logs the cardholder claim against a Card Statement transaction and opens the Chargeback Case, recording the claim within the Reg Z 60-day billing-error window.
