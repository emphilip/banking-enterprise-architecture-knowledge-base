# Adjudicate Dispute Outcome

**Definition.** Adjudicate Dispute Outcome determines the dispute outcome and decides whether to raise a chargeback.

## Relationships
- Adjudicate Dispute Outcome is defined in the Dispute Resolution process.
- Adjudicate Dispute Outcome causes Finalize Dispute Credit.
- Adjudicate Dispute Outcome depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Adjudicate Dispute Outcome mentions Chargeback Case.
- Adjudicate Dispute Outcome mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Adjudicate Dispute Outcome decides the dispute on the evidence within the Reg E 45/90-day investigation deadline and determines whether to raise a chargeback.
