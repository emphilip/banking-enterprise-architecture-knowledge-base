# Approve Rebalance Plan

**Definition.** Approve Rebalance Plan obtains four-eyes approval of the compliant rebalance plan.
**Also known as:** Rebalance Approval Step, Sign Off Rebalance Step.

## Relationships
- Approve Rebalance Plan is defined in the Portfolio Rebalancing process.
- Approve Rebalance Plan causes Raise Rebalance Orders.
- Approve Rebalance Plan depends on the Portfolio Management capability.
- Approve Rebalance Plan mentions Portfolio Manager.

## Details
The Portfolio Manager obtains four-eyes approval of the compliant rebalance plan from the compliance result and proposal. Controls include four-eyes approval and audit trail.
