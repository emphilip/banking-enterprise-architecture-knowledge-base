# Attest Change Compliance

**Definition.** Attest Change Compliance attests that the change is embedded and operating, with full traceability, and emits the Regulatory Obligation Updated Event.
**Also known as:** Sign Off Change, Change Attestation Step.

## Relationships
- Attest Change Compliance is defined in the Regulatory Change Management process.
- Attest Change Compliance depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.
- Attest Change Compliance mentions Regulatory Change Manager.
- Attest Change Compliance mentions Regulatory Obligation Updated Event.

## Details
The Regulatory Change Manager attests that the implemented controls embed the obligation. Controls include attestation, complete traceability for regulatory defence and audit trail, with completion signalled by the Regulatory Obligation Updated Event. This is the final step of Regulatory Change Management.
