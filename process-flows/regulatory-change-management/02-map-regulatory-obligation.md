# Map Regulatory Obligation

**Definition.** Map Regulatory Obligation maps each change to affected internal Regulatory Obligations, policies, controls and owners.
**Also known as:** Map Obligation, Obligation Mapping Step.

## Relationships
- Map Regulatory Obligation is defined in the Regulatory Change Management process.
- Map Regulatory Obligation causes Assess Regulatory Impact.
- Map Regulatory Obligation depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.
- Map Regulatory Obligation mentions Regulatory Change Manager.
- Map Regulatory Obligation mentions Regulatory Obligation.

## Details
The Regulatory Change Manager maps identified change items and the obligation library into a mapped Regulatory Obligation. Controls include obligation mapping, ownership assignment and traceability.
