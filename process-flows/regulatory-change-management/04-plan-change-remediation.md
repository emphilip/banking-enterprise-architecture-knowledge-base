# Plan Change Remediation

**Definition.** Plan Change Remediation is the decision point that decides whether remediation is required and plans control and process changes and dates.
**Also known as:** Plan Remediation, Remediation Planning Step.

## Relationships
- Plan Change Remediation is defined in the Regulatory Change Management process.
- Plan Change Remediation causes Implement Compliance Control.
- Plan Change Remediation depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Plan Change Remediation mentions Regulatory Change Manager.
- Plan Change Remediation mentions Regulatory Obligation.

## Details
The Regulatory Change Manager uses the impact assessment and priority to produce a remediation plan. Controls include remediation planning, a branch on action versus no-action and target dates.
