# Scan Regulatory Change

**Definition.** Scan Regulatory Change horizon-scans regulatory feeds and publications to detect emerging or finalised regulatory change.
**Also known as:** Horizon Scan, Regulatory Scanning Step.

## Relationships
- Scan Regulatory Change is defined in the Regulatory Change Management process.
- Scan Regulatory Change causes Map Regulatory Obligation.
- Scan Regulatory Change depends on the Regulatory Compliance capability.
- Scan Regulatory Change mentions Regulatory Change Manager.
- Scan Regulatory Change mentions Regulatory Obligation.

## Details
The Regulatory Change Manager scans regulatory feeds and rule publications to produce identified change items. Controls include horizon-scanning coverage, source completeness and early-signal detection.
