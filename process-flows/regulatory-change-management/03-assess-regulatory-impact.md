# Assess Regulatory Impact

**Definition.** Assess Regulatory Impact assesses business, operational, cost and timeline impact and assigns a priority classification.
**Also known as:** Evaluate Change Impact, Regulatory Impact Assessment Step.

## Relationships
- Assess Regulatory Impact is defined in the Regulatory Change Management process.
- Assess Regulatory Impact causes Plan Change Remediation.
- Assess Regulatory Impact depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.
- Assess Regulatory Impact mentions Regulatory Change Manager.
- Assess Regulatory Impact mentions Regulatory Obligation.

## Details
The Regulatory Change Manager assesses the mapped Regulatory Obligation to produce an impact assessment and priority. Controls include a multi-dimension impact methodology, priority classification and SME input.
