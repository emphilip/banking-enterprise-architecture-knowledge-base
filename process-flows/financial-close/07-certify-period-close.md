# Certify Period Close

**Definition.** Certify Period Close reviews reconciliations and statements, evidences ICFR controls and obtains controller sign-off to certify the period close.

## Relationships
- Certify Period Close is defined in the Financial Close process.
- Certify Period Close depends on the Financial Reporting capability.
- Certify Period Close mentions Financial Controller.
- Certify Period Close mentions Consolidated Financial Statements.

## Details
The Financial Controller reviews the Consolidated Financial Statements and control evidence and certifies the close. Inputs are the Consolidated Financial Statements and control evidence; outputs are the close certification and signed-off statements. Controls include SOX/ICFR evidencing and controller sign-off.
