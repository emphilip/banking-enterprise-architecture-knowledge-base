# Post Closing Journals

**Definition.** Post Closing Journals posts recurring and adjusting period-end journals (accruals, prepaids, depreciation, reclasses) with maker-checker approval.
**Also known as:** Post Adjusting Entries, Book Period-End Journals.

## Relationships
- Post Closing Journals is defined in the Financial Close process.
- Post Closing Journals causes Reconcile Ledger Accounts.
- Post Closing Journals depends on the General Ledger Accounting capability.
- Post Closing Journals mentions Financial Controller.
- Post Closing Journals mentions Journal Entry.

## Details
The General Ledger Accountant posts the period-end Journal Entry set. Inputs are accruals, depreciation schedules and the GL feed; outputs are the posted Journal Entry set. Controls centre on journal approval and segregation of duties, with maker-checker enforced so the preparer cannot self-approve.
