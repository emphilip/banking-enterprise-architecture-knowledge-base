# Block Compromised Card

**Definition.** Block Compromised Card blocks the compromised card/credential to stop further authorizations.

## Relationships
- Block Compromised Card is defined in the Card Fraud Handling process.
- Block Compromised Card causes Reissue Card.
- Block Compromised Card depends on the Card Processing capability.
- Block Compromised Card mentions Card Fraud Analyst.

## Details
Block Compromised Card hotlists the compromised card and Card Credential with immediate effect and block confirmation, stopping further authorizations.
