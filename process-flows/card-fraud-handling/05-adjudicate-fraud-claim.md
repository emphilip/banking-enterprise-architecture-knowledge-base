# Adjudicate Fraud Claim

**Definition.** Adjudicate Fraud Claim investigates and adjudicates the fraud claim and applies Reg E liability.

## Relationships
- Adjudicate Fraud Claim is defined in the Card Fraud Handling process.
- Adjudicate Fraud Claim causes Refer Suspicious Activity.
- Adjudicate Fraud Claim depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Adjudicate Fraud Claim mentions Card Fraud Analyst.

## Details
Adjudicate Fraud Claim investigates and adjudicates the claim, applying Reg E unauthorized-EFT liability with the burden of proof on the issuer.
