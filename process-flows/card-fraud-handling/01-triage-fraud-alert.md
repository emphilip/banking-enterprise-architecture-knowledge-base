# Triage Fraud Alert

**Definition.** Triage Fraud Alert triages and prioritizes a Fraud Alert raised by scoring/velocity rules.

## Relationships
- Triage Fraud Alert is defined in the Card Fraud Handling process.
- Triage Fraud Alert causes Verify Cardholder Activity.
- Triage Fraud Alert depends on the Fraud Analytics capability.
- Triage Fraud Alert mentions Fraud Alert.
- Triage Fraud Alert mentions Card Fraud Analyst.

## Details
Triage Fraud Alert prioritizes a Fraud Alert raised by real-time scoring and velocity rules, ordering cases for action under alert-prioritization controls.
