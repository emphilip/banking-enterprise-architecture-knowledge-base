# Refer Suspicious Activity

**Definition.** Refer Suspicious Activity refers confirmed fraud for SAR consideration and regulatory reporting, emitting the Fraud Detected Event.

## Relationships
- Refer Suspicious Activity is defined in the Card Fraud Handling process.
- Refer Suspicious Activity depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.
- Refer Suspicious Activity mentions Fraud Detected Event.
- Refer Suspicious Activity mentions Card Fraud Analyst.

## Details
Refer Suspicious Activity refers confirmed fraud for SAR consideration against filing thresholds, records the audit trail and raises the Fraud Detected Event. This is the final step of Card Fraud Handling.
