# Reissue Card

**Definition.** Reissue Card instructs reissue/re-credential of the card and re-provisions wallet tokens.

## Relationships
- Reissue Card is defined in the Card Fraud Handling process.
- Reissue Card causes Adjudicate Fraud Claim.
- Reissue Card depends on the Card Processing capability.
- Reissue Card mentions Card Credential.
- Reissue Card mentions Card Fraud Analyst.

## Details
Reissue Card instructs secure reissue and re-credentialing of the card and re-provisions the network tokens to the cardholder's digital wallets.
