# Assemble Capital Return

**Definition.** Assemble Capital Return assembles the COREP/FINREP and FR Y-9C/FR Y-14 schedules into the Capital Adequacy Return.
**Also known as:** Build Capital Return, Compile COREP.

## Relationships
- Assemble Capital Return is defined in the Regulatory Capital Reporting process.
- Assemble Capital Return causes Validate Return Quality.
- Assemble Capital Return depends on the Regulatory Capital Management capability.
- Assemble Capital Return mentions Regulatory Reporting Specialist.
- Assemble Capital Return mentions Capital Adequacy Return.

## Details
The Regulatory Reporting Specialist assembles the COREP/FINREP and FR Y-9C/FR Y-14 schedules into the Capital Adequacy Return. Inputs are own funds, capital ratios and schedule templates; outputs are the Capital Adequacy Return. Controls include schedule completeness control and template version control.
