# Attest Capital Return

**Definition.** Attest Capital Return evidences ICFR over regulatory reporting and obtains CFO and board attestation of the return and capital plan.
**Also known as:** Sign Off Capital Return, Attest FR Y-14.

## Relationships
- Attest Capital Return is defined in the Regulatory Capital Reporting process.
- Attest Capital Return causes Submit Capital Return.
- Attest Capital Return depends on the Regulatory Capital Management capability.
- Attest Capital Return mentions Regulatory Reporting Specialist.
- Attest Capital Return mentions Capital Adequacy Return.

## Details
The Chief Financial Officer attests the validated Capital Adequacy Return after the Regulatory Reporting Specialist evidences ICFR over regulatory reporting. Inputs are the validated return and control evidence; outputs are the attested return and board attestation. Controls include ICFR over regulatory reporting, FR Y-14 attestation and board/CFO sign-off.
