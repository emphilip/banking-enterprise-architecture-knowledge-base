# Submit Capital Return

**Definition.** Submit Capital Return submits the attested return and capital plan to the regulator through the secure channel and archives the filing.

## Relationships
- Submit Capital Return is defined in the Regulatory Capital Reporting process.
- Submit Capital Return depends on the Regulatory Capital Management capability.
- Submit Capital Return mentions Regulatory Reporting Specialist.
- Submit Capital Return mentions Capital Adequacy Return.

## Details
The Regulatory Reporting Specialist submits the attested Capital Adequacy Return and capital plan to the regulator through the secure channel and archives the filing. Inputs are the attested return and board attestation; outputs are the regulatory submission and filing receipt. Controls include CCAR submission, submission-channel control and filing archive.
