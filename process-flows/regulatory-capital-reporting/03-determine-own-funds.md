# Determine Own Funds

**Definition.** Determine Own Funds determines Tier 1 and Tier 2 own funds and computes capital-adequacy ratios from risk-weighted assets.
**Also known as:** Compute Own Funds, Determine Capital Base.

## Relationships
- Determine Own Funds is defined in the Regulatory Capital Reporting process.
- Determine Own Funds causes Assemble Capital Return.
- Determine Own Funds depends on the Regulatory Capital Management capability.
- Determine Own Funds mentions Regulatory Reporting Specialist.

## Details
The Regulatory Reporting Specialist determines Tier 1 and Tier 2 own funds and computes capital ratios. Inputs are own-funds data and risk-weighted assets; outputs are own funds and capital ratios. Controls include own-funds reconciliation to the GL and ratio review.
