# Confirm Resolution

**Definition.** Confirm Resolution notifies the customer that the request is complete, shares outcome details and confirms satisfaction.
**Also known as:** Notify Customer, Resolution Confirmation Step.

## Relationships
- Confirm Resolution is defined in the Service Request Handling process.
- Confirm Resolution causes Close Request.
- Confirm Resolution depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Confirm Resolution mentions Service Advisor.
- Confirm Resolution mentions Service Request Ticket.

## Details
The Service Advisor notifies the customer of completion. Inputs are the fulfilled request; outputs are a confirmed resolution. Controls include resolution confirmation and an SLA-met check.
