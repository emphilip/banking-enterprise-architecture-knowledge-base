# Settle Trade

**Definition.** Settle Trade settles the trade on the T+1 cycle, exchanging cash and securities and reconciling against custody.
**Also known as:** Trade Settlement Step, Securities Settlement Step.

## Relationships
- Settle Trade is defined in the Trade Order Management process.
- Settle Trade depends on the Brokerage & Trading capability.
- Settle Trade mentions Settlement Operations Officer.

## Details
The Settlement Operations Officer settles the trade from the account allocations on the T+1 cycle. Controls include T+1 settlement, trade-vs-custody reconciliation, and fails management.
