# Allocate Fills

**Definition.** Allocate Fills allocates block fills fairly across client accounts and sleeves.
**Also known as:** Trade Allocation Step, Block Allocation Step.

## Relationships
- Allocate Fills is defined in the Trade Order Management process.
- Allocate Fills causes Settle Trade.
- Allocate Fills depends on the Brokerage & Trading capability.
- Allocate Fills mentions Settlement Operations Officer.
- Allocate Fills mentions Trade Confirmation.

## Details
The Settlement Operations Officer allocates block fills across client accounts from the execution fills and Trade Confirmation. Controls include allocation fairness and average-price allocation.
