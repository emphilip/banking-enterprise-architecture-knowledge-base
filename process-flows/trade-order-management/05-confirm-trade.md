# Confirm Trade

**Definition.** Confirm Trade confirms executed fills with the counterparty or broker and issues the Trade Confirmation.
**Also known as:** Trade Confirmation Step, Affirm Trade Step.

## Relationships
- Confirm Trade is defined in the Trade Order Management process.
- Confirm Trade causes Allocate Fills.
- Confirm Trade depends on the Brokerage & Trading capability.
- Confirm Trade mentions Trader.
- Confirm Trade mentions Trade Confirmation.

## Details
The Trader confirms executed fills with the counterparty and issues the Trade Confirmation. Controls include trade affirmation and confirmation matching.
