# Execute Advisory Mandate

**Definition.** Execute Advisory Mandate executes the advisory or discretionary mandate authorising portfolio management.
**Also known as:** Sign Mandate, Mandate Execution Step.

## Relationships
- Execute Advisory Mandate is defined in the Wealth Onboarding process.
- Execute Advisory Mandate causes Fund Investment Account.
- Execute Advisory Mandate depends on the Portfolio Management capability.
- Execute Advisory Mandate mentions Wealth Relationship Manager.

## Details
The Wealth Relationship Manager executes the advisory mandate from the account agreement. Controls include mandate execution and disclosure delivery.
