# Assess Investor Suitability

**Definition.** Assess Investor Suitability assesses suitability and best interest of the proposed service against the Risk Profile, branching on suitable or not suitable.
**Also known as:** Suitability Check Step, Best Interest Step.

## Relationships
- Assess Investor Suitability is defined in the Wealth Onboarding process.
- Assess Investor Suitability causes Open Investment Account.
- Assess Investor Suitability depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Assess Investor Suitability mentions Wealth Relationship Manager.
- Assess Investor Suitability mentions Risk Profile.

## Details
The Wealth Relationship Manager assesses suitability against the Risk Profile and product universe, branching suitable or unsuitable. Controls include MiFID II/Reg BI suitability and conflict checks; an unsuitable outcome halts onboarding pending revision.
