# Fund Investment Account

**Definition.** Fund Investment Account funds the account and confirms readiness to invest.
**Also known as:** Initial Investment Funding, Account Funding Step.

## Relationships
- Fund Investment Account is defined in the Wealth Onboarding process.
- Fund Investment Account depends on the Portfolio Management capability.
- Fund Investment Account mentions Wealth Relationship Manager.

## Details
The Wealth Relationship Manager funds the account from the funding instruction and the signed mandate, confirming readiness to invest. Controls include funding verification and a source-of-funds check.
