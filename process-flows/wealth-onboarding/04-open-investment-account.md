# Open Investment Account

**Definition.** Open Investment Account opens the investment and custody account once suitability passes.
**Also known as:** Establish Investment Account, Account Opening Step.

## Relationships
- Open Investment Account is defined in the Wealth Onboarding process.
- Open Investment Account causes Execute Advisory Mandate.
- Open Investment Account depends on the Portfolio Management capability.
- Open Investment Account mentions Wealth Relationship Manager.

## Details
The Wealth Relationship Manager opens the investment and custody account from the suitability outcome. Controls include custody account controls and account-opening KYC.
