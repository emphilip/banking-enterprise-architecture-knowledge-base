# Capture Risk Profile

**Definition.** Capture Risk Profile assesses risk tolerance and capacity for loss and records the Risk Profile.
**Also known as:** Profile Risk, Risk Profiling Step.

## Relationships
- Capture Risk Profile is defined in the Wealth Onboarding process.
- Capture Risk Profile causes Assess Investor Suitability.
- Capture Risk Profile depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Capture Risk Profile mentions Wealth Relationship Manager.
- Capture Risk Profile mentions Risk Profile.

## Details
The Wealth Relationship Manager records the Risk Profile from a risk questionnaire. Controls include the risk-profiling control and MiFID II information-gathering obligations.
