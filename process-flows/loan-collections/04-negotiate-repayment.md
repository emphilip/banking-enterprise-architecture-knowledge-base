# Negotiate Repayment

**Definition.** Negotiate Repayment agrees a promise-to-pay or repayment plan, or branches to escalation if unresolved.
**Also known as:** Arrange Repayment, Promise-To-Pay Step.

## Relationships
- Negotiate Repayment is defined in the Loan Collections process.
- Negotiate Repayment causes Escalate To Recovery.
- Negotiate Repayment depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Negotiate Repayment mentions Collections Agent.

## Details
The Collections Agent negotiates terms. Inputs are the borrower contact record; outputs are a promise to pay. Controls include promise-to-pay logging and affordability of the plan. The step branches on agreement versus default to escalation.
