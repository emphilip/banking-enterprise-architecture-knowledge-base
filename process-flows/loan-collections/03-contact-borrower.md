# Contact Borrower

**Definition.** Contact Borrower reaches the borrower via preferred channels with dunning notices and reminders.
**Also known as:** Dunning Outreach, Borrower Contact Step.

## Relationships
- Contact Borrower is defined in the Loan Collections process.
- Contact Borrower causes Negotiate Repayment.
- Contact Borrower depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Contact Borrower mentions Collections Agent.

## Details
The Collections Agent reaches out. Inputs are the collections strategy; outputs are a borrower contact record. Controls include FDCPA-aligned contact rules, call-time limits and contact logging.
