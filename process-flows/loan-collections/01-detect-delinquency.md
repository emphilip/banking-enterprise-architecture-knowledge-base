# Detect Delinquency

**Definition.** Detect Delinquency identifies missed payments and risk signals and buckets accounts by ageing.
**Also known as:** Identify Arrears, Delinquency Detection Step.

## Relationships
- Detect Delinquency is defined in the Loan Collections process.
- Detect Delinquency causes Segment Arrears.
- Detect Delinquency depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Detect Delinquency mentions Collections Agent.

## Details
The Collections Agent reviews flagged accounts. Inputs are a delinquent account; outputs are a delinquency case. Controls include ageing buckets (1-30/30-60/60+) and vulnerable-customer flagging.
