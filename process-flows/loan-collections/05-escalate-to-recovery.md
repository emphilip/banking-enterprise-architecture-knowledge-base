# Escalate To Recovery

**Definition.** Escalate To Recovery escalates persistent arrears to restructure, charge-off or settlement decisioning.
**Also known as:** Escalate Arrears, Recovery Escalation Step.

## Relationships
- Escalate To Recovery is defined in the Loan Collections process.
- Escalate To Recovery causes Charge Off Debt.
- Escalate To Recovery depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Escalate To Recovery mentions Recovery Officer.

## Details
The Recovery Officer takes the escalation. Inputs are the promise to pay that has lapsed; outputs are a recovery case. Controls include charge-off policy, settlement authority and escalation governance.
