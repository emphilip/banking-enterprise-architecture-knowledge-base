# Segment Arrears

**Definition.** Segment Arrears prioritises and routes accounts to treatment strategies by risk and balance.
**Also known as:** Prioritise Accounts, Arrears Segmentation Step.

## Relationships
- Segment Arrears is defined in the Loan Collections process.
- Segment Arrears causes Contact Borrower.
- Segment Arrears depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Segment Arrears mentions Collections Agent.

## Details
The Collections Agent applies the segmentation. Inputs are the delinquency case; outputs are a collections strategy. Controls include strategy governance, fair-treatment policy and the segmentation model.
