# Issue Letter Of Credit

**Definition.** Issue Letter Of Credit drafts and issues the credit and transmits it to the advising bank for the beneficiary.
**Also known as:** Issue LC, Credit Issuance Step.

## Relationships
- Issue Letter Of Credit is defined in the Trade Finance Processing process.
- Issue Letter Of Credit causes Advise Beneficiary.
- Issue Letter Of Credit depends on the Integration Platform capability.
- Issue Letter Of Credit mentions Trade Finance Officer.
- Issue Letter Of Credit mentions Letter Of Credit.

## Details
The Trade Finance Officer drafts and issues. Inputs are the approved trade limit; outputs are a Letter Of Credit. Controls include UCP 600 / ISP98 drafting, four-eyes on issuance and SWIFT controls.
