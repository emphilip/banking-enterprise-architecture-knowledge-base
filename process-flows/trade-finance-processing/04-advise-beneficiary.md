# Advise Beneficiary

**Definition.** Advise Beneficiary advises and optionally confirms the credit to the beneficiary through the advising bank.
**Also known as:** Advise LC, Beneficiary Advising Step.

## Relationships
- Advise Beneficiary is defined in the Trade Finance Processing process.
- Advise Beneficiary causes Examine Trade Documents.
- Advise Beneficiary depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Advise Beneficiary mentions Trade Finance Officer.
- Advise Beneficiary mentions Letter Of Credit.

## Details
The Trade Finance Officer advises the credit. Inputs are the Letter Of Credit; outputs are an advised credit. Controls include the authenticity check, the confirmation decision and amendment handling.
