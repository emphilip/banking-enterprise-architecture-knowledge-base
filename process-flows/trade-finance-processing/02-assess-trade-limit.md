# Assess Trade Limit

**Definition.** Assess Trade Limit checks the applicant's credit limit and collateral and screens counterparties for sanctions.
**Also known as:** Trade Limit Check, Credit Margining Step.

## Relationships
- Assess Trade Limit is defined in the Trade Finance Processing process.
- Assess Trade Limit causes Issue Letter Of Credit.
- Assess Trade Limit depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.
- Assess Trade Limit mentions Trade Finance Officer.

## Details
The Trade Finance Officer checks limits. Inputs are the captured LC request; outputs are an approved trade limit. Controls include the credit-limit check, sanctions/AML screening and collateral margining.
