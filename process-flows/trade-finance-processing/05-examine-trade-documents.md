# Examine Trade Documents

**Definition.** Examine Trade Documents examines presented documents on their face against credit terms within five banking days.
**Also known as:** Check Documents, Document Examination Step.

## Relationships
- Examine Trade Documents is defined in the Trade Finance Processing process.
- Examine Trade Documents causes Settle Trade Payment.
- Examine Trade Documents depends on the Document Management capability.
- Examine Trade Documents mentions Trade Finance Officer.

## Details
The Trade Finance Officer examines the documents. Inputs are the presented trade documents; outputs are an examination result. Controls include the UCP 600 Article 14 standard and the 5-banking-day limit. The step branches on a discrepancy versus a compliant presentation.
