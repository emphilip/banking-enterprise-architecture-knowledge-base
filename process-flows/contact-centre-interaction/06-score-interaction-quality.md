# Score Interaction Quality

**Definition.** Score Interaction Quality is the QA review of a sampled interaction against scorecards with coaching feedback.
**Also known as:** QA Scoring, Quality Review.

## Relationships
- Score Interaction Quality is defined in the Contact Centre Interaction process.
- Score Interaction Quality depends on the Contact Center Platform capability.
- Score Interaction Quality mentions Contact Centre Agent.
- Score Interaction Quality mentions Interaction Record.

## Details
The Contact Centre Agent's work is reviewed in QA. Inputs are the Interaction Record and the recording; outputs are a QA score. Controls include QA scorecards, recording governance and calibration.
