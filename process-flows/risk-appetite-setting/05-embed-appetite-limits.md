# Embed Appetite Limits

**Definition.** Embed Appetite Limits embeds approved limits into business plans and monitoring and emits the Risk Appetite Approved Event.
**Also known as:** Operationalize Appetite, Appetite Embedding Step.

## Relationships
- Embed Appetite Limits is defined in the Risk Appetite Setting process.
- Embed Appetite Limits depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Embed Appetite Limits mentions Chief Risk Officer.
- Embed Appetite Limits mentions Risk Appetite Approved Event.

## Details
The Chief Risk Officer embeds the approved Risk Appetite Statement limits into plans and emits the Risk Appetite Approved Event. Controls include embedding into business plans, limit-system configuration and a monitoring linkage. This is the final step of Risk Appetite Setting.
