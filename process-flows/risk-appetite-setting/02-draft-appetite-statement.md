# Draft Appetite Statement

**Definition.** Draft Appetite Statement drafts qualitative appetite statements by risk type aligned to strategy and capacity.
**Also known as:** Author RAS, Appetite Drafting Step.

## Relationships
- Draft Appetite Statement is defined in the Risk Appetite Setting process.
- Draft Appetite Statement causes Cascade Risk Limits.
- Draft Appetite Statement depends on the Enterprise Risk Management capability.
- Draft Appetite Statement mentions Chief Risk Officer.
- Draft Appetite Statement mentions Risk Appetite Statement.

## Details
The Chief Risk Officer authors the draft Risk Appetite Statement from the risk capacity estimate and prior statement. Controls include FSB RAF principles and coverage of hard-to-quantify risks such as conduct and reputation.
