# Approve Risk Appetite

**Definition.** Approve Risk Appetite presents the RAS and limits to the board or risk committee for formal approval.
**Also known as:** Set Risk Appetite, Appetite Approval Step.

## Relationships
- Approve Risk Appetite is defined in the Risk Appetite Setting process.
- Approve Risk Appetite causes Embed Appetite Limits.
- Approve Risk Appetite depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Approve Risk Appetite mentions Chief Risk Officer.
- Approve Risk Appetite mentions Risk Appetite Statement.

## Details
The Chief Risk Officer presents the draft Risk Appetite Statement and risk limits to the board for an approved Risk Appetite Statement. Controls include board or committee approval, three-lines ownership and a minuted decision.
