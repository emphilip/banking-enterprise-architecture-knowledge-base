# Cascade Risk Limits

**Definition.** Cascade Risk Limits translates qualitative appetite into quantitative limits and tolerances per risk type and business line.
**Also known as:** Set Risk Limits, Limit Cascade Step.

## Relationships
- Cascade Risk Limits is defined in the Risk Appetite Setting process.
- Cascade Risk Limits causes Approve Risk Appetite.
- Cascade Risk Limits depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Cascade Risk Limits mentions Chief Risk Officer.
- Cascade Risk Limits mentions Risk Appetite Statement.

## Details
The Chief Risk Officer derives risk limits and tolerances from the draft Risk Appetite Statement. Controls include the limit cascade, metric calibration and traceability of every limit back to the RAS.
