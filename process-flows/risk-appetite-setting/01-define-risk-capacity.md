# Define Risk Capacity

**Definition.** Define Risk Capacity determines the maximum risk the bank can bear (capital, liquidity, earnings) given regulatory constraints.
**Also known as:** Assess Risk Capacity, Capacity Definition Step.

## Relationships
- Define Risk Capacity is defined in the Risk Appetite Setting process.
- Define Risk Capacity causes Draft Appetite Statement.
- Define Risk Capacity depends on the Enterprise Risk Management capability.
- Define Risk Capacity mentions Chief Risk Officer.

## Details
The Chief Risk Officer estimates risk capacity from the strategy and business plan and the capital and liquidity position. Controls include the capacity-versus-appetite distinction, regulatory minima and CRO/CFO input, consistent with FSB Risk Appetite Framework principles.
