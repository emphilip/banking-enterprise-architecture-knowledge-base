# Capture Client Goals

**Definition.** Capture Client Goals gathers client goals, financial situation and knowledge and experience in a fact find.
**Also known as:** Client Fact Find Step, Goal Capture Step.

## Relationships
- Capture Client Goals is defined in the Financial Planning process.
- Capture Client Goals causes Determine Risk Tolerance.
- Capture Client Goals depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Capture Client Goals mentions Financial Advisor.
- Capture Client Goals mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The Financial Advisor completes the fact find from client data and the KYC Profile. Controls include KYC freshness, MiFID II information-gathering, and Reg BI care.
