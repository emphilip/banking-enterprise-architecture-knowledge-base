# Agree Investment Plan

**Definition.** Agree Investment Plan presents and obtains client agreement to the Investment Policy Statement and delivers the suitability report and disclosures.
**Also known as:** Agree Plan Step, IPS Sign-Off Step.

## Relationships
- Agree Investment Plan is defined in the Financial Planning process.
- Agree Investment Plan depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Agree Investment Plan mentions Financial Advisor.
- Agree Investment Plan mentions Investment Plan Agreed Event.

## Details
The Financial Advisor obtains client agreement to the draft Investment Policy Statement and delivers disclosures, raising the Investment Plan Agreed Event. Controls include disclosure delivery, client acknowledgement, and record-keeping.
