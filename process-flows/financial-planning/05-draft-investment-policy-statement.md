# Draft Investment Policy Statement

**Definition.** Draft Investment Policy Statement drafts the Investment Policy Statement codifying objectives, allocation, constraints and rebalancing rules.
**Also known as:** Draft IPS Step, IPS Drafting Step.

## Relationships
- Draft Investment Policy Statement is defined in the Financial Planning process.
- Draft Investment Policy Statement causes Agree Investment Plan.
- Draft Investment Policy Statement depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Draft Investment Policy Statement mentions Financial Advisor.
- Draft Investment Policy Statement mentions Investment Policy Statement.

## Details
The Financial Advisor drafts the Investment Policy Statement from the suitability report and target allocation. Controls include IPS governance and disclosure content.
