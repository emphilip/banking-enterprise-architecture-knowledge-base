# Determine Risk Tolerance

**Definition.** Determine Risk Tolerance determines risk tolerance and capacity for loss and produces the Risk Profile.
**Also known as:** Risk Tolerance Step, Determine Risk Appetite.

## Relationships
- Determine Risk Tolerance is defined in the Financial Planning process.
- Determine Risk Tolerance causes Formulate Investment Plan.
- Determine Risk Tolerance depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Determine Risk Tolerance mentions Financial Advisor.
- Determine Risk Tolerance mentions Risk Profile.

## Details
The Financial Advisor produces the Risk Profile from the fact find record. Controls include the risk-profiling control.
