# Formulate Investment Plan

**Definition.** Formulate Investment Plan sets the target asset allocation and identifies suitable products for the plan.
**Also known as:** Construct Plan Step, Plan Formulation Step.

## Relationships
- Formulate Investment Plan is defined in the Financial Planning process.
- Formulate Investment Plan causes Confirm Suitability.
- Formulate Investment Plan depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Formulate Investment Plan mentions Financial Advisor.
- Formulate Investment Plan mentions Risk Profile.

## Details
The Financial Advisor sets the target allocation and selects products against the Risk Profile and product universe. Controls include asset-allocation policy and product governance.
