# Confirm Suitability

**Definition.** Confirm Suitability runs the suitability and best-interest check on the recommendation, branching suitable or revise.
**Also known as:** Suitability Confirmation Step, Best Interest Check Step.

## Relationships
- Confirm Suitability is defined in the Financial Planning process.
- Confirm Suitability causes Draft Investment Policy Statement.
- Confirm Suitability depends on the Investment Advisory capability.
- Confirm Suitability mentions Financial Advisor.
- Confirm Suitability mentions Risk Profile.

## Details
The Financial Advisor runs the suitability and best-interest check on the draft plan against the Risk Profile, branching suitable or revise. Controls include MiFID II/Reg BI suitability and conflict-of-interest checks; a revise outcome loops back to plan formulation.
