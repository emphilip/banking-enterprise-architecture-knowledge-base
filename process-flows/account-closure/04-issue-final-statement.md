# Issue Final Statement

**Definition.** Issue Final Statement produces the final statement evidencing zero balance and closure.
**Also known as:** Produce Closing Statement, Final Statement Step.

## Relationships
- Issue Final Statement is defined in the Account Closure process.
- Issue Final Statement causes Deactivate Account.
- Issue Final Statement depends on the Document Management capability.
- Issue Final Statement mentions Statement Production Officer.
- Issue Final Statement mentions Bank Account Statement.

## Details
The Statement Production Officer produces a final Bank Account Statement evidencing the zero balance. Controls include final statement accuracy.
