# Issue Dormancy Notice

**Definition.** Issue Dormancy Notice identifies dormant accounts and sends the Dormancy Notice before escheatment.
**Also known as:** Send Dormancy Notice, Dormancy Notification Step.

## Relationships
- Issue Dormancy Notice is defined in the Account Closure process.
- Issue Dormancy Notice causes Escheat Dormant Account.
- Issue Dormancy Notice depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Issue Dormancy Notice mentions Unclaimed Property Officer.
- Issue Dormancy Notice mentions Dormancy Notice.
- Issue Dormancy Notice mentions Account Dormant Event.

## Details
The Unclaimed Property Officer identifies dormant accounts from the dormancy report and sends the Dormancy Notice. Controls include dormancy rules and documented contact attempts; the Account Dormant Event triggers this branch.
