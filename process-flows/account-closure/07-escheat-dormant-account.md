# Escheat Dormant Account

**Definition.** Escheat Dormant Account reports and remits unclaimed funds to the state per escheatment rules.
**Also known as:** Remit Unclaimed Property, Escheatment Step.

## Relationships
- Escheat Dormant Account is defined in the Account Closure process.
- Escheat Dormant Account depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.
- Escheat Dormant Account mentions Unclaimed Property Officer.
- Escheat Dormant Account mentions Dormancy Notice.

## Details
The Unclaimed Property Officer reports and remits the unclaimed balance to the state after the Dormancy Notice. Controls include escheatment and CASS rules and state filing deadlines.
