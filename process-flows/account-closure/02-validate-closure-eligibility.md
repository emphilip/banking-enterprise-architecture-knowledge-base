# Validate Closure Eligibility

**Definition.** Validate Closure Eligibility verifies signatories and checks holds, pending items and linked services.
**Also known as:** Check Closure Eligibility, Eligibility Validation Step.

## Relationships
- Validate Closure Eligibility is defined in the Account Closure process.
- Validate Closure Eligibility causes Settle Residual Balance.
- Validate Closure Eligibility depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Validate Closure Eligibility mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.
- Validate Closure Eligibility mentions Account Closure Request.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk verifies signatories and checks holds and pending items to produce an eligible closure. Controls include signatory verification and hold checks.
