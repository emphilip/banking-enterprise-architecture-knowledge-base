# Settle Residual Balance

**Definition.** Settle Residual Balance disburses remaining funds to zero by transfer, draft or cash.
**Also known as:** Disburse Residual Funds, Balance Settlement Step.

## Relationships
- Settle Residual Balance is defined in the Account Closure process.
- Settle Residual Balance causes Issue Final Statement.
- Settle Residual Balance depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Settle Residual Balance mentions Deposit Operations Clerk.

## Details
The Deposit Operations Clerk disburses the residual balance from an eligible closure to reach a zero balance. Controls include zero-balance confirmation.
