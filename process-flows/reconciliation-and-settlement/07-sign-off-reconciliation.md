# Sign Off Reconciliation

**Definition.** Sign Off Reconciliation reviews residual items, certifies the reconciliation and produces the recon report for control/audit.
**Also known as:** Recon Certification, Reconciliation Sign-Off.

## Relationships
- Sign Off Reconciliation is defined in the Reconciliation & Settlement process.
- Sign Off Reconciliation depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Sign Off Reconciliation mentions Settlement Officer.
- Sign Off Reconciliation mentions Reconciliation Report.

## Details
The Settlement Officer certifies the reconciliation. Inputs are ledger adjustments; outputs are the Reconciliation Report. As the final step of the flow it has no successor step. Controls include certification sign-off and a full audit trail.
