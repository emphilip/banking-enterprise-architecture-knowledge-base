# Post Adjustment

**Definition.** Post Adjustment posts correcting/clearing ledger entries for resolved breaks under maker-checker control.
**Also known as:** Adjustment Posting, Correcting Entry.

## Relationships
- Post Adjustment is defined in the Reconciliation & Settlement process.
- Post Adjustment causes Sign Off Reconciliation.
- Post Adjustment depends on the General Ledger Engine capability.
- Post Adjustment mentions Settlement Officer.

## Details
The Settlement Officer posts correcting entries for resolved breaks. Inputs are resolved breaks; outputs are ledger adjustments. Controls include maker-checker and journal authorization.
