# Fund Mortgage Loan

**Definition.** Fund Mortgage Loan signs closing documents, disburses funds and records the mortgage.
**Also known as:** Close And Fund, Mortgage Funding Step.

## Relationships
- Fund Mortgage Loan is defined in the Mortgage Origination process.
- Fund Mortgage Loan depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Fund Mortgage Loan mentions Mortgage Closer.
- Fund Mortgage Loan mentions Loan Agreement.

## Details
The Mortgage Closer signs and funds. Inputs are the Closing Disclosure and executed Loan Agreement; outputs are a funded mortgage. Controls include signing controls, four-eyes on funding and recording of the security. This terminal step emits the Loan Funded Event.
