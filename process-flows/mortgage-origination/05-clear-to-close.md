# Clear To Close

**Definition.** Clear To Close confirms all underwriting conditions are satisfied and issues final clear-to-close approval.
**Also known as:** CTC Step, Final Approval.

## Relationships
- Clear To Close is defined in the Mortgage Origination process.
- Clear To Close causes Deliver Closing Disclosure.
- Clear To Close depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.
- Clear To Close mentions Credit Decision.

## Details
The mortgage underwriter confirms conditions. Inputs are the title report; outputs are a clear-to-close decision recorded against the Credit Decision. Controls include conditions sign-off, the clear-to-close gate and a final-approval audit.
