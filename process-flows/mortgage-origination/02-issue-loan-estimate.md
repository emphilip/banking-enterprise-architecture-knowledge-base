# Issue Loan Estimate

**Definition.** Issue Loan Estimate delivers the TRID Loan Estimate disclosing terms and fees within three business days.
**Also known as:** Deliver LE, TRID Disclosure Step.

## Relationships
- Issue Loan Estimate is defined in the Mortgage Origination process.
- Issue Loan Estimate causes Order Property Appraisal.
- Issue Loan Estimate depends on the Document Management capability.
- Issue Loan Estimate mentions Mortgage Loan Originator.

## Details
The Mortgage Loan Originator delivers the disclosure. Inputs are the captured mortgage file; outputs are a Loan Estimate. Controls include the TRID 3-business-day rule, fee tolerances and disclosure-delivery proof.
