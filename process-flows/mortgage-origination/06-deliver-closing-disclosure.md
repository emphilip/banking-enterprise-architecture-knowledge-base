# Deliver Closing Disclosure

**Definition.** Deliver Closing Disclosure provides the Closing Disclosure at least three business days before consummation.
**Also known as:** Deliver CD, Closing Disclosure Step.

## Relationships
- Deliver Closing Disclosure is defined in the Mortgage Origination process.
- Deliver Closing Disclosure causes Fund Mortgage Loan.
- Deliver Closing Disclosure depends on the Document Management capability.
- Deliver Closing Disclosure mentions Mortgage Closer.

## Details
The Mortgage Closer delivers the disclosure. Inputs are the clear-to-close decision; outputs are a Closing Disclosure. Controls include the TRID 3-business-day waiting period and tolerance reconciliation.
