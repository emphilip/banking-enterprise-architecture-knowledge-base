# Review Title And Liens

**Definition.** Review Title And Liens runs title and lien searches and flood determination to clear the collateral.
**Also known as:** Title Examination Step, Lien Search.

## Relationships
- Review Title And Liens is defined in the Mortgage Origination process.
- Review Title And Liens causes Clear To Close.
- Review Title And Liens depends on the Document Management capability.
- Review Title And Liens mentions Loan Processor.

## Details
The Loan Processor runs the searches. Inputs are the Collateral Appraisal; outputs are a title report. Controls include title/lien search, flood determination and encumbrance review.
