# Escalate To Arbitration

**Definition.** Escalate To Arbitration escalates to pre-arbitration/arbitration where the dispute remains contested.

## Relationships
- Escalate To Arbitration is defined in the Chargeback Processing process.
- Escalate To Arbitration causes Settle Chargeback.
- Escalate To Arbitration depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Escalate To Arbitration mentions Chargeback Case.
- Escalate To Arbitration mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Escalate To Arbitration files pre-arbitration/arbitration within the 10-day escalation window where the dispute remains contested, awaiting the scheme ruling.
