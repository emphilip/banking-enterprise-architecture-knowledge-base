# Review Representment Evidence

**Definition.** Review Representment Evidence reviews representment evidence against the reason code and cardholder claim.

## Relationships
- Review Representment Evidence is defined in the Chargeback Processing process.
- Review Representment Evidence causes Escalate To Arbitration.
- Review Representment Evidence depends on the Document Management capability.
- Review Representment Evidence mentions Chargeback Case.
- Review Representment Evidence mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Review Representment Evidence assesses the merchant evidence for sufficiency and reason-code match against the cardholder claim to decide whether escalation is warranted.
