# Receive Representment

**Definition.** Receive Representment receives and logs the merchant/acquirer representment and its evidence.

## Relationships
- Receive Representment is defined in the Chargeback Processing process.
- Receive Representment causes Review Representment Evidence.
- Receive Representment depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Receive Representment mentions Chargeback Case.
- Receive Representment mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Receive Representment logs the merchant/acquirer second presentment and its evidence against the Chargeback Case within the 30-day phase response window.
