# Settle Chargeback

**Definition.** Settle Chargeback applies the final credit/debit to the cardholder account and closes the case.

## Relationships
- Settle Chargeback is defined in the Chargeback Processing process.
- Settle Chargeback depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Settle Chargeback mentions Chargeback Case.
- Settle Chargeback mentions Dispute Resolution Specialist.

## Details
Settle Chargeback applies the final credit/debit to the cardholder account, recovers or reverses funds with final-settlement accuracy, and closes the Chargeback Case. This is the final step of Chargeback Processing.
