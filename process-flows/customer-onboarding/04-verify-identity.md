# Verify Identity

**Definition.** Verify Identity proves applicant identity (CIP) via document and biometric/liveness checks, branching on pass, fail or manual review.
**Also known as:** Prove Identity, Identity Proofing Step.

## Relationships
- Verify Identity is defined in the Customer Onboarding process.
- Verify Identity causes Screen Customer.
- Verify Identity depends on the Identity Verification capability.
- Verify Identity mentions KYC Analyst.
- Verify Identity mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The KYC Analyst proves applicant identity. Inputs are the document package; outputs are a verified identity recorded in the KYC Profile. The identity proofing and liveness/biometric control branches across pass, fail and manual review.
