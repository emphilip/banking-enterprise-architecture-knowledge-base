# Capture Consent

**Definition.** Capture Consent presents terms, privacy and data-use notices and marketing opt-ins, then records explicit consent and e-signature.
**Also known as:** Record Consent, Consent Capture Step.

## Relationships
- Capture Consent is defined in the Customer Onboarding process.
- Capture Consent causes Collect Documents.
- Capture Consent depends on the Customer Identity capability.
- Capture Consent mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Capture Consent mentions Consent Record.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist captures consent. Inputs are the Onboarding Case; outputs are a Consent Record. Controls include the consent capture control, e-signature and an audit trail.
