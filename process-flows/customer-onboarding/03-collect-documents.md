# Collect Documents

**Definition.** Collect Documents collects and classifies identity, address and tax documents via upload or capture and extracts key data.
**Also known as:** Gather Documents, Document Collection Step.

## Relationships
- Collect Documents is defined in the Customer Onboarding process.
- Collect Documents causes Verify Identity.
- Collect Documents depends on the Intelligent Document Processing capability.
- Collect Documents mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Collect Documents mentions Onboarding Case.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist collects documents. Inputs are the Onboarding Case; outputs are a document package for verification. Controls include document authenticity checks and data-extraction validation.
