# Create Customer Record

**Definition.** Create Customer Record creates the golden customer/party record and KYC Profile in master data and provisions the product.
**Also known as:** Establish Party Record, Customer Record Creation.

## Relationships
- Create Customer Record is defined in the Customer Onboarding process.
- Create Customer Record causes Activate Customer.
- Create Customer Record depends on the Master Data Management capability.
- Create Customer Record mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Create Customer Record mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist creates the golden party record and KYC Profile. Inputs are the approved onboarding; outputs are an active customer profile. Controls include a unique-party/dedupe control and data-quality validation.
