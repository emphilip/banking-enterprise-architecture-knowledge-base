# Approve Onboarding

**Definition.** Approve Onboarding makes the accept, decline or refer decision against policy and applies four-eyes approval for elevated risk.
**Also known as:** Decide Onboarding, Onboarding Approval Step.

## Relationships
- Approve Onboarding is defined in the Customer Onboarding process.
- Approve Onboarding causes Create Customer Record.
- Approve Onboarding depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Approve Onboarding mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Approve Onboarding mentions Onboarding Case.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist makes the decision against policy on the Onboarding Case. Inputs are the risk-rated case; outputs are an approved onboarding. The approval gate and four-eyes control for high-risk branches across accept, decline and refer.
