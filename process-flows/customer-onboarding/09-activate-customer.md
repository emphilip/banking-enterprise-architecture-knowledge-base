# Activate Customer

**Definition.** Activate Customer activates channel access and the product, delivers welcome communications and enrols the customer in ongoing monitoring.
**Also known as:** Welcome Customer, Customer Activation Step.

## Relationships
- Activate Customer is defined in the Customer Onboarding process.
- Activate Customer depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Activate Customer mentions Onboarding Specialist.
- Activate Customer mentions Customer Activated Event.

## Details
The Onboarding Specialist activates access and the product. Inputs are the active customer profile; outputs are an onboarded customer, signalled by the Customer Activated Event. Controls include activation logging and ongoing-monitoring enrolment. This is the final step of the Customer Onboarding flow.
