# Assess Customer Risk

**Definition.** Assess Customer Risk performs CDD and assigns a risk rating, triggering EDD for high-risk customers such as PEPs or high-risk geographies.
**Also known as:** Risk Rate Customer, CDD Assessment Step.

## Relationships
- Assess Customer Risk is defined in the Customer Onboarding process.
- Assess Customer Risk causes Approve Onboarding.
- Assess Customer Risk depends on the Customer Due Diligence capability.
- Assess Customer Risk mentions KYC Analyst.
- Assess Customer Risk mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The KYC Analyst performs CDD and assigns a risk rating in the KYC Profile. Inputs are the screened applicant; outputs are a risk-rated case. The CDD/EDD and source-of-funds control branches across standard versus EDD.
