# Screen CDD Parties

**Definition.** Screen CDD Parties screens the customer and beneficial owners against sanctions, PEP and adverse-media lists.
**Also known as:** Diligence Screening, CDD Screening Step.

## Relationships
- Screen CDD Parties is defined in the Customer Due Diligence Onboarding process.
- Screen CDD Parties causes Rate Customer Risk.
- Screen CDD Parties depends on the Sanctions Screening capability.
- Screen CDD Parties mentions AML Compliance Officer.
- Screen CDD Parties mentions Sanctions Hit.

## Details
The AML Compliance Officer screens the CIP record and beneficial-ownership record against current lists to produce screening results and potential matches. Controls include sanctions/PEP/adverse-media screening, list currency and match handling, raising a Sanctions Hit where applicable.
