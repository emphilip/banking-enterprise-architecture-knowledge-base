# Trigger Enhanced Diligence

**Definition.** Trigger Enhanced Diligence is the decision point that, where high-risk or PEP, performs Enhanced Due Diligence including source of wealth and funds.
**Also known as:** Perform EDD, Enhanced Diligence Step.

## Relationships
- Trigger Enhanced Diligence is defined in the Customer Due Diligence Onboarding process.
- Trigger Enhanced Diligence causes Approve CDD Outcome.
- Trigger Enhanced Diligence depends on the Customer Due Diligence capability.
- Trigger Enhanced Diligence mentions AML Compliance Officer.
- Trigger Enhanced Diligence mentions Customer Risk Score.

## Details
The AML Compliance Officer uses the Customer Risk Score and potential matches to produce EDD findings. Controls include a risk-based EDD trigger, PEP escalation, source-of-wealth and funds verification, and a branch on standard versus EDD.
