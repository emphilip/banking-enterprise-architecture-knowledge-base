# Establish CDD Profile

**Definition.** Establish CDD Profile persists the approved diligence outcome into the KYC Profile and sets monitoring, and emits the Customer Risk Rated Event.
**Also known as:** Create CDD Profile, Profile Establishment Step.

## Relationships
- Establish CDD Profile is defined in the Customer Due Diligence Onboarding process.
- Establish CDD Profile depends on the KYC Management capability.
- Establish CDD Profile mentions AML Compliance Officer.
- Establish CDD Profile mentions Customer Risk Rated Event.

## Details
The AML Compliance Officer persists the CDD decision and Customer Risk Score into the KYC Profile and sets a monitoring plan. Controls include profile persistence, ongoing-monitoring linkage and review cadence, with completion signalled by the Customer Risk Rated Event. This is the final step of Customer Due Diligence Onboarding.
