# Identify Beneficial Owner

**Definition.** Identify Beneficial Owner, for legal entities, identifies and verifies ultimate beneficial owners and control persons.
**Also known as:** Capture UBO, Beneficial Ownership Step.

## Relationships
- Identify Beneficial Owner is defined in the Customer Due Diligence Onboarding process.
- Identify Beneficial Owner causes Screen CDD Parties.
- Identify Beneficial Owner depends on the Customer Due Diligence capability.
- Identify Beneficial Owner mentions AML Compliance Officer.
- Identify Beneficial Owner mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The AML Compliance Officer analyses the entity data and ownership structure to produce a beneficial-ownership record. Controls include the 25% UBO threshold, control-prong identification and ownership unwrap.
