# Capture CIP Data

**Definition.** Capture CIP Data captures the Customer Identification Program data set (name, DOB, address, ID number) for the applicant.
**Also known as:** Collect CIP Data, CIP Capture Step.

## Relationships
- Capture CIP Data is defined in the Customer Due Diligence Onboarding process.
- Capture CIP Data causes Identify Beneficial Owner.
- Capture CIP Data depends on the Customer Due Diligence capability.
- Capture CIP Data mentions AML Compliance Officer.
- Capture CIP Data mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The AML Compliance Officer captures applicant data and identity documents into the CIP record. Controls include the CIP minimum-data requirement, data quality and consent under the BSA/AML program.
