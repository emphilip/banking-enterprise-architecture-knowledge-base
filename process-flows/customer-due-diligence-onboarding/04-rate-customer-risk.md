# Rate Customer Risk

**Definition.** Rate Customer Risk synthesises screening, geography, product and behaviour into a Customer Risk Score and tier.
**Also known as:** Score Customer Risk, Risk Rating Step.

## Relationships
- Rate Customer Risk is defined in the Customer Due Diligence Onboarding process.
- Rate Customer Risk causes Trigger Enhanced Diligence.
- Rate Customer Risk depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.
- Rate Customer Risk mentions AML Compliance Officer.
- Rate Customer Risk mentions Customer Risk Score.

## Details
The AML Compliance Officer combines screening results and customer attributes into the Customer Risk Score. Controls include a risk-based scoring model, jurisdiction and product risk and score calibration.
