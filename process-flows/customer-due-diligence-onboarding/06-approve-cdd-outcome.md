# Approve CDD Outcome

**Definition.** Approve CDD Outcome approves, rejects or conditions onboarding under four-eyes and records the CDD decision.
**Also known as:** Decide CDD Outcome, CDD Approval Step.

## Relationships
- Approve CDD Outcome is defined in the Customer Due Diligence Onboarding process.
- Approve CDD Outcome causes Establish CDD Profile.
- Approve CDD Outcome depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Approve CDD Outcome mentions AML Compliance Officer.
- Approve CDD Outcome mentions Customer Risk Score.

## Details
The AML Compliance Officer weighs the Customer Risk Score and EDD findings to produce the CDD decision. Controls include four-eyes approval, accept/reject criteria and escalation of unresolved risk.
