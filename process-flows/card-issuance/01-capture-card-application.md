# Capture Card Application

**Definition.** Capture Card Application captures the Card Account Application with applicant and product/limit selection.

## Relationships
- Capture Card Application is defined in the Card Issuance process.
- Capture Card Application causes Decision Card Application.
- Capture Card Application depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.
- Capture Card Application mentions Card Account Application.
- Capture Card Application mentions Card Issuance Officer.

## Details
Capture Card Application records the applicant identity, product choice and requested limit, enforcing data-completeness and product-eligibility checks before decisioning.
