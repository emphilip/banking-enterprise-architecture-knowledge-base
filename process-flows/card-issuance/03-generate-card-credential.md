# Generate Card Credential

**Definition.** Generate Card Credential generates the PAN, expiry and CVV credential and creates the card profile.

## Relationships
- Generate Card Credential is defined in the Card Issuance process.
- Generate Card Credential causes Personalize Card.
- Generate Card Credential depends on the Card Processing capability.
- Generate Card Credential mentions Card Credential.
- Generate Card Credential mentions Card Issuance Officer.

## Details
Generate Card Credential mints the PAN, expiry and CVV under PCI DSS PAN protection and secure key handling, establishing the card profile for personalization.
