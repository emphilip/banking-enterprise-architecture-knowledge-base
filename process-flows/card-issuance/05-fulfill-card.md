# Fulfill Card

**Definition.** Fulfill Card fulfills the card to the customer by secure mail or virtual delivery.

## Relationships
- Fulfill Card is defined in the Card Issuance process.
- Fulfill Card causes Activate Card.
- Fulfill Card depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Fulfill Card mentions Card Issuance Officer.

## Details
Fulfill Card dispatches the personalized card by secure physical mail or virtual delivery, applying secure-fulfillment and delivery-confirmation controls.
