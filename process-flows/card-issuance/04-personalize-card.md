# Personalize Card

**Definition.** Personalize Card personalizes the physical/virtual card with cardholder and EMV chip data.

## Relationships
- Personalize Card is defined in the Card Issuance process.
- Personalize Card causes Fulfill Card.
- Personalize Card depends on the Card Processing capability.
- Personalize Card mentions Card Credential.
- Personalize Card mentions Card Issuance Officer.

## Details
Personalize Card encodes the EMV chip and personalizes the card body with cardholder and credential data, enforcing data-integrity controls during EMV personalization.
