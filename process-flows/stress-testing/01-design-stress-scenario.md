# Design Stress Scenario

**Definition.** Design Stress Scenario designs coherent baseline, adverse and severely-adverse scenarios from supervisory and internal assumptions.
**Also known as:** Build Scenario, Scenario Design Step.

## Relationships
- Design Stress Scenario is defined in the Stress Testing process.
- Design Stress Scenario causes Validate Risk Models.
- Design Stress Scenario depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Design Stress Scenario mentions Stress Testing Lead.
- Design Stress Scenario mentions Stress Scenario.

## Details
The Stress Testing Lead builds the Stress Scenario from supervisory scenarios and macro assumptions. Controls include scenario coherence and internal consistency and governance of the assumptions used.
