# Obtain Board Approval

**Definition.** Obtain Board Approval presents results and the capital plan to the board for sign-off.
**Also known as:** Approve Capital Plan, Board Sign-Off Step.

## Relationships
- Obtain Board Approval is defined in the Stress Testing process.
- Obtain Board Approval causes Submit Capital Plan.
- Obtain Board Approval depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Obtain Board Approval mentions Stress Testing Lead.
- Obtain Board Approval mentions Stress Test Result.

## Details
The Stress Testing Lead presents the Stress Test Result and capital adequacy assessment to the board for a board-approved capital plan. Controls include board sign-off of the capital plan and governance documentation.
