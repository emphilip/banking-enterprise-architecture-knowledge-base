# Assess Capital Adequacy

**Definition.** Assess Capital Adequacy is the decision point that assesses projected capital against minima and buffers and identifies management actions.
**Also known as:** Evaluate Capital Adequacy, Adequacy Assessment Step.

## Relationships
- Assess Capital Adequacy is defined in the Stress Testing process.
- Assess Capital Adequacy causes Obtain Board Approval.
- Assess Capital Adequacy depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Assess Capital Adequacy mentions Stress Testing Lead.
- Assess Capital Adequacy mentions Stress Test Result.

## Details
The Stress Testing Lead turns capital projections into the Stress Test Result and a capital adequacy assessment. Controls include capital-minima and buffer thresholds, branching on pass or shortfall, and identification of management actions.
