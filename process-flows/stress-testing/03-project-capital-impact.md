# Project Capital Impact

**Definition.** Project Capital Impact runs validated models over the scenarios to project losses, revenue and capital ratios.
**Also known as:** Run Stress Models, Capital Projection Step.

## Relationships
- Project Capital Impact is defined in the Stress Testing process.
- Project Capital Impact causes Assess Capital Adequacy.
- Project Capital Impact depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Project Capital Impact mentions Stress Testing Lead.
- Project Capital Impact mentions Stress Scenario.

## Details
The Stress Testing Lead runs the validated models over the Stress Scenario and portfolio data into capital projections. Controls include projection accuracy, the nine-quarter horizon and reproducibility of results.
