# Validate Risk Models

**Definition.** Validate Risk Models validates the models used (conceptual soundness, ongoing monitoring and outcomes analysis) under SR 11-7.
**Also known as:** Govern Models, Model Validation Step.

## Relationships
- Validate Risk Models is defined in the Stress Testing process.
- Validate Risk Models causes Project Capital Impact.
- Validate Risk Models depends on the Machine Learning Platform capability.
- Validate Risk Models mentions Stress Testing Lead.

## Details
The Stress Testing Lead validates candidate models against the model inventory. Controls include SR 11-7 and OCC 2011-12 three-element validation and an independent second line under the three lines of defence.
