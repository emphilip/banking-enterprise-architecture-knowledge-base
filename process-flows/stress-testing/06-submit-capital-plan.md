# Submit Capital Plan

**Definition.** Submit Capital Plan submits the capital plan and stress results to the regulator (CCAR/DFAST) and emits the Stress Test Completed Event.
**Also known as:** Submit CCAR Plan, Capital Plan Submission Step.

## Relationships
- Submit Capital Plan is defined in the Stress Testing process.
- Submit Capital Plan depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.
- Submit Capital Plan mentions Stress Testing Lead.
- Submit Capital Plan mentions Stress Test Completed Event.

## Details
The Stress Testing Lead submits the board-approved capital plan to the regulator and emits the Stress Test Completed Event. Controls include CCAR/DFAST regulatory submission, completeness and a documentation trail. This is the final step of Stress Testing.
