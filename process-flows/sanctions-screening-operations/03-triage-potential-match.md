# Triage Potential Match

**Definition.** Triage Potential Match performs L1 triage, clearing obvious false positives or passing credible matches to L2 review on a clear/review/block basis.
**Also known as:** Screen Potential Match, L1 Triage Step.

## Relationships
- Triage Potential Match is defined in the Sanctions Screening Operations process.
- Triage Potential Match causes Resolve Sanctions Hit.
- Triage Potential Match depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.
- Triage Potential Match mentions Compliance Screening Officer.
- Triage Potential Match mentions Sanctions Hit.

## Details
The Compliance Screening Officer triages the Sanctions Hit into a triage decision. Controls include three-tier triage, risk-based filtering and documentation.
