# Replenish ATM Cash

**Definition.** Replenish ATM Cash replenishes ATM cassettes under dual custody; the CIT confirms or adjusts expected versus actual loaded amount.
**Also known as:** ATM Replenishment, Cassette Loading.

## Relationships
- Replenish ATM Cash is defined in the ATM Cash Servicing process.
- Replenish ATM Cash causes Reconcile ATM Cash.
- Replenish ATM Cash depends on the ATM Management capability.
- Replenish ATM Cash mentions ATM Custodian.
- Replenish ATM Cash mentions ATM Replenishment Record.

## Details
The ATM Custodian oversees replenishment. Inputs are the CIT schedule and vault cash; outputs are a replenished ATM and a load record. Controls include dual custody of keys and cassettes and expected-versus-actual confirmation by the CIT.
