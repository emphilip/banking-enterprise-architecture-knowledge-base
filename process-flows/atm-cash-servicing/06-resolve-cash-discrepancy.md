# Resolve Cash Discrepancy

**Definition.** Resolve Cash Discrepancy investigates and resolves cash variances and exceptions and posts adjustments.
**Also known as:** Cash Variance Resolution, Exception Handling (ATM).

## Relationships
- Resolve Cash Discrepancy is defined in the ATM Cash Servicing process.
- Resolve Cash Discrepancy depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Resolve Cash Discrepancy mentions ATM Custodian.
- Resolve Cash Discrepancy mentions ATM Replenishment Record.

## Details
The ATM Custodian resolves the variance. Inputs are the discrepancy report; outputs are a resolved variance and an adjustment. Controls include variance / exception management and adjustment approval, closing the dual-custody reconciliation loop.
