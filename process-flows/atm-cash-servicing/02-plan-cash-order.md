# Plan Cash Order

**Definition.** Plan Cash Order converts the forecast into cash orders and per-terminal load plans by denomination.
**Also known as:** Cash Order Planning, Load Planning.

## Relationships
- Plan Cash Order is defined in the ATM Cash Servicing process.
- Plan Cash Order causes Schedule Cash Delivery.
- Plan Cash Order depends on the ATM Management capability.
- Plan Cash Order mentions ATM Custodian.
- Plan Cash Order mentions ATM Cash Forecast.

## Details
The ATM Custodian plans the order. Inputs are the ATM Cash Forecast; outputs are a cash order and a load plan. Controls include order limits and denomination mix rules.
