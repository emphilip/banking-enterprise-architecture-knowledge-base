# Schedule Cash Delivery

**Definition.** Schedule Cash Delivery schedules armoured cash-in-transit delivery routes against the load plan.
**Also known as:** CIT Scheduling, Delivery Scheduling.

## Relationships
- Schedule Cash Delivery is defined in the ATM Cash Servicing process.
- Schedule Cash Delivery causes Replenish ATM Cash.
- Schedule Cash Delivery depends on the Integration Platform capability.
- Schedule Cash Delivery mentions ATM Custodian.

## Details
The ATM Custodian schedules delivery. Inputs are the load plan; outputs are a CIT schedule. Controls include CIT chain-of-custody and route security.
