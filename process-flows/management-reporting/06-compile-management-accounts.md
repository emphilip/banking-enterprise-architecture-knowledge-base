# Compile Management Accounts

**Definition.** Compile Management Accounts assembles the management accounts and board MI pack with KPIs, segment performance and variance commentary.

## Relationships
- Compile Management Accounts is defined in the Management Reporting process.
- Compile Management Accounts causes Present Board Pack.
- Compile Management Accounts depends on the Financial Reporting capability.
- Compile Management Accounts mentions FP&A Manager.
- Compile Management Accounts mentions Management Accounts.

## Details
The FP&A Manager assembles the Management Accounts and board MI pack. Inputs are the variance analysis, commentary and KPIs; outputs are the Management Accounts and board MI pack. Controls include segment KPI integrity and pack review.
