# Capture Actual Results

**Definition.** Capture Actual Results captures period actuals from the closed ledger and maps them to the management reporting hierarchy and segments.
**Also known as:** Load Actuals, Capture Actuals.

## Relationships
- Capture Actual Results is defined in the Management Reporting process.
- Capture Actual Results causes Analyse Budget Variance.
- Capture Actual Results depends on the Financial Reporting capability.
- Capture Actual Results mentions FP&A Manager.
- Capture Actual Results mentions Trial Balance.

## Details
The FP&A Analyst captures actuals from the approved Trial Balance and maps them to the management reporting hierarchy and segments. Inputs are the approved Trial Balance and segment mapping; outputs are mapped actuals. Controls include actual-vs-ledger reconciliation and segment mapping integrity.
