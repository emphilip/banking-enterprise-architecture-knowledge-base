# Present Board Pack

**Definition.** Present Board Pack presents the management accounts to the CFO and board, obtains sign-off and distributes the approved pack.

## Relationships
- Present Board Pack is defined in the Management Reporting process.
- Present Board Pack depends on the Financial Reporting capability.
- Present Board Pack mentions FP&A Manager.
- Present Board Pack mentions Management Accounts.

## Details
The FP&A Manager presents the Management Accounts to the CFO and board, obtains sign-off and distributes the approved pack. Inputs are the Management Accounts and board MI pack; outputs are the approved board pack and decisions. Controls include CFO/board MI sign-off and distribution control.
