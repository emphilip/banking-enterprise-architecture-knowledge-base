# Roll Forward Forecast

**Definition.** Roll Forward Forecast updates the rolling forecast for actual trends and revised assumptions on a periodic cadence.

## Relationships
- Roll Forward Forecast is defined in the Management Reporting process.
- Roll Forward Forecast causes Capture Actual Results.
- Roll Forward Forecast depends on the Financial Reporting capability.
- Roll Forward Forecast mentions FP&A Manager.

## Details
The FP&A Analyst updates the rolling forecast against the approved budget and latest actuals. Inputs are the approved budget and latest actuals; outputs are the updated forecast. Controls include forecast cadence governance and assumption review.
