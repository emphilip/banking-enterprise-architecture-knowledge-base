# Build Operating Budget

**Definition.** Build Operating Budget constructs the annual operating budget from strategic targets and driver assumptions, consolidating business-unit submissions.
**Also known as:** Construct Budget, Prepare Budget.

## Relationships
- Build Operating Budget is defined in the Management Reporting process.
- Build Operating Budget causes Approve Budget Plan.
- Build Operating Budget depends on the Financial Reporting capability.
- Build Operating Budget mentions FP&A Manager.

## Details
The FP&A Analyst constructs the draft operating budget from the strategic plan, driver assumptions and prior actuals, consolidating business-unit submissions. Inputs are the strategic plan, driver assumptions and prior actuals; outputs are the draft operating budget. Controls include assumption governance and bottom-up/top-down reconciliation.
