# Approve Budget Plan

**Definition.** Approve Budget Plan reviews and approves the operating budget at the management and board level and loads it as the baseline.
**Also known as:** Lock Budget, Sign Off Budget.

## Relationships
- Approve Budget Plan is defined in the Management Reporting process.
- Approve Budget Plan causes Roll Forward Forecast.
- Approve Budget Plan depends on the Financial Reporting capability.
- Approve Budget Plan mentions FP&A Manager.

## Details
The FP&A Manager reviews and approves the draft operating budget and loads it as the locked baseline. Inputs are the draft operating budget; outputs are the approved budget baseline. Controls include budget approval and baseline lock.
