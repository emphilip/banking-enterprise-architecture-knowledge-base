# Aggregate Balances

**Definition.** Aggregate Balances collects intraday and prior-day balances across all bank accounts, entities and currencies.
**Also known as:** Balance Aggregation, Gather Balances.

## Relationships
- Aggregate Balances is defined in the Cash Management process.
- Aggregate Balances causes Determine Cash Position.
- Aggregate Balances depends on the Integration Platform capability.
- Aggregate Balances mentions Treasury Cash Manager.
- Aggregate Balances mentions Bank Statement Message.

## Details
The Treasury Cash Manager collects balances. Inputs are the Bank Statement Message; outputs are aggregated balances. Controls include balance completeness checks and account coverage validation.
