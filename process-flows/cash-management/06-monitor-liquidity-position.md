# Monitor Liquidity Position

**Definition.** Monitor Liquidity Position tracks post-funding balances and intraday liquidity against limits and alerts on breaches.
**Also known as:** Liquidity Monitoring, Position Monitoring.

## Relationships
- Monitor Liquidity Position is defined in the Cash Management process.
- Monitor Liquidity Position causes Report Cash Status.
- Monitor Liquidity Position depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Monitor Liquidity Position mentions Treasury Cash Manager.

## Details
The Treasury Cash Manager tracks post-funding liquidity. Inputs are funding instructions; outputs are a liquidity status. Controls include intraday liquidity limit monitoring, branching across within-limit versus breach alert.
