# Report Cash Status

**Definition.** Report Cash Status produces cash position, forecast and liquidity reports for treasury, finance and regulators.
**Also known as:** Cash Reporting, Treasury Reporting.

## Relationships
- Report Cash Status is defined in the Cash Management process.
- Report Cash Status depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Report Cash Status mentions Treasury Cash Manager.
- Report Cash Status mentions Cash Forecast.

## Details
The Treasury Cash Manager produces the reporting output. Inputs are the liquidity status; outputs are a cash management report. As the final step of the flow it has no successor step. Controls include reporting review and data lineage.
