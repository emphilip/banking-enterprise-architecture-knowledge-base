# Forecast Cash Flow

**Definition.** Forecast Cash Flow projects future inflows/outflows and surpluses/shortfalls from position, schedules and history.
**Also known as:** Cash Forecasting Step, Liquidity Forecast.

## Relationships
- Forecast Cash Flow is defined in the Cash Management process.
- Forecast Cash Flow causes Decide Funding Actions.
- Forecast Cash Flow depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Forecast Cash Flow mentions Treasury Cash Manager.
- Forecast Cash Flow mentions Cash Forecast.

## Details
The Treasury Cash Manager projects future cash flows. Inputs are the cash position; outputs are the Cash Forecast. Controls include forecast variance review and explicit scenario assumptions.
