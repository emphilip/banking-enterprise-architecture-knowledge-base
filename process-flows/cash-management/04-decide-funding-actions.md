# Decide Funding Actions

**Definition.** Decide Funding Actions decides sweeps, concentration, investment of surplus or borrowing to cover shortfalls against targets.
**Also known as:** Funding Decisioning, Liquidity Decision.

## Relationships
- Decide Funding Actions is defined in the Cash Management process.
- Decide Funding Actions causes Execute Funding Transfer.
- Decide Funding Actions depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Decide Funding Actions mentions Treasury Cash Manager.

## Details
The Treasury Cash Manager decides funding actions. Inputs are the Cash Forecast; outputs are a funding decision. Controls include target/threshold rules and approval limits, branching across investing surplus versus funding a shortfall.
