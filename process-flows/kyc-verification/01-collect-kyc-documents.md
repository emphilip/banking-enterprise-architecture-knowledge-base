# Collect KYC Documents

**Definition.** Collect KYC Documents collects the identity documents and PII required to satisfy the Customer Identification Program.
**Also known as:** Gather KYC Documents, KYC Intake Step.

## Relationships
- Collect KYC Documents is defined in the KYC Verification process.
- Collect KYC Documents causes Validate Customer Identity.
- Collect KYC Documents depends on the Intelligent Document Processing capability.
- Collect KYC Documents mentions KYC Analyst.
- Collect KYC Documents mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The KYC Analyst gathers applicant data and identity documents into a document package. Controls include the CIP minimum-data requirement, document completeness and consent capture to ensure a complete intake before verification.
