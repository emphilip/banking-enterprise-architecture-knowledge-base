# Approve KYC Outcome

**Definition.** Approve KYC Outcome is the decision point that approves, requests remediation or escalates the KYC Profile under four-eyes.
**Also known as:** Decide KYC Outcome, KYC Approval Step.

## Relationships
- Approve KYC Outcome is defined in the KYC Verification process.
- Approve KYC Outcome causes Schedule KYC Review.
- Approve KYC Outcome depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Approve KYC Outcome mentions KYC Analyst.
- Approve KYC Outcome mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The KYC Analyst reviews the KYC Profile and screening results to produce a KYC approval decision. Controls include four-eyes approval, a branch on pass versus escalate, and escalation of unresolved sanctions/PEP hits before the relationship proceeds.
