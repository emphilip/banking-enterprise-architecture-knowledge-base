# Screen Against Watchlists

**Definition.** Screen Against Watchlists screens the verified customer and related parties against sanctions, PEP and adverse-media lists.
**Also known as:** Run KYC Screening.

## Relationships
- Screen Against Watchlists is defined in the KYC Verification process.
- Screen Against Watchlists causes Compile KYC Profile.
- Screen Against Watchlists depends on the Sanctions Screening capability.
- Screen Against Watchlists mentions KYC Analyst.
- Screen Against Watchlists mentions Sanctions Hit.

## Details
The KYC Analyst screens the verified identity against current sanctions lists to produce screening results. Controls include sanctions/PEP/adverse-media screening, fuzzy matching and list currency, with potential matches raised as a Sanctions Hit for resolution.
