# Validate Customer Identity

**Definition.** Validate Customer Identity verifies the documents and PII against authoritative sources and biometrics to prove identity.
**Also known as:** Prove Customer Identity.

## Relationships
- Validate Customer Identity is defined in the KYC Verification process.
- Validate Customer Identity causes Screen Against Watchlists.
- Validate Customer Identity depends on the Identity Verification capability.
- Validate Customer Identity mentions KYC Analyst.
- Validate Customer Identity mentions KYC Profile.

## Details
The KYC Analyst verifies the document package to produce a verified identity. Controls include document authenticity, biometric/liveness, authoritative-register cross-check and tamper detection under the CIP standard.
