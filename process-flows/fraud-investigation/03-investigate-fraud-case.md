# Investigate Fraud Case

**Definition.** Investigate Fraud Case investigates the case by gathering evidence, contacting the customer and confirming or dismissing fraud.
**Also known as:** Examine Fraud Case, Fraud Investigation Step.

## Relationships
- Investigate Fraud Case is defined in the Fraud Investigation process.
- Investigate Fraud Case causes Recover Fraud Loss.
- Investigate Fraud Case depends on the Case Management capability.
- Investigate Fraud Case mentions Fraud Investigator.
- Investigate Fraud Case mentions Fraud Case.

## Details
The Fraud Investigator works the Fraud Case and account activity into investigation findings. Controls include evidence standards, documented customer contact and a reasonable, timely investigation.
