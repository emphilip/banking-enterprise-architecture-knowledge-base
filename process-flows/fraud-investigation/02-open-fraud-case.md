# Open Fraud Case

**Definition.** Open Fraud Case opens a Fraud Case in the case-management system and assembles account context.
**Also known as:** Create Case, Case Opening Step.

## Relationships
- Open Fraud Case is defined in the Fraud Investigation process.
- Open Fraud Case causes Investigate Fraud Case.
- Open Fraud Case depends on the Case Management capability.
- Open Fraud Case mentions Fraud Investigator.
- Open Fraud Case mentions Fraud Case.

## Details
The Fraud Investigator opens the Fraud Case and assembles account context from the triage decision and account activity. Controls include case logging and account-history review (for example the prior six months).
