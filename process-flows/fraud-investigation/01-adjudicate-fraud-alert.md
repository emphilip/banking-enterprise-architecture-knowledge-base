# Adjudicate Fraud Alert

**Definition.** Adjudicate Fraud Alert triages the routed alert and decides whether it has enough signal to open a case.
**Also known as:** Triage Alert, Alert Adjudication Step.

## Relationships
- Adjudicate Fraud Alert is defined in the Fraud Investigation process.
- Adjudicate Fraud Alert causes Open Fraud Case.
- Adjudicate Fraud Alert depends on the Case Management capability.
- Adjudicate Fraud Alert mentions Fraud Investigator.
- Adjudicate Fraud Alert mentions Fraud Alert.

## Details
The Fraud Investigator triages the routed Fraud Alert into a triage decision. Controls include triage decisioning and escalate-or-close criteria to avoid opening cases on weak signals.
