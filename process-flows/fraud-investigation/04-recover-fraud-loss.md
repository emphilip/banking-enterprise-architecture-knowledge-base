# Recover Fraud Loss

**Definition.** Recover Fraud Loss blocks or reverses funds and pursues loss recovery on confirmed fraud.
**Also known as:** Recover Funds, Loss Recovery Step.

## Relationships
- Recover Fraud Loss is defined in the Fraud Investigation process.
- Recover Fraud Loss causes File Suspicious Activity Report.
- Recover Fraud Loss depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Recover Fraud Loss mentions Fraud Investigator.

## Details
The Fraud Investigator turns investigation findings into a recovery outcome and fraud disposition. Controls include recovery controls, reversal authority and liability rules governing who bears the loss.
