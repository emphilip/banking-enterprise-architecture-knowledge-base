# Check Overdraft Opt-In

**Definition.** Check Overdraft Opt-In checks Reg E opt-in status for one-time debit and ATM items.
**Also known as:** Verify Opt-In Status, Opt-In Check Step.

## Relationships
- Check Overdraft Opt-In is defined in the Overdraft Servicing process.
- Check Overdraft Opt-In causes Evaluate Overdraft Limit.
- Check Overdraft Opt-In depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Check Overdraft Opt-In mentions Overdraft Operations Analyst.
- Check Overdraft Opt-In mentions Reg E Opt-In Record.

## Details
The Overdraft Operations Analyst checks the Reg E Opt-In Record for one-time debit and ATM items. Controls include the Reg E opt-in requirement for covering such items.
