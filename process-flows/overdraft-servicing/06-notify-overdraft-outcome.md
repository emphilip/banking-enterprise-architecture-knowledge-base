# Notify Overdraft Outcome

**Definition.** Notify Overdraft Outcome notifies the customer of the overdraft or NSF outcome and the fee.
**Also known as:** Send Overdraft Notice, Outcome Notification Step.

## Relationships
- Notify Overdraft Outcome is defined in the Overdraft Servicing process.
- Notify Overdraft Outcome depends on the Notification Services capability.
- Notify Overdraft Outcome mentions Overdraft Operations Analyst.
- Notify Overdraft Outcome mentions Overdraft Fee.

## Details
The Overdraft Operations Analyst notifies the customer of the outcome and the Overdraft Fee. Controls include notice timing and required fee disclosure.
