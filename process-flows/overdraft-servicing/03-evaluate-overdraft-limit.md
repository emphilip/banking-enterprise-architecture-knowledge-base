# Evaluate Overdraft Limit

**Definition.** Evaluate Overdraft Limit compares the shortfall against the assigned overdraft coverage limit.
**Also known as:** Assess Overdraft Limit, Limit Evaluation Step.

## Relationships
- Evaluate Overdraft Limit is defined in the Overdraft Servicing process.
- Evaluate Overdraft Limit causes Decide Pay Or Return.
- Evaluate Overdraft Limit depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Evaluate Overdraft Limit mentions Overdraft Operations Analyst.

## Details
The Overdraft Operations Analyst compares the shortfall against the overdraft coverage limit. Controls include the overdraft coverage limit and fair-access criteria.
