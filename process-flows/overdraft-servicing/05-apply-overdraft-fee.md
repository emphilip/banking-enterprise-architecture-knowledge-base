# Apply Overdraft Fee

**Definition.** Apply Overdraft Fee assesses the applicable overdraft or NSF fee per the disclosed schedule.
**Also known as:** Assess Overdraft Fee, Fee Application Step.

## Relationships
- Apply Overdraft Fee is defined in the Overdraft Servicing process.
- Apply Overdraft Fee causes Notify Overdraft Outcome.
- Apply Overdraft Fee depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Apply Overdraft Fee mentions Overdraft Operations Analyst.
- Apply Overdraft Fee mentions Overdraft Fee.

## Details
The Overdraft Operations Analyst assesses the Overdraft Fee from the pay/return decision. Controls include fee disclosure and the distinction between NSF and overdraft fee rules.
