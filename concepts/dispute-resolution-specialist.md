# Dispute Resolution Specialist

**Definition.** Dispute Resolution Specialist is the role that manages cardholder disputes end to end: registering the claim, validating Reg Z / Reg E rights, issuing provisional credit, selecting the scheme reason code, raising the chargeback, handling representment and arbitration, and finalising the credit or debit. Dispute Resolution Specialist protects the cardholder's regulatory rights while pursuing recovery from the acquirer/merchant.

**Also known as:** Chargeback Analyst, Disputes Specialist.

## Details
Dispute Resolution Specialist opens the Chargeback Case against a posted transaction, verifies the claim falls within the Reg Z 60-day billing-error window or Reg E error-resolution timelines, and issues provisional credit (within 10 business days under Reg E). The role gathers evidence, assigns the correct scheme reason code (e.g. Visa 10-13, Mastercard 4837/4853), files the chargeback within the 120-day limit, assesses merchant representment within the 30-day phase window, and escalates to pre-arbitration/arbitration where contested. Dispute Resolution Specialist finalises credit/debit and notifies the cardholder, closing the case.
