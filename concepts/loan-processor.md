# Loan Processor

**Definition.** Loan Processor is the operations role that verifies income, employment and assets and completes the loan file so it can be decisioned and funded. Loan Processor ensures document completeness and data quality in the middle stages of origination.

**Also known as:** Loan Processing Clerk, File Processor.

## Relationships

- Loan Processor mentions Collect Supporting Documents.
- Loan Processor mentions Verify Borrower Income.

## Details
Loan Processor gathers and classifies income, identity and asset documents needed to complete the file, then verifies income, employment and assets against the submitted documentation. The role enforces a document checklist, completeness controls and AML screening, applying four-eyes on overrides before the file is passed for decisioning. Loan Processor works between the Loan Officer, who captures and offers the loan, and the Credit Underwriter, who adjudicates it, and also supports mortgage files during processing.
