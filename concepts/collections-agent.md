# Collections Agent

**Definition.** Collections Agent is the early-stage collections role that detects delinquency, contacts the borrower and arranges a promise-to-pay in the early ageing buckets. Collections Agent works the soft-collections stage before recovery escalation.

**Also known as:** Collector, Arrears Agent.

## Relationships
- Collections Agent is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Collections Agent mentions Loan Collections.
- Collections Agent mentions Early-Stage Collections.
- Collections Agent mentions Detect Delinquency.
- Collections Agent mentions Contact Borrower.

## Details
Collections Agent identifies missed payments and risk signals, buckets accounts by ageing (such as 1-30, 30-60 and 60-plus days), and prioritises and routes them to treatment strategies. The role reaches the borrower via preferred channels with dunning notices and reminders under FDCPA-aligned contact rules and call-time limits, then agrees a promise-to-pay or repayment plan. Collections Agent applies vulnerable-customer treatment and escalates persistent arrears to the Recovery Officer when unresolved.
