# Regulatory Reporting Specialist

**Definition.** Regulatory Reporting Specialist prepares prudential capital returns (COREP / FINREP, FR Y-9C / FR Y-14), calculates risk-weighted assets and own funds, validates returns against the supervisor taxonomy and submits them to the regulator. Regulatory Reporting Specialist owns the prudential reporting cycle within the finance and treasury function.

**Also known as:** Prudential Reporting Analyst, Capital Reporting Specialist.

## Relationships
- Regulatory Reporting Specialist is related to the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Regulatory Reporting Specialist mentions Regulatory Capital Reporting.
- Regulatory Reporting Specialist mentions Calculate RWA.
- Regulatory Reporting Specialist mentions Submit Capital Return.

## Details
Regulatory Reporting Specialist runs the Regulatory Capital Reporting process: sourcing and reconciling general-ledger and risk-exposure data for the reference date, calculating risk-weighted assets across credit, market and operational risk, determining Tier 1 / Tier 2 own funds and capital ratios, and assembling the Capital Adequacy Return. The role validates returns against the regulator DPM / XBRL taxonomy, supports CFO and board attestation, and submits returns and the capital plan through the secure regulatory channel under CCAR. Regulatory Reporting Specialist enforces data-quality reconciliation, RWA calculation governance and ICFR over regulatory reporting.
