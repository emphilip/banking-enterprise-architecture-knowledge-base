# Interaction Record

**Definition.** Interaction Record is the logged record of a customer interaction, capturing the channel, intent, outcome and disposition. Interaction Record is the output artifact that closes channel and contact-centre servicing flows and builds cross-channel interaction history.

**Also known as:** Contact Record, Interaction Log.

## Relationships
- Interaction Record is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Interaction Record mentions Record Channel Interaction.
- Interaction Record mentions Wrap Up Interaction.
- Interaction Record is related to Contact Centre Interaction.

## Details
Interaction Record is persisted at the end of a servicing interaction, whether in the Channel Servicing flow or during contact-centre wrap-up, recording the channel used, the customer's intent, the resolution or handoff, and the agent disposition and notes. It forms the audit trail and the interaction history that lets later servicing follow the customer across channels, and it is governed by data-protection and recording-retention controls.
