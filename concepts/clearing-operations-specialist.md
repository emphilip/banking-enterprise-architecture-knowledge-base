# Clearing Operations Specialist

**Definition.** Clearing Operations Specialist is the specialist role that formats and submits payments to clearing rails and manages dialogue with ACH operators, Fedwire and instant-payment networks. Clearing Operations Specialist ensures clearing messages are correct and dispatched within scheme cut-off windows.

**Also known as:** Clearing Operator, Rail Operations Specialist.

## Relationships
- Clearing Operations Specialist is related to the Payments domain.
- Clearing Operations Specialist mentions Payment Processing.
- Clearing Operations Specialist mentions Format Clearing Message.
- Clearing Operations Specialist mentions Submit To Clearing.
- Clearing Operations Specialist is related to Payment Clearing & Settlement.

## Details
Clearing Operations Specialist owns the later submission stages of the Payment Processing flow, transforming routed payments into rail-specific formats such as Nacha entries, pacs.008 or Fedwire messages and transmitting them to the operator. The role monitors transmission acknowledgements and enforces message integrity and cut-off controls.
