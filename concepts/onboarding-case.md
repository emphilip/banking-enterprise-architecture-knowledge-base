# Onboarding Case

**Definition.** Onboarding Case is the case object tracking an onboarding instance, its documents, checks, risk rating and decision. Onboarding Case is the working artifact carried through the Customer Onboarding flow.

**Also known as:** Onboarding Record, Onboarding File.

## Relationships
- Onboarding Case is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Onboarding Case mentions Receive Application.
- Onboarding Case mentions Approve Onboarding.
- Onboarding Case mentions Customer Onboarding.

## Details
Onboarding Case is opened when an application is received and accumulates the consent record, document package, verification results, screening outcome and risk rating as the flow progresses. It holds the audit trail and decision state that the Onboarding Specialist uses to make the accept, decline or refer decision before the customer record is created and activated.
