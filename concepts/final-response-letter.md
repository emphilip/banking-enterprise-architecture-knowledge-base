# Final Response Letter

**Definition.** Final Response Letter is the firm's final written response to a complaint, including outcome, redress and Ombudsman referral rights. Final Response Letter is the output artifact of the Complaint Handling flow.

**Also known as:** Final Response, FRL.

## Relationships
- Final Response Letter is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Final Response Letter mentions Issue Final Response.
- Final Response Letter mentions Complaint Investigation & Redress.
- Final Response Letter mentions Complaint Handling.

## Details
Final Response Letter communicates whether the complaint is upheld, the redress applied and, where relevant, the customer's right to escalate to the Ombudsman within the regulatory deadline. It is issued by the Complaints Officer after the outcome and redress are decided and precedes root-cause analysis and case closure.
