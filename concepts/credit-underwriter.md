# Credit Underwriter

**Definition.** Credit Underwriter is the risk-assessment role that evaluates a borrower against the 5 Cs of credit, scores credit risk, tests affordability and renders the adjudication decision. Credit Underwriter is the decision authority in the underwriting flow.

**Also known as:** Underwriter, Credit Analyst.

## Details
Credit Underwriter pulls bureau credit reports under a permissible purpose, applies scorecards and models to derive a risk grade, and tests repayment capacity by weighing income against obligations using DTI and DSR limits. The role assesses collateral adequacy and loan-to-value, tests the consolidated case against credit policy, then renders the approve, conditionally approve, decline or refer decision under delegated authority and four-eyes controls. Credit Underwriter sets terms and conditions that govern the resulting offer.
