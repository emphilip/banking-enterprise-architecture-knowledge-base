# Cash Forecast

**Definition.** Cash Forecast is the projection of future cash inflows, outflows and net position by value date. Cash Forecast is the artifact produced during cash management to inform funding decisions.

**Also known as:** Liquidity Forecast Report, Cash Flow Forecast.

## Relationships
- Cash Forecast is related to the Payments domain.
- Cash Forecast mentions Forecast Cash Flow.
- Cash Forecast mentions Decide Funding Actions.
- Cash Forecast is related to Cash Management.
- Cash Forecast is related to Liquidity Forecasting.

## Details
Cash Forecast is generated by projecting future flows and surpluses or shortfalls from the current cash position, payment schedules and historical patterns. The artifact drives the decision to sweep, concentrate, invest surplus or borrow to cover shortfalls against target balances, and is reviewed for forecast variance against assumptions.
