# Overdraft Operations Analyst

**Definition.** Overdraft Operations Analyst is the role that handles overdraft and NSF decisioning, coverage-limit checks and fee application under Regulation E. Overdraft Operations Analyst decides whether to pay or return items presented against insufficient funds.

**Also known as:** Overdraft Analyst, NSF Operations Analyst.

## Relationships
- Overdraft Operations Analyst is related to the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Operations Analyst mentions Overdraft Servicing.
- Overdraft Operations Analyst mentions Decide Pay Or Return.
- Overdraft Operations Analyst mentions Apply Overdraft Fee.

## Details
Overdraft Operations Analyst detects insufficient funds against available balance, checks the Reg E Opt-In Record for one-time debit and ATM items, compares the shortfall to the assigned overdraft coverage limit, and decides to pay as an overdraft or return as NSF. When an item is paid into overdraft, Overdraft Operations Analyst applies the disclosed Overdraft Fee, distinct from an NSF return fee, and notifies the customer. Fair-access underwriting and fee-disclosure controls govern the decision.
