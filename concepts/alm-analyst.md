# ALM Analyst

**Definition.** ALM Analyst builds and applies the funds transfer pricing curve and attributes net interest margin to business units under asset-liability management and ALCO governance. ALM Analyst owns transfer-pricing methodology and margin attribution in the finance and treasury function.

**Also known as:** Asset-Liability Analyst, FTP Analyst.

## Relationships
- ALM Analyst is related to the Finance & Treasury domain.
- ALM Analyst mentions Funds Transfer Pricing.
- ALM Analyst mentions Build FTP Curve.
- ALM Analyst mentions Attribute Net Margin.

## Details
ALM Analyst runs the Funds Transfer Pricing process: assembling market funding curves, building the matched-maturity FTP Curve with liquidity-premium and contingency add-ons, setting behavioural assumptions for non-maturity deposits and prepayment, and presenting the methodology to ALCO for approval. After approval the role applies transfer rates to assets and liabilities, attributes net interest margin between customer spread and funding cost, and reports outcomes to ALCO and business units. ALM Analyst enforces matched-maturity methodology governance, behavioural-assumption review and margin-attribution reconciliation.
