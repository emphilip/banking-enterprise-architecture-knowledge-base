# Loan Officer

**Definition.** Loan Officer is the front-line origination role that captures the borrower's request, runs pre-qualification, renders the lending decision and presents the credit offer. Loan Officer owns the customer-facing stages of consumer loan origination from intake through funding.

**Also known as:** Loan Originator, Lending Officer.

## Relationships
- Loan Officer is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Officer mentions Loan Origination Workflow.
- Loan Officer mentions Capture Loan Application.
- Loan Officer mentions Render Loan Decision.
- Loan Officer mentions Issue Loan Offer.

## Details
Loan Officer drives the Loan Origination Workflow, capturing the application and personal and financial data into the origination platform, obtaining consent to the credit pull and running a soft pre-qualification check against minimum eligibility criteria. The role applies credit policy and delegated authority to approve, decline or refer the application, then generates and presents the credit offer with terms, pricing and regulatory disclosures before disbursement. Loan Officer hands the file to the Loan Processor for income and document verification and to the Credit Underwriter for adjudication on referred cases.
