# Digital Application

**Definition.** Digital Application is the online account-opening application captured during the digital onboarding journey across web and mobile. Digital Application is the input artifact that opens and carries the self-service onboarding flow.

**Also known as:** Online Application, DAO Application.

## Relationships
- Digital Application is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Application mentions Start Digital Application.
- Digital Application mentions Capture Applicant Details.
- Digital Application is related to Digital Onboarding Journey.

## Details
Digital Application holds the applicant's personal and contact data, product selection and captured consents, supporting pre-fill and save-and-resume so the customer can complete onboarding over multiple sessions. It is created at the start of the Digital Onboarding Journey and enriched through detail capture, then feeds electronic identity verification, strong-authentication enrolment, funding and channel activation, and the hand-off to back-office Customer Onboarding and KYC.
