# Performance Analyst

**Definition.** Performance Analyst is the investment-reporting role that values portfolios, reconciles positions, measures returns against benchmark with attribution, and produces and delivers the client Performance Report in the wealth flow.

**Also known as:** Investment Reporting Analyst, Performance Measurement Analyst.

## Relationships
- Performance Analyst is related to the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Performance Analyst mentions Performance Reporting.
- Performance Analyst mentions Value Portfolio.

## Details
Performance Analyst values all holdings and cash at period end using market prices, captures issuer corporate actions and adjusts positions and cash, then reconciles internal positions and cash against custodian records, investigating breaks. Performance Analyst computes time-weighted returns for the period and since inception following GIPS methodology, compares returns to the benchmark, and decomposes excess return into allocation and selection effects via attribution. Performance Analyst assembles, signs off under four-eyes review and delivers the Performance Report with returns, attribution and GIPS disclosures, maintaining valuation, reconciliation and disclosure-accuracy controls throughout.
