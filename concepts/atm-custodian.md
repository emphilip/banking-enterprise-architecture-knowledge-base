# ATM Custodian

**Definition.** ATM Custodian is the custodian responsible for ATM cash forecasting, replenishment of terminals under dual custody, and daily reconciliation of dispensed versus loaded cash. ATM Custodian operates the ATM cash supply chain flow across the terminal estate.

**Also known as:** ATM Cash Officer, ATM Operations Custodian.

- ATM Custodian mentions ATM Cash Management.
- ATM Custodian mentions Forecast ATM Demand.
- ATM Custodian mentions Replenish ATM Cash.
- ATM Custodian mentions Reconcile ATM Cash.

## Details
ATM Custodian runs the ATM Cash Management flow: forecasting per-terminal cash demand, converting forecasts into cash orders and load plans, scheduling armoured cash-in-transit delivery, replenishing cassettes under dual custody, and reconciling electronic-journal and host records against loaded amounts. The role enforces dual custody of keys and cassettes, cash-in-transit chain-of-custody, daily ATM general-ledger reconciliation, and investigation of cash variances and exceptions.
