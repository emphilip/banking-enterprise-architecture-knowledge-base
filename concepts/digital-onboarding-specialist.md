# Digital Onboarding Specialist

**Definition.** Digital Onboarding Specialist is the operator and owner of the digital account-opening journey across web and mobile, handling application capture, eKYC and activation exceptions, and the hand-off to back-office onboarding. Digital Onboarding Specialist keeps the self-service onboarding journey flowing and intervenes when an application stalls.

**Also known as:** Digital Onboarding Officer, DAO Specialist.

## Details
Digital Onboarding Specialist runs the Digital Onboarding Journey end to end: starting the application with save-and-resume, capturing applicant details and consents, triggering electronic identity verification, enrolling strong customer authentication, processing initial funding, and provisioning digital banking access. The role manages eKYC failures and abandonment, enforces consent capture and accessibility controls, and coordinates the hand-off to back-office Customer Onboarding and KYC.
