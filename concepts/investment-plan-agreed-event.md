# Investment Plan Agreed Event

**Definition.** Investment Plan Agreed Event is the business event signalling that the client has agreed and signed the Investment Policy Statement, finalising the advisory plan in the wealth flow.

**Also known as:** IPS Signed Event, Plan Agreed Event.

## Relationships
- Investment Plan Agreed Event is related to the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Investment Plan Agreed Event mentions Financial Planning.
- Investment Plan Agreed Event causes Agree Investment Plan.

## Details
Investment Plan Agreed Event fires when the client acknowledges the recommendation and signs the Investment Policy Statement, after the suitability report and required disclosures have been delivered. Investment Plan Agreed Event carries the signed IPS reference, the agreed target allocation, constraints and rebalancing rules, and the client acknowledgement timestamp. Investment Plan Agreed Event confirms the advice is documented and consented to under MiFID II / Reg BI record-keeping obligations, and it authorises the portfolio to be funded and managed to the agreed targets. Downstream rebalancing and monitoring use the agreed IPS targets as the reference baseline for drift detection.
