# Loan Agreement

**Definition.** Loan Agreement is the executed credit contract, including the promissory note, that governs the disbursed loan and binds the borrower to its terms. Loan Agreement is the legal artifact executed before disbursement.

**Also known as:** Credit Agreement, Promissory Note.

## Relationships
- Loan Agreement is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Agreement mentions Loan Origination Workflow.
- Loan Agreement mentions Disburse Loan Funds.
- Loan Agreement mentions Fund Mortgage Loan.

## Details
Loan Agreement sets out the principal, interest rate, repayment schedule, fees, covenants, default and security provisions and the borrower's promise to repay. It is executed at Disburse Loan Funds in the Loan Origination Workflow and at Fund Mortgage Loan in the Mortgage Origination flow, where signing controls and four-eyes on funding apply. For secured lending the Loan Agreement is accompanied by security documents that perfect the lender's collateral interest, and its execution gates the release of funds.
