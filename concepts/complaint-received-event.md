# Complaint Received Event

**Definition.** Complaint Received Event is the trigger raised when a complaint is received on any channel, starting the Complaint Handling flow. Complaint Received Event signals that a complaint must be logged and acknowledged.

**Also known as:** Complaint Raised Event, Complaint Logged.

## Relationships
- Complaint Received Event is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Received Event causes Log Complaint.
- Complaint Received Event mentions Complaint Handling.
- Complaint Received Event is related to Complaint Intake & Triage.

## Details
Complaint Received Event is raised when a customer expresses dissatisfaction through any channel. It initiates the Complaint Handling flow, prompting the Complaints Officer to record the complaint with a reference number, acknowledge it within SLA and triage it for investigation and redress.
