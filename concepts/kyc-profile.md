# KYC Profile

**Definition.** KYC Profile is the structured customer due-diligence record holding identity evidence, screening results and risk rating. KYC Profile is produced during the due-diligence steps of the Customer Onboarding flow.

**Also known as:** CDD Profile, Customer Risk Profile.

## Relationships
- KYC Profile is related to the Customer Management domain.
- KYC Profile mentions Assess Customer Risk.
- KYC Profile mentions Identity & Due Diligence.
- KYC Profile mentions Create Customer Record.

## Details
KYC Profile consolidates the verified identity, sanctions, PEP and adverse-media screening outcomes and the CDD/EDD assessment that the KYC Analyst compiles to assign a risk rating. It is created alongside the golden customer record and supports ongoing monitoring after the customer is activated.
