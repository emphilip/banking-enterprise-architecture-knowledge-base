# Wealth Relationship Manager

**Definition.** Wealth Relationship Manager is the onboarding and relationship role that captures investor information, runs investor suitability and KYC, and sets up the investment and custody account at the start of a wealth relationship.

**Also known as:** Private Banker, Wealth RM.

## Relationships
- Wealth Relationship Manager is related to the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Wealth Relationship Manager mentions Wealth Onboarding.
- Wealth Relationship Manager mentions Capture Investor Profile.

## Details
Wealth Relationship Manager captures investor identity, financial situation and objectives, then assesses risk tolerance and capacity for loss to record the Risk Profile. Applying KYC for investors and MiFID II / Reg BI suitability rules, Wealth Relationship Manager assesses whether the proposed service is suitable, branching on a suitable or unsuitable outcome. On a pass, Wealth Relationship Manager opens the investment/custody account under account-opening KYC controls, executes the advisory or discretionary mandate authorising portfolio management, and funds the account after a source-of-funds check so the client is ready to invest.
