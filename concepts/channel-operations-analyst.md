# Channel Operations Analyst

**Definition.** Channel Operations Analyst is the operator of omnichannel servicing sessions who opens and authenticates channel sessions, orchestrates the journey across channels, and manages escalation to assisted servicing. Channel Operations Analyst keeps cross-channel context intact through the channel servicing flow.

**Also known as:** Channel Ops Analyst, Omnichannel Analyst.

## Relationships
- Channel Operations Analyst is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Channel Operations Analyst mentions Channel Servicing.
- Channel Operations Analyst mentions Open Channel Session.
- Channel Operations Analyst mentions Authenticate Channel User.
- Channel Operations Analyst mentions Record Channel Interaction.

## Details
Channel Operations Analyst runs the Channel Servicing flow: initiating a customer channel session, authenticating the user with strong or step-up authentication as risk requires, identifying servicing intent, orchestrating routing between self-service and assisted servicing, and persisting the interaction record. The role enforces session integrity, cross-channel context preservation, accessibility, and context-preserving warm handoffs so customers do not have to repeat themselves on escalation.
