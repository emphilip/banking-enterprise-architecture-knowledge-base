# Mortgage Closer

**Definition.** Mortgage Closer is the closing-specialist role that delivers the Closing Disclosure, runs the signing and funds the mortgage once it is clear to close. Mortgage Closer owns the final closing and funding stage of the mortgage flow.

**Also known as:** Closing Officer, Funder.

## Relationships
- Mortgage Closer is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Mortgage Closer mentions Mortgage Origination.
- Mortgage Closer mentions Closing & Mortgage Funding.
- Mortgage Closer mentions Deliver Closing Disclosure.
- Mortgage Closer mentions Fund Mortgage Loan.

## Details
Mortgage Closer provides the Closing Disclosure at least three business days before consummation, reconciling fee tolerances against the original Loan Estimate. The role coordinates the signing, applies four-eyes controls on funding, disburses funds and arranges recording of the security interest. Mortgage Closer acts after the Mortgage Loan Originator and Loan Processor have completed application, disclosure and diligence and after underwriting has reached the clear-to-close gate.
