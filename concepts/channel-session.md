# Channel Session

**Definition.** Channel Session is a stateful customer interaction session spanning one or more channels with preserved context. Channel Session is the artifact that carries authentication state and intent through the channel servicing flow.

**Also known as:** Customer Session, Omnichannel Session.

## Relationships
- Channel Session is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Channel Session mentions Open Channel Session.
- Channel Session mentions Authenticate Channel User.
- Channel Session is related to Channel Servicing.

## Details
Channel Session is instantiated when a customer engages a channel and identifies the entry point and intent. It holds authentication and step-up state, captured context, and journey progress so that orchestration can route between self-service and assisted servicing without losing information. The session preserves context across channel switches and warm handoffs, and its outcome is persisted to the interaction history that underpins cross-channel servicing continuity.
