# Reconciliation Report

**Definition.** Reconciliation Report is the certified output evidencing reconciliation status, breaks and adjustments for control and audit. Reconciliation Report is the artifact produced at the end of reconciliation and settlement.

**Also known as:** Recon Report, Reconciliation Certificate.

## Relationships
- Reconciliation Report is related to the Payments domain.
- Reconciliation Report mentions Sign Off Reconciliation.
- Reconciliation Report mentions Reconciliation & Settlement.
- Reconciliation Report is related to Account Reconciliation.
- Reconciliation Report is related to Exception Investigation.

## Details
Reconciliation Report is created when residual items are reviewed and the reconciliation is certified at sign-off. The artifact records matched balances, outstanding breaks and posted adjustments, providing the certification and audit trail required by control functions and supporting downstream financial and regulatory reporting.
