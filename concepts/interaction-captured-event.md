# Interaction Captured Event

**Definition.** Interaction Captured Event is the event raised when a contact-centre interaction is captured or answered. Interaction Captured Event signals the start of the inbound contact-centre interaction flow at the IVR.

**Also known as:** Contact Received Event, Interaction Logged Event.

## Relationships
- Interaction Captured Event is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Interaction Captured Event causes Capture IVR Selection.
- Interaction Captured Event mentions Contact Centre Interaction.
- Interaction Captured Event is related to Interaction Record.

## Details
Interaction Captured Event marks an inbound contact arriving at the channel, prompting the IVR to greet the caller and capture menu selections or stated intent. The event initiates the Contact Centre Interaction lifecycle through caller authentication and skills-based routing, and is the entry point against which IVR containment, call recording and consent, and queue and service-level controls are applied.
