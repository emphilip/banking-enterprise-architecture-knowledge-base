# Customer Application

**Definition.** Customer Application is the submitted application data and product selection that initiate onboarding. Customer Application is the input artifact that opens the Customer Onboarding flow.

**Also known as:** Onboarding Application, Account Application.

## Relationships
- Customer Application is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Application mentions Receive Application.
- Customer Application mentions Application Intake & Capture.
- Customer Application mentions Customer Onboarding.

## Details
Customer Application carries the prospect's personal and contact details, product choice and supporting information submitted from a digital channel, branch or API. It is received and pre-checked at the start of the Customer Onboarding flow, where the Onboarding Specialist opens an onboarding case for consent capture, document collection and downstream verification.
