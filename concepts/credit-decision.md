# Credit Decision

**Definition.** Credit Decision is the recorded adjudication outcome of underwriting, capturing approve, decline or refer along with terms and conditions. Credit Decision is the output artifact of the underwriting flow.

**Also known as:** Underwriting Outcome, Adjudication Record.

## Relationships
- Credit Decision is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Credit Decision mentions Credit Underwriting.
- Credit Decision mentions Adjudicate Credit.
- Credit Decision mentions Credit Adjudication.

## Details
Credit Decision records the adjudication outcome (approve, conditionally approve, decline or refer), the approved amount, rate and term, any conditions precedent and the decision rationale and reason codes. It is produced at Adjudicate Credit in the Credit Underwriting flow under delegated authority and four-eyes controls and forms the decision audit trail. Credit Decision drives the credit offer in the Loan Origination Workflow and, where the outcome is a decline, the generation of an adverse-action notice.
