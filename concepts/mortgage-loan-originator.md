# Mortgage Loan Originator

**Definition.** Mortgage Loan Originator is the licensed origination role that takes the URLA/Form 1003 mortgage application and issues TRID disclosures within regulatory timelines. Mortgage Loan Originator owns the application and disclosure stages of the mortgage flow.

**Also known as:** MLO, Mortgage Consultant.

- Mortgage Loan Originator mentions Issue Loan Estimate.

## Details
Mortgage Loan Originator captures the URLA/Form 1003 application, authorizes the credit pull and ensures application completeness and KYC capture. The role delivers the TRID Loan Estimate disclosing terms and fees within three business days of application, observing fee tolerances and retaining proof of disclosure delivery. Mortgage Loan Originator is licensed under the SAFE Act and hands the file to the Loan Processor for property and title diligence and to the Mortgage Closer for closing and funding.
