# Collateral Appraisal

**Definition.** Collateral Appraisal is the independent valuation establishing the market value of pledged collateral. Collateral Appraisal is the valuation artifact used to size and secure an exposure.

**Also known as:** Property Appraisal, Valuation Report.

## Relationships
- Collateral Appraisal is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Collateral Appraisal mentions Mortgage Origination.
- Collateral Appraisal mentions Order Property Appraisal.
- Collateral Appraisal mentions Evaluate Collateral.

## Details
Collateral Appraisal records the appraised market value of the property or asset, the valuation method and comparables, the effective date and the appraiser's identity and credentials. It is produced at Order Property Appraisal in the Mortgage Origination flow under appraiser-independence and valuation-standard controls and is also consumed at Evaluate Collateral in the Credit Underwriting flow. The appraised value drives loan-to-value calculation, collateral adequacy and the security position supporting the credit.
