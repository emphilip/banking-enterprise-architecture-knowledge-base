# Credit Report

**Definition.** Credit Report is the bureau report of a borrower's credit history and score used to assess character and capacity during underwriting. Credit Report is the key bureau input artifact in the credit-assessment flow.

**Also known as:** Bureau Report, Credit File.

## Relationships
- Credit Report is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Credit Report mentions Credit Underwriting.
- Credit Report mentions Pull Credit Report.
- Credit Report mentions Risk & Affordability Assessment.

## Details
Credit Report contains the borrower's identifying information, tradelines and account balances, payment history and delinquencies, public records, recent credit inquiries, and one or more credit scores. It is obtained from a credit bureau under a permissible purpose with an audit trail, ordered at Pull Credit Report in the Credit Underwriting flow. The Credit Underwriter uses it to score credit risk, derive a risk grade and inform the affordability and policy assessment, and it underpins fair-lending and adverse-action requirements.
