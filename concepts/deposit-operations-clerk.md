# Deposit Operations Clerk

**Definition.** Deposit Operations Clerk is the operations role that executes deposit account servicing across opening, maintenance and closure, including signature-card capture, static-data changes and final balance settlement. Deposit Operations Clerk performs the hands-on processing steps in deposit and account flows.

**Also known as:** Deposit Ops Clerk, Account Servicing Clerk.

## Relationships
- Deposit Operations Clerk is related to the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Operations Clerk mentions Account Maintenance.
- Deposit Operations Clerk mentions Account Closure.
- Deposit Operations Clerk mentions Capture Signature Card.

## Details
Deposit Operations Clerk owns the manual and assisted tasks in deposit servicing: capturing the Signature Card and operating mandate at opening, applying address and detail amendments, processing stop payments and standing instructions, posting interest and fee changes, and disbursing residual balances at closure. Work is performed under dual control on static-data changes and signatory verification on mandate amendments, with a change-in-terms notice issued under Reg DD when account terms move. Deposit Operations Clerk hands escheatment cases to the Unclaimed Property Officer when accounts reach dormancy.
