# Financial Advisor

**Definition.** Financial Advisor is the client-facing advisory role that profiles a client, assesses suitability and best interest, formulates the investment plan and asset allocation, and produces and agrees the Investment Policy Statement in the wealth advisory flow.

**Also known as:** Wealth Advisor, Investment Adviser.

## Relationships
- Financial Advisor is related to the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Financial Advisor mentions Financial Planning.
- Financial Advisor mentions Capture Client Goals.

## Details
Financial Advisor conducts the fact find, gathering client goals, financial situation, knowledge and experience, then determines risk tolerance and capacity for loss to produce the Risk Profile. Working within MiFID II / ESMA suitability rules and the FINRA Reg BI care, disclosure and conflict-of-interest obligations, Financial Advisor sets a target asset allocation, identifies suitable products, runs the suitability check and documents the recommendation. Financial Advisor drafts, presents and obtains client agreement to the Investment Policy Statement, delivers the suitability report and required disclosures, and maintains records evidencing the advice given.
