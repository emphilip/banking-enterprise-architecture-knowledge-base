# Complaint Record

**Definition.** Complaint Record is the case record of a complaint with reference, category, investigation findings, outcome and redress. Complaint Record is the working artifact carried through the Complaint Handling flow.

**Also known as:** Complaint Case, Complaint File.

## Relationships
- Complaint Record is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Record mentions Log Complaint.
- Complaint Record mentions Complaint Intake & Triage.
- Complaint Record mentions Complaint Handling.

## Details
Complaint Record is created when a complaint is logged and accumulates the category, eligibility check, investigation findings, redress decision and final response as the Complaints Officer works the case. It is retained after closure and feeds root-cause analysis and complaints MI conducted by the Quality Assurance Reviewer.
