# ATM Cash Forecast

**Definition.** ATM Cash Forecast is the per-terminal forecast of cash demand that drives cash orders and load plans for the ATM estate. ATM Cash Forecast is the output artifact of forecasting that opens the ATM cash supply chain flow.

**Also known as:** ATM Demand Forecast, Cash Load Forecast.

## Relationships
- ATM Cash Forecast is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- ATM Cash Forecast mentions Forecast ATM Demand.
- ATM Cash Forecast mentions Plan Cash Order.
- ATM Cash Forecast is related to ATM Cash Management.

## Details
ATM Cash Forecast is produced from usage history and seasonality for each terminal and is the basis for converting demand into cash orders and per-terminal load plans by denomination. It feeds armoured cash-in-transit scheduling and replenishment, and is governed by forecast model governance and idle-cash limits that trade off availability and cash-out risk against the carrying cost of cash held in machines.
