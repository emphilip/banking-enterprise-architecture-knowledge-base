# Bank Account Statement

**Definition.** Bank Account Statement is the periodic statement of account activity, balances and required disclosures delivered to a deposit customer. Bank Account Statement is the primary output artifact of the statement-generation flow.

**Also known as:** Account Statement, Periodic Statement.

## Relationships
- Bank Account Statement is related to the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Bank Account Statement mentions Render Statement Output.
- Bank Account Statement mentions Statement Delivery.

## Details
Bank Account Statement lists opening and closing balances, posted debits and credits, interest credited, fees charged and the cycle period, together with Reg E error-resolution and Reg DD interest and fee disclosures. Bank Account Statement is assembled from reconciled ledger activity, rendered to print, PDF or e-statement format per delivery preference, delivered and then archived for retention and self-service retrieval. Delivery of Bank Account Statement opens the customer's window to report errors or unauthorized transactions.
