# ATM Replenishment Record

**Definition.** ATM Replenishment Record is the record of a cash-in-transit replenishment, capturing expected versus actual loaded cash by denomination for each terminal. ATM Replenishment Record is the output artifact of replenishment that feeds reconciliation in the ATM cash supply chain flow.

**Also known as:** Cash Load Record, ATM Load Sheet.

## Relationships
- ATM Replenishment Record is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- ATM Replenishment Record mentions Replenish ATM Cash.
- ATM Replenishment Record mentions Reconcile ATM Cash.
- ATM Replenishment Record is related to ATM Cash Management.

## Details
ATM Replenishment Record is created when cassettes are loaded under dual custody and the cash-in-transit carrier confirms or adjusts expected against actual loaded amounts by denomination. It is the source document for daily reconciliation of dispensed cash, drawn from electronic-journal and host records, against loaded cash and the ATM general ledger, and it supports variance detection and the chain-of-custody and dual-custody controls over terminal cash.
