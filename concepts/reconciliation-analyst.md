# Reconciliation Analyst

**Definition.** Reconciliation Analyst is the role that matches bank statements to internal ledger records and investigates the breaks that result during reconciliation and settlement. Reconciliation Analyst surfaces, classifies and drives resolution of unmatched items.

**Also known as:** Recon Analyst, Reconciliations Specialist.

## Relationships
- Reconciliation Analyst is related to the Payments domain.
- Reconciliation Analyst mentions Reconciliation & Settlement.
- Reconciliation Analyst mentions Match Transactions.
- Reconciliation Analyst mentions Investigate Break.
- Reconciliation Analyst is related to Account Reconciliation.

## Details
Reconciliation Analyst loads nostro and clearing statements, auto-matches entries to internal payment and ledger records using references and tolerances, and identifies discrepancies as reconciliation breaks. The role then investigates each break, raises investigation messages or recalls, and routes items to owners for resolution under segregation-of-duties controls.
