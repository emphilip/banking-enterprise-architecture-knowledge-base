# Stress Test Result

**Definition.** Stress Test Result is the output of a stress-testing cycle, presenting projected losses, revenues and capital ratios under each scenario together with the capital plan.

**Also known as:** Stress Result, Capital Projection Result.

## Relationships
- Stress Test Result is related to the Risk Management domain.
- Stress Test Result mentions Project Capital Impact.
- Stress Test Result mentions Stress Testing.

## Details
Stress Test Result quantifies, for each Stress Scenario, the projected pre-provision net revenue, credit and trading losses, the path of regulatory capital ratios (such as CET1) across the planning horizon, and the minimum (trough) ratio relative to supervisory minima and buffers. It documents the assumptions, model coverage and any planned management actions, and underpins the capital adequacy assessment and the board-approved capital plan submitted under CCAR/DFAST. Stress Test Result is a key input to risk reporting and to recalibrating risk appetite and limits, and supervisors expect a clear documentation trail linking scenarios, validated models, data and the resulting capital conclusions.
