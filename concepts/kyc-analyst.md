# KYC Analyst

**Definition.** KYC Analyst is the compliance and operations role that performs identity verification, screening and CDD/EDD during onboarding to assign a customer risk rating. KYC Analyst owns the due-diligence steps of the Customer Onboarding flow.

**Also known as:** KYC Officer, Due Diligence Analyst.

## Relationships
- KYC Analyst is related to the Customer Management domain.
- KYC Analyst mentions Customer Onboarding.
- KYC Analyst mentions Verify Identity.
- KYC Analyst mentions Screen Customer.
- KYC Analyst mentions Assess Customer Risk.

## Details
KYC Analyst proves applicant identity through document and biometric checks, screens against sanctions, PEP and adverse-media lists, and performs CDD with EDD for high-risk customers before assigning a risk rating. The role escalates screening hits and high-risk cases, then returns the cleared case to the Onboarding Specialist for the approval decision.
