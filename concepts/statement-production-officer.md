# Statement Production Officer

**Definition.** Statement Production Officer is the role responsible for producing, rendering and delivering periodic deposit account statements within required disclosure rules. Statement Production Officer drives the statement run from cycle determination through delivery and archival.

**Also known as:** Statement Officer, Statement Production Clerk.

## Relationships
- Statement Production Officer is related to the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Statement Production Officer mentions Statement Generation.
- Statement Production Officer mentions Assemble Statement Document.
- Statement Production Officer mentions Render Statement Output.

## Details
Statement Production Officer determines the statement cycle population, extracts postings and balances, composes statement content with required Reg E and Reg DD periodic disclosures, renders print, PDF and e-statement formats per delivery preference, and archives output for retention and self-service retrieval. Cycle consistency is held within the regulatory four-day window, and electronic delivery requires prior consent. Statement Production Officer also issues the final statement evidencing a zero balance during account closure.
