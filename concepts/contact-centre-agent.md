# Contact Centre Agent

**Definition.** Contact Centre Agent is the contact-centre representative who authenticates callers, handles and resolves inbound interactions, and completes wrap-up and quality work. Contact Centre Agent is the front-line operator of the inbound contact-centre interaction flow.

**Also known as:** Call Centre Agent, Customer Service Representative.

## Relationships
- Contact Centre Agent is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Contact Centre Agent mentions Contact Centre Interaction.
- Contact Centre Agent mentions Authenticate Caller.
- Contact Centre Agent mentions Handle Customer Interaction.
- Contact Centre Agent mentions Wrap Up Interaction.

## Details
Contact Centre Agent works the Contact Centre Interaction flow after IVR capture and skills-based routing: authenticating the caller with knowledge factors or voice biometrics, handling and resolving the interaction with agent-assist and customer context, and completing after-call work such as disposition, notes and the interaction record. The role operates under call recording and consent controls, quality-assurance scorecards, and average-handle-time and first-contact-resolution targets.
