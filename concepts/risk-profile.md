# Risk Profile

**Definition.** Risk Profile is the record of a client's risk tolerance and capacity for loss, together with knowledge and experience, used to drive suitability decisions and asset allocation in the wealth flow.

**Also known as:** Investor Risk Profile, Risk Tolerance Profile.

## Relationships
- Risk Profile is related to the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Risk Profile mentions Financial Planning.
- Risk Profile mentions Determine Risk Tolerance.

## Details
Risk Profile captures the client's willingness to take risk (risk attitude/tolerance) and ability to bear loss (risk capacity), derived from a questionnaire and the fact find covering goals, time horizon, income, assets, liabilities and liquidity needs, plus investment knowledge and experience. Risk Profile is produced by determining risk tolerance during planning or onboarding and is a mandatory input to the suitability and best-interest assessment under MiFID II / ESMA and FINRA Reg BI. Risk Profile shapes the target asset allocation set in the Investment Policy Statement and must be kept current; material life events trigger a refresh before further advice or trading.
