# Service Advisor

**Definition.** Service Advisor is the service-agent role that captures, authenticates, triages, fulfils and closes customer service requests. Service Advisor owns the Service Request Handling flow end to end.

**Also known as:** Customer Service Agent, Service Desk Agent.

## Relationships
- Service Advisor is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Service Advisor mentions Service Request Handling.
- Service Advisor mentions Capture Request.
- Service Advisor mentions Authenticate Requester.
- Service Advisor mentions Fulfil Request.

## Details
Service Advisor captures requests from any channel, verifies the requester's identity and entitlement, classifies and prioritises against SLA, fulfils the change in the system of record and confirms resolution before closing the ticket. The role escalates or converts requests to a complaint where eligibility or approval checks require it.
