# Complaints Officer

**Definition.** Complaints Officer is the role that owns a complaint case, acknowledging, triaging, investigating, deciding redress and issuing the final response. Complaints Officer drives the Complaint Handling flow from logging through resolution.

**Also known as:** Complaint Handler, Dispute Resolution Officer.

## Relationships
- Complaints Officer is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Complaints Officer mentions Complaint Handling.
- Complaints Officer mentions Log Complaint.
- Complaints Officer mentions Investigate Complaint.
- Complaints Officer mentions Issue Final Response.

## Details
Complaints Officer records inbound complaints, sends prompt acknowledgements within SLA, classifies and prioritises cases, investigates impartially, decides whether to uphold and what redress applies, and issues the final response with Ombudsman referral rights. The role hands closed cases to the Quality Assurance Reviewer for root-cause analysis and reporting.
