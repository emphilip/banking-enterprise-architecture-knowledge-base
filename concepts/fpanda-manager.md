# FP&A Manager

**Definition.** FP&A Manager leads financial planning and analysis, owning the operating budget and rolling forecast and the production of the management accounts and board management-information pack. FP&A Manager turns closed-ledger actuals into forward-looking decision support for the finance and treasury function.

**Also known as:** Head of FP&A, Planning & Analysis Manager.

## Relationships
- FP&A Manager is related to the Finance & Treasury domain.
- FP&A Manager mentions Management Reporting.
- FP&A Manager mentions Compile Management Accounts.
- FP&A Manager mentions Present Board Pack.

## Details
FP&A Manager owns the Management Reporting process: constructing and approving the annual operating budget, maintaining rolling forecasts, capturing actuals against plan, analysing variances and assembling the Management Accounts with segment KPIs and management commentary. The role presents the board MI pack to the CFO and board, obtains sign-off and steers performance management. FP&A Manager governs the integrity of budget assumptions, the actual-versus-budget reconciliation and the variance-commentary process, bridging the controllership data and executive decision-making.
