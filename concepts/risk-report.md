# Risk Report

**Definition.** Risk Report is the periodic board and committee report aggregating risk exposures, limit utilisation and the bank's profile versus appetite across all material risk types, produced under BCBS 239.

**Also known as:** Board Risk Report, Risk MI Pack.

## Relationships
- Risk Report is related to the Risk Management domain.
- Risk Report mentions Compile Risk Report.
- Risk Report mentions Risk Reporting.

## Details
Risk Report covers all material risk types (credit, market, liquidity, operational and others), presenting current exposures against approved limits and appetite, limit-breach status and escalations, key risk indicators, stress-test results and forward-looking commentary. Under BCBS 239 it must be accurate, complete, clear and useful, produced with adequate frequency and supported by reconciled data with documented lineage. Risk Report is the primary instrument through which the board and risk committee oversee the firm's risk profile and through which regulatory risk returns are framed. Its quality depends on upstream data aggregation, reconciliation to source and finance systems, and the data-governance controls that ensure no material risk is omitted or misstated.
