# Customer Activated Event

**Definition.** Customer Activated Event is the business event signalling that a new customer's profile and product have been activated. Customer Activated Event marks the completion of the Customer Onboarding flow.

**Also known as:** Onboarding Completed Event, Account Activated.

## Relationships
- Customer Activated Event is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Activated Event causes Activate Customer.
- Customer Activated Event mentions Customer Onboarding.
- Customer Activated Event is related to Account Setup & Activation.

## Details
Customer Activated Event is emitted once channel access and the product are live, welcome communications are sent and the customer is enrolled in ongoing monitoring. It confirms the onboarded customer is fully provisioned and can trigger downstream servicing, marketing and relationship-management activity.
