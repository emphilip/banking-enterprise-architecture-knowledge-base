# Account Opening Form

**Definition.** Account Opening Form is the application document capturing applicant details, product selection and consent at the start of deposit account opening. Account Opening Form is the entry artifact that initiates the account-opening flow.

**Also known as:** Account Application Form, New Account Form.

## Relationships
- Account Opening Form is related to the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Opening Form mentions Capture Account Application.
- Account Opening Form mentions Application & Identity Capture.

## Details
Account Opening Form records the applicant's legal name, date of birth, residential address and taxpayer identification number required under the Customer Identification Program, plus the selected deposit product, ownership type and beneficiary or signatory details. Account Opening Form captures consent for identity verification and ChexSystems inquiry, and feeds CIP collection and applicant verification. Completeness and product-eligibility checks are applied before the account is established on the core ledger.
