# FTP Curve Approved Event

**Definition.** FTP Curve Approved Event is the business event marking ALCO approval of the funds transfer pricing methodology and curve, enabling transfer-rate application. FTP Curve Approved Event gates the application of transfer prices in the finance and treasury function.

**Also known as:** Curve Approved Event, ALCO Approval Event.

## Relationships
- FTP Curve Approved Event is related to the Finance & Treasury domain.
- FTP Curve Approved Event causes Apply Transfer Rates.
- FTP Curve Approved Event mentions Approve FTP Methodology.

## Details
FTP Curve Approved Event fires when ALCO signs off the matched-maturity transfer-pricing methodology, the constructed FTP Curve and the behavioural assumptions presented for the period. The event records the approved curve version and effective date and authorises its use across the balance sheet. FTP Curve Approved Event is the trigger that releases the Apply Transfer Rates step within Funds Transfer Pricing, allowing transfer charges and credits to be assigned to assets and liabilities and net interest margin to be attributed to business units under ALCO governance.
