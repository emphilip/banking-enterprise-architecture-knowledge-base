# Onboarding Specialist

**Definition.** Onboarding Specialist is the front-office role that owns the onboarding case, capturing the application, driving it through approval and activating the new customer. Onboarding Specialist coordinates the Customer Onboarding flow end to end across intake, decisioning and activation.

**Also known as:** Onboarding Officer, New Accounts Officer.

## Relationships
- Onboarding Specialist is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Onboarding Specialist mentions Customer Onboarding.
- Onboarding Specialist mentions Receive Application.
- Onboarding Specialist mentions Approve Onboarding.
- Onboarding Specialist mentions Activate Customer.

## Details
Onboarding Specialist works the start and end of the Customer Onboarding flow, taking in the application, capturing consent, collecting documents, making the accept/decline decision under four-eyes for elevated risk, and creating and activating the customer record. The role hands the case to the KYC Analyst for identity, screening and due-diligence steps and resumes ownership at approval and activation.
