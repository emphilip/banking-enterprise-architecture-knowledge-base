# Teller

**Definition.** Teller is the branch counter staff member who serves customers at the counter, executes cash, cheque and account transactions, and balances a cash drawer at end of day. Teller is the front-line operator of the in-branch counter servicing flow.

**Also known as:** Bank Teller, Counter Clerk.

## Relationships
- Teller is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Teller mentions Branch Operations.
- Teller mentions Serve Counter Customer.
- Teller mentions Process Teller Transaction.
- Teller mentions Balance Teller Drawer.

## Details
Teller works the counter through the Branch Operations flow: identifying the customer, capturing the requested transaction, posting cash or cheque entries to the core banking system, and buying or selling cash with the vault to keep the drawer within approved limits. The role enforces identity verification, transaction limits and dual control on large or override items, and reconciles the drawer to system balances at close of day, escalating variances for investigation.
