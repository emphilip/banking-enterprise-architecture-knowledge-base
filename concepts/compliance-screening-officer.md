# Compliance Screening Officer

**Definition.** Compliance Screening Officer is the compliance role that adjudicates sanctions and watchlist hits raised against payments before they are released. Compliance Screening Officer holds, escalates or clears flagged payments to keep payment processing compliant.

**Also known as:** Sanctions Officer, Screening Analyst.

## Relationships
- Compliance Screening Officer is related to the Payments domain.
- Compliance Screening Officer mentions Payment Processing.
- Compliance Screening Officer mentions Screen Sanctions.
- Compliance Screening Officer is related to Sanctions Screening.
- Compliance Screening Officer is related to Payment Screening.

## Details
Compliance Screening Officer acts at the sanctions step of the Payment Processing flow, reviewing debtor, creditor and message data against watchlists. The role applies the sanctions screening control, deciding whether a hit is a true match that must be held and escalated or a false positive that can be cleared so the payment can continue.
