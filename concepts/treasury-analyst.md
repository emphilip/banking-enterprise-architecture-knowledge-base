# Treasury Analyst

**Definition.** Treasury Analyst runs daily cash and liquidity operations, aggregating nostro and central-bank balances, projecting the intraday and short-term position, monitoring liquidity buffers and reconciling end-of-day cash. Treasury Analyst keeps the bank within its liquidity limits across the finance and treasury function.

**Also known as:** Liquidity Analyst, Treasury Operations Analyst.

## Relationships
- Treasury Analyst is related to the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Treasury Analyst mentions Cash & Liquidity Management.
- Treasury Analyst mentions Aggregate Cash Balances.
- Treasury Analyst mentions Report Liquidity Position.

## Details
Treasury Analyst executes the Cash & Liquidity Management process: aggregating posted nostro and central-bank balances to set the opening position, projecting the intraday and short-term Liquidity Position from known and forecast payment flows, assessing the position against LCR / NSFR buffers and internal limits, deciding and executing funding or placement actions, and reconciling end-of-day cash movements. The role produces the intraday and daily liquidity report for ALCO and the treasurer, enforces nostro reconciliation, intraday liquidity monitoring and funding-authority limits, and escalates limit breaches to the treasurer.
