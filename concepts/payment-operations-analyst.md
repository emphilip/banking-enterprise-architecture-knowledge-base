# Payment Operations Analyst

**Definition.** Payment Operations Analyst is the operations role that captures, validates, authorizes and routes payment instructions as they move through payment processing. Payment Operations Analyst keeps straight-through processing healthy and intervenes when a payment requires manual repair.

**Also known as:** Payment Ops Analyst, Payments Operator.

## Relationships
- Payment Operations Analyst is related to the Payments domain.
- Payment Operations Analyst mentions Payment Processing.
- Payment Operations Analyst mentions Receive Payment Instruction.
- Payment Operations Analyst mentions Validate Payment.
- Payment Operations Analyst is related to Payment Execution.

## Details
Payment Operations Analyst operates the early stages of the Payment Processing flow, taking in instructions, validating fields and accounts, applying authorization decisions, and selecting the appropriate clearing rail. The role enforces controls such as duplicate detection and limit checks, and escalates exceptions to specialist roles when sanctions hits or breaks arise.
