# Card Statement

**Definition.** Card Statement is the periodic statement of card transactions, balances and minimum payment delivered to the cardholder. Card Statement is the artifact that documents a billing cycle and anchors the cardholder's dispute and error-resolution rights.

**Also known as:** Credit Card Statement, Card Billing Statement.

## Relationships
- Card Statement is related to the Cards domain.
- Card Statement mentions Dispute Resolution.
- Card Statement mentions Register Dispute Claim.

## Details
Card Statement lists posted transactions for the cycle with dates, merchants and amounts, the opening and closing balances, payments and credits, fees and interest (APR/finance charge), the new balance, minimum payment and payment due date, plus Reg Z billing-rights and error-notice disclosures. It is produced at cycle close from cleared and posted transactions and delivered physically or electronically. Card Statement is the reference a cardholder uses to identify an erroneous or unauthorised charge, and a dispute against a statement item opens the Chargeback Case under the Reg Z 60-day billing-error window.
