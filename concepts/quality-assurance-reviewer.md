# Quality Assurance Reviewer

**Definition.** Quality Assurance Reviewer is the second-line role that conducts root-cause analysis, MI reporting and quality assurance over resolved complaints. Quality Assurance Reviewer owns the closure and learning steps of the Complaint Handling flow.

**Also known as:** QA Analyst, Complaints QA Reviewer.

## Relationships
- Quality Assurance Reviewer is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Quality Assurance Reviewer mentions Complaint Handling.
- Quality Assurance Reviewer mentions Conduct Root Cause.
- Quality Assurance Reviewer mentions Close Complaint.
- Quality Assurance Reviewer is related to Complaint Closure & Learning.

## Details
Quality Assurance Reviewer analyses drivers across complaints to identify systemic root causes, raises improvement actions and produces complaints MI and regulatory reporting. The role confirms resolution quality and supports closure and record retention after the Complaints Officer issues the final response.
