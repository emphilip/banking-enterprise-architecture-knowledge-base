# Portfolio Manager

**Definition.** Portfolio Manager is the discretionary investment role that maintains client portfolios to the Investment Policy Statement, detects drift from target allocation, and generates and approves rebalance plans and orders in the wealth flow.

**Also known as:** PM, Discretionary Manager.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Manager is related to the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Portfolio Manager mentions Portfolio Rebalancing.
- Portfolio Manager mentions Measure Portfolio Drift.

## Details
Portfolio Manager measures current holdings against the Investment Policy Statement target allocation, evaluates drift against tolerance bands, and decides whether to rebalance. When a band is breached, Portfolio Manager generates a rebalance proposal of buys and sells, weighing tax lots, wash-sale rules and transaction costs, then runs pre-trade compliance against mandate and IPS limits and restricted lists. Portfolio Manager obtains four-eyes approval of the compliant plan and raises the resulting orders (Order Tickets) into Trade Order Management for execution. Portfolio Manager operates under discretionary authority granted through the executed advisory mandate.
