# Liquidity Position

**Definition.** Liquidity Position is the projected intraday and short-term cash and liquidity position across nostro and central-bank accounts measured against regulatory and internal buffers. Liquidity Position is the central decision artifact of treasury cash operations in the finance and treasury function.

**Also known as:** Cash Position, Intraday Position.

## Relationships
- Liquidity Position is related to the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Liquidity Position mentions Project Liquidity Position.
- Liquidity Position mentions Intraday Liquidity Positioning.

## Details
Liquidity Position aggregates posted nostro and central-bank balances with known and forecast payment inflows and outflows by value date to project the running intraday position and the short-term funding gap. It is assessed against LCR and NSFR buffers, high-quality-liquid-asset stock and internal limits to surface surpluses and shortfalls, and underpins funding, placement and buffer decisions. The position is monitored throughout the day for intraday liquidity management per BCBS 248 monitoring tools, reconciled at end of day against nostro statements, and reported to ALCO and the treasurer.
