# Trade Finance Officer

**Definition.** Trade Finance Officer is the specialist role that issues, advises, examines and settles documentary and standby credits under UCP 600 and ISP98 rules. Trade Finance Officer owns the trade-finance flow end to end.

**Also known as:** Trade Finance Specialist, LC Officer.

## Details
Trade Finance Officer receives the applicant's documentary or standby credit request, checks the applicant's credit limit and collateral, screens counterparties for sanctions, and drafts and issues the credit for transmission to the advising bank. The role advises and optionally confirms the credit to the beneficiary, then examines presented documents on their face against the credit terms within five banking days under the UCP 600 Article 14 standard, and honors compliant documents or refuses on discrepancies. Trade Finance Officer applies four-eyes on issuance and payment and AML controls on settlement.
