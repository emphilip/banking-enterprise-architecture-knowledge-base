# Consent Record

**Definition.** Consent Record is the captured record of customer consents, e-signature and marketing opt-ins with timestamp and version. Consent Record is produced during the Customer Onboarding flow.

**Also known as:** Consent Artifact, Opt-In Record.

## Relationships
- Consent Record is related to the Customer Management domain.
- Consent Record mentions Capture Consent.
- Consent Record mentions Application Intake & Capture.
- Consent Record mentions Customer Onboarding.

## Details
Consent Record stores the explicit consents to terms, privacy and data-use notices, marketing opt-ins and the e-signature gathered when the Onboarding Specialist presents disclosures. It provides the timestamped, versioned audit trail required for downstream data processing and communication preferences after the customer is onboarded.
