# Signature Card

**Definition.** Signature Card is the record of authorized signatories and the operating mandate for a deposit account. Signature Card establishes who may transact on the account and under what authority within deposit servicing.

**Also known as:** Signatory Mandate, Account Mandate Card.

## Relationships
- Signature Card is related to the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Signature Card mentions Capture Signature Card.
- Signature Card mentions Account Setup & Funding.

## Details
Signature Card captures each authorized signatory's specimen signature, identifying details and role, the operating mandate such as sole, joint or two-to-sign authority, and any limits or restrictions on the account. Signature Card binds the account terms and conditions to the named parties and is the reference for verifying authority on later maintenance, stop-payment and closure requests. Updates to Signature Card flow through Mandate & Signatory Maintenance under signatory verification and dual control.
