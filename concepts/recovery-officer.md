# Recovery Officer

**Definition.** Recovery Officer is the late-stage collections role that handles escalation, restructure, charge-off and post-charge-off recovery or agency placement. Recovery Officer takes accounts that have not cured in early collections.

**Also known as:** Recoveries Officer, Recovery Specialist.

## Relationships
- Recovery Officer is related to the Lending & Credit domain.
- Recovery Officer mentions Loan Collections.
- Recovery Officer mentions Late-Stage Recovery.
- Recovery Officer mentions Escalate To Recovery.
- Recovery Officer mentions Charge Off Debt.

## Details
Recovery Officer escalates persistent arrears to restructure, charge-off or settlement decisioning under charge-off policy and settlement authority. The role writes the debt off as uncollectable, typically after a threshold such as 180 days past due, applies the relevant accounting controls and places the account for internal recovery or external agency collection. Recovery Officer manages collateral and repossession controls where security is held and takes over from the Collections Agent once early treatment has failed.
