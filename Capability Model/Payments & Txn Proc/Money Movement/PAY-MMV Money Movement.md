# Money Movement (PAY-MMV)

**L0:** [[PAY Payments & Txn Proc.|Payments & Txn Proc.]]

Money Movement covers the payment rails the bank uses to move funds — ACH, wires, real-time rails, e-transfer, and bill pay.
