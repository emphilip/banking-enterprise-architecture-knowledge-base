# Txn Processing (PAY-TXN)

**L0:** [[PAY Payments & Txn Proc.|Payments & Txn Proc.]]

Txn Processing covers authorization, clearing, and the chargeback, reversal, and exception handling of card and payment transactions.

|---|---|---|
| PAY-TXN-01 | [[PAY-TXN-01 Authorization\|Authorization]] | Authorizing card and payment transactions |

| PAY-TXN-03 | [[PAY-TXN-03 Clearing\|Clearing]] | Clearing of transactions with networks |
| PAY-TXN-04 | [[PAY-TXN-04 Chargebacks\|Chargebacks]] | Processing chargebacks on disputed transactions |
| PAY-TXN-05 | [[PAY-TXN-05 Reversals\|Reversals]] | Reversing transactions |
