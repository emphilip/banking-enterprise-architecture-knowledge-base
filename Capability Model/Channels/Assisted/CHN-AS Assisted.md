# Assisted (CHN-AS)

**L0:** [[CHN Channels|Channels]]

Assisted covers human-supported channels where a customer interacts with a banker or agent. They handle complex needs, exceptions, and high-touch sales and service that self-serve cannot resolve.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| CHN-AS-01 | [[CHN-AS-01 Branch\|Branch]] | Physical branch network for in-person advice, cash services, complex transactions, and identity-sensitive servicing |
| CHN-AS-02 | [[CHN-AS-02 Contact Centre\|Contact Centre]] | Inbound and outbound telephone servicing and sales staffed by agents — the primary escalation path from digital channels |
| CHN-AS-03 | [[CHN-AS-03 Live Chat\|Live Chat]] | Real-time text chat with a human agent embedded in web and mobile channels |
| CHN-AS-04 | [[CHN-AS-04 Video Chat\|Video Chat]] | Live video sessions with a banker for advice, co-browsing, and remote identity-sensitive interactions |
| CHN-AS-05 | [[CHN-AS-05 Email\|Email]] | Asynchronous secure email and message correspondence with the bank for servicing and documentation |
| CHN-AS-06 | [[CHN-AS-06 SMS\|SMS]] | Two-way text messaging for servicing interactions, confirmations, and agent follow-up |
