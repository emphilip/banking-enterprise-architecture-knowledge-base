# Channels (CHN)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Channels is the set of customer-facing touchpoints through which a bank delivers products, servicing, and engagement. It spans unassisted digital surfaces and human-assisted channels, and is the delivery layer that [[INT Interaction Mgmt.|Interaction Mgmt.]] orchestrates and that [[IAA Identity, Auth & Access|Identity, Auth & Access]] secures.

## L1 Capability Groups

| ID | Capability Group | L2s | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| CHN-SS | [[CHN-SS Self-Serve\|Self-Serve]] | 6 | Self-Serve covers the unassisted digital and self-operated channels where customers transact without a human agent |
| CHN-AS | [[CHN-AS Assisted\|Assisted]] | 6 | Assisted covers human-supported channels where a customer interacts with a banker or agent |
