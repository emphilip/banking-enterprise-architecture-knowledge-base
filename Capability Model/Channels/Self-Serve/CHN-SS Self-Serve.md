# Self-Serve (CHN-SS)

**L0:** [[CHN Channels|Channels]]

Self-Serve covers the unassisted digital and self-operated channels where customers transact without a human agent. These are the primary, lowest-cost-to-serve surfaces for everyday banking and the entry points for most originations.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| CHN-SS-01 | [[CHN-SS-01 Public Web\|Public Web]] | The bank's public website and authenticated web banking — product discovery, applications, and full account servicing in a browser |
| CHN-SS-02 | [[CHN-SS-02 Mobile App\|Mobile App]] | Native iOS and Android banking app delivering account access, payments, alerts, and increasingly the primary day-to-day channel |
| CHN-SS-03 | [[CHN-SS-03 ATM\|ATM]] | Automated teller machines for cash withdrawal and deposit, balance inquiry, transfers, and limited servicing across the bank's fleet and shared networks |
| CHN-SS-04 | [[CHN-SS-04 IVR\|IVR]] | Interactive voice response that automates routine phone servicing such as balances, payments, and card activation, and routes callers before agent handoff |
| CHN-SS-05 | [[CHN-SS-05 Chatbot\|Chatbot]] | Automated conversational self-service on web and mobile that answers queries and completes simple tasks, escalating to an agent when needed |
| CHN-SS-06 | [[CHN-SS-06 Virtual Asst.\|Virtual Asst.]] | AI-driven virtual assistant offering proactive, personalized help and guided task completion across digital channels |
