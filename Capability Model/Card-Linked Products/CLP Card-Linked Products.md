# Card-Linked Products (CLP)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Card-Linked Products covers the value-added capabilities that ride on top of card products: PIN management, loyalty programs, and rewards. These differentiate card propositions and deepen cardholder engagement, and are configured through the [[00 - Card Issuing and Offer Management Index|Card Issuing & Offer Management]] flows.

## L1 Capability Groups

| ID | Capability Group | L2s | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| CLP-PIN | [[CLP-PIN PIN Management\|PIN Management]] | 8 | PIN Management covers the secure issuance, reset, change, verification, and audit of card PINs |
| CLP-LOY | [[CLP-LOY Loyalty\|Loyalty]] | 8 | Loyalty covers multi-tender and tiered loyalty programs, affinity and partner arrangements, and the analytics and member management behind them |
| CLP-RWD | [[CLP-RWD Rewards\|Rewards]] | 8 | Rewards covers how cardholders earn, accrue, track, and redeem rewards, including presentment and merchant offers |
