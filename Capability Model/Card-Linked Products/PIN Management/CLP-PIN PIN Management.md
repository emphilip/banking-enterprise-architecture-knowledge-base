# PIN Management (CLP-PIN)

**L0:** [[CLP Card-Linked Products|Card-Linked Products]]

PIN Management covers the secure issuance, reset, change, verification, and audit of card PINs.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| CLP-PIN-01 | [[CLP-PIN-01 PIN Issuance\|PIN Issuance]] | Issuing PINs for new cards |
| CLP-PIN-02 | [[CLP-PIN-02 PIN Reset\|PIN Reset]] | Resetting a cardholder's PIN |
| CLP-PIN-03 | [[CLP-PIN-03 PIN Change\|PIN Change]] | Allowing a cardholder to change their PIN |
| CLP-PIN-04 | [[CLP-PIN-04 PIN Verification\|PIN Verification]] | Verifying PINs during transactions |
| CLP-PIN-05 | [[CLP-PIN-05 PIN Block\|PIN Block]] | Blocking PINs after failed attempts or on compromise |
| CLP-PIN-06 | [[CLP-PIN-06 Digital PIN\|Digital PIN]] | Delivering and managing PINs through digital channels |
| CLP-PIN-07 | [[CLP-PIN-07 PIN Encryption\|PIN Encryption]] | Encrypting and protecting PIN data end to end |
| CLP-PIN-08 | [[CLP-PIN-08 PIN Audit\|PIN Audit]] | Auditing PIN events and access for security and compliance |
