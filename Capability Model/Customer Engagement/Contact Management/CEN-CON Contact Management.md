# Contact Management (CEN-CON)

**L0:** [[CEN Customer Engagement|Customer Engagement]]

Contact Management governs how often and through which channels the bank reaches customers, with contact history and consent tracking.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| CEN-CON-01 | [[CEN-CON-01 Frequency\|Frequency]] | Managing contact frequency and over-contact rules |
| CEN-CON-02 | [[CEN-CON-02 Call Reasons\|Call Reasons]] | Capturing and categorizing reasons for contact |
| CEN-CON-03 | [[CEN-CON-03 Contact History\|Contact History]] | Maintaining a history of customer contacts across channels |
| CEN-CON-04 | [[CEN-CON-04 Channel Preference\|Channel Preference]] | Respecting the customer's preferred contact channels |
| CEN-CON-05 | [[CEN-CON-05 Outreach Mgmt.\|Outreach Mgmt.]] | Managing proactive outreach to customers |
| CEN-CON-06 | [[CEN-CON-06 Consent Tracking\|Consent Tracking]] | Tracking customer consents for contact and marketing |
