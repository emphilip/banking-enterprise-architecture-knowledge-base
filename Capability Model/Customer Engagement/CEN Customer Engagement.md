# Customer Engagement (CEN)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Customer Engagement owns the bank's view of and relationship with the customer: the customer information file, relationship and contact management, offers, content, experience, and accessibility. It is how the bank knows, communicates with, and tailors itself to each customer across the lifecycle.
