# Corporate Functions (CORP)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Corporate Functions is the horizontal supporting band on Slide 4 that underpins all thirteen customer-facing and enterprise L0 domains. Unlike the other domains, it is **not decomposed into L2 capabilities** on the source model — it is shown as a single band of enterprise-wide corporate functions. It is captured here as one note for completeness so the master model reflects the full slide.

## Corporate Functions (band members)

| Function | Scope |
|---|---|
| Business Strategy | Enterprise strategy, planning, and corporate development. |
| Product Mgmt. | Cross-enterprise product management and governance. |
| Project Mgmt. | Programme and project management / delivery (PMO). |
| HR & Talent | Human resources, talent, and people operations. |
| Legal | Legal counsel, contracts, and corporate governance. |
| Vendor / Partner / Supplier | Vendor, partner, and supplier management and procurement. |
| Facilities | Corporate real estate and facilities management. |
| Comms & Investor Rel. | Corporate communications and investor relations. |
| Corporate Responsibility (CRA) | Corporate responsibility and community reinvestment. |
