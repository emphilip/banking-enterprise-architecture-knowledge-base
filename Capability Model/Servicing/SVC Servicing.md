# Servicing (SVC)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Servicing covers the non-monetary and monetary maintenance of accounts after they are opened — from inquiries, statements, and account changes to fees, rate management, adjustments, and disputes. It is the largest day-to-day touchpoint with existing customers.

## L1 Capability Groups

| ID | Capability Group | L2s | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| SVC-NON | [[SVC-NON Non-Monetary\|Non-Monetary]] | 14 | Non-Monetary servicing covers account inquiries, maintenance, statements, card and account controls, and the many non-financial changes customers request |
| SVC-MON | [[SVC-MON Monetary\|Monetary]] | 14 | Monetary servicing covers the financial maintenance of accounts: rates and fees, adjustments, refunds, payment allocation, disputes, credit-limit changes, and interest and charge posting |
