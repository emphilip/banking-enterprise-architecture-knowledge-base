# Monetary (SVC-MON)

**L0:** [[SVC Servicing|Servicing]]

Monetary servicing covers the financial maintenance of accounts: rates and fees, adjustments, refunds, payment allocation, disputes, credit-limit changes, and interest and charge posting.
