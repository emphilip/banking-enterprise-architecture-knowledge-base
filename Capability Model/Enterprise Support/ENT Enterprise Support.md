# Enterprise Support (ENT)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Enterprise Support is the shared technology, data, and finance backbone that every customer-facing domain depends on. It holds the books of record, runs the bank's data and AI platforms, produces reporting and financials, and operates the infrastructure, documents, and integrations that bind the model together.
