# Books of Record (ENT-BOR)

**L0:** [[ENT Enterprise Support|Enterprise Support]]

Books of Record are the authoritative systems of record for customers, accounts, transactions, products, and reference data.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| ENT-BOR-01 | [[ENT-BOR-01 Customer BoR\|Customer BoR]] | The authoritative customer information file and golden customer record |
| ENT-BOR-02 | [[ENT-BOR-02 Acct. BoR\|Acct. BoR]] | The system of record for account master data and balances |
| ENT-BOR-03 | [[ENT-BOR-03 Txn BoR\|Txn BoR]] | The authoritative ledger of posted transactions |
| ENT-BOR-04 | [[ENT-BOR-04 Product BoR\|Product BoR]] | The system of record for product definitions and configuration |
| ENT-BOR-05 | [[ENT-BOR-05 Compliance BoR\|Compliance BoR]] | Authoritative records maintained for regulatory and compliance purposes |
| ENT-BOR-06 | [[ENT-BOR-06 Reference Data\|Reference Data]] | Shared reference and lookup data used across the enterprise |
