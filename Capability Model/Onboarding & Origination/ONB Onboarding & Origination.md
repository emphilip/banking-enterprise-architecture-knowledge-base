# Onboarding & Origination (ONB)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Onboarding & Origination covers every capability needed to take a prospect from expressed product interest to an opened, funded, documented, activated, and enrolled account or credit facility. It spans deposit, card, and lending products and is exercised end-to-end by the origination process flows in the companion [[00 - Onboarding and Origination Index|Origination & Onboarding]] library.
