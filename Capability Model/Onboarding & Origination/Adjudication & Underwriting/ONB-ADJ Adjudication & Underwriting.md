# Adjudication & Underwriting (ONB-ADJ)

**L0:** [[ONB Onboarding & Origination|Onboarding & Origination]]

Adjudication & Underwriting covers credit decisioning, the decision engine, bureau access, underwriting, and risk assessment that determine eligibility and approval.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| ONB-ADJ-01 | [[ONB-ADJ-01 Credit Decisioning\|Credit Decisioning]] | Producing eligibility and approval decisions for the applied product |
| ONB-ADJ-02 | [[ONB-ADJ-02 Instant Decision\|Instant Decision]] | Returning real-time automated decisions at the point of application |
| ONB-ADJ-03 | [[ONB-ADJ-03 Decision Engine\|Decision Engine]] | The centralized engine that owns eligibility and approval logic, invoked by the front end |
| ONB-ADJ-04 | [[ONB-ADJ-04 Bureau Access\|Bureau Access]] | Retrieving credit bureau data via soft and hard inquiries to support decisioning |
| ONB-ADJ-05 | [[ONB-ADJ-05 Underwriting\|Underwriting]] | Assessing applications, including manual underwriting of referred or documented cases |
| ONB-ADJ-06 | [[ONB-ADJ-06 Risk Assess.\|Risk Assess.]] | Evaluating applicant credit and fraud risk to inform decisions and terms |
