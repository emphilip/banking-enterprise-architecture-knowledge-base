# Account Setup & Fulfillment (ONB-ASF)

**L0:** [[ONB Onboarding & Origination|Onboarding & Origination]]

Account Setup & Fulfillment covers opening the account, funding it, and issuing and activating cards and plastics.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| ONB-ASF-01 | [[ONB-ASF-01 Account Opening\|Account Opening]] | Creating the account or credit facility in the books of record once approved |
| ONB-ASF-02 | [[ONB-ASF-02 Funding Account\|Funding Account]] | Disbursing funds or setting up funding and repayment for the new account |
| ONB-ASF-03 | [[ONB-ASF-03 Card Issuance\|Card Issuance]] | Issuing a card product to the new or existing customer |
| ONB-ASF-04 | [[ONB-ASF-04 Plastic Production\|Plastic Production]] | Physical card production and personalization for fulfillment |
| ONB-ASF-05 | [[ONB-ASF-05 Virtual - Instant Issue\|Virtual / Instant Issue]] | Provisioning a virtual or instantly-issued card for immediate use |
| ONB-ASF-06 | [[ONB-ASF-06 Card Activation\|Card Activation]] | Activating an issued card for transacting |
