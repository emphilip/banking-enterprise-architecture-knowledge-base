# AML / KYC / Compliance (ONB-AKC)

**L0:** [[ONB Onboarding & Origination|Onboarding & Origination]]

AML / KYC / Compliance covers the regulatory identity, screening, and reporting checks required to onboard a customer.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| ONB-AKC-01 | [[ONB-AKC-01 KYC Check\|KYC Check]] | Know-your-customer identity verification and customer due diligence at onboarding |
| ONB-AKC-02 | [[ONB-AKC-02 AML Check\|AML Check]] | Anti-money-laundering screening of applicants and onboarding activity |
| ONB-AKC-03 | [[ONB-AKC-03 Reg. Check\|Reg. Check]] | Regulatory eligibility and rule checks applicable to the product and jurisdiction |
| ONB-AKC-04 | [[ONB-AKC-04 Credit Check\|Credit Check]] | Compliance-driven credit checks supporting responsible-lending obligations |
| ONB-AKC-05 | [[ONB-AKC-05 Sanctions - OFAC\|Sanctions / OFAC]] | Screening applicants against sanctions and watchlists |
| ONB-AKC-06 | [[ONB-AKC-06 Enhanced Due Diligence\|Enhanced Due Diligence]] | Deeper investigation for higher-risk applicants flagged during screening |
| ONB-AKC-07 | [[ONB-AKC-07 SAR Filing\|SAR Filing]] | Filing suspicious activity and transaction reports with regulators where required |
| ONB-AKC-08 | [[ONB-AKC-08 ID Validation\|ID Validation]] | Validating identity documents and data authenticity during verification |
