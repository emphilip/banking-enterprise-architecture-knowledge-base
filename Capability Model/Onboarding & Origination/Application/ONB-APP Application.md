# Application (ONB-APP)

**L0:** [[ONB Onboarding & Origination|Onboarding & Origination]]

The Application group covers how the bank receives, captures, manages, and reviews a customer's request for a product, from the first entry point through a complete submitted application.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| ONB-APP-01 | [[ONB-APP-01 Intake\|Intake]] | Receiving the expression of product interest and initiating an application session, with entry-point, channel, and province gating |
| ONB-APP-02 | [[ONB-APP-02 App. Capture\|App. Capture]] | Collecting application data through guided, validated, step-based forms with in-flow consent and contact verification |
| ONB-APP-03 | [[ONB-APP-03 App. Mgmt.\|App. Mgmt.]] | Managing the lifecycle and state of an application across sessions, channels, and statuses |
| ONB-APP-04 | [[ONB-APP-04 App. Review\|App. Review]] | Reviewing captured data — by the applicant before submission and by the bank before decisioning |
| ONB-APP-05 | [[ONB-APP-05 Up - Cross-Sell\|Up / Cross-Sell]] | Presenting additional or alternative products during origination, such as creditor insurance or alternates on ineligibility |
| ONB-APP-06 | [[ONB-APP-06 Pre-Qualification\|Pre-Qualification]] | A soft-inquiry eligibility stage that lets an applicant learn if they can proceed without a hard credit inquiry |
