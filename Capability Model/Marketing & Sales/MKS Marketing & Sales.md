# Marketing & Sales (MKS)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Marketing & Sales drives demand and conversion through marketing strategy, brand, campaigns, and segmentation, and the CRM and sales-management capabilities that turn prospects into customers. It works closely with [[CEN Customer Engagement|Customer Engagement]] offers and content.

## L1 Capability Groups

| ID | Capability Group | L2s | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| MKS-MKT | [[MKS-MKT Marketing\|Marketing]] | 5 | Marketing covers strategy, brand, campaign, and segment management and the generation of leads and prospects |
| MKS-CRM | [[MKS-CRM Sales & CRM\|Sales & CRM]] | 7 | Sales & CRM covers customer relationship management and the sales pipeline, tracking, referrals, and lead generation that convert interest into sales |
