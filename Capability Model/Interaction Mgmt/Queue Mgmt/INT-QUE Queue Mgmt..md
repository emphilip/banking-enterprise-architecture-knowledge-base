# Queue Mgmt. (INT-QUE)

**L0:** [[INT Interaction Mgmt.|Interaction Mgmt.]]

Queue Mgmt. holds, orders, and manages waiting interactions across channels and offers wait-time relief options.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| INT-QUE-01 | [[INT-QUE-01 Universal Queue\|Universal Queue]] | A single cross-channel queue unifying voice, chat, email, and digital work items |
| INT-QUE-02 | [[INT-QUE-02 Click-to-Callback\|Click-to-Callback]] | Lets a waiting customer request a callback instead of holding in queue |
| INT-QUE-03 | [[INT-QUE-03 Virtual Hold\|Virtual Hold]] | Reserves a customer's place in line so they can disconnect and be called back at their turn |
| INT-QUE-04 | [[INT-QUE-04 Wait Indicator\|Wait Indicator]] | Communicates estimated wait time and queue position to set expectations |
