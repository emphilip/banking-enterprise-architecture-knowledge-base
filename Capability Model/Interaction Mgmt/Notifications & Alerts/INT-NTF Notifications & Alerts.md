# Notifications & Alerts (INT-NTF)

**L0:** [[INT Interaction Mgmt.|Interaction Mgmt.]]

Notifications & Alerts deliver proactive, event-driven messages to customers across channels.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| INT-NTF-01 | [[INT-NTF-01 Push\|Push]] | App push notifications for transactional and engagement messages |
| INT-NTF-02 | [[INT-NTF-02 Real-Time\|Real-Time]] | Immediate event-triggered alerts delivered as activity occurs |
| INT-NTF-03 | [[INT-NTF-03 Account Alerts\|Account Alerts]] | Configurable balance, transaction, and account-activity notifications |
| INT-NTF-04 | [[INT-NTF-04 Card Alerts\|Card Alerts]] | Card-specific alerts for authorizations, declines, and suspicious activity |
