# Fraud Management (FRR-FRD)

**L0:** [[FRR Fraud & Risk|Fraud & Risk]]

Fraud Management detects, investigates, and resolves fraud across products and channels, from strategy through case handling and mass-compromise events.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| FRR-FRD-01 | [[FRR-FRD-01 Fraud Strategy\|Fraud Strategy]] | Setting the bank's fraud-prevention strategy, appetite, and control framework |
| FRR-FRD-02 | [[FRR-FRD-02 Fraud Monitoring\|Fraud Monitoring]] | Real-time and batch monitoring of transactions and accounts for fraud signals |
| FRR-FRD-03 | [[FRR-FRD-03 Fraud Detection\|Fraud Detection]] | Detecting suspected fraud using rules, models, and analytics |
| FRR-FRD-04 | [[FRR-FRD-04 Fraud Investigation\|Fraud Investigation]] | Investigating flagged cases to confirm or clear suspected fraud |
| FRR-FRD-05 | [[FRR-FRD-05 Fraud Queue\|Fraud Queue]] | Managing the work queue of fraud alerts and cases for analyst review |
| FRR-FRD-06 | [[FRR-FRD-06 Mass Comp.\|Mass Comp.]] | Handling mass-compromise events such as bulk card-data breaches |
