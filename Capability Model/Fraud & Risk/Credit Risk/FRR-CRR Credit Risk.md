# Credit Risk (FRR-CRR)

**L0:** [[FRR Fraud & Risk|Fraud & Risk]]

Credit Risk manages the bank's lending exposure through assessment, limits, portfolio monitoring, and loss forecasting.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| FRR-CRR-01 | [[FRR-CRR-01 Credit Risk Mgmt.\|Credit Risk Mgmt.]] | Overall management of the bank's credit-risk framework and appetite |
| FRR-CRR-02 | [[FRR-CRR-02 Credit Risk Assess.\|Credit Risk Assess.]] | Assessing the credit risk of applicants, customers, and exposures |
| FRR-CRR-03 | [[FRR-CRR-03 Portfolio Risk\|Portfolio Risk]] | Monitoring credit-risk concentration and performance across the portfolio |
| FRR-CRR-04 | [[FRR-CRR-04 Loss Forecasting\|Loss Forecasting]] | Forecasting expected credit losses and provisioning needs |
| FRR-CRR-05 | [[FRR-CRR-05 Credit Limits\|Credit Limits]] | Setting and maintaining credit limits across customers and products |
| FRR-CRR-06 | [[FRR-CRR-06 Restrict Txn\|Restrict Txn]] | Restricting or blocking transactions where credit risk warrants |
