# Compliance (FRR-CMP)

**L0:** [[FRR Fraud & Risk|Fraud & Risk]]

Compliance ensures the bank meets its regulatory, network, and policy obligations, including disclosures, reporting, and PCI.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| FRR-CMP-01 | [[FRR-CMP-01 Policy Mgmt.\|Policy Mgmt.]] | Defining and maintaining compliance policies and standards |
| FRR-CMP-02 | [[FRR-CMP-02 Reg. Disclosures\|Reg. Disclosures]] | Managing mandated regulatory disclosures to customers |
| FRR-CMP-03 | [[FRR-CMP-03 Reg. Reporting\|Reg. Reporting]] | Preparing and filing regulatory reports and returns |
| FRR-CMP-04 | [[FRR-CMP-04 Network Comp.\|Network Comp.]] | Ensuring compliance with card-network rules and mandates |
| FRR-CMP-05 | [[FRR-CMP-05 PCI\|PCI]] | Maintaining PCI DSS compliance for cardholder data |
| FRR-CMP-06 | [[FRR-CMP-06 Federal - State\|Federal / State]] | Meeting federal and provincial or state regulatory requirements |
