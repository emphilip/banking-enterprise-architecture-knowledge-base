# Fraud & Risk (FRR)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Fraud & Risk protects the bank and its customers across fraud, credit, market, operational, compliance, audit, and security risk. It sets risk strategy and policy, detects and investigates fraud, manages credit and portfolio exposure, and ensures regulatory and information-security obligations are met.

## L1 Capability Groups

| ID | Capability Group | L2s | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| FRR-FRD | [[FRR-FRD Fraud Management\|Fraud Management]] | 6 | Fraud Management detects, investigates, and resolves fraud across products and channels, from strategy through case handling and mass-compromise events |
| FRR-CRR | [[FRR-CRR Credit Risk\|Credit Risk]] | 6 | Credit Risk manages the bank's lending exposure through assessment, limits, portfolio monitoring, and loss forecasting |
| FRR-MOR | [[FRR-MOR Market & Op Risk\|Market & Op Risk]] | 6 | Market & Op Risk covers market, liquidity, and operational risk and the strategy, reporting, and analysis that govern them |
| FRR-CMP | [[FRR-CMP Compliance\|Compliance]] | 6 | Compliance ensures the bank meets its regulatory, network, and policy obligations, including disclosures, reporting, and PCI |
| FRR-AUS | [[FRR-AUS Audit & Security\|Audit & Security]] | 6 | Audit & Security covers internal and external audit, privacy, information and cyber security, and business continuity |
