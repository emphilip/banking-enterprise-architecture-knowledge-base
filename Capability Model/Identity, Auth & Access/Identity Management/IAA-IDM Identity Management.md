# Identity Management (IAA-IDM)

**L0:** [[IAA Identity, Auth & Access|Identity, Auth & Access]]

Identity Management establishes and maintains digital identities, their attributes, roles, and lifecycle across the bank.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| IAA-IDM-01 | [[IAA-IDM-01 Credentials\|Credentials]] | Issuance, storage, and lifecycle of user credentials such as usernames and secrets |
| IAA-IDM-02 | [[IAA-IDM-02 ID Proofing\|ID Proofing]] | Verifying a person's real-world identity at enrolment using documents and data |
| IAA-IDM-03 | [[IAA-IDM-03 User Groups & Roles\|User Groups & Roles]] | Defining groups and roles that structure access and entitlements |
| IAA-IDM-04 | [[IAA-IDM-04 Entitlements\|Entitlements]] | Granting and maintaining the specific permissions attached to an identity |
| IAA-IDM-05 | [[IAA-IDM-05 Digital ID\|Digital ID]] | Reusable digital identity credentials and federation with trusted ID schemes |
| IAA-IDM-06 | [[IAA-IDM-06 Profile Sync\|Profile Sync]] | Keeping identity attributes consistent across systems and channels |
