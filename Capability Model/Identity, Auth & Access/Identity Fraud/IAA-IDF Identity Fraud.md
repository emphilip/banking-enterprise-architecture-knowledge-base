# Identity Fraud (IAA-IDF)

**L0:** [[IAA Identity, Auth & Access|Identity, Auth & Access]]

Identity Fraud detects and prevents impersonation and fraudulent enrolment, and captures consent during onboarding.

## L2 Capabilities

| ID | Capability | Summary |
|---|---|---|
| IAA-IDF-01 | [[IAA-IDF-01 Identity Fraud Detect\|Identity Fraud Detect]] | Detecting impersonation and synthetic-identity fraud signals during access and origination |
| IAA-IDF-02 | [[IAA-IDF-02 Enrollment\|Enrollment]] | Securely enrolling new identities while screening for fraud |
| IAA-IDF-03 | [[IAA-IDF-03 Consent Capture\|Consent Capture]] | Recording explicit user consents during identity and onboarding events |
| IAA-IDF-04 | [[IAA-IDF-04 Step-Up Triggers\|Step-Up Triggers]] | Rules and signals that trigger additional verification when risk is elevated |
