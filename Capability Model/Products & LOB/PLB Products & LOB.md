# Products & LOB (PLB)

**Parent:** [[00 - Master Capability Model]]

Products & LOB is the bank's product factory — the deposit, card, lending, mortgage, and insurance products themselves, including their setup, pricing rules, and lifecycle. Other domains originate, service, and engage around the products defined here.

## L1 Capability Groups

| ID | Capability Group | L2s | Summary |
|---|---|---|---|
| PLB-DEP | [[PLB-DEP Deposits\|Deposits]] | 14 | Deposits covers chequing, savings, registered, and related deposit products and the processing of deposits, withdrawals, drafts, and money orders |
| PLB-CRD | [[PLB-CRD Cards\|Cards]] | 11 | Cards covers the bank's credit, debit, prepaid, and BNPL card products and their limits, balance transfers, installments, and convenience features |
| PLB-LND | [[PLB-LND Lending & Mortgages\|Lending & Mortgages]] | 16 | Lending & Mortgages covers personal and secured lending, lines of credit, and residential mortgages across their full lifecycle from disbursement to renewal and recovery |
| PLB-INS | [[PLB-INS Insurance\|Insurance]] | 16 | Insurance covers the bank's insurance products and the quoting, policy lifecycle, certificates, and claims handling that support them |
