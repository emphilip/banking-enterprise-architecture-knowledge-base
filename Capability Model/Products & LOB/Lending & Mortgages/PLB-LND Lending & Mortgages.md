# Lending & Mortgages (PLB-LND)

**L0:** [[PLB Products & LOB|Products & LOB]]

Lending & Mortgages covers personal and secured lending, lines of credit, and residential mortgages across their full lifecycle from disbursement to renewal and recovery.
