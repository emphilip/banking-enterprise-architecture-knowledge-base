# Wealth & Investments

**Definition.** Wealth & Investments is the business value-stream domain covering portfolio management, investment advisory, and brokerage and trading services for retail, affluent, and private banking clients.
**Also known as:** Wealth Domain, Investments Domain.

## Relationships
- Wealth Management is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Portfolio Management is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Investment Advisory is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Brokerage & Trading is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Wealth & Investments is related to the Customer Management domain.

## Details
The Wealth & Investments domain spans discretionary and advisory portfolio management, financial planning, and brokerage execution and trading. It aligns with BIAN investment portfolio and advisory service domains, and increasingly leverages generative AI for advisor productivity and personalized advice.
