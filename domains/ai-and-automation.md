# AI & Automation

**Definition.** AI & Automation is the technology domain covering machine learning, fraud analytics, conversational and generative AI, intelligent document processing, and robotic process automation across the bank.
**Also known as:** AI Domain, Automation Domain.

## Relationships
- Machine Learning Platform is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Fraud Analytics is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Conversational AI is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Generative AI Platform is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- Robotic Process Automation is defined in the AI & Automation domain.
- AI & Automation is related to the Data & Analytics domain.

## Details
The AI & Automation domain provides ML platforms, fraud analytics, AML transaction monitoring platforms, conversational AI, generative AI platforms, intelligent document processing, and RPA. These capabilities increasingly supersede legacy rules engines and IVR systems, enabling personalized, automated, and risk-aware banking.
