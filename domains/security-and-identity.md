# Security & Identity

**Definition.** Security & Identity is the technology domain covering identity and access management, customer identity, and threat detection capabilities that protect the bank and authenticate its users.
**Also known as:** Security Domain, Identity Domain.

## Relationships
- Identity Access Management is defined in the Security & Identity domain.
- Customer Identity is defined in the Security & Identity domain.
- Threat Detection is defined in the Security & Identity domain.
- Security & Identity is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.

## Details
The Security & Identity domain provides workforce IAM, customer identity and access management (CIAM), and threat detection (SIEM/cybersecurity). It enables secure channel access and zero-trust architectures, with modernization moving from legacy on-prem identity managers to cloud identity platforms such as Okta and Ping Identity.
