# Compliance & Financial Crime

**Definition.** Compliance & Financial Crime is the business value-stream domain covering KYC, AML, transaction monitoring, sanctions screening, and regulatory compliance and reporting obligations.
**Also known as:** Financial Crime Domain, Compliance Domain.

## Relationships
- Financial Crime Compliance is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- KYC Management is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- AML Monitoring is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Regulatory Compliance is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Compliance & Financial Crime is related to the Risk Management domain.

## Details
The Compliance & Financial Crime domain spans Know Your Customer, anti-money-laundering monitoring, transaction surveillance, sanctions/watchlist screening, and regulatory compliance and reporting. It aligns with BIAN compliance and fraud/AML service domains, and increasingly relies on AI transaction monitoring platforms to reduce false positives.
