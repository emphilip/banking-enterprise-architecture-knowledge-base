# Data & Analytics

**Definition.** Data & Analytics is the technology domain covering data warehousing, streaming, analytics and BI, governance, master data, and regulatory reporting capabilities that turn raw data into insight.
**Also known as:** Data Domain, Analytics Domain.

## Relationships
- Data Warehousing is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Data Streaming is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Analytics Platform is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Data Governance is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Master Data Management is defined in the Data & Analytics domain.
- Data & Analytics is related to the AI & Automation domain.

## Details
The Data & Analytics domain provides data warehousing, event streaming, analytics and BI, data governance and cataloging, master data management, document management, and regulatory reporting engines. Modernization shifts from on-prem warehouses such as Teradata to cloud-native lakehouses like Snowflake and Databricks.
