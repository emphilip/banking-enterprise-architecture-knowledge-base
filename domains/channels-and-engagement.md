# Channels & Engagement

**Definition.** Channels & Engagement is the business value-stream domain covering the digital, branch, contact center, and ATM channels through which customers interact with the bank.
**Also known as:** Channels Domain, Engagement Domain.

## Relationships
- Channel Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Branch Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Contact Center is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- ATM Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Channels & Engagement is related to the Customer Management domain.

## Details
The Channels & Engagement domain spans digital banking (mobile and online), branch networks, contact centers, and ATM management. It aligns with BIAN channel and customer interaction service domains, and is realized by digital channel platforms, CRM, and conversational AI for omnichannel engagement.
