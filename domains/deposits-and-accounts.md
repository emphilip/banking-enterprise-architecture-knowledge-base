# Deposits & Accounts

**Definition.** Deposits & Accounts is the business value-stream domain covering the opening, servicing, and administration of deposit and transaction accounts, including interest calculation and overdraft handling.
**Also known as:** Deposits Domain, Accounts Domain.

## Relationships
- Deposits is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Opening is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Servicing is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Interest Calculation is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Management is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposits & Accounts is related to the Core Processing domain.

## Details
The Deposits & Accounts domain covers current, savings, and term deposit products, from account origination through ongoing servicing, interest accrual, and overdraft management. It aligns with BIAN current account and deposit service domains and is typically realized by the core banking ledger.
