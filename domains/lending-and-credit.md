# Lending & Credit

**Definition.** Lending & Credit is the business value-stream domain covering the origination, underwriting, servicing, and recovery of loans and credit facilities across retail, commercial, and mortgage portfolios.
**Also known as:** Lending Domain, Credit Domain.

## Relationships
- Lending is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Origination is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Servicing is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Collateral Management is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Collections & Recovery is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Lending & Credit is related to the Risk Management domain.

## Details
The Lending & Credit domain spans loan origination and credit decisioning, ongoing loan servicing, collateral and security management, and collections and recovery. It aligns with BIAN consumer and corporate loan service domains, and depends heavily on credit decisioning engines and risk models for sound underwriting.
