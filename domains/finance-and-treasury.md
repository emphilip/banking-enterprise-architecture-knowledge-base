# Finance & Treasury

**Definition.** Finance & Treasury is the business value-stream domain covering general ledger accounting, financial and regulatory capital reporting, and treasury, liquidity, and asset-liability management.
**Also known as:** Finance Domain, Treasury Domain.

## Relationships
- Finance Management is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Treasury Management is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- General Ledger Accounting is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Financial Reporting is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Liquidity Management is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Finance & Treasury is related to the Risk Management domain.

## Details
The Finance & Treasury domain spans GL accounting, financial and management reporting, regulatory capital management, and treasury functions including liquidity and asset-liability management. It aligns with BIAN financial accounting and treasury service domains, and is realized by general ledger engines and ERP platforms.
