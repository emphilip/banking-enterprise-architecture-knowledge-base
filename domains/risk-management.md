# Risk Management

**Definition.** Risk Management is the business value-stream domain covering the identification, measurement, monitoring, and mitigation of credit, market, operational, liquidity, and fraud risks across the bank.
**Also known as:** Risk Domain.

## Relationships
- Enterprise Risk Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Credit Risk Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Market Risk Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Operational Risk Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Fraud Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Risk Management is related to the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.

## Details
The Risk Management domain spans enterprise risk disciplines including credit, market, operational, and liquidity risk, plus fraud management. It aligns with BIAN risk service domains and Basel frameworks, and depends on data warehousing, analytics, and machine learning platforms for modeling and monitoring.
