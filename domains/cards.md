# Cards

**Definition.** Cards is the business value-stream domain covering the issuance, authorization, lifecycle management, and dispute handling of debit, credit, and prepaid payment cards.
**Also known as:** Cards Domain, Card Services.

## Relationships
- Cards Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Issuing is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Authorization is defined in the Cards domain.
- Dispute Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Lifecycle Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Cards is related to the Payments domain.

## Details
The Cards domain spans card issuing, real-time authorization, lifecycle management (activation, renewal, replacement), and dispute/chargeback handling. It aligns with BIAN card service domains and is realized by card processing platforms integrated with scheme networks such as Visa and Mastercard.
