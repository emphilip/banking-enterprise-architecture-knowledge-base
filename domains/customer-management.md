# Customer Management

**Definition.** Customer Management is the business value-stream domain covering how a bank acquires, identifies, services, and deepens relationships with customers and other parties across their lifecycle.
**Also known as:** Party Management, Customer Domain.

## Relationships
- Customer Lifecycle Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Marketing Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Acquisition is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Party Data Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Servicing is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Management is related to the Channels & Engagement domain.

## Details
The Customer Management domain spans acquisition, due diligence, profile and party-data management, servicing, and relationship management. It aligns with BIAN Party reference data and customer relationship service domains. Marketing capabilities such as Campaign Management and Customer Segmentation also sit here, enabling targeted engagement and personalization.
