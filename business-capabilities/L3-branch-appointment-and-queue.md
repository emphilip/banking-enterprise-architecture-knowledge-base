# Branch Appointment & Queue

**Definition.** Branch Appointment & Queue is the Channels & Engagement capability that schedules customer visits and manages in-branch flow and waiting, supporting the BIAN Branch Location Management service domain; it is an APQC-aligned channel-operations practice extension.
**Also known as:** Appointment Booking, Queue Management.

## Relationships
- Branch Appointment & Queue is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Branch Appointment & Queue is derived from Branch Banking.
- Branch Appointment & Queue depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Branch Appointment & Queue supports BIAN Branch Location Management by matching customer intent to the right specialist (mortgage, FX, business banking) and managing same-day walk-in flow with ticketing and digital signage. Pre-booked appointments captured online carry omnichannel context so the colleague sees the reason and any in-progress digital journey before the meeting. Wait-time and capacity analytics drive staffing, aligning to APQC PCF channel-operations measures, and accessible booking (WCAG-conformant online scheduling plus walk-in assistance) is required so the entry point does not exclude assisted-technology users.
