# Compliance Monitoring & Testing

**Definition.** Compliance Monitoring & Testing performs independent assurance over controls and obligations, delivering the BSA "independent testing" pillar and the OFAC 2019 Framework testing-and-auditing component.
**Also known as:** Compliance Assurance, Independent Testing.

## Relationships
- Compliance Monitoring & Testing is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Compliance Monitoring & Testing is derived from Regulatory Compliance.
- Compliance Monitoring & Testing depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Compliance Monitoring & Testing depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
The BSA requires independent testing of the AML program, commensurate with risk, typically every 12-18 months and conducted by parties not involved in day-to-day compliance operations. The OFAC Framework lists testing and auditing as a core component to identify program weaknesses and deficiencies. This capability designs the assurance plan, executes sample-based and continuous control tests (e.g. CTR/SAR timeliness, screening effectiveness), and reports findings into issue and remediation tracking.
