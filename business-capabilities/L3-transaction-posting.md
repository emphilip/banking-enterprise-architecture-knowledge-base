# Transaction Posting

**Definition.** Transaction Posting records debits, credits, holds and value-dating against deposit accounts, realising the core fulfilment of the BIAN Current Account and Savings Account service domains.
**Also known as:** Ledger Posting, Account Posting.

## Relationships
- Transaction Posting is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Transaction Posting is derived from Account Servicing.
- Transaction Posting depends on the Transaction Posting Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current/Savings Account fulfilment posts entries to the account ledger; Regulation E (12 CFR 1005) governs the posting and error-resolution treatment of consumer electronic fund transfers, requiring investigation of disputed debits within prescribed timeframes.
