# Overdraft Limit Assessment

**Definition.** Overdraft Limit Assessment evaluates eligibility and sizes the authorised overdraft limit at arrangement, supporting the assessment behaviour of the BIAN Overdraft Facility service domain.
**Also known as:** Limit Sizing, Overdraft Eligibility.

## Relationships
- Overdraft Limit Assessment is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Limit Assessment is derived from Overdraft Facility Setup.
- Overdraft Limit Assessment depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Overdraft Facility assessment behaviour sizes the limit; because an overdraft line is an extension of credit, eligibility and limit decisions can trigger Regulation B / ECOA (12 CFR 1002) adverse-action notice obligations where credit is denied or reduced.
