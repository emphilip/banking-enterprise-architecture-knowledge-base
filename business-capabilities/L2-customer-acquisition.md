# Customer Acquisition

**Definition.** Customer Acquisition is the L2 business capability for attracting prospects and converting them into onboarded customers, covering application intake, eligibility, identity proofing, and initial provisioning in line with the BIAN Customer Relationship Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Onboarding Capability.

## Relationships
- Customer Acquisition is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Acquisition is derived from Customer Lifecycle Management.
- Customer Acquisition depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Customer Acquisition is related to Identity Verification.

## Details
Customer Acquisition orchestrates the prospect-to-customer journey, drawing on BIAN service domains including Customer Offer, Party Lifecycle Management, and Customer Relationship Management. Typical sub-functions include lead capture, application processing, eligibility assessment, and account provisioning. It is the parent of Identity Verification and Customer Due Diligence and underpins the Customer Onboarding business process. AI-enabled implementations use intelligent document processing to accelerate application intake.
