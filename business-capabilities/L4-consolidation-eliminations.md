# Consolidation Eliminations

**Definition.** Consolidation Eliminations process group-level elimination and adjustment journals during the consolidation run under IFRS 10, including investment elimination and non-controlling-interest measurement.
**Also known as:** Consolidation Adjustments, Group Eliminations, NCI Adjustments.

## Relationships
- Consolidation Eliminations is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Consolidation Eliminations is derived from Financial Consolidation.
- Consolidation Eliminations depends on the Financial Consolidation Platform capability.

## Details
Consolidation Eliminations book the group-only entries IFRS 10 requires: eliminating the parent's investment against the subsidiary's equity at acquisition (recognising goodwill), measuring and rolling forward non-controlling interests, and posting fair-value and equity-method adjustments for associates. These adjustments exist only at the consolidation layer, never in entity books, so the capability maintains a separate adjustment ledger with full audit trail and rolls balances forward each period to keep the group equity reconciliation intact.
