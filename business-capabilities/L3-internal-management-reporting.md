# Internal Management Reporting

**Definition.** Internal Management Reporting produces internal performance, budget and profitability reporting for management decision-making, aligning to the BIAN Management Accounting service domain and APQC PCF "Perform planning and management accounting".
**Also known as:** MI Reporting, Performance Reporting (Finance), Internal Reporting.

## Relationships
- Internal Management Reporting is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Internal Management Reporting is derived from Financial Reporting.
- Internal Management Reporting depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Internal Management Reporting delivers the management view of results — segment, product, customer and business-unit P&L, budget-versus-actual variance and KPI dashboards — distinct from externally-filed statutory accounts. APQC PCF "Perform planning and management accounting" frames the activity. Because management reporting allocates funding cost and overheads, it consumes Funds Transfer Pricing Management outputs and cost-allocation results so that business-unit profitability nets internal funding charges and credits.
