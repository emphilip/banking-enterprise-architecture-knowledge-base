# Rewards Management

**Definition.** Rewards Management accrues, redeems and administers loyalty points and cashback for cardholders, realising the BIAN Reward Points Account service domain.
**Also known as:** Loyalty Management, Rewards & Points, Cashback Management.

## Relationships
- Rewards Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Rewards Management is derived from Card Lifecycle Management.
- Rewards Management depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Reward Points Account service domain maintains the points/cashback balance as a sub-ledger, applying earn rates that vary by MCC (ISO 18245 merchant category code) — for example elevated points on dining or travel categories — and tracking sign-up bonuses, tier multipliers and point expiry. Redemptions (statement credit, travel, gift cards) are posted against the cardholder account, and reward liability is accrued for finance reporting.
