# Customer Event Detection

**Definition.** Customer Event Detection is the L4 business capability that identifies meaningful life and financial trigger events from customer activity to prompt timely engagement, realising the BIAN Customer Event History service domain.
**Also known as:** Trigger Detection, Life Event Detection.

## Relationships
- Customer Event Detection is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Event Detection is derived from Behavioural Analytics.
- Customer Event Detection depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Customer Event Detection extends its parent Behavioural Analytics within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Customer Event Detection relies on the Analytics Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Customer Event Detection at scale.
