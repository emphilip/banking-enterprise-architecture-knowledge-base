# Wealth Management

**Definition.** Wealth Management is the top-level business capability that delivers holistic financial planning, investment management, and advisory services to high-net-worth and mass-affluent clients. It aligns with the BIAN Investment Management service domains and the APQC manage financial resources framework.
**Also known as:** Private Banking.

## Relationships
- Wealth Management is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Wealth Management depends on the Generative AI Platform capability.
- Wealth Management is related to Portfolio Management.

## Details
Wealth Management spans goal-based planning, discretionary and advisory portfolio management, brokerage execution, and reporting for individuals, families, and trusts. Hybrid advice models combine human relationship managers with robo-advisory and generative-AI assistants, while integration with CRM and analytics platforms supports holistic household views and next-best-action recommendations.
