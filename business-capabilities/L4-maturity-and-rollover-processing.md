# Maturity & Rollover Processing

**Definition.** Maturity & Rollover Processing handles term-deposit maturity instructions, renewals and rollovers at the rate then prevailing, realising the maturity behaviour of the BIAN Term Deposit service domain.
**Also known as:** Rollover Processing, Renewal Handling.

## Relationships
- Maturity & Rollover Processing is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Maturity & Rollover Processing is derived from Term Deposit Lifecycle.
- Maturity & Rollover Processing depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Term Deposit maturity behaviour renews the deposit; for CDs with terms longer than one year that renew automatically, Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030.5) requires a pre-maturity notice with the maturity date and new-term rate before the grace period closes.
