# Campaign Management

**Definition.** Campaign Management is the L2 business capability for designing, executing, and optimising targeted marketing campaigns across channels, including audience selection, message orchestration, and response tracking.
**Also known as:** Campaigns.

## Relationships
- Campaign Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Campaign Management is derived from Marketing Management.
- Campaign Management depends on the CRM Platform capability.
- Campaign Management is related to Customer Segmentation.

## Details
Campaign Management aligns to the BIAN Marketing Campaign and Customer Campaign Management service domains. Typical sub-functions include campaign planning, audience definition, channel orchestration, A/B testing, and response and conversion measurement. It consumes audiences from Customer Segmentation and is realized through CRM and campaign platforms. Analytics and machine learning support next-best-offer targeting.
