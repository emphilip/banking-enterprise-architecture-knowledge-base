# Financial Planning Advisory

**Definition.** Financial Planning Advisory develops holistic plans covering cash flow, retirement, tax, and estate objectives for a client; it complements the BIAN Customer Investment Profile service domain, since comprehensive wealth planning extends beyond BIAN's retail core, per CFA Institute practice.
**Also known as:** Wealth Planning Capability, Comprehensive Planning.

## Relationships
- Financial Planning Advisory is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Financial Planning Advisory is derived from Investment Advisory.
- Financial Planning Advisory depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Financial Planning Advisory depends on the CRM Platform capability.

## Details
Per CFA Institute practice, financial planning gathers the client's full balance sheet, income, and goals to set the return and risk requirements that feed the investment policy statement. Concretely, Financial Planning Advisory models cash-flow needs, retirement funding gaps, tax-efficient withdrawal sequencing, and estate transfer, drawing on the client profile held in the BIAN Customer Investment Profile service domain.
