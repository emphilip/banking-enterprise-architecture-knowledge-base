# Retirement Projection

**Definition.** Retirement Projection models contributions, drawdown, and probability of meeting retirement income goals under scenarios; it complements the BIAN Customer Investment Profile service domain, reflecting CFA Institute goal-based practice.
**Also known as:** Retirement Modelling, Decumulation Planning.

## Relationships
- Retirement Projection is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Retirement Projection is derived from Goal Planning.
- Retirement Projection depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Retirement Projection depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Retirement Projection runs Monte Carlo simulation over capital market assumptions to estimate the probability that accumulated savings fund a target income through retirement, modelling the decumulation phase and sustainable withdrawal rates (such as the 4% rule) against longevity and sequence-of-returns risk. The funded-status output drives Goal Planning and feeds the client profile in the BIAN Customer Investment Profile service domain.
