# Best Interest Compliance

**Definition.** Best Interest Compliance ensures recommendations meet care, disclosure, and conflict-of-interest obligations and documents the basis of advice; it is grounded in SEC Regulation Best Interest and FINRA rules, as advice conduct sits outside BIAN's retail core.
**Also known as:** Reg BI Compliance, Care Obligation Management.

## Relationships
- Best Interest Compliance is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Best Interest Compliance is derived from Investment Advisory.
- Best Interest Compliance depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Best Interest Compliance depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
SEC Regulation Best Interest (Reg BI) requires broker-dealers to act in the retail customer's best interest at the time of a recommendation, satisfying four obligations: Disclosure, Care, Conflict of Interest, and Compliance. Best Interest Compliance enforces these by capturing the rationale for each recommendation, delivering Form CRS relationship summaries, identifying and mitigating conflicts, and retaining the evidentiary record needed to demonstrate the care obligation under FINRA oversight.
