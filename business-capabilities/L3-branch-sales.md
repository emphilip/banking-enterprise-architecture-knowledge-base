# Branch Sales

**Definition.** Branch Sales is the Channels & Engagement capability that conducts face-to-face needs analysis, product matching and origination referral in the branch, fronting the BIAN Sales Product Agreement and Point of Service service domains.
**Also known as:** In-Branch Sales, Counter Sales.

## Relationships
- Branch Sales is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Branch Sales is derived from Branch Banking.
- Branch Sales depends on the CRM Platform capability.

## Details
Branch Sales fronts BIAN Sales Product Agreement at the Point of Service: a colleague runs a needs analysis, presents matched products and either originates on the spot or refers a lead to specialist channels. Conduct rules dominate the control set, with suitability assessment, fair-treatment evidencing and disclosure of fees and risks logged for review under conduct regimes such as the FCA Consumer Duty. Leads and outcomes write back to the CRM so omnichannel context follows the customer. Tablet-based assisted-selling tools (ebankIT, Backbase) pre-fill applications and surface Next-Best-Action prompts at the counter.
