# Consent Lifecycle

**Definition.** Consent Lifecycle is the L4 business capability that captures, versions, renews and revokes customer consents with auditable proof for regulatory and Open Banking purposes, supporting the BIAN Party Authentication and Customer Profile service domains.
**Also known as:** Consent Tracking, Consent Versioning.

## Relationships
- Consent Lifecycle is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Consent Lifecycle is derived from Consent & Preference Management.
- Consent Lifecycle depends on the Master Data Management capability.

## Details
Consent Lifecycle extends its parent Consent & Preference Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Consent Lifecycle relies on the Master Data Management technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Consent Lifecycle at scale.
