# Card Lifecycle Management

**Definition.** Card Lifecycle Management is the business capability that administers a card from activation through renewal, replacement, blocking, and closure across its entire operational life. It corresponds to the BIAN Card Administration service domain.
**Also known as:** Card Lifecycle.

## Relationships
- Card Lifecycle Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Lifecycle Management is derived from Cards Management.
- Card Lifecycle Management depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
Card Lifecycle Management governs activation, PIN management, limit changes, card replacement for lost or stolen cards, expiry-driven reissuance, and account closure. Self-service controls exposed through digital channels let cardholders freeze and unfreeze cards and manage spend controls, with cloud-native processors providing real-time state changes and audit trails.
