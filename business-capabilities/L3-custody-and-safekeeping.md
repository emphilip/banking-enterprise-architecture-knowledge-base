# Custody & Safekeeping

**Definition.** Custody & Safekeeping holds and safeguards client securities, maintains custody records, and provides asset servicing; it is realised through the BIAN Custody Administration service domain.
**Also known as:** Custody Administration, Asset Servicing.

## Relationships
- Custody & Safekeeping is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Custody & Safekeeping is derived from Brokerage & Trading.
- Custody & Safekeeping depends on the Order Management System capability.
- Custody & Safekeeping depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Custody Administration service domain safeguards assets on behalf of clients. In practice most US securities are held in book-entry form, immobilised at the DTC central securities depository, with the custodian holding positions through DTCC and recording beneficial ownership. Custody & Safekeeping maintains those safekeeping records, reconciles to the depository daily, and performs asset servicing such as income collection and corporate-action handling.
