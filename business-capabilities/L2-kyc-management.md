# KYC Management

**Definition.** KYC Management is the business capability that collects, verifies, and maintains customer identity and risk information to meet know-your-customer obligations. It corresponds to the BIAN Customer Due Diligence and Party Authentication service domains.
**Also known as:** Know Your Customer.

## Relationships
- KYC Management is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- KYC Management is derived from Financial Crime Compliance.
- KYC Management depends on the Intelligent Document Processing capability.

## Details
KYC Management performs identity verification, beneficial ownership and UBO checks, PEP and adverse media screening, and customer risk rating with periodic and event-driven reviews. Intelligent document processing and orchestration platforms accelerate onboarding and enable perpetual KYC by continuously refreshing risk profiles from internal and external data sources.
