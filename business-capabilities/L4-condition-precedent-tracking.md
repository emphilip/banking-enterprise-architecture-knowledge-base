# Condition Precedent Tracking

**Definition.** Condition Precedent Tracking is the capability that monitors satisfaction of pre-disbursement conditions and covenants attached to an approved facility, mapping to the BIAN Credit Facility service domain.
**Also known as:** CP Tracking, Covenant Pre-Disbursement Tracking.

## Relationships
- Condition Precedent Tracking is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Condition Precedent Tracking is derived from Loan Approval.
- Condition Precedent Tracking depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Condition Precedent Tracking depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
The BIAN Credit Facility service domain attaches conditions and covenants to a facility, which Condition Precedent Tracking monitors to gate drawdown. A concrete banking specific: a corporate facility cannot be drawn until conditions precedent (executed security, board resolutions, insurance, legal opinions) are satisfied, and a clear-to-close decision in mortgage lending serves the same gating role.
