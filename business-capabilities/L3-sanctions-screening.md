# Sanctions Screening

**Definition.** Sanctions Screening is the business capability that checks customers, counterparties, and payments against sanctions, watchlists, and embargo lists to prevent prohibited dealings. It aligns with the BIAN Financial Crime screening service domain.
**Also known as:** Watchlist Screening.

## Relationships
- Sanctions Screening is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Sanctions Screening is derived from AML Monitoring.
- Sanctions Screening depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.

## Details
Sanctions Screening performs name and payment filtering against OFAC, EU, UN, and HMT lists using fuzzy matching, transliteration, and list management. Real-time payment screening within ISO 20022 flows, false-positive reduction, and good-guy lists are key, with vendor list curation and AI matching improving precision and throughput.
