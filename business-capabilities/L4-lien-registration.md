# Lien Registration

**Definition.** Lien Registration is the capability that files and maintains security registrations with external registries to perfect charges, mapping to the BIAN Collateral Asset Administration service domain.
**Also known as:** Charge Filing, Security Registration.

## Relationships
- Lien Registration is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Lien Registration is derived from Collateral Perfection.
- Lien Registration depends on the Document Management capability.
- Lien Registration depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Collateral Asset Administration service domain records the security interest that Lien Registration perfects with the relevant external registry. A concrete banking specific: a security interest over personal property is filed as a UCC-1 financing statement under UCC Article 9 (lapsing after five years unless continued), while charges over UK companies are registered at Companies House within 21 days of creation.
