# Term Deposit Lifecycle

**Definition.** Term Deposit Lifecycle manages placement, maturity, rollover and early-withdrawal penalties for fixed-term deposits, realising the BIAN Term Deposit service domain.
**Also known as:** Fixed Deposit Management, Time Deposit Lifecycle.

## Relationships
- Term Deposit Lifecycle is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Term Deposit Lifecycle is derived from Interest Calculation.
- Term Deposit Lifecycle depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Term Deposit service domain governs time deposits; Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030.5) sets pre-maturity and renewal disclosure rules for certificates of deposit, and early withdrawal triggers an interest penalty that must be disclosed at account opening.
