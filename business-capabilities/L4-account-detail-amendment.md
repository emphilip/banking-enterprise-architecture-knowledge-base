# Account Detail Amendment

**Definition.** Account Detail Amendment updates account-level attributes, instructions and linkages, realising the maintenance fulfilment of the BIAN Deposit Account service domain.
**Also known as:** Attribute Amendment, Account Update.

## Relationships
- Account Detail Amendment is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Detail Amendment is derived from Account Maintenance Servicing.
- Account Detail Amendment depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Deposit Account maintenance fulfilment updates account attributes; changes to interest-bearing terms or fees that reduce the consumer's benefit require advance change-in-terms notice under Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030.5).
