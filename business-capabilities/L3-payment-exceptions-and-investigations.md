# Payment Exceptions & Investigations

**Definition.** Payment Exceptions & Investigations handles non-straight-through payments, traces, recalls and inquiries between institutions, mapping to the exception-handling responsibilities within BIAN payment operations.
**Also known as:** Payment Investigations, Exception Handling, Recalls.

## Relationships
- Payment Exceptions & Investigations is defined in the Payments domain.
- Payment Exceptions & Investigations is derived from Payment Clearing & Settlement.
- Payment Exceptions & Investigations depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Payment Exceptions & Investigations grounds in the exception-handling responsibilities within BIAN payment operations. It handles non-straight-through payments, traces, recalls and inter-institution inquiries. Case-driven workflows coordinate investigation, response and resolution across counterparties.
