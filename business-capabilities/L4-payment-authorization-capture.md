# Payment Authorization Capture

**Definition.** Payment Authorization Capture obtains and records the customer or signatory approval and applies counterparty limits and preferences to an order, realising the authorisation step of the BIAN Payment Order service domain.
**Also known as:** Order Authorization, Payment Approval.

## Relationships
- Payment Authorization Capture is defined in the Payments domain.
- Payment Authorization Capture is derived from Payment Order Capture.
- Payment Authorization Capture depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Payment Authorization Capture grounds in the authorisation step of the BIAN Payment Order service domain. It obtains and records customer or signatory approval and applies counterparty limits and preferences to an order. It is distinct from card Card Authorization, covering the approval of customer-initiated payment orders.
