# Pricing Exception Handling

**Definition.** Pricing Exception Handling is the capability that manages discretionary pricing overrides and approvals outside standard rate cards, mapping to the BIAN Credit Facility service domain.
**Also known as:** Rate Override Management, Pricing Override.

## Relationships
- Pricing Exception Handling is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Pricing Exception Handling is derived from Loan Pricing.
- Pricing Exception Handling depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Credit Facility service domain records the agreed priced terms, including any approved exception that Pricing Exception Handling routes and authorises. A concrete banking specific: discretionary rate concessions must be controlled to avoid disparate-impact fair-lending risk under ECOA, so overrides are typically capped, justified against documented reasons and approved within a pricing-authority matrix.
