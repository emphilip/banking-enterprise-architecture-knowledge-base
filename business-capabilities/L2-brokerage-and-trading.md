# Brokerage & Trading

**Definition.** Brokerage & Trading is the business capability that executes, routes, and settles securities orders on behalf of clients across equities, fixed income, and funds. It corresponds to the BIAN Trade Execution and Securities Position Keeping service domains.
**Also known as:** Trading, Brokerage.

## Relationships
- Brokerage & Trading is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Brokerage & Trading is derived from Wealth Management.
- Brokerage & Trading is related to Portfolio Management.

## Details
Brokerage & Trading covers order capture, smart order routing, best-execution monitoring, allocation, and post-trade clearing and settlement. Integration with market data, custody, and the general ledger supports position keeping and corporate actions, while fractional-share and real-time settlement models reshape retail brokerage operations.
