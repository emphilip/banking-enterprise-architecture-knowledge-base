# Application Eligibility Screening

**Definition.** Application Eligibility Screening is the capability that checks applicants against product criteria and policy rules before full assessment, mapping to the BIAN Consumer Loan service domain.
**Also known as:** Pre-Qualification, Eligibility Pre-Screen.

## Relationships
- Application Eligibility Screening is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Application Eligibility Screening is derived from Loan Application Management.
- Application Eligibility Screening depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Consumer Loan service domain's eligibility rules are exercised by Application Eligibility Screening to filter clearly ineligible applicants early. A concrete banking specific: a pre-qualification typically uses a soft credit inquiry (which does not affect the applicant's score) plus product gates such as minimum age, residency and stated income, before a full underwriting hard pull is performed.
