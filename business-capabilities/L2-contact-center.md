# Contact Center

**Definition.** Contact Center is the business capability that handles inbound and outbound customer interactions across voice, chat, email, and messaging channels. It corresponds to the BIAN Contact Handling service domain.
**Also known as:** Call Center.

## Relationships
- Contact Center is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Contact Center is derived from Channel Management.
- Contact Center depends on the Contact Center Platform capability.

## Details
Contact Center covers intelligent routing, IVR and conversational self-service, agent desktops, knowledge management, and quality monitoring. Cloud contact-center platforms such as Amazon Connect and Genesys Cloud supersede legacy on-premise systems, while conversational and generative AI deflect routine queries and assist agents in real time.
