# Collections & Recovery

**Definition.** Collections & Recovery is the L2 business capability for managing delinquent and defaulted accounts through contact strategies, repayment arrangements, and recovery or write-off, aligned to the BIAN Collections and Customer Workbench service domains.
**Also known as:** Collections, Recoveries.

## Relationships
- Collections & Recovery is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Collections & Recovery is derived from Lending.
- Collections & Recovery depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Collections & Recovery is related to Loan Servicing.

## Details
Collections & Recovery maps to BIAN Collections and Recovery service domains. Typical sub-functions include early arrears management, contact and treatment strategy, payment arrangements and forbearance, default management, and recovery, litigation, or write-off. It underpins the Loan Collections business process. Analytics-driven segmentation prioritises outreach, while workflow orchestration manages treatment paths and regulatory affordability requirements.
