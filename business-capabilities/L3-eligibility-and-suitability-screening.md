# Eligibility & Suitability Screening

**Definition.** Eligibility & Suitability Screening checks applicant eligibility, product suitability and account-opening rules ahead of origination, supporting the qualification behaviour across the BIAN deposit account service domains.
**Also known as:** Account Eligibility Check, Opening Suitability.

## Relationships
- Eligibility & Suitability Screening is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Eligibility & Suitability Screening is derived from Account Opening.
- Eligibility & Suitability Screening depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN deposit account service domains express opening eligibility rules; banks commonly screen new-account applicants against deposit-account history databases (e.g. ChexSystems) whose use is governed by the Fair Credit Reporting Act (15 USC 1681), requiring adverse-action notice if an account is declined.
