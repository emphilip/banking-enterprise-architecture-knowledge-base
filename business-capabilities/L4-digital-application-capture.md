# Digital Application Capture

**Definition.** Digital Application Capture is the Channels & Engagement capability that gathers and validates product application data through guided digital forms and pre-fill, supporting the BIAN eBranch and Sales Product Agreement service domains.
**Also known as:** Online Application Capture, Digital Form Capture.

## Relationships
- Digital Application Capture is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Application Capture is derived from Digital Onboarding.
- Digital Application Capture depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.

## Details
Digital Application Capture supports BIAN eBranch and Sales Product Agreement by collecting application data through progressive, validated forms with pre-fill from verified identity and external sources, then passing a clean record to Application Processing. Inline validation, conditional questions, document upload and save-and-resume reduce the abandonment that dominates digital origination economics. Forms must meet WCAG 2.2 so they are operable by screen-reader and keyboard users, and required disclosures (APR, fees, terms) are presented and timestamped to satisfy lending and conduct rules. Captured PII is encrypted in transit and at rest under PCI/GDPR controls.
