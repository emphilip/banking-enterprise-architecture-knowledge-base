# Deposit Tax Withholding

**Definition.** Deposit Tax Withholding applies and reports withholding tax on credit interest and consolidates customer tax reporting, realising the BIAN Customer Tax Handling service domain for deposit products.
**Also known as:** Withholding Tax Handling, Interest Tax Reporting.

## Relationships
- Deposit Tax Withholding is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Tax Withholding is derived from Interest Calculation.
- Deposit Tax Withholding depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Customer Tax Handling service domain applies tax to interest; in the US, interest of $10 or more is reported on IRS Form 1099-INT, and 24% backup withholding applies where the customer fails to certify a correct TIN on Form W-9.
