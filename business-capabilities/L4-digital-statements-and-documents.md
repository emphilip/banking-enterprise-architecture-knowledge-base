# Digital Statements & Documents

**Definition.** Digital Statements & Documents is the Channels & Engagement capability that presents and delivers statements, notices and secure documents within digital channels, supporting the BIAN Correspondence and Channel Activity History service domains.
**Also known as:** E-Statements, Secure Document Inbox.

## Relationships
- Digital Statements & Documents is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Statements & Documents is derived from Digital Self-Service.
- Digital Statements & Documents depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
Digital Statements & Documents supports BIAN Correspondence and Channel Activity History by rendering statements, tax forms, regulatory notices and contracts into a secure in-app inbox with download and retention. Paperless enrolment in the US requires ESIGN Act consent and demonstrable ability to access the format before suppressing paper, and electronic disclosures must meet Reg E / Reg DD clear-and-conspicuous and timing rules. Documents are tamper-evident, retained per record-keeping schedules, and accessible (tagged PDF / WCAG) for assistive technology. Delivery events write to Channel Activity History and trigger Notification Services alerts.
