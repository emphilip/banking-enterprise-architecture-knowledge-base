# Op Risk Scenario Analysis

**Definition.** Op Risk Scenario Analysis estimates the frequency and severity of rare, high-impact operational events through structured expert workshops feeding capital and stress testing, a Basel operational-risk tool supported by the BIAN Operational Risk Models service domain.
**Also known as:** Operational Scenario Analysis, Tail-Risk Scenarios.

## Relationships
- Op Risk Scenario Analysis is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Op Risk Scenario Analysis is derived from Operational Risk Management.
- Op Risk Scenario Analysis depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Op Risk Scenario Analysis depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
Op Risk Scenario Analysis applies the BCBS d292 scenario-analysis tool to assess exposure to tail events (rogue trading, cyber breach, major conduct fines, processing failures) where internal loss history is too sparse to be reliable. Structured workshops elicit expert estimates of frequency and severity distributions, calibrated against internal and ORX external loss data to mitigate bias, and feed the loss-distribution and capital models plus operational-risk stress scenarios. Outputs are challenged and governed for plausibility and consistency.
