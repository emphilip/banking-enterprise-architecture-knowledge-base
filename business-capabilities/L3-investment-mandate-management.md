# Investment Mandate Management

**Definition.** Investment Mandate Management captures and enforces investment policy statements, restrictions, and discretionary mandates against portfolio activity; it aligns with the BIAN Investment Portfolio Planning service domain.
**Also known as:** Mandate Compliance, IPS Management.

## Relationships
- Investment Mandate Management is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Investment Mandate Management is derived from Portfolio Management.
- Investment Mandate Management depends on the Portfolio Management System capability.
- Investment Mandate Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Per CFA Institute practice, the investment policy statement (IPS) codifies objectives, return and risk targets, time horizon, liquidity needs, and constraints. Investment Mandate Management encodes these as machine-checkable rules — permitted asset classes, concentration and sector limits, ESG exclusions — and runs pre-trade and post-trade compliance checks against the BIAN Investment Portfolio Planning service domain so that breaches are blocked or flagged.
