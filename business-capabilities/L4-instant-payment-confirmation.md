# Instant Payment Confirmation

**Definition.** Instant Payment Confirmation returns real-time acceptance or rejection and funds availability to participants within seconds on instant rails, realising the immediate-confirmation behaviour of the BIAN Payment Execution service domain.
**Also known as:** Real-Time Confirmation, Positive Acknowledgement.

## Relationships
- Instant Payment Confirmation is defined in the Payments domain.
- Instant Payment Confirmation is derived from Real-Time Payments.
- Instant Payment Confirmation depends on the Data Streaming capability.

## Details
Instant Payment Confirmation grounds in the immediate-confirmation behaviour of the BIAN Payment Execution service domain. It returns real-time acceptance or rejection and funds availability to participants within seconds on instant rails. Streaming pipelines deliver the positive acknowledgement back to the originator.
