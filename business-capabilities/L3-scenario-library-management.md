# Scenario Library Management

**Definition.** Scenario Library Management curates, versions and governs macroeconomic and idiosyncratic stress scenarios and their parameters for reuse across stress tests, supporting the BCBS stress-testing principles.
**Also known as:** Scenario Design, Scenario Catalogue, Macro Scenario Management.

## Relationships
- Scenario Library Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Scenario Library Management is derived from Stress Testing Management.
- Scenario Library Management depends on the Data Warehousing capability.
- Scenario Library Management depends on the Risk Data Aggregation capability.

## Details
Scenario Library Management maintains a governed catalogue of narratives and macro variable paths (GDP, unemployment, house-price and equity indices, rates and FX) consistent with the EBA EU-wide stress test methodology and Fed CCAR supervisory scenarios. It versions baseline, adverse and severely adverse scenarios, controls scenario expansion to risk-factor shocks, and supports BCBS d450 Principle 5 (scenarios should be severe but plausible and cover firm-specific vulnerabilities). Reverse-stress scenarios are stored alongside forward scenarios.
