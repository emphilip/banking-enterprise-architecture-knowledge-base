# Account Opening

**Definition.** Account Opening is the L2 business capability for originating new deposit accounts, including product selection, eligibility, account setup, and funding, aligned to the BIAN Current Account and Savings Account fulfillment service domains.
**Also known as:** Account Origination.

## Relationships
- Account Opening is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Opening is derived from Deposits.
- Account Opening depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Account Opening is related to Account Servicing.

## Details
Account Opening maps to BIAN Current Account, Savings Account, and Term Deposit fulfillment service domains. Typical sub-functions include product selection, eligibility and screening checks, account creation in the core ledger, mandate and signatory setup, and initial funding. It underpins the Deposit Account Opening business process and relies on Customer Acquisition and KYC checks. Core banking platforms provision the account record.
