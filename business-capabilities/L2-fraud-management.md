# Fraud Management

**Definition.** Fraud Management is the business capability that detects, prevents, and investigates fraudulent activity across payments, cards, accounts, and digital channels. It corresponds to the BIAN Fraud Resolution and Fraud Detection service domains.
**Also known as:** Fraud Risk Management.

## Relationships
- Fraud Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Fraud Management is derived from Risk Management.
- Fraud Management depends on the Fraud Analytics capability.

## Details
Fraud Management spans real-time transaction scoring, device and behavioral biometrics, mule and account-takeover detection, and case investigation. It relies on streaming data and machine-learning models, with modern platforms such as Feedzai and Featurespace superseding legacy engines to reduce false positives and counter authorized push payment scams.
