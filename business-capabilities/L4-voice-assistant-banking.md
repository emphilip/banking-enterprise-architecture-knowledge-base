# Voice Assistant Banking

**Definition.** Voice Assistant Banking is the Channels & Engagement capability that enables spoken banking interactions through smart-speaker and in-app voice assistants, extending the BIAN Session Dialogue service domain to the voice modality.
**Also known as:** Voice Banking, Smart Speaker Banking.

## Relationships
- Voice Assistant Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Voice Assistant Banking is derived from Conversational Banking.
- Voice Assistant Banking depends on the Conversational AI capability.

## Details
Voice Assistant Banking extends BIAN Session Dialogue to spoken interaction over Alexa, Google Assistant and in-app voice, using ASR and NLU to fulfil balance, transaction and payment intents. The shared, often non-private nature of smart speakers makes authentication the hard problem: voice biometrics plus device-bound tokens, with sensitive actions deferred to a confirmed app and PSD2 SCA before money moves. Sensitive financial information is not read aloud without an extra factor, and voice recordings are handled under GDPR with explicit consent and retention limits. Accessibility is a strength of the channel for visually impaired customers.
