# Customer Survey Management

**Definition.** Customer Survey Management is the L4 business capability that designs, distributes and analyses customer surveys and feedback including NPS and CSAT to inform insight, realising the BIAN Customer Surveys service domain.
**Also known as:** Voice of Customer, Survey Management, Feedback Management.

## Relationships
- Customer Survey Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Survey Management is derived from Customer Insight.
- Customer Survey Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Customer Survey Management extends its parent Customer Insight within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Customer Survey Management relies on the Analytics Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Customer Survey Management at scale.
