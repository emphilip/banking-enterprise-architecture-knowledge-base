# Income Verification

**Definition.** Income Verification is the capability that confirms declared borrower income against documentary and open-banking evidence, mapping to the BIAN Customer Credit Rating service domain.
**Also known as:** Income & Expense Verification, Earnings Verification.

## Relationships
- Income Verification is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Income Verification is derived from Affordability Assessment.
- Income Verification depends on the Document Management capability.
- Income Verification depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Customer Credit Rating service domain depends on verified income inputs supplied by Income Verification. A concrete banking specific: income may be evidenced via payslips, tax returns (e.g. IRS Form 4506-C transcript requests in US mortgage lending) or, increasingly, categorised transaction data drawn through Open Banking account-information APIs to confirm salary credits.
