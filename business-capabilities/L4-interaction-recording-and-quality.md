# Interaction Recording & Quality

**Definition.** Interaction Recording & Quality is the Channels & Engagement capability that captures, stores and scores contact interactions for compliance and quality assurance, supporting the BIAN Channel Activity History and Contact Center Operations service domains.
**Also known as:** Call Recording, Contact Quality Monitoring.

## Relationships
- Interaction Recording & Quality is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Interaction Recording & Quality is derived from Inbound Contact Servicing.
- Interaction Recording & Quality depends on the Contact Center Platform capability.

## Details
Interaction Recording & Quality records voice and digital contacts, transcribes them, and scores agent performance and conduct against scorecards, writing the activity to BIAN Channel Activity History. Recording must satisfy two-party-consent disclosure rules and MiFID II requirements to record advice and order-related conversations and retain them five-plus years. PCI DSS requires card data to be paused/masked in recordings, and GDPR requires PII redaction, lawful basis and retention limits. AI-driven speech analytics now scores 100% of contacts for compliance breaches, sentiment and complaint indicators rather than a manual sample, feeding coaching and root-cause analysis.
