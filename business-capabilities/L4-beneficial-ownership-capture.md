# Beneficial Ownership Capture

**Definition.** Beneficial Ownership Capture records account holders, joint parties and signatory arrangements at opening, supporting the party-linkage behaviour of the BIAN deposit account service domains.
**Also known as:** Signatory Capture, Joint Holder Setup.

## Relationships
- Beneficial Ownership Capture is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Beneficial Ownership Capture is derived from Deposit Account Origination.
- Beneficial Ownership Capture depends on the Deposit Account Management capability.

## Details
BIAN deposit account party-linkage records holders and signatories; for legal-entity customers the FinCEN Beneficial Ownership rule (31 CFR 1010.230) requires identifying each individual owning 25% or more and one control person at account opening.
