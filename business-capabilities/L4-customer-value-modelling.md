# Customer Value Modelling

**Definition.** Customer Value Modelling is the L4 business capability that estimates customer lifetime value, profitability and propensity to support segmentation, pricing and targeting, supporting the BIAN Customer Behavior Insights service domain.
**Also known as:** Lifetime Value Modelling, CLV Analytics, Propensity Modelling.

## Relationships
- Customer Value Modelling is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Value Modelling is derived from Customer Insight.
- Customer Value Modelling depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Customer Value Modelling extends its parent Customer Insight within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Customer Value Modelling relies on the Analytics Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Customer Value Modelling at scale.
