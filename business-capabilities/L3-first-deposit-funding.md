# First Deposit Funding

**Definition.** First Deposit Funding handles the initial funding and minimum-balance settlement of a newly opened account, aligned to the BIAN Current Account and Savings Account fulfilment behaviours for initial deposit capture.
**Also known as:** Initial Funding, Account Funding.

## Relationships
- First Deposit Funding is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- First Deposit Funding is derived from Account Opening.
- First Deposit Funding depends on the Transaction Posting Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current/Savings Account fulfilment behaviours capture the opening deposit; funds availability for that first deposit is governed by Regulation CC (12 CFR 229) hold rules, with next-day availability for cash and certain check deposits.
