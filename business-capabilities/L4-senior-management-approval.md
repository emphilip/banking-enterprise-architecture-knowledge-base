# Senior Management Approval

**Definition.** Senior Management Approval captures the documented escalation and sign-off required to onboard or retain higher-risk and PEP relationships, per FATF Recommendation 12 and UK MLR 2017 regulation 35.
**Also known as:** EDD Approval, High-Risk Sign-Off.

## Relationships
- Senior Management Approval is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Senior Management Approval is derived from Enhanced Due Diligence Management.
- Senior Management Approval depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Senior Management Approval depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
FATF R.12 requires obtaining senior-management approval before establishing (or continuing, for existing customers) a business relationship with a PEP. UK MLR 2017 regulation 35 specifically mandates senior-management approval for PEP relationships, with regulation 33 requiring it for other high-risk cases. This capability routes EDD cases to the appropriate approver level based on residual risk, records the rationale and decision, and maintains the audit trail that examiners review to confirm the control operated before account activation.
