# Performance Measurement

**Definition.** Performance Measurement computes portfolio returns, benchmarks, and attribution and reports GIPS-compliant results; it informs the BIAN Investment Portfolio Analysis service domain.
**Also known as:** Performance & Attribution, GIPS Reporting.

## Relationships
- Performance Measurement is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Performance Measurement is derived from Portfolio Management.
- Performance Measurement depends on the Portfolio Management System capability.
- Performance Measurement depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Investment Portfolio Analysis service domain assesses portfolio outcomes. Concretely, Performance Measurement calculates time-weighted return (TWR) to remove the effect of client cash flows and money-weighted return where the client controls timing, and presents results under the CFA Institute Global Investment Performance Standards (GIPS), including composite construction and disclosure for fair, comparable reporting.
