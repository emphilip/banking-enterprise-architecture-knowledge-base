# Deposit Account Origination

**Definition.** Deposit Account Origination establishes a new deposit relationship and account record across the BIAN Current Account, Savings Account and Term Deposit service domains, from application capture to account instantiation on the ledger.
**Also known as:** Account Origination Capability, New Account Establishment.

## Relationships
- Deposit Account Origination is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Account Origination is derived from Account Opening.
- Deposit Account Origination depends on the Deposit Account Management capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current Account, Savings Account and Term Deposit service domains govern account establishment; US origination must complete CIP identity verification (collecting name, DOB, address and TIN under 31 CFR 1020.220) before the account is opened.
