# Customer Servicing

**Definition.** Customer Servicing is the L2 business capability for handling customer enquiries, requests, and complaints across channels, providing post-sale support aligned to the BIAN Customer Case Management and Servicing Order service domains.
**Also known as:** Customer Service.

## Relationships
- Customer Servicing is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Servicing is derived from Customer Lifecycle Management.
- Customer Servicing depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Customer Servicing depends on the Conversational AI capability.

## Details
Customer Servicing covers BIAN service domains such as Customer Case Management, Servicing Order, and Customer Behavior Insights. Typical sub-functions include enquiry handling, service request fulfilment, and complaint resolution, and it is the parent of Case Management. It underpins the Service Request Handling and Complaint Handling business processes. Conversational AI and generative AI increasingly assist agents and self-service interactions.
