# Cross-Border Fee Calculation

**Definition.** Cross-Border Fee Calculation determines and allocates correspondent, lifting and scheme charges (e.g. OUR/SHA/BEN) on international payments, supporting the BIAN Correspondent Bank Operations service domain.
**Also known as:** Charge Bearer Handling, Correspondent Fees.

## Relationships
- Cross-Border Fee Calculation is defined in the Payments domain.
- Cross-Border Fee Calculation is derived from Cross-Border Payments.
- Cross-Border Fee Calculation depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Cross-Border Fee Calculation grounds in the BIAN Correspondent Bank Operations service domain. It determines and allocates correspondent, lifting and scheme charges on international payments according to the charge bearer (OUR/SHA/BEN). It computes deductions and beneficiary amounts along the payment chain.
