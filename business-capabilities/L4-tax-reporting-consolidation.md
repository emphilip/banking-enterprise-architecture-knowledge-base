# Tax Reporting Consolidation

**Definition.** Tax Reporting Consolidation aggregates interest and tax activity into year-end customer tax reports, realising the consolidation-and-reporting behaviour of the BIAN Customer Tax Handling service domain.
**Also known as:** Year-End Tax Reporting, Tax Statement Consolidation.

## Relationships
- Tax Reporting Consolidation is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Tax Reporting Consolidation is derived from Deposit Tax Withholding.
- Tax Reporting Consolidation depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
The BIAN Customer Tax Handling consolidation-and-reporting behaviour produces year-end forms; banks must file IRS Form 1099-INT for each customer paid $10 or more in interest, and furnish the recipient copy by January 31.
