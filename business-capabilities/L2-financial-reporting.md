# Financial Reporting

**Definition.** Financial Reporting is the business capability that produces statutory, management, and external financial statements and analyses. It aligns with the BIAN Financial Statements service domain.
**Also known as:** Management Reporting.

## Relationships
- Financial Reporting is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Financial Reporting is derived from Finance Management.
- Financial Reporting depends on the General Ledger Engine capability.

## Details
Financial Reporting prepares balance sheets, income statements, consolidation, segment and profitability reporting, and disclosures under IFRS and US GAAP. It draws on the general ledger and data warehousing for consolidated and analytical reporting, with finance data hubs and reporting automation accelerating close-to-disclose cycles.
