# Payment Rail Connectivity

**Definition.** Payment Rail Connectivity manages the technical and operational links to clearing rails and scheme gateways (ACH, Faster Payments, FedNow, SWIFT), realising the rail-facing mechanics of the BIAN Payment Execution service domain.
**Also known as:** Rail Integration, Scheme Connectivity, Gateway Management.

## Relationships
- Payment Rail Connectivity is defined in the Payments domain.
- Payment Rail Connectivity is derived from Payment Execution.
- Payment Rail Connectivity depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Payment Rail Connectivity grounds in the rail-facing mechanics of the BIAN Payment Execution service domain. It maintains technical and operational links to clearing rails and scheme gateways including ACH, Faster Payments, FedNow and SWIFT. It manages adapters, certificates and session handling to dispatch and receive scheme traffic.
