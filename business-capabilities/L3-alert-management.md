# Alert Management

**Definition.** Alert Management triages, prioritises and dispositions AML and sanctions alerts to reduce false positives and route true risk for investigation, consistent with Wolfsberg Group monitoring guidance.
**Also known as:** AML Alert Triage, Alert Disposition.

## Relationships
- Alert Management is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Alert Management is derived from AML Monitoring.
- Alert Management depends on the Machine Learning Platform capability.
- Alert Management depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.

## Details
The Wolfsberg Group statement on effectiveness stresses that monitoring programs should focus resources on the highest-risk alerts rather than processing volume for its own sake; transaction-monitoring systems commonly generate very high false-positive volumes, so triage and disposition discipline is essential. Alert Management captures L1 triage decisions, escalation to investigation, and the audit trail of dispositions that feed BSA suspicious-activity decisions and FinCEN SAR filing where warranted.
