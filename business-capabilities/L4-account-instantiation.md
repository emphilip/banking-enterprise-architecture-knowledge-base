# Account Instantiation

**Definition.** Account Instantiation creates the account record, number and ledger entry on the core platform, realising the establish behaviour of the BIAN Deposit Account service domain.
**Also known as:** Account Record Creation, Account Provisioning.

## Relationships
- Account Instantiation is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Instantiation is derived from Deposit Account Origination.
- Account Instantiation depends on the Deposit Account Management capability.

## Details
The BIAN Deposit Account establish behaviour provisions the account of record; the assigned account number and routing/transit number conform to ABA numbering so the account can receive ACH and wire credits, and the account is tagged to an FDIC deposit-insurance ownership category.
