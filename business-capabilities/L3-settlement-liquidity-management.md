# Settlement Liquidity Management

**Definition.** Settlement Liquidity Management positions and funds settlement accounts (including FedNow liquidity management transfers) to ensure obligations can be met across 24/7 instant-payment cycles, supporting the settlement side of the BIAN Clearing and Settlement area.
**Also known as:** Settlement Funding, Liquidity Positioning, LMT.

## Relationships
- Settlement Liquidity Management is defined in the Payments domain.
- Settlement Liquidity Management is derived from Payment Clearing & Settlement.
- Settlement Liquidity Management depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Settlement Liquidity Management grounds in the settlement side of the BIAN Clearing and Settlement area. It positions and funds settlement accounts, including FedNow liquidity management transfers, so obligations can be met across 24/7 instant-payment cycles. It monitors balances and triggers funding actions to avoid settlement shortfalls.
