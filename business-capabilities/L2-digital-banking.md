# Digital Banking

**Definition.** Digital Banking is the business capability that delivers self-service banking through web and mobile applications, including account access, payments, and servicing. It corresponds to the BIAN Digital Channel service domain.
**Also known as:** Digital Channels.

## Relationships
- Digital Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Banking is derived from Channel Management.
- Digital Banking depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.

## Details
Digital Banking encompasses authentication, dashboards, money movement, alerts, and personalized engagement across mobile and online sub-capabilities. Composable engagement-banking platforms such as Backbase and Q2 supersede legacy internet banking, integrating with API management and customer identity to deliver secure, real-time, personalized experiences.
