# Credit Risk Assessment

**Definition.** Credit Risk Assessment evaluates obligor and facility creditworthiness, estimating default likelihood and loss to inform limits and pricing, realised through the BIAN Credit Risk Models and Credit Risk Operating Model service domains.
**Also known as:** Counterparty Credit Assessment, Credit Risk Analysis.

## Relationships
- Credit Risk Assessment is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Credit Risk Assessment is derived from Credit Risk Management.
- Credit Risk Assessment depends on the Machine Learning Platform capability.
- Credit Risk Assessment depends on the Risk Analytics Engine capability.

## Details
Credit Risk Assessment analyses financial statements, behavioural data and bureau information to judge an obligor's capacity and willingness to repay. Under the Basel IRB approach (CRE30) it distinguishes obligor-grade assessment (driving PD) from facility-grade assessment (driving LGD and EAD), and separates the foundation IRB (regulatory LGD/EAD) from advanced IRB (own estimates). Assessment outputs feed the master-scale rating, pricing, and limit setting, with a clear split between the assessment and the independent validation of its models.
