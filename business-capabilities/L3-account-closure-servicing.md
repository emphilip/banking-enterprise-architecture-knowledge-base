# Account Closure Servicing

**Definition.** Account Closure Servicing manages final-balance settlement, residual interest and orderly closure of deposit accounts, realising the closure behaviour of the BIAN Current Account and Savings Account service domains.
**Also known as:** Deposit Closure, Account Termination.

## Relationships
- Account Closure Servicing is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Closure Servicing is derived from Account Servicing.
- Account Closure Servicing depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current/Savings Account closure behaviour settles the final balance; closure must pay residual accrued interest and, where the balance is escheatable, route remaining funds to unclaimed-property handling rather than retaining them.
