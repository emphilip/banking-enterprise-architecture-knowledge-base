# Instant Payment Clearing

**Definition.** Instant Payment Clearing executes immediate, irrevocable individual clearing of instant credit transfers on rails such as RTP and FedNow 24/7/365, a real-time specialisation within the BIAN Payment Execution service domain.
**Also known as:** RTP Clearing, FedNow Clearing.

## Relationships
- Instant Payment Clearing is defined in the Payments domain.
- Instant Payment Clearing is derived from Real-Time Payments.
- Instant Payment Clearing depends on the Data Streaming capability.

## Details
Instant Payment Clearing grounds in the BIAN Payment Execution service domain as a real-time specialisation. It executes immediate, irrevocable individual clearing of instant credit transfers on rails such as RTP and FedNow, operating 24/7/365. Low-latency event processing handles each message individually.
