# Loan Servicing

**Definition.** Loan Servicing is the L2 business capability for administering loans through their life, including repayment scheduling, interest accrual, statements, and amendments, aligned to the BIAN Loan service domain.
**Also known as:** Loan Administration.

## Relationships
- Loan Servicing is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Servicing is derived from Lending.
- Loan Servicing depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Loan Servicing is related to Collections & Recovery.

## Details
Loan Servicing maps to the BIAN Loan and Consumer Loan service domains. Typical sub-functions include drawdown management, repayment processing, interest and fee accrual, restructuring and amendments, payoff, and statement generation. It handovers delinquent accounts to Collections & Recovery. Core banking platforms realize the underlying loan accounting and servicing ledger.
