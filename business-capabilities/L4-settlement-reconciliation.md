# Settlement Reconciliation

**Definition.** Settlement Reconciliation matches settled positions against expected obligations and resolves breaks across scheme and central-bank accounts, supporting the settlement functions of the BIAN Clearing and Settlement area.
**Also known as:** Settlement Matching, Position Reconciliation.

## Relationships
- Settlement Reconciliation is defined in the Payments domain.
- Settlement Reconciliation is derived from Interbank Settlement.
- Settlement Reconciliation depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Settlement Reconciliation grounds in the settlement functions of the BIAN Clearing and Settlement area. It matches settled positions against expected obligations and resolves breaks across scheme and central-bank accounts. It is distinct from the process Reconciliation & Settlement, focusing on the capability of position matching.
