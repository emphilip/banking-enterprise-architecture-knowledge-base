# Contact Complaint Intake

**Definition.** Contact Complaint Intake is the Channels & Engagement capability that captures and triages complaints raised through contact-center channels and routes them to resolution, fronting the BIAN Servicing Issue service domain; it is distinct from the Customer Management Complaint Management capability.
**Also known as:** Complaint Capture, Complaint Logging (Channel).

## Relationships
- Contact Complaint Intake is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Contact Complaint Intake is derived from Contact Center.
- Contact Complaint Intake depends on the Contact Center Platform capability.

## Details
Contact Complaint Intake fronts BIAN Servicing Issue at the channel edge: it recognises an expression of dissatisfaction in voice or chat, classifies it, and opens a complaint case routed to Complaint Management. Regulated timelines govern it, the FCA DISP rules require prompt acknowledgement and a final response within eight weeks, and the US CFPB tracks complaint handling, so the intake stamps receipt time and category at first contact. Omnichannel context links the complaint to the originating interaction and any related service case so root-cause analytics downstream sees the full thread.
