# Deposits

**Definition.** Deposits is the L1 business capability covering the bank's ability to open, service, and administer current, savings, and term deposit accounts, including interest and overdraft handling, aligned to the BIAN Current Account and Deposit reference business areas.
**Also known as:** Deposit Services.

## Relationships
- Deposits is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposits depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Deposits is related to Payment Services.
- Deposits is related to Lending.

## Details
Deposits spans BIAN service domains including Current Account, Savings Account, Term Deposit, and Deposit Account. It decomposes into Account Opening, Account Servicing, Interest Calculation, and Overdraft Management. Typical scope covers account origination, transaction posting, balance and interest management, statements, and overdraft facilities. Cloud-native core banking platforms increasingly realize real-time, product-flexible deposit processing.
