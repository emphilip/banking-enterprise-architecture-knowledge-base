# Party Relationship Mapping

**Definition.** Party Relationship Mapping is the L4 business capability that records hierarchies and links between parties such as households, beneficial ownership and group structures within the BIAN Party Reference Data Directory service domain.
**Also known as:** Relationship Hierarchy Management, Household Linking, Beneficial Ownership Mapping.

## Relationships
- Party Relationship Mapping is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Party Relationship Mapping is derived from Party Reference Data.
- Party Relationship Mapping depends on the Master Data Management capability.

## Details
Party Relationship Mapping extends its parent Party Reference Data within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Party Relationship Mapping relies on the Master Data Management technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Party Relationship Mapping at scale.
