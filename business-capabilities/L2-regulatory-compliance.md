# Regulatory Compliance

**Definition.** Regulatory Compliance is the business capability that ensures the bank adheres to applicable laws, regulations, and supervisory expectations across jurisdictions. It corresponds to the BIAN Regulatory Compliance service domain.
**Also known as:** Compliance Management.

## Relationships
- Regulatory Compliance is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Regulatory Compliance is derived from Financial Crime Compliance.
- Regulatory Compliance depends on the Data Governance capability.

## Details
Regulatory Compliance covers regulatory change management, policy and obligation mapping, controls monitoring, conduct compliance, and regulatory reporting. It relies on data governance and lineage for trusted regulatory data, with RegTech tooling automating horizon scanning and evidencing compliance to supervisors.
