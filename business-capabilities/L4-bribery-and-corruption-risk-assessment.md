# Bribery & Corruption Risk Assessment

**Definition.** Bribery & Corruption Risk Assessment evaluates corruption exposure across markets, intermediaries and transactions to size ABC controls, per US FCPA guidance and the UK Bribery Act 2010 "adequate procedures" principle.
**Also known as:** ABC Risk Assessment, Corruption Risk Scoring.

## Relationships

- Bribery & Corruption Risk Assessment depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Bribery & Corruption Risk Assessment depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
The UK Ministry of Justice guidance on the Bribery Act names risk assessment as one of the six principles for adequate procedures, requiring periodic, informed and documented assessment of internal and external bribery risk. The DOJ/SEC FCPA Resource Guide expects risk-based compliance, weighting controls toward higher-risk markets (e.g. high on the Transparency International Corruption Perceptions Index), public-official touchpoints, and use of third-party intermediaries. Scores drive the depth of integrity due diligence and approval thresholds for gifts and hospitality.
