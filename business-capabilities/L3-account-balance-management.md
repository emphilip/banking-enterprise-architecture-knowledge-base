# Account Balance Management

**Definition.** Account Balance Management maintains available, ledger and cleared balances and applies funds-availability rules, supporting the position-keeping behaviour of the BIAN Current Account service domain.
**Also known as:** Balance Keeping, Available Balance Control.

## Relationships
- Account Balance Management is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Balance Management is derived from Account Servicing.
- Account Balance Management depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current Account position-keeping behaviour distinguishes ledger from available balance; available-balance computation depends on Regulation CC (12 CFR 229) deposit-hold schedules that determine when deposited funds become withdrawable.
