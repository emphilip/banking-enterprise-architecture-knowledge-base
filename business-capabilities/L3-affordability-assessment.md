# Affordability Assessment

**Definition.** Affordability Assessment is the capability that evaluates a borrower's income, expenditure and serviceability against a proposed loan, mapping to the BIAN Customer Credit Rating and Credit Management service domains.
**Also known as:** Serviceability Assessment, Affordability Check.

## Relationships
- Affordability Assessment is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Affordability Assessment is derived from Loan Origination.
- Affordability Assessment depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.
- Affordability Assessment depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Customer Credit Rating service domain maintains the bank's assessment of a customer's creditworthiness, which Affordability Assessment consumes alongside disposable-income analysis. A concrete banking specific: UK lenders must perform a Financial Conduct Authority CONC 5.2A creditworthiness and affordability check before granting regulated consumer credit, testing whether repayments are sustainable without the borrower having to borrow further.
