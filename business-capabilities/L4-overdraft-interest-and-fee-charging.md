# Overdraft Interest & Fee Charging

**Definition.** Overdraft Interest & Fee Charging computes and applies debit interest and overdraft fees on utilised and unarranged balances, supporting the charging behaviour of the BIAN Overdraft Facility service domain.
**Also known as:** Debit Interest Charging, Overdraft Fee Application.

## Relationships
- Overdraft Interest & Fee Charging is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Interest & Fee Charging is derived from Unauthorised Overdraft Handling.
- Overdraft Interest & Fee Charging depends on the Interest & Charges Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Overdraft Facility charging behaviour applies debit interest and fees; overdraft and NSF fees are constrained by Regulation E (12 CFR 1005.17) opt-in for ATM/one-time debit transactions and by CFPB UDAAP scrutiny of representment and authorize-positive-settle-negative fee practices.
