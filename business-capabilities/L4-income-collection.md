# Income Collection

**Definition.** Income Collection collects dividends, interest, and other income on held securities and credits client accounts; it supports the BIAN Custody Administration service domain.
**Also known as:** Dividend Collection, Coupon Processing.

## Relationships
- Income Collection is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Income Collection is derived from Custody & Safekeeping.
- Income Collection depends on the Order Management System capability.
- Income Collection depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
Income Collection accrues entitlements on record date and posts cash on pay-date for cash dividends and bond coupons, reconciling receipts against the depository (DTC pays most US distributions to participants). It handles record-date versus settlement-date claims for trades around ex-date, applies withholding tax and treaty relief for cross-border holdings, and credits net income to client accounts through the BIAN Custody Administration service domain.
