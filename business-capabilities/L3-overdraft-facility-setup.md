# Overdraft Facility Setup

**Definition.** Overdraft Facility Setup establishes authorised overdraft limits, terms and pricing against current accounts, realising the facility-arrangement behaviour of the BIAN Overdraft Facility service domain.
**Also known as:** Overdraft Arrangement, Limit Setup.

## Relationships
- Overdraft Facility Setup is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Facility Setup is derived from Overdraft Management.
- Overdraft Facility Setup depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Overdraft Facility service domain arranges authorised limits; for ATM and one-time debit-card overdrafts, Regulation E (12 CFR 1005.17) requires the bank to obtain the consumer's affirmative opt-in before charging overdraft fees on those transactions.
