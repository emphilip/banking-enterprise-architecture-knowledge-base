# Loan Application Management

**Definition.** Loan Application Management is the capability that captures, validates and progresses loan and credit applications, mapping to the BIAN Consumer Loan and Corporate Loan service domains at the application-intake stage.
**Also known as:** Application Intake (Lending), Credit Application Handling.

## Relationships
- Loan Application Management is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Application Management is derived from Loan Origination.
- Loan Application Management depends on the Loan Origination Platform capability.
- Loan Application Management depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Consumer Loan and Corporate Loan service domains fulfil and service individual loan agreements; Loan Application Management exercises their Initiate and Update service operations as an application moves from submission to a complete, decision-ready file. It aligns to APQC PCF category 9.0 (Manage Financial Resources) lending intake activities. A concrete banking specific: a consumer loan or credit-card application must be logged and tracked end-to-end so that, where it is declined, an adverse-action notice can be issued under the US ECOA / Regulation B within 30 days.
