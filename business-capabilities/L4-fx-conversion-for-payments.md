# FX Conversion For Payments

**Definition.** FX Conversion For Payments applies currency conversion, rate sourcing and margin to cross-border instructions, supporting the multi-currency execution served by the BIAN Payment Execution and Correspondent Bank Operations service domains.
**Also known as:** Payment FX, Currency Conversion.

## Relationships
- FX Conversion For Payments is defined in the Payments domain.
- FX Conversion For Payments is derived from Cross-Border Payments.
- FX Conversion For Payments depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
FX Conversion For Payments grounds in the multi-currency execution served by the BIAN Payment Execution and Correspondent Bank Operations service domains. It applies currency conversion, rate sourcing and margin to cross-border instructions. It selects rates and computes converted amounts before the payment is dispatched.
