# Service Fulfilment

**Definition.** Service Fulfilment is the L3 business capability that executes customer servicing requests and orders that may span multiple products and processing steps, realising the BIAN Servicing Order service domain.
**Also known as:** Service Order Fulfilment, Servicing Order Management, Request Fulfilment.

## Relationships
- Service Fulfilment is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Service Fulfilment is derived from Customer Servicing.
- Service Fulfilment depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Service Fulfilment extends its parent Customer Servicing within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Service Fulfilment relies on the Workflow Orchestration technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Service Fulfilment at scale.
