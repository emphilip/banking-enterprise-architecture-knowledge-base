# Budgeting & Forecasting

**Definition.** Budgeting & Forecasting prepares annual budgets, rolling forecasts and variance analysis, aligning to APQC PCF "Perform planning and management accounting".
**Also known as:** Planning & Forecasting, Budget Management, Variance Analysis.

## Relationships
- Budgeting & Forecasting is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Budgeting & Forecasting is derived from Internal Management Reporting.
- Budgeting & Forecasting depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Budgeting & Forecasting builds the bank's income, cost, balance-sheet and headcount plans bottom-up and top-down, maintains driver-based rolling forecasts, and reports actual-versus-budget variances with explanations. APQC frames it under "Perform planning and management accounting" and benchmarks planning cycle time and forecast accuracy. It feeds the financial baseline for capital and liquidity planning, and modern connected-planning tools enable continuous re-forecasting against actuals rather than a single annual budget event.
