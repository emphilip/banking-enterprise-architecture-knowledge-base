# Customer Insight

**Definition.** Customer Insight is the L3 business capability that derives analytical understanding of customer needs, value, propensity and lifetime value to inform segmentation and targeting, mapping to the BIAN Customer Behavior Insights and Customer Surveys service domains.
**Also known as:** Customer Analytics, Customer Intelligence, Customer Value Analytics.

## Relationships
- Customer Insight is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Insight is derived from Customer Segmentation.
- Customer Insight depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Customer Insight extends its parent Customer Segmentation within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Customer Insight relies on the Analytics Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Customer Insight at scale.
