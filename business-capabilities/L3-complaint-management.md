# Complaint Management

**Definition.** Complaint Management is the L3 business capability that logs, investigates, resolves and reports customer complaints with regulatory tracking and root-cause feedback, mapping to the BIAN Customer Case service domain.
**Also known as:** Complaints Management, Grievance Management.

## Relationships
- Complaint Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Management is derived from Customer Servicing.
- Complaint Management depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Complaint Management instantiates the BIAN Customer Case service domain to log, investigate, resolve and report complaints, and aligns to APQC PCF category 6.0 (Manage Customer Service). In UK retail banking it operationalises the FCA DISP rules — prompt acknowledgement, investigating each complaint competently, diligently and impartially, issuing a final response within the regulatory deadline, and feeding root-cause analysis back to the business — coordinated through the Workflow Orchestration technology capability with audit trails and management information.
