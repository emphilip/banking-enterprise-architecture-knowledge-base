# Complaint Management

**Definition.** Complaint Management is the L3 business capability that logs, investigates, resolves and reports customer complaints with regulatory tracking and root-cause feedback, mapping to the BIAN Customer Case Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Complaints Management, Grievance Management.

## Relationships
- Complaint Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Management is derived from Customer Servicing.
- Complaint Management depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Complaint Management extends its parent Customer Servicing within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Complaint Management relies on the Workflow Orchestration technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Complaint Management at scale.
