# Customer Profile Management

**Definition.** Customer Profile Management is the L3 business capability that curates and maintains the attributes, preferences, contact details, and consents that make up an individual customer profile within the party record.
**Also known as:** Profile Management.

## Relationships
- Customer Profile Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Profile Management is derived from Party Data Management.
- Customer Profile Management depends on the Master Data Management capability.
- Customer Profile Management is related to Customer Segmentation.

## Details
Customer Profile Management maps to the BIAN Customer Profile and Customer Reference Data Management service domains. Typical sub-functions include preference and consent management, contact point maintenance, and profile enrichment. It depends on the golden record produced by Party Data Management and supplies attributes used by Customer Segmentation and Relationship Management. Privacy and consent handling are central given GDPR-style obligations.
