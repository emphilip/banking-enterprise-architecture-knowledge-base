# Conversational Banking

**Definition.** Conversational Banking is the Channels & Engagement capability that delivers banking interactions through chat, messaging and voice assistants, fronting the BIAN Session Dialogue and Servicing Issue service domains; it is a digital-engagement practice extension realised on conversational AI rather than a single BIAN domain.
**Also known as:** Chat Banking, Messaging Banking.

## Relationships
- Conversational Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Conversational Banking is derived from Digital Banking.
- Conversational Banking depends on the Conversational AI capability.
- Conversational Banking depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.

## Details
Conversational Banking layers natural-language interaction over BIAN Session Dialogue, holding intent, slots and authentication state across turns and across handed-off channels (WhatsApp, in-app chat, RCS, voice). Authenticated transactions still require PSD2 SCA before money moves, so the assistant escalates to a step-up factor mid-conversation. The Session Dialogue domain preserves omnichannel context so a chat thread can resume with a live agent without losing history. Generative-AI assistants add retrieval-grounded answers but require guardrails against hallucinated financial advice and logging for conduct review.
