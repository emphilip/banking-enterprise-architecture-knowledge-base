# Party Data Quality

**Definition.** Party Data Quality is the L3 business capability that profiles, cleanses, de-duplicates and standardises party data to sustain accuracy and completeness across the golden record, supporting the BIAN Party Reference Data Directory service domain.
**Also known as:** Customer Data Quality, Data Cleansing, Deduplication Management.

## Relationships
- Party Data Quality is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Party Data Quality is derived from Party Data Management.
- Party Data Quality depends on the Master Data Management capability.

## Details
Party Data Quality extends its parent Party Data Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Party Data Quality relies on the Master Data Management technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Party Data Quality at scale.
