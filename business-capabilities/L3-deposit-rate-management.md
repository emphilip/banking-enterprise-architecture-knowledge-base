# Deposit Rate Management

**Definition.** Deposit Rate Management defines, versions and applies credit-interest rate tables, tiers and promotional rates, realising the BIAN Interest Determination and Interest Rate Management service domains.
**Also known as:** Interest Rate Management, Rate Table Management.

## Relationships
- Deposit Rate Management is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Rate Management is derived from Interest Calculation.
- Deposit Rate Management depends on the Interest & Charges Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Interest Determination/Interest Rate Management domains hold rate tables; a change to a tier or promotional rate that reduces the customer's APY triggers Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030.5) advance change-in-terms notice requirements.
