# Branch Servicing

**Definition.** Branch Servicing is the Channels & Engagement capability that handles assisted account maintenance, enquiries and case raising at the branch, fronting the BIAN Customer Workbench and Servicing Issue service domains in a face-to-face setting.
**Also known as:** In-Branch Servicing, Assisted Servicing.

## Relationships
- Branch Servicing is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Branch Servicing is derived from Branch Banking.
- Branch Servicing depends on the CRM Platform capability.

## Details
Branch Servicing surfaces the BIAN Customer Workbench to a branch colleague who completes assisted maintenance: address and mandate changes, statement reprints, dispute lodging and beneficiary updates. Identity at the counter is verified to FFIEC authentication expectations using government ID rather than a digital factor, and high-risk changes require dual authorisation. Servicing cases opened here carry omnichannel context so a customer can continue the same case later in-app or by phone via Servicing Issue. ADA accessibility and branch-level disclosure rules govern the physical and procedural experience.
