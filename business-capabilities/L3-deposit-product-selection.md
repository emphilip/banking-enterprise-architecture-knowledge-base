# Deposit Product Selection

**Definition.** Deposit Product Selection matches a customer's needs to an eligible deposit product and its terms, drawing on the BIAN Product Directory and Sales Product service domains to present and configure current, savings and term-deposit offerings.
**Also known as:** Product Matching, Deposit Product Fit.

## Relationships
- Deposit Product Selection is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Product Selection is derived from Account Opening.
- Deposit Product Selection depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Product Directory/Sales Product domains hold product terms; under Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030) the bank must disclose the annual percentage yield, rate, minimum-balance and fee schedule before the consumer selects a deposit account.
