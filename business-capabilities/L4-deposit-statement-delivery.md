# Deposit Statement Delivery

**Definition.** Deposit Statement Delivery dispatches statements and advices across paper and digital channels per customer preference, supporting the delivery behaviour of the BIAN Account Statement service domain.
**Also known as:** Statement Dispatch, Advice Delivery.

## Relationships
- Deposit Statement Delivery is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Statement Delivery is derived from Deposit Statement & Reporting.
- Deposit Statement Delivery depends on the Notification Services capability.

## Details
The BIAN Account Statement delivery behaviour distributes statements; electronic delivery of a required disclosure or statement is only permitted after the consumer's consent under the E-SIGN Act (15 USC 7001), otherwise paper delivery is required.
