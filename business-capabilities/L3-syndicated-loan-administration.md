# Syndicated Loan Administration

**Definition.** Syndicated Loan Administration is the capability that coordinates participations, drawdowns and distributions across lender groups, mapping to the BIAN Loan Syndication service domain.
**Also known as:** Syndication Servicing, Participation Management.

## Relationships
- Syndicated Loan Administration is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Syndicated Loan Administration is derived from Loan Servicing.
- Syndicated Loan Administration depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Syndicated Loan Administration depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Loan Syndication service domain administers facilities funded by a group of lenders under a single agreement, which Syndicated Loan Administration services from the agent-bank role. A concrete banking specific: the agent calculates each lender's pro-rata share of drawdowns, interest and fees and distributes them, commonly settling and documenting trades and positions on platforms aligned to LSTA market standards.
