# Compliance Training Management

**Definition.** Compliance Training Management delivers and attests role-based AML and sanctions training, the BSA "training" pillar and the OFAC 2019 Framework training component.
**Also known as:** AML Training, Awareness Management.

## Relationships
- Compliance Training Management is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Compliance Training Management is derived from Compliance Policy Management.
- Compliance Training Management depends on the Document Management capability.
- Compliance Training Management depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
Training is one of the five BSA/AML pillars: the FFIEC manual expects ongoing, risk-based training tailored to staff roles (front-line, operations, the BSA officer, the board) covering red flags, CTR/SAR obligations and the institution's policies. The OFAC Framework similarly requires periodic sanctions training for all relevant personnel. This capability assigns curricula by role, schedules refresher cycles, records completion and attestation, and reports gaps — providing examiners the evidence that staff are equipped to detect and escalate financial crime.
