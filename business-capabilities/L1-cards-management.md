# Cards Management

**Definition.** Cards Management is the top-level business capability that governs the issuance, authorization, servicing, and lifecycle of debit, credit, and prepaid cards across a bank's card portfolio. It aligns with the BIAN card-related service domains and the APQC card management process group.
**Also known as:** Card Services Capability.

## Relationships
- Cards Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Cards Management depends on the Card Processing capability.
- Cards Management is related to Card Issuing.

## Details
Cards Management spans card product design, BIN management, network connectivity to Visa and Mastercard, and downstream settlement. Modern implementations decompose this capability into issuing, authorization, dispute, and lifecycle sub-capabilities delivered via cloud-native processors such as Marqeta and Galileo, superseding legacy card management systems. It coordinates with fraud and compliance capabilities to control card-not-present risk and interchange economics.
