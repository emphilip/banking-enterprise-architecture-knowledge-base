# Channel Management

**Definition.** Channel Management is the top-level business capability that orchestrates customer interactions consistently across digital, branch, contact center, and ATM channels. It aligns with the BIAN Channel Management service domains.
**Also known as:** Channels.

## Relationships
- Channel Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Channel Management depends on the Integration Platform capability.
- Channel Management is related to Digital Banking.

## Details
Channel Management governs omnichannel journeys, session continuity, and consistent product and servicing experiences across assisted and self-service touchpoints. It relies on integration and API platforms to expose shared services, with engagement-banking platforms unifying digital, branch, and contact-center experiences on a common orchestration layer.
