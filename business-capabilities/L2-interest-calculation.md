# Interest Calculation

**Definition.** Interest Calculation is the L2 business capability for accruing, computing, and posting credit and debit interest on deposit accounts according to product rates, tiers, and accrual conventions, aligned to BIAN deposit account service domains.
**Also known as:** Interest Processing.

## Relationships
- Interest Calculation is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Interest Calculation is derived from Deposits.
- Interest Calculation depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Interest Calculation is related to Account Servicing.

## Details
Interest Calculation maps to BIAN Current Account, Savings Account, and Term Deposit service domains. Typical sub-functions include daily accrual, tiered and promotional rate application, capitalisation and posting, and tax withholding where applicable. It depends on the product and rate configuration held in the core banking platform. Accurate accrual is essential for net interest income and customer statements.
