# Funding Risk Management

**Definition.** Funding Risk Management monitors funding concentration, maturity mismatch and contingency funding, running the ILAAP and contingency funding plan, aligned with the Basel sound liquidity principles and supervisory ILAAP expectations.
**Also known as:** Contingency Funding, ILAAP, Funding Concentration Management.

## Relationships
- Funding Risk Management is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Funding Risk Management is derived from Liquidity Risk Management.
- Funding Risk Management depends on the Risk Analytics Engine capability.
- Funding Risk Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Funding Risk Management diversifies and monitors funding by source, counterparty, currency, instrument and tenor to limit concentration, consistent with the BCBS d188 principles on funding strategy and diversification. It maintains the Contingency Funding Plan with early-warning indicators and graduated liquidity actions, and produces the Internal Liquidity Adequacy Assessment Process (ILAAP) demonstrating sufficient liquidity resources under stress. It incorporates the supervisory lessons of BCBS d144 on liquidity buffers and cross-border funding.
