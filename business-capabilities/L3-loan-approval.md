# Loan Approval

**Definition.** Loan Approval is the capability that applies delegated authority, sanctioning and offer issuance to credit applications, mapping to the BIAN Credit Management and Consumer Loan service domains.
**Also known as:** Credit Sanctioning, Offer Issuance.

## Relationships
- Loan Approval is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Approval is derived from Loan Origination.
- Loan Approval depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Loan Approval depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Credit Management service domain governs the bank's overall credit policy and approval mandates, which Loan Approval enforces when sanctioning a facility within delegated lending authority. A concrete banking specific: larger exposures are routed to a credit committee per the bank's delegated-authority matrix, and an approved consumer credit offer in the EU triggers the Standard European Consumer Credit Information (SECCI) pre-contractual disclosure.
