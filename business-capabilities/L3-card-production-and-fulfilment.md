# Card Production & Fulfilment

**Definition.** Card Production & Fulfilment manufactures, personalises and dispatches physical and virtual cards, realising the BIAN Issued Device Administration service domain for newly issued devices.
**Also known as:** Card Manufacturing, Card Fulfilment, Card Production.

## Relationships
- Card Production & Fulfilment is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Production & Fulfilment is derived from Card Issuing.
- Card Production & Fulfilment depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Issued Device Administration service domain tracks the device from manufacture through fulfilment. Physical production must run in a card-personalisation facility certified under the PCI Card Production and Provisioning Security Requirements (physical and logical), and the embossed/encoded card carries an EMV chip personalised with issuer keys. Output of this capability feeds card personalisation and dispatch, and updates device status so the card can later be activated.
