# Payments

**Definition.** Payments is the L1 business capability covering the bank's ability to initiate, execute, clear, and settle monetary transfers across domestic, cross-border, and real-time rails, aligned to the BIAN Payments reference business area.
**Also known as:** Payment Services.

## Relationships
- Payments is defined in the Payments domain.
- Payments depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.
- Payments depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Payments is related to Deposits.

## Details
Payments spans BIAN service domains including Payment Order, Payment Execution, ACH Operations, and Bilateral Operations. It decomposes into Payment Execution, Payment Clearing & Settlement, Payment Initiation, and Direct Debit Management. Typical scope covers payment initiation, validation, routing, clearing, settlement, and reconciliation across scheme rails. ISO 20022 messaging and payment hubs are increasingly central to modern payments architecture.
