# Deposit Statement Production

**Definition.** Deposit Statement Production generates, formats and renders periodic deposit statements for delivery, realising the production behaviour of the BIAN Account Statement service domain.
**Also known as:** Statement Rendering, Statement Generation Engine.

## Relationships
- Deposit Statement Production is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Statement Production is derived from Deposit Statement & Reporting.
- Deposit Statement Production depends on the Account Statement Generator capability.

## Details
The BIAN Account Statement production behaviour renders the statement; for consumer accounts Regulation E (12 CFR 1005.9) and Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030.6) specify the required statement content, including interest earned, APY earned, and fees by category.
