# Account Reconciliation Servicing

**Definition.** Account Reconciliation Servicing matches deposit-account entries against expected positions and resolves discrepancies, realising the BIAN Account Reconciliation service domain at the deposit-account level.
**Also known as:** Deposit Recon, Account Matching.

## Relationships
- Account Reconciliation Servicing is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Reconciliation Servicing is derived from Account Servicing.
- Account Reconciliation Servicing depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Account Reconciliation service domain matches booked entries to expected positions; daily reconciliation underpins the bank's general-ledger control and supports Regulation E error resolution when a customer disputes a posted electronic transfer.
