# Loan Pricing

**Definition.** Loan Pricing is the capability that determines interest rates, fees and risk-based pricing for offered facilities, mapping to the BIAN Credit Facility and Consumer Loan service domains.
**Also known as:** Risk-Based Pricing, Rate Setting.

## Relationships
- Loan Pricing is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Pricing is derived from Loan Origination.
- Loan Pricing depends on the Credit Decisioning Engine capability.
- Loan Pricing depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Credit Facility service domain administers the terms of an extended credit facility, including the priced rate and fees set by Loan Pricing. Risk-based pricing ties the APR to the applicant's assigned risk grade and loss-given-default. A concrete banking specific: in the US the priced APR and finance charge must be disclosed under the Truth in Lending Act (Regulation Z), and risk-based-pricing notices are required when a consumer receives less favourable terms based on a credit report.
