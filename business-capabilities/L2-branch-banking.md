# Branch Banking

**Definition.** Branch Banking is the business capability that delivers in-person banking services through the physical branch network, including teller, advisory, and servicing functions. It aligns with the BIAN Branch Currency Management and Channel Management service domains.
**Also known as:** Branch Network.

## Relationships
- Branch Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Branch Banking is derived from Channel Management.
- Branch Banking depends on the Integration Platform capability.

## Details
Branch Banking covers teller operations, cash and vault management, account servicing, advisory appointments, and queue management. Branch transformation trends emphasize universal bankers, appointment booking, and tablet-based assisted journeys integrated with the same core and orchestration services used by digital channels.
