# Customer Segmentation

**Definition.** Customer Segmentation is the L2 business capability for grouping customers by behaviour, value, needs, and risk to enable targeted propositions, pricing, and engagement strategies.
**Also known as:** Segmentation.

## Relationships
- Customer Segmentation is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Customer Segmentation is derived from Marketing Management.
- Customer Segmentation depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Customer Segmentation is related to Campaign Management.

## Details
Customer Segmentation maps to BIAN Customer Behavior Insights and Customer Portfolio service domains. Typical sub-functions include behavioural and value-based segmentation, propensity and lifetime-value modelling, and segment maintenance. It supplies audiences to Campaign Management and informs Relationship Management strategies. Analytics and machine learning platforms realize the modelling, increasingly using real-time behavioural signals.
