# Chatbot Servicing

**Definition.** Chatbot Servicing is the Channels & Engagement capability that resolves customer enquiries and simple transactions through automated text assistants, realising self-service conversational flows over the BIAN Session Dialogue service domain.
**Also known as:** Virtual Assistant Banking, Chatbot Banking.

## Relationships
- Chatbot Servicing is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Chatbot Servicing is derived from Conversational Banking.
- Chatbot Servicing depends on the Conversational AI capability.

## Details
Chatbot Servicing realises self-service flows over BIAN Session Dialogue, handling balance enquiries, transaction lookups, card freezes and FAQs in text, with seamless escalation to a live agent that carries the conversation context. PSD2 SCA gates any payment or sensitive change initiated mid-chat. Generative-AI assistants raise resolution rates but require retrieval grounding, prompt-injection defences and conduct logging to avoid hallucinated advice, and intent-confidence thresholds trigger human handoff. Containment rate (enquiries resolved without an agent) and CSAT are the governing measures, with PII redaction in transcripts under GDPR.
