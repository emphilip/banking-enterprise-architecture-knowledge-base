# Party Data Management

**Definition.** Party Data Management is the L2 business capability that maintains accurate, deduplicated, and governed reference data for all parties, delivering a single trusted view of the customer aligned to the BIAN Party Reference Data Directory service domain.
**Also known as:** Customer Information Management.

## Relationships
- Party Data Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Party Data Management is derived from Customer Lifecycle Management.
- Party Data Management depends on the Master Data Management capability.
- Party Data Management is related to Customer Profile Management.

## Details
Party Data Management aligns to BIAN Party Reference Data Directory and Customer Reference Data Management service domains. Typical sub-functions include party creation, matching and merging, hierarchy and relationship modelling, and data quality stewardship. It is the parent of Customer Profile Management and provides the golden-record foundation consumed across Customer Servicing, Marketing, and Compliance. Master data management platforms typically realize this capability.
