# Overdraft Limit Monitoring

**Definition.** Overdraft Limit Monitoring tracks utilisation against authorised limits and triggers alerts and reviews, realising the monitoring behaviour of the BIAN Overdraft Facility service domain.
**Also known as:** Overdraft Monitoring, Utilisation Monitoring.

## Relationships
- Overdraft Limit Monitoring is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Overdraft Limit Monitoring is derived from Overdraft Management.
- Overdraft Limit Monitoring depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Overdraft Facility monitoring behaviour tracks utilisation; sustained or repeated overdraft use is a CFPB supervisory focus, and excessive-fee patterns drive the bank's reassessment of the customer's opt-in status and limit under Regulation E.
