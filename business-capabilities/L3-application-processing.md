# Application Processing

**Definition.** Application Processing is the L3 business capability that manages the capture, validation, decisioning and tracking of a customer's product and relationship application during acquisition, mapping to the BIAN Customer Offer and Sales Product Agreement service domains.
**Also known as:** Application Management, Account Application Handling, Origination Intake.

## Relationships
- Application Processing is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Application Processing is derived from Customer Acquisition.
- Application Processing depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Application Processing extends its parent Customer Acquisition within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Application Processing relies on the Workflow Orchestration technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Application Processing at scale.
