# Goal Planning

**Definition.** Goal Planning defines, tracks, and projects client financial goals and probability of achievement, driving goal-based investing; it complements the BIAN Customer Investment Profile service domain, reflecting CFA Institute goal-based practice.
**Also known as:** Goal-Based Investing, Goal Tracking.

## Relationships
- Goal Planning is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Goal Planning is derived from Investment Advisory.
- Goal Planning depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Goal Planning depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Per CFA Institute goal-based practice, each client objective (retirement, education, home purchase) is given a time horizon, funding target, and priority, and capital is mentally bucketed by goal rather than managed as one pool. Goal Planning runs Monte Carlo projections to estimate the probability of meeting each goal and tracks funded status over time, feeding the client profile held in the BIAN Customer Investment Profile service domain.
