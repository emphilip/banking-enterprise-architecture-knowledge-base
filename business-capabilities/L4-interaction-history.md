# Interaction History

**Definition.** Interaction History is the L4 business capability that captures and classifies servicing and relationship events across channels to build a unified contact record, realising the BIAN Customer Event History service domain.
**Also known as:** Contact History, Channel Activity History.

## Relationships
- Interaction History is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Interaction History is derived from Contact Dialogue Management.
- Interaction History depends on the CRM Platform capability.

## Details
Interaction History extends its parent Contact Dialogue Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Interaction History relies on the CRM Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Interaction History at scale.
