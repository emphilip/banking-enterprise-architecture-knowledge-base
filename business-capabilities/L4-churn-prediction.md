# Churn Prediction

**Definition.** Churn Prediction is the L4 business capability that models attrition risk and retention drivers to enable proactive retention treatment, supporting the BIAN Customer Behavior Insights service domain.
**Also known as:** Attrition Modelling, Retention Analytics.

## Relationships
- Churn Prediction is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Churn Prediction is derived from Behavioural Analytics.
- Churn Prediction depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Churn Prediction extends its parent Behavioural Analytics within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Churn Prediction relies on the Analytics Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Churn Prediction at scale.
