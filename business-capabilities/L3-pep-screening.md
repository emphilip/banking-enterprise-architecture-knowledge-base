# PEP Screening

**Definition.** PEP Screening identifies politically exposed persons together with their family members and close associates so that heightened controls apply, per FATF Recommendations 12 and 22.
**Also known as:** Politically Exposed Person Screening, PEP Identification.

## Relationships
- PEP Screening is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- PEP Screening is derived from KYC Management.
- PEP Screening depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.
- PEP Screening depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
FATF Recommendation 12 requires, for foreign PEPs, senior-management approval to establish or continue the relationship, establishing source of wealth and source of funds, and enhanced ongoing monitoring; the same measures extend to domestic PEPs and international-organisation PEPs where higher risk is present. Recommendation 22 extends PEP obligations to designated non-financial businesses and professions. Screening must also cover relatives and close associates (RCAs), and PEP status persists for a risk-based period after the person leaves office.
