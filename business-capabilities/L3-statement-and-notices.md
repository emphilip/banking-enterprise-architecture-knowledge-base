# Statement & Notices

**Definition.** Statement & Notices is the capability that produces loan statements, repayment reminders and regulatory notices for borrowers, mapping to the BIAN Consumer Loan service domain.
**Also known as:** Loan Statements, Borrower Notices.

## Relationships
- Statement & Notices is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Statement & Notices is derived from Loan Servicing.
- Statement & Notices depends on the Document Management capability.
- Statement & Notices depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Consumer Loan service domain provides the balance and transaction data that Statement & Notices renders into periodic statements. A concrete banking specific: US mortgage servicers must send a periodic statement each billing cycle meeting Regulation Z (12 CFR 1026.41) content rules, including the payment amount, amounts applied to principal/interest/escrow, and delinquency information once an account is 45+ days past due.
