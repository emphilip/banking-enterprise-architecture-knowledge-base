# Finance Management

**Definition.** Finance Management is the top-level business capability that governs the bank's accounting, financial control, reporting, and capital management functions. It aligns with the BIAN Finance service domains and the APQC manage financial resources framework.
**Also known as:** Finance.

## Relationships
- Finance Management is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Finance Management depends on the General Ledger Engine capability.
- Finance Management is related to Treasury Management.

## Details
Finance Management spans general ledger accounting, financial and management reporting, and regulatory capital management. It depends on a centralized ledger and reporting platforms for a single version of financial truth, with cloud ERP and finance data hubs enabling faster close, granular sub-ledger accounting, and analytical finance.
