# Dispute Evidence Management

**Definition.** Dispute Evidence Management collates compelling evidence and documentation supporting a dispute outcome, supporting the BIAN Card Case service domain.
**Also known as:** Evidence Collation, Compelling Evidence Management.

## Relationships
- Dispute Evidence Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Dispute Evidence Management is derived from Dispute Investigation.
- Dispute Evidence Management depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
This capability assembles the documentary record for the BIAN Card Case — receipts, delivery confirmation, IP/device data, prior transaction history — to meet scheme "compelling evidence" standards such as Visa Compelling Evidence 3.0 (CE 3.0) for dispute condition 10.4, which lets an acquirer remedy a card-absent fraud chargeback with two prior undisputed transactions sharing identifiers. Evidence must be packaged to the scheme's format and retained for audit.
