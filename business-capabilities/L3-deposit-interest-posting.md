# Deposit Interest Posting

**Definition.** Deposit Interest Posting capitalises and credits accrued interest to accounts on schedule and at maturity, realising the interest-payment behaviour of the BIAN Savings Account and Term Deposit service domains.
**Also known as:** Interest Capitalisation, Interest Credit.

## Relationships
- Deposit Interest Posting is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Interest Posting is derived from Interest Calculation.
- Deposit Interest Posting depends on the Interest & Charges Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Savings/Term Deposit interest-payment behaviour credits accrued interest; the credited interest and the compounding/crediting frequency must reconcile to the annual percentage yield disclosed under Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030).
