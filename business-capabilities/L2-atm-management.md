# ATM Management

**Definition.** ATM Management is the business capability that operates and maintains the automated teller machine network, including cash management, transaction processing, and device monitoring. It aligns with the BIAN ATM Network Management service domain.
**Also known as:** ATM Network.

## Relationships
- ATM Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- ATM Management is derived from Channel Management.
- ATM Management depends on the Integration Platform capability.

## Details
ATM Management covers cash forecasting and replenishment, transaction switching, EMV and contactless acceptance, fault monitoring, and security against skimming and jackpotting. Integration with the card and core platforms enables withdrawals, deposits, and cardless transactions, with ATM-as-a-service and software-defined estates reducing operating cost.
