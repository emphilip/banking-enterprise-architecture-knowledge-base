# Portfolio Rebalancing Management

**Definition.** Portfolio Rebalancing Management detects drift from a portfolio's target allocation and generates corrective trades within tolerance bands and rebalancing policy; it is realised through the BIAN Investment Portfolio Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Rebalancing Capability, Drift Management Capability.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Rebalancing Management is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Portfolio Rebalancing Management is derived from Portfolio Management.
- Portfolio Rebalancing Management depends on the Portfolio Management System capability.
- Portfolio Rebalancing Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Investment Portfolio Management service domain handles ongoing maintenance of a live portfolio. Per CFA Institute practice, rebalancing is triggered when asset-class weights breach calendar or percentage tolerance bands around the strategic asset allocation; the resulting corrective orders are passed to Order Management, and tax-aware variants defer or net trades to limit realised gains.
