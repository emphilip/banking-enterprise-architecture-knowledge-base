# Next-Best-Action

**Definition.** Next-Best-Action is the L3 business capability that recommends the most relevant offer, retention or service action for a customer at a point of interaction, drawing on the BIAN Customer Behavior Insights and Customer Offer service domains.
**Also known as:** NBA, Next Best Offer, Recommendation Management.

## Relationships
- Next-Best-Action is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Next-Best-Action is derived from Relationship Management.
- Next-Best-Action depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Next-Best-Action extends its parent Relationship Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Next-Best-Action relies on the Analytics Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Next-Best-Action at scale.
