# Interbank Clearing

**Definition.** Interbank Clearing exchanges, nets and reconciles payment obligations between participant banks within a scheme prior to settlement, aligning with the clearing functions of the BIAN Payment Execution, Clearing and Settlement area.
**Also known as:** Clearing Operations, Net Position Calculation.

## Relationships
- Interbank Clearing is defined in the Payments domain.
- Interbank Clearing is derived from Payment Clearing & Settlement.
- Interbank Clearing depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Interbank Clearing grounds in the clearing functions of the BIAN Payment Execution, Clearing and Settlement area. It exchanges, nets and reconciles payment obligations between participant banks within a scheme before settlement. Net position calculation produces the obligations passed to interbank settlement.
