# Portfolio Management

**Definition.** Portfolio Management is the business capability that constructs, monitors, and rebalances client investment portfolios against mandates, risk tolerance, and benchmarks. It corresponds to the BIAN Portfolio Planning and Investment Portfolio Management service domains.
**Also known as:** Portfolio Administration.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Management is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Portfolio Management is derived from Wealth Management.
- Portfolio Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Portfolio Management covers asset allocation, model portfolios, drift monitoring, tax-loss harvesting, and performance attribution. It relies on market data feeds, risk analytics, and rebalancing engines, and increasingly applies machine learning for optimization and scenario analysis while maintaining suitability and fiduciary controls.
