# Dispute Management

**Definition.** Dispute Management is the business capability that handles cardholder disputes, chargebacks, and representments through the card network dispute lifecycle. It aligns with the BIAN Card Case and dispute resolution service domains.
**Also known as:** Chargeback Management.

## Relationships
- Dispute Management is defined in the Cards domain.
- Dispute Management is derived from Cards Management.
- Dispute Management depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
Dispute Management orchestrates the Visa and Mastercard dispute rules, including reason-code classification, evidence collection, provisional credits, and arbitration timelines. Automation and AI-assisted triage reduce manual handling, while integration with fraud and case management functions supports recovery rate optimization and Reg E and Reg Z compliance.
