# Dormancy Management

**Definition.** Dormancy Management identifies, flags and administers inactive and unclaimed deposit accounts and escheatment obligations, supporting the dormant-account handling behaviour within the BIAN Account Management service domains.
**Also known as:** Inactive Account Management, Unclaimed Balance Handling.

## Relationships
- Dormancy Management is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Dormancy Management is derived from Account Servicing.
- Dormancy Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
BIAN Account Management dormant-account handling flags inactivity; US state unclaimed-property laws set a dormancy period (commonly 3-5 years) after which a deposit balance must be reported and escheated to the state, with prior owner due-diligence notice.
