# Repossession Management

**Definition.** Repossession Management is the capability that coordinates seizure, holding and disposal of secured assets on defaulted facilities, mapping to the BIAN Collection/Recovery service domain.
**Also known as:** Asset Repossession, Collateral Liquidation.

## Relationships
- Repossession Management is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Repossession Management is derived from Recovery & Write-Off.
- Repossession Management depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Repossession Management depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Collection/Recovery service domain pursues secured recovery, which Repossession Management coordinates from seizure to disposal. A concrete banking specific: a US auto repossession and subsequent sale are governed by UCC Article 9, which requires the disposal to be commercially reasonable and a deficiency or surplus to be calculated and notified to the borrower.
