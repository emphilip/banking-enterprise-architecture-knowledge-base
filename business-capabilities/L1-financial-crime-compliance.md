# Financial Crime Compliance

**Definition.** Financial Crime Compliance is the top-level business capability that protects the bank from money laundering, terrorist financing, sanctions breaches, and other financial crime. It aligns with the BIAN Financial Crime service domains and FATF recommendations.
**Also known as:** FinCrime, Compliance.

## Relationships
- Financial Crime Compliance is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Financial Crime Compliance depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.
- Financial Crime Compliance is related to KYC Management.

## Details
Financial Crime Compliance covers customer due diligence, transaction and sanctions monitoring, suspicious activity reporting, and regulatory compliance. It integrates KYC, AML, screening, and reporting sub-capabilities on shared data and AI platforms, with perpetual KYC and graph-based network analytics improving detection and reducing false positives.
