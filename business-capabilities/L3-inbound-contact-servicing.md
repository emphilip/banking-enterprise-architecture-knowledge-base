# Inbound Contact Servicing

**Definition.** Inbound Contact Servicing is the Channels & Engagement capability that handles inbound voice and messaging service contacts and their resolution, realising the BIAN Contact Center Operations and Servicing Issue service domains; it is named to avoid the Customer Management Contact Dialogue Management capability.
**Also known as:** Inbound Servicing, Inbound Voice Servicing.

## Relationships
- Inbound Contact Servicing is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Inbound Contact Servicing is derived from Contact Center.
- Inbound Contact Servicing depends on the Contact Center Platform capability.

## Details
Inbound Contact Servicing realises BIAN Contact Center Operations and Servicing Issue: an inbound call, chat or email is authenticated, matched to a skilled agent, and worked to resolution or escalation with the case state preserved. FFIEC authentication guidance requires the caller to be verified to a strength matched to transaction risk, with knowledge-based or voice-biometric factors and step-up for high-risk requests. First-contact resolution and average handle time are the governing measures. Omnichannel context from Session Dialogue lets the agent continue a journey that began in-app, avoiding re-authentication and re-keying.
