# Nostro Reconciliation

**Definition.** Nostro Reconciliation matches nostro account movements and statements against expected correspondent activity, supporting the BIAN Correspondent Bank Operations and Correspondent Bank Directory service domains.
**Also known as:** Nostro Matching, Vostro Reconciliation.

## Relationships
- Nostro Reconciliation is defined in the Payments domain.
- Nostro Reconciliation is derived from Correspondent Banking.
- Nostro Reconciliation depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Nostro Reconciliation grounds in the BIAN Correspondent Bank Operations and Correspondent Bank Directory service domains. It matches nostro account movements and statements against expected correspondent activity. It identifies and routes unmatched items and exposures across the correspondent network.
