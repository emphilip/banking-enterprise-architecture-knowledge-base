# Closure Settlement

**Definition.** Closure Settlement calculates and disburses final balances and residual interest on closure, realising the settle-and-close behaviour of the BIAN Current Account and Savings Account service domains.
**Also known as:** Final Balance Settlement, Closure Payout.

## Relationships
- Closure Settlement is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Closure Settlement is derived from Account Closure Servicing.
- Closure Settlement depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current/Savings Account settle-and-close behaviour pays out the final balance; closure must credit interest accrued to the closing date per Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030.4) and net any outstanding fees before disbursing the residual to the customer.
