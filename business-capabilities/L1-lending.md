# Lending

**Definition.** Lending is the L1 business capability covering the bank's ability to originate, decision, service, and recover credit products across retail and commercial portfolios, aligned to the BIAN Loans & Deposits and Collateral reference business areas.
**Also known as:** Credit Services.

## Relationships
- Lending is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Lending depends on the Loan Origination Platform capability.
- Lending depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Lending is related to Credit Risk Management.

## Details
Lending spans BIAN service domains including Consumer Loan, Corporate Loan, Loan, Collateral Administration, and Collections. It decomposes into Loan Origination, Loan Servicing, Collateral Management, and Collections & Recovery. Typical scope covers product origination, credit decisioning, disbursement, ongoing servicing, and arrears management. AI-driven credit decisioning and cloud loan origination platforms are central to modern lending architecture.
