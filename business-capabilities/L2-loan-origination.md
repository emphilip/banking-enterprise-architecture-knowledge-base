# Loan Origination

**Definition.** Loan Origination is the L2 business capability for taking a credit application from intake through assessment, decision, and disbursement, aligned to the BIAN Consumer Loan and Corporate Loan service domains.
**Also known as:** Loan Onboarding.

## Relationships
- Loan Origination is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Origination is derived from Lending.
- Loan Origination depends on the Loan Origination Platform capability.
- Loan Origination depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
Loan Origination maps to BIAN Consumer Loan, Corporate Loan, and Loan service domains. It is the parent of Credit Decisioning and underpins the Loan Origination Workflow and Mortgage Origination business processes. Typical sub-functions include application capture, document collection, affordability assessment, decisioning orchestration, offer generation, and disbursement. Cloud loan origination platforms and intelligent document processing streamline the journey.
