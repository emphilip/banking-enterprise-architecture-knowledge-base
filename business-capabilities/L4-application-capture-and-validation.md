# Application Capture & Validation

**Definition.** Application Capture & Validation intakes and validates deposit account-opening applications and supporting data, feeding the origination behaviour of the BIAN Current Account and Deposit Account service domains.
**Also known as:** Account Application Capture, Opening Data Validation.

## Relationships
- Application Capture & Validation is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Application Capture & Validation is derived from Deposit Account Origination.
- Application Capture & Validation depends on the Deposit Account Management capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current/Deposit Account origination behaviour ingests the application; validation must capture the four CIP data elements (name, date of birth, address, and TIN) required by 31 CFR 1020.220 before the account record can be created.
