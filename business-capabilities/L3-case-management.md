# Case Management

**Definition.** Case Management is the L3 business capability for creating, tracking, and resolving service cases such as complaints, disputes, and multi-step service requests through to closure with full audit history.
**Also known as:** Service Case Management.

## Relationships
- Case Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Case Management is derived from Customer Servicing.
- Case Management depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.
- Case Management is related to Customer Servicing.

## Details
Case Management aligns to the BIAN Customer Case Management service domain. Typical sub-functions include case creation and categorisation, assignment and routing, SLA tracking, escalation, and resolution recording. It supports the Complaint Handling, Service Request Handling, and Fraud Investigation business processes. Workflow orchestration platforms typically realize the case lifecycle and routing logic.
