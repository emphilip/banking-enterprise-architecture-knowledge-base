# Debt Sale Management

**Definition.** Debt Sale Management is the capability that packages, sells and transfers non-performing portfolios to third-party purchasers, mapping to the BIAN Collection/Recovery service domain.
**Also known as:** Portfolio Debt Sale, NPL Sale.

## Relationships
- Debt Sale Management is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Debt Sale Management is derived from Recovery & Write-Off.
- Debt Sale Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Debt Sale Management depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
The BIAN Collection/Recovery service domain supports onward sale of unrecoverable debt, which Debt Sale Management runs. A concrete banking specific: charged-off non-performing loan (NPL) portfolios are stratified, priced and sold to debt purchasers under a sale-and-purchase agreement with data-room due diligence, and UK sellers must ensure the purchaser treats customers per FCA CONC standards.
