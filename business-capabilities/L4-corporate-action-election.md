# Corporate Action Election

**Definition.** Corporate Action Election captures and processes client elections on voluntary corporate actions and proxy voting within deadlines; it supports the BIAN Corporate Action service domain.
**Also known as:** Voluntary Election Handling, Proxy Voting.

## Relationships
- Corporate Action Election is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Corporate Action Election is derived from Corporate Actions Processing.
- Corporate Action Election depends on the Order Management System capability.
- Corporate Action Election depends on the Notification Services capability.

## Details
For voluntary events (tender offers, rights issues, optional dividends) and shareholder votes, Corporate Action Election notifies eligible holders, captures their instructions before the issuer's response deadline, applies a default option to non-responders, and submits aggregated elections and proxy votes upstream through DTCC. Missing the deadline forfeits value, so the capability emphasises timely client notification and deadline tracking within the BIAN Corporate Action service domain.
