# Party Reference Data

**Definition.** Party Reference Data is the L3 business capability that maintains the authoritative golden record of party identifiers, demographics, roles and relationships, realising the BIAN Party Reference Data Directory service domain.
**Also known as:** Party Master Data, Customer Reference Data, Golden Record.

## Relationships
- Party Reference Data is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Party Reference Data is derived from Party Data Management.
- Party Reference Data depends on the Master Data Management capability.

## Details
Party Reference Data extends its parent Party Data Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Party Reference Data relies on the Master Data Management technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Party Reference Data at scale.
