# Relationship Planning

**Definition.** Relationship Planning is the L3 business capability that develops and maintains the customer plan setting relationship goals, coverage and product matching for relationship managers, realising the BIAN Customer Relationship Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Customer Planning, Account Planning, Relationship Strategy.

## Relationships
- Relationship Planning is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Relationship Planning is derived from Relationship Management.
- Relationship Planning depends on the CRM Platform capability.

## Details
Relationship Planning extends its parent Relationship Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Relationship Planning relies on the CRM Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Relationship Planning at scale.
