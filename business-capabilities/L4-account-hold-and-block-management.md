# Account Hold & Block Management

**Definition.** Account Hold & Block Management places and releases holds, earmarks and blocks on balances for legal, fraud or settlement reasons, supporting the funds-control behaviour of the BIAN Current Account service domain.
**Also known as:** Funds Hold Management, Earmark Management.

## Relationships
- Account Hold & Block Management is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Hold & Block Management is derived from Account Balance Management.
- Account Hold & Block Management depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current Account funds-control behaviour earmarks balances; deposit holds must follow Regulation CC (12 CFR 229.13) exception-hold rules, while legal blocks include garnishments and OFAC-sanctions freezes that ringfence funds from withdrawal.
