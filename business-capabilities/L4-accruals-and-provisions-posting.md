# Accruals & Provisions Posting

**Definition.** Accruals & Provisions Posting recognises period-end accruals, deferrals and provision entries in the ledger consistent with IAS 37 and accrual-basis accounting.
**Also known as:** Accrual Posting, Provision Posting, Period-End Accruals.

## Relationships
- Accruals & Provisions Posting is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Accruals & Provisions Posting is derived from Journal Management.
- Accruals & Provisions Posting depends on the General Ledger Engine capability.

## Details
Accruals & Provisions Posting books expenses and income in the period they relate to rather than when cash moves, and recognises provisions under IAS 37 where a present obligation arises from a past event, an outflow is probable and the amount can be reliably estimated (e.g. litigation, restructuring, onerous contracts). It manages accrual reversal in the following period, supporting documentation for each provision, and the discounting of long-dated obligations, ensuring liabilities are neither overstated nor omitted at close.
