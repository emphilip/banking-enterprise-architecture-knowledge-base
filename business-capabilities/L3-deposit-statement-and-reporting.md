# Deposit Statement & Reporting

**Definition.** Deposit Statement & Reporting produces periodic and on-demand statements, advices and balance reports for deposit accounts, realising the BIAN Account Statement service domain.
**Also known as:** Account Statement Capability, Deposit Reporting.

## Relationships
- Deposit Statement & Reporting is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Deposit Statement & Reporting is derived from Account Servicing.
- Deposit Statement & Reporting depends on the Account Statement Generator capability.

## Details
The BIAN Account Statement service domain renders periodic statements; for consumer accounts with electronic fund transfers, Regulation E (12 CFR 1005.9) mandates periodic statements showing transaction detail, fees and the error-resolution notice.
