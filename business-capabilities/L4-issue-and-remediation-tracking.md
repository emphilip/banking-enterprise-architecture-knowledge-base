# Issue & Remediation Tracking

**Definition.** Issue & Remediation Tracking logs, prioritises and tracks compliance issues and remediation actions through closure, supporting the OFAC 2019 Framework remediation expectations and the FFIEC BSA/AML manual.
**Also known as:** Findings Management, Remediation Tracking.

## Relationships
- Issue & Remediation Tracking is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Issue & Remediation Tracking is derived from Compliance Monitoring & Testing.
- Issue & Remediation Tracking depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.
- Issue & Remediation Tracking depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The OFAC Framework states that, on identifying a deficiency, an effective program will take immediate and effective action to identify and remediate root causes. This capability captures findings from testing, audits, exams and self-identification, assigns severity, owner and due date, and tracks remediation to verified closure with evidence. Aging and overdue items are escalated and reported to the BSA officer and committees, since unremediated repeat findings (especially examiner MRAs) are a common escalation path to enforcement.
