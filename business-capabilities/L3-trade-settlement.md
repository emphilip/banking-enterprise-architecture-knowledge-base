# Trade Settlement

**Definition.** Trade Settlement confirms, affirms, clears, and settles executed securities trades against payment within the market settlement cycle; it is realised through the BIAN Trade Confirmation Matching and Securities Fulfillment service domains.
**Also known as:** Securities Settlement, Clearing & Settlement (Securities).

## Relationships
- Trade Settlement is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Trade Settlement is derived from Brokerage & Trading.
- Trade Settlement depends on the Order Management System capability.
- Trade Settlement depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Trade Confirmation Matching and Securities Fulfillment service domains govern post-trade processing. Since May 2024 US cash equities settle on a T+1 cycle, requiring trade affirmation by 9:00 p.m. ET on trade date so that DTCC's NSCC and DTC can net and settle delivery-versus-payment the next business day. Trade Settlement orchestrates matching, instruction, and settlement against payment.
