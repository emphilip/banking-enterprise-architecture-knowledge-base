# Account Maintenance Servicing

**Definition.** Account Maintenance Servicing handles changes to account attributes, mandates, signatories and instructions over the life of the account, realising the maintenance behaviour of the BIAN Current Account and Deposit Account service domains.
**Also known as:** Account Amendments, Account Care.

## Relationships
- Account Maintenance Servicing is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Maintenance Servicing is derived from Account Servicing.
- Account Maintenance Servicing depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current/Deposit Account maintenance behaviour records attribute and mandate changes; address, ownership and signatory updates must keep the account's CIP record current under 31 CFR 1020.220 and feed FDIC deposit-insurance ownership-category determination.
