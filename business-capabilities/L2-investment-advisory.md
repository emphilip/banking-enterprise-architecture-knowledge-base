# Investment Advisory

**Definition.** Investment Advisory is the business capability that provides personalized investment recommendations, financial planning, and suitability assessments to clients. It aligns with the BIAN Investment Advisory service domain.
**Also known as:** Advisory Services.

## Relationships
- Investment Advisory is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Investment Advisory is derived from Wealth Management.
- Investment Advisory depends on the Generative AI Platform capability.

## Details
Investment Advisory encompasses risk profiling, goal-based planning, product suitability and best-interest checks, and the generation of advice narratives. Robo and hybrid advisory channels combine rule-based engines with generative-AI copilots that draft client-ready proposals, all governed by suitability, disclosure, and record-keeping obligations under MiFID II and Reg BI.
