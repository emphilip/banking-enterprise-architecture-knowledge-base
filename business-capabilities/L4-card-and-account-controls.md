# Card & Account Controls

**Definition.** Card & Account Controls is the Channels & Engagement capability that lets customers self-manage limits, freezes, alerts and travel notices in digital channels, surfacing BIAN Customer Workbench actions against card and account services.
**Also known as:** Self-Service Controls, Card Management Self-Service.

## Relationships
- Card & Account Controls is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Card & Account Controls is derived from Digital Self-Service.
- Card & Account Controls depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.

## Details
Card & Account Controls surfaces BIAN Customer Workbench actions against card and account services so customers instantly freeze or unfreeze a card, set per-channel spend and ATM limits, toggle international or contactless use, and configure transaction alerts. These calls invoke the issuer's Authorization Controls in real time, turning fraud response from a call-centre task into an in-app tap that reduces fraud loss and dispute volume. Reg E / PSD2 give consumers control and dispute rights that these controls operationalise, and high-impact changes (raising a limit) require SCA step-up. Alert preferences sync to Notification Services.
