# Prepayment Handling

**Definition.** Prepayment Handling is the capability that processes partial and ad-hoc overpayments and recalculates balances and break costs, mapping to the BIAN Repayment service domain.
**Also known as:** Overpayment Handling, Partial Prepayment.

## Relationships
- Prepayment Handling is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Prepayment Handling is derived from Repayment Processing.
- Prepayment Handling depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Repayment service domain accommodates early and additional payments, which Prepayment Handling processes. A concrete banking specific: a fixed-rate mortgage prepayment may attract an early-repayment charge or break cost, and US higher-priced and qualified mortgages face Regulation Z limits on the prepayment penalties a lender may impose.
