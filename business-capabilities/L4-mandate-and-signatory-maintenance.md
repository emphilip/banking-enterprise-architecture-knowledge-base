# Mandate & Signatory Maintenance

**Definition.** Mandate & Signatory Maintenance manages changes to operating mandates, authorities and signatories over the account life, supporting the BIAN Current Account maintenance behaviour.
**Also known as:** Signatory Maintenance, Operating Mandate Update.

## Relationships
- Mandate & Signatory Maintenance is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Mandate & Signatory Maintenance is derived from Account Maintenance Servicing.
- Mandate & Signatory Maintenance depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current Account maintenance behaviour updates operating mandates; adding or removing signatories on a business account requires re-verifying the control person under the FinCEN Beneficial Ownership rule (31 CFR 1010.230) and updating board/authority resolutions on file.
