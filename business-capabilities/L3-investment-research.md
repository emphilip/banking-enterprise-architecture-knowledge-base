# Investment Research

**Definition.** Investment Research produces market, security, and macro analysis and maintains research coverage that feeds advice and portfolio decisions; it supports the BIAN Investment Portfolio Analysis service domain.
**Also known as:** Securities Research, Market Research (Investments).

## Relationships
- Investment Research is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Investment Research is derived from Investment Advisory.
- Investment Research depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Investment Research depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Investment Portfolio Analysis service domain consumes research inputs to evaluate instruments and strategies. Concretely, Investment Research builds and maintains the capital market assumptions — expected returns, volatilities, and correlations by asset class per CFA Institute methodology — alongside fundamental security coverage and conviction ratings that drive Asset Allocation and Advice Generation, with MiFID II requiring research costs to be unbundled from execution commissions.
