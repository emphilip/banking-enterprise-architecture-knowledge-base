# Payment Screening

**Definition.** Payment Screening applies watch-list, embargo and regulatory checks to outbound and inbound payment instructions, realising the compliance-check responsibilities of the BIAN Payment Order service domain.
**Also known as:** Payment Compliance Check, Watchlist Filtering.

## Relationships
- Payment Screening is defined in the Payments domain.
- Payment Screening is derived from Payment Initiation.
- Payment Screening depends on the Transaction Monitoring Platform capability.

## Details
Payment Screening grounds in the compliance-check responsibilities of the BIAN Payment Order service domain. It applies watch-list, embargo and regulatory checks to inbound and outbound instructions. It is named to avoid collision with the Compliance domain capability Sanctions Screening, and relies on a transaction monitoring platform for list matching.
