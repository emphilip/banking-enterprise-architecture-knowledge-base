# Product Recommendation

**Definition.** Product Recommendation matches suitable instruments and model portfolios to a client's profile and goals; it is realised through the BIAN Financial Advisory service domain.
**Also known as:** Investment Recommendation, Model Portfolio Matching.

## Relationships
- Product Recommendation is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Product Recommendation is derived from Advice Generation.
- Product Recommendation depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Product Recommendation depends on the Generative AI Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Financial Advisory service domain matches products to a client. Product Recommendation maps the assessed suitability profile to a model portfolio or specific instruments, filtering against the product's MiFID II target market so that recommendations stay inside the negative and positive target-market criteria. Generative-AI assistants can draft rationale text, but the firm remains accountable for suitability of each recommendation.
