# Welcome Servicing

**Definition.** Welcome Servicing is the L4 business capability that delivers initial welcome communications, fulfilment and first-use enablement after activation, supporting the BIAN Party Lifecycle Management and Contact Dialogue service domains.
**Also known as:** Welcome Journey, First-Use Enablement.

## Relationships
- Welcome Servicing is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Welcome Servicing is derived from Onboarding Orchestration.
- Welcome Servicing depends on the Notification Services capability.

## Details
Welcome Servicing extends its parent Onboarding Orchestration within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Welcome Servicing relies on the Notification Services technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Welcome Servicing at scale.
