# Account Activation Control

**Definition.** Account Activation Control transitions a funded account to live status and enables transacting, realising the activation behaviour of the BIAN Current Account and Deposit Account service domains.
**Also known as:** Deposit Activation, Go-Live Control.

## Relationships
- Account Activation Control is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Account Activation Control is derived from Account Opening.
- Account Activation Control depends on the Notification Services capability.

## Details
The BIAN Current/Deposit Account activation behaviour flips an account to transactable status; activation is gated on completed CIP verification under the USA PATRIOT Act section 326 (31 CFR 1020.220) before access to funds and channels is granted.
