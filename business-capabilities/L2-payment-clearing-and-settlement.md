# Payment Clearing & Settlement

**Definition.** Payment Clearing & Settlement is the L2 business capability for exchanging, netting, and settling payment obligations between institutions, including position management and finality across clearing and settlement systems.
**Also known as:** Clearing and Settlement.

## Relationships
- Payment Clearing & Settlement is defined in the Payments domain.
- Payment Clearing & Settlement is derived from Payments.
- Payment Clearing & Settlement depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.
- Payment Clearing & Settlement is related to Payment Execution.

## Details
Payment Clearing & Settlement maps to BIAN Bilateral Operations, ACH Operations, and Card Clearing service domains. Typical sub-functions include clearing message exchange, multilateral netting, settlement position management, RTGS settlement, and finality confirmation. It underpins the Reconciliation & Settlement business process. Integration with central bank RTGS and scheme settlement windows is essential.
