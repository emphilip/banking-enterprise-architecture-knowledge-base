# Compliance Risk Assessment

**Definition.** Compliance Risk Assessment evaluates inherent and residual financial-crime and regulatory risk across the enterprise to drive the risk-based program required by the BSA 5 Pillars and the OFAC 2019 Framework for Compliance Commitments.
**Also known as:** Financial Crime Risk Assessment, AML Risk Assessment.

## Relationships
- Compliance Risk Assessment is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Compliance Risk Assessment is derived from Regulatory Compliance.
- Compliance Risk Assessment depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Compliance Risk Assessment depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
The OFAC Framework names root-cause risk assessment as the first essential component of an effective sanctions compliance program, and the FFIEC manual treats a documented BSA/AML risk assessment as the foundation for the risk-based approach. Assessment scores inherent risk across products, customers, geographies and channels, evaluates control effectiveness, and derives residual risk that calibrates monitoring thresholds, EDD triggers and resourcing. It must be refreshed when the business or threat environment changes materially.
