# Payment Status Tracking

**Definition.** Payment Status Tracking monitors a payment from submission to confirmation and surfaces status to originators (e.g. ISO 20022 pain.002), realising the tracking responsibilities of the BIAN Payment Execution service domain.
**Also known as:** Payment Tracking, Status Reporting, gpi Tracking.

## Relationships
- Payment Status Tracking is defined in the Payments domain.
- Payment Status Tracking is derived from Payment Execution.
- Payment Status Tracking depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Payment Status Tracking grounds in the tracking responsibilities of the BIAN Payment Execution service domain. It monitors a payment from submission through to confirmation and surfaces status to originators, including ISO 20022 pain.002 reporting and SWIFT gpi end-to-end tracking.
