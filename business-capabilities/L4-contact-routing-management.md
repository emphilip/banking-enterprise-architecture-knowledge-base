# Contact Routing Management

**Definition.** Contact Routing Management is the L4 business capability that selects the next available and best-suited servicing resource for an active customer contact, realising the BIAN Contact Routing service domain.
**Also known as:** Interaction Routing, Skills-Based Routing.

## Relationships
- Contact Routing Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Contact Routing Management is derived from Contact Dialogue Management.
- Contact Routing Management depends on the Contact Center Platform capability.

## Details
Contact Routing Management extends its parent Contact Dialogue Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Contact Routing Management relies on the Contact Center Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Contact Routing Management at scale.
