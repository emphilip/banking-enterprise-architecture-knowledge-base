# Mobile Banking

**Definition.** Mobile Banking is the business capability that provides banking services through native and progressive mobile applications optimized for smartphones and devices. It aligns with the BIAN Digital Channel service domain.
**Also known as:** Mobile App Banking.

## Relationships
- Mobile Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Mobile Banking is derived from Digital Banking.
- Mobile Banking depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.

## Details
Mobile Banking delivers biometric login, push notifications, mobile check deposit, card controls, and in-app payments. It leverages device security, customer identity, and push-based authentication, with mobile-first design and embedded AI assistants driving engagement and reducing reliance on assisted channels.
