# ATM Network Management

**Definition.** ATM Network Management is the Channels & Engagement capability that plans the ATM estate, connectivity and interchange participation, realising the BIAN Branch Network Management and ATM Network Operations service domains at the network level.
**Also known as:** ATM Estate Management, ATM Fleet Management.

## Relationships
- ATM Network Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- ATM Network Management is derived from ATM Management.
- ATM Network Management depends on the Integration Platform capability.

## Details
ATM Network Management realises BIAN Branch Network Management and ATM Network Operations at the estate level: site selection, device lifecycle and capital planning, software/firmware fleet upgrades, and participation in shared interchange networks (Visa Plus, Mastercard Cirrus, regional schemes). It governs interchange-fee economics and surcharge disclosure required by network rules, and it ensures terminal software meets EMV and PCI compliance across the fleet before deployment. The capability connects acquiring switches and host processors through the integration layer, manages encryption-key distribution, and tracks estate-wide availability and cost-per-transaction.
