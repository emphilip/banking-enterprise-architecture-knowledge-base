# Complaint Root-Cause Analysis

**Definition.** Complaint Root-Cause Analysis is the L4 business capability that aggregates complaint themes to identify systemic issues and feed product and process improvement, supporting the BIAN Customer Case Management and Customer Surveys service domains.
**Also known as:** Complaint Analytics, Root Cause Feedback.

## Relationships
- Complaint Root-Cause Analysis is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Root-Cause Analysis is derived from Complaint Management.
- Complaint Root-Cause Analysis depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Complaint Root-Cause Analysis extends its parent Complaint Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Complaint Root-Cause Analysis relies on the Analytics Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Complaint Root-Cause Analysis at scale.
