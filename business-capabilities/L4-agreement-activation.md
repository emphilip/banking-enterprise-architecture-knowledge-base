# Agreement Activation

**Definition.** Agreement Activation is the L4 business capability that establishes the in-force customer and product agreements and provisions initial access at the close of onboarding, realising the BIAN Customer Agreement and Sales Product Agreement service domains.
**Also known as:** Account Activation, Agreement Setup.

## Relationships
- Agreement Activation is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Agreement Activation is derived from Onboarding Orchestration.
- Agreement Activation depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
Agreement Activation extends its parent Onboarding Orchestration within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Agreement Activation relies on the Core Banking Processing technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Agreement Activation at scale.
