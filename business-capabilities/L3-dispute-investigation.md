# Dispute Investigation

**Definition.** Dispute Investigation gathers evidence, validates cardholder claims and determines liability for a card dispute case within the BIAN Card Case service domain.
**Also known as:** Dispute Case Investigation, Claim Investigation.

## Relationships
- Dispute Investigation is defined in the Cards domain.
- Dispute Investigation is derived from Dispute Management.
- Dispute Investigation depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Card Case service domain drives the investigation that satisfies Reg E (12 CFR 1005.11) and Reg Z (12 CFR 1026.13) error-resolution duties: confirming the transaction, contacting the cardholder, assessing fraud vs non-fraud, and deciding within the regulatory windows (Reg E 45/90 days; Reg Z 2 billing cycles). The outcome determines whether a chargeback is raised, the provisional credit is finalised, or the claim is denied with a written explanation.
