# Correspondent Banking

**Definition.** Correspondent Banking fulfils nostro/vostro arrangements and routes cross-border value through partner banks, mapping to the BIAN Correspondent Bank Operations and Correspondent Bank Directory service domains.
**Also known as:** Nostro Vostro Management, Correspondent Bank Operations.

## Relationships
- Correspondent Banking is defined in the Payments domain.
- Correspondent Banking is derived from Payment Execution.
- Correspondent Banking depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Correspondent Banking grounds in the BIAN Correspondent Bank Operations and Correspondent Bank Directory service domains. It maintains nostro and vostro arrangements and routes cross-border value through partner banks. It manages correspondent relationships, account hierarchies and the routing of international payments.
