# Tiered Interest Determination

**Definition.** Tiered Interest Determination selects the applicable rate from balance tiers, bonus and promotional conditions, supporting the rate-determination behaviour of the BIAN Interest Determination service domain.
**Also known as:** Tier Rate Selection, Bonus Interest Determination.

## Relationships
- Tiered Interest Determination is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Tiered Interest Determination is derived from Deposit Interest Accrual.
- Tiered Interest Determination depends on the Interest & Charges Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Interest Determination rate-determination behaviour picks the tier; where a tiered account pays different rates on balance bands, Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030, Appendix A) prescribes how the APY is calculated and disclosed for each tier so consumers can compare yields.
