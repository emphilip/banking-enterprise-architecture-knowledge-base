# Close Task Orchestration

**Definition.** Close Task Orchestration schedules, tracks and certifies the financial-close task checklist to compress close cycle time, aligning to APQC close-cycle measures.
**Also known as:** Close Calendar Management, Close Checklist Orchestration, Close Certification.

## Relationships
- Close Task Orchestration is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Close Task Orchestration is derived from Reconciliation & Close.
- Close Task Orchestration depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Close Task Orchestration drives the period-end close calendar — sequencing and assigning each task (sub-ledger cut-off, accruals, reconciliations, intercompany matching, consolidation, sign-off), tracking status and dependencies, and capturing reviewer certification. It targets the APQC benchmark "cycle time in days to perform the annual/monthly close," surfacing bottlenecks and overdue tasks in real time and providing the SOX evidence trail that each close control was performed and certified by the responsible owner.
