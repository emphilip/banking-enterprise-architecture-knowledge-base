# Interest Accrual Calculation

**Definition.** Interest Accrual Calculation computes daily accrued interest using product day-count and tier rules, supporting the accrual fulfilment of the BIAN Interest Determination service domain.
**Also known as:** Daily Accrual Calculation, Accrual Engine.

## Relationships
- Interest Accrual Calculation is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Interest Accrual Calculation is derived from Deposit Interest Accrual.
- Interest Accrual Calculation depends on the Interest & Charges Engine capability.

## Details
The BIAN Interest Determination accrual fulfilment computes daily interest; Regulation DD (12 CFR 1030.7) requires the daily-balance or average-daily-balance method applied to the full principal and prescribes the APY/APY-earned formulas in Appendix A.
