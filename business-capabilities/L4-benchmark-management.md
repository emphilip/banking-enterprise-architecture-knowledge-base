# Benchmark Management

**Definition.** Benchmark Management selects, blends, and maintains performance benchmarks and composites for portfolios; it supports GIPS-aligned reporting and the BIAN Investment Portfolio Analysis service domain.
**Also known as:** Composite Management, Benchmark Administration.

## Relationships
- Benchmark Management is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Benchmark Management is derived from Performance Measurement.
- Benchmark Management depends on the Portfolio Management System capability.
- Benchmark Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Per CFA Institute guidance, a valid benchmark must be specified in advance, investable, measurable, and reflect the portfolio's mandate. Benchmark Management assigns appropriate indices, builds blended benchmarks matching the strategic asset allocation, and constructs GIPS composites that group portfolios of similar strategy so that fee- and time-weighted returns are fairly comparable in the BIAN Investment Portfolio Analysis service domain.
