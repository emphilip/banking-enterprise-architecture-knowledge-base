# Suitability Report Production

**Definition.** Suitability Report Production generates the client-facing report explaining how advice meets the client's circumstances, as required by MiFID II; it complements the BIAN Financial Advisory service domain.
**Also known as:** Suitability Statement, Advice Disclosure.

## Relationships
- Suitability Report Production is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Suitability Report Production is derived from Suitability Assessment.
- Suitability Report Production depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Suitability Report Production depends on the Document Management capability.

## Details
MiFID II requires firms providing investment advice to give retail clients a suitability statement before the transaction, setting out the advice given and how it meets the client's preferences, objectives, and other characteristics. Suitability Report Production assembles this statement from the assessed profile and the recommendation, records it as durable evidence, and delivers it through the BIAN Financial Advisory service domain.
