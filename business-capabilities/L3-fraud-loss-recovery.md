# Fraud Loss Recovery

**Definition.** Fraud Loss Recovery pursues recovery of confirmed fraud losses through reimbursement, chargebacks, recall and recourse, and records fraud loss outcomes, supporting the BIAN Fraud Model service domain.
**Also known as:** Fraud Recovery, Fraud Reimbursement.

## Relationships
- Fraud Loss Recovery is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Fraud Loss Recovery is derived from Fraud Management.
- Fraud Loss Recovery depends on the Fraud Analytics capability.
- Fraud Loss Recovery depends on the Governance Risk & Compliance Platform capability.

## Details
Fraud Loss Recovery manages recovery channels after a confirmed fraud: card-scheme chargeback and dispute rights, payment recall and SWIFT/RTP return messages for misdirected funds, insurance and bond claims, and customer-reimbursement decisions (e.g. UK APP scam mandatory reimbursement rules). It records gross loss, recoveries and net loss to reconcile with the operational-risk loss database under BCBS d292, feeding fraud-rate reporting and the calibration of preventive controls. Recovery outcomes inform liability allocation across parties.
