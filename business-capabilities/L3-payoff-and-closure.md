# Payoff & Closure

**Definition.** Payoff & Closure is the capability that calculates settlement figures, processes final payments and closes loan facilities, mapping to the BIAN Consumer Loan and Credit Facility service domains.
**Also known as:** Loan Settlement, Loan Closure.

## Relationships
- Payoff & Closure is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Payoff & Closure is derived from Loan Servicing.
- Payoff & Closure depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Consumer Loan and Credit Facility service domains close out the agreement once the outstanding balance is cleared. A concrete banking specific: a mortgage payoff requires a written payoff statement (good through a specified date including per-diem interest) and, after settlement, the lien release/satisfaction of mortgage must be recorded so the borrower's title is cleared.
