# Approval Authority Routing

**Definition.** Approval Authority Routing is the capability that routes credit decisions to the appropriate delegated lending authority and credit committee, mapping to the BIAN Credit Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Delegated Authority Routing, Mandate Routing.

## Relationships
- Approval Authority Routing is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Approval Authority Routing is derived from Loan Approval.
- Approval Authority Routing depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Credit Management service domain defines the bank's approval mandates, which Approval Authority Routing applies per exposure. A concrete banking specific: a delegated-lending-authority matrix routes a decision to an individual underwriter, a senior credit officer or a credit committee based on exposure size, product and risk grade, preserving an auditable approval trail.
