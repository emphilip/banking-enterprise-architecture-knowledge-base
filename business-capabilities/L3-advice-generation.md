# Advice Generation

**Definition.** Advice Generation creates personalised investment recommendations and suitability reports for clients, increasingly with model and AI support; it is realised through the BIAN Financial Advisory service domain.
**Also known as:** Advisory Recommendation, Robo-Advice.

## Relationships
- Advice Generation is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Advice Generation is derived from Investment Advisory.
- Advice Generation depends on the Investment Advisory Platform capability.
- Advice Generation depends on the Generative AI Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Financial Advisory service domain produces recommendations for a client. Under the ESMA MiFID II suitability guidelines, every personal recommendation must be matched to the assessed client profile and accompanied by a suitability statement explaining why it fits. Robo-advice and generative-AI assistants automate this, but the guidelines hold the firm to the same suitability standard regardless of channel.
