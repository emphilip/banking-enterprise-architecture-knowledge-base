# Escheatment Processing

**Definition.** Escheatment Processing manages reporting and remittance of unclaimed balances to authorities, supporting the dormant-account obligation behaviour within the BIAN Account Management service domains.
**Also known as:** Unclaimed Property Remittance, Escheat Handling.

## Relationships
- Escheatment Processing is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Escheatment Processing is derived from Dormancy Management.
- Escheatment Processing depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
BIAN Account Management dormant-account obligation handling remits unclaimed funds; under state unclaimed-property law (modeled on the RUUPA) the holder must perform due-diligence notice to the owner before filing the annual report and escheating the balance to the state.
