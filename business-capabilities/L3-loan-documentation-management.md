# Loan Documentation Management

**Definition.** Loan Documentation Management is the capability that prepares, executes and stores credit agreements and supporting documents, mapping to the BIAN Consumer Loan and Corporate Loan service domains.
**Also known as:** Credit Agreement Management, Loan Doc Preparation.

## Relationships
- Loan Documentation Management is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Documentation Management is derived from Loan Origination.
- Loan Documentation Management depends on the Document Management capability.
- Loan Documentation Management depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Consumer Loan and Corporate Loan service domains rely on the executed loan agreement as the master record of obligations; Loan Documentation Management produces, e-signs and retains it. A concrete banking specific: in the US, mortgage closing packages bundle the promissory note and security instrument, and disclosures such as the Closing Disclosure must be retained for record-keeping periods set under TRID and Regulation Z.
