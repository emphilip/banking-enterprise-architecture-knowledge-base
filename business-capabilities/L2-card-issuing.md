# Card Issuing

**Definition.** Card Issuing is the business capability that produces and provisions physical and virtual payment cards to customers, including card personalization, tokenization, and activation. It maps to the BIAN Card Capture and Card Provisioning service domains.
**Also known as:** Card Issuance Capability.

## Relationships
- Card Issuing is defined in the Cards domain.
- Card Issuing is derived from Cards Management.
- Card Issuing depends on the Card Processing capability.

## Details
Card Issuing covers BIN allocation, embossing and fulfillment vendor coordination, EMV chip and contactless provisioning, and digital wallet tokenization for Apple Pay and Google Pay. Instant-issuance and virtual-first models are increasingly delivered through API-based processors such as Marqeta and Galileo, replacing batch-oriented legacy issuance on platforms like TSYS TS2.
