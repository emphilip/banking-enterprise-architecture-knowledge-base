# Complaint Resolution

**Definition.** Complaint Resolution is the L4 business capability that investigates, adjudicates and remediates a logged complaint including redress and regulatory reporting, supporting the BIAN Customer Case Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Complaint Investigation, Redress Management.

## Relationships
- Complaint Resolution is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Complaint Resolution is derived from Complaint Management.
- Complaint Resolution depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Complaint Resolution extends its parent Complaint Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Complaint Resolution relies on the Workflow Orchestration technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Complaint Resolution at scale.
