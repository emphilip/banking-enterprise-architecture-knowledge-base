# Application Decisioning

**Definition.** Application Decisioning is the L4 business capability that evaluates a completed application against eligibility, policy and risk rules to approve, refer or decline, supporting the BIAN Customer Offer and Sales Product Agreement service domains.
**Also known as:** Application Adjudication, Eligibility Decisioning.

## Relationships
- Application Decisioning is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Application Decisioning is derived from Application Processing.
- Application Decisioning depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Application Decisioning extends its parent Application Processing within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Application Decisioning relies on the Workflow Orchestration technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Application Decisioning at scale.
