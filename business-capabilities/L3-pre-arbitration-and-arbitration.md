# Pre-Arbitration & Arbitration

**Definition.** Pre-Arbitration & Arbitration escalates unresolved disputes to network pre-arbitration and binding arbitration, supporting the BIAN Card Network Participant Facility and Card Case service domains.
**Also known as:** Arbitration, Pre-Arbitration Handling, Dispute Escalation.

## Relationships
- Pre-Arbitration & Arbitration is defined in the Cards domain.
- Pre-Arbitration & Arbitration is derived from Dispute Management.
- Pre-Arbitration & Arbitration depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
When a second presentment does not resolve a dispute, the issuer escalates through scheme pre-arbitration (Visa) or pre-arbitration/arbitration case filing (Mastercard Mastercom), and ultimately binding arbitration where the network rules on liability and levies arbitration fees and fines against the losing party. This capability supports the BIAN Card Case and Card Network Participant Facility domains, tracking strict scheme deadlines (e.g. Mastercard 45-day pre-arbitration response) and the financial exposure of an adverse ruling.
