# Loan Modification

**Definition.** Loan Modification is the capability that restructures the terms of an existing loan including rate, term and repayment changes, mapping to the BIAN Consumer Loan and Credit Facility service domains.
**Also known as:** Loan Restructuring, Variation Management.

## Relationships
- Loan Modification is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Loan Modification is derived from Loan Servicing.
- Loan Modification depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.
- Loan Modification depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
The BIAN Consumer Loan and Credit Facility service domains support amendment of an existing agreement's terms, which Loan Modification orchestrates. A concrete banking specific: a permanent loan modification may capitalise arrears, extend the term or reduce the rate, and is distinct from short-term forbearance; modified terms must be re-disclosed and the new amortisation schedule rebuilt in the core.
