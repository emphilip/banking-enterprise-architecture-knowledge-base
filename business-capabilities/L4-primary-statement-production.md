# Primary Statement Production

**Definition.** Primary Statement Production assembles the statement of financial position, profit or loss and other comprehensive income, changes in equity and cash flows under IAS 1 (and the IFRS 18 presentation reforms from 2027).
**Also known as:** Financial Statement Production, Primary Statements, IAS 1 Statements.

## Relationships
- Primary Statement Production is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Primary Statement Production is derived from Statutory Reporting.
- Primary Statement Production depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.

## Details
Primary Statement Production builds the four IAS 1 primary statements plus comparatives from the consolidated ledger, applying the required line-item structure, current/non-current classification and the cash-flow statement (IAS 7). It enforces internal consistency (e.g. equity roll-forward ties to OCI and retained earnings, cash-flow closing cash ties to the balance sheet). Under IFRS 18, effective 1 January 2027, the P&L is reorganised into operating, investing and financing categories with new subtotals, which this capability will produce.
