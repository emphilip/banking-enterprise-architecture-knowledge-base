# Fraud Risk Assessment

**Definition.** Fraud Risk Assessment evaluates fraud exposure across products, channels and customer segments and sets controls and thresholds, realised through the BIAN Fraud Model service domain.
**Also known as:** Fraud Risk Evaluation, Fraud Exposure Assessment.

## Relationships
- Fraud Risk Assessment is defined in the Risk Management domain.
- Fraud Risk Assessment is derived from Fraud Management.
- Fraud Risk Assessment depends on the Fraud Analytics capability.
- Fraud Risk Assessment depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
Fraud Risk Assessment profiles inherent fraud exposure by typology — application/first-party fraud, account takeover, authorised push payment (APP) scams, card-not-present, and synthetic identity — across each product and channel, and rates the effectiveness of preventive and detective controls. As external fraud is a defined Basel event type under BCBS d292, assessment outputs feed the operational-risk loss and scenario framework, and calibrate the rule thresholds and model cut-offs used by Fraud Monitoring against false-positive and loss-rate appetite.
