# Currency Translation

**Definition.** Currency Translation converts foreign-subsidiary financials into the group presentation currency and records translation reserves under IAS 21 The Effects of Changes in Foreign Exchange Rates.
**Also known as:** FX Translation, Foreign Currency Translation, CTA Management.

## Relationships
- Currency Translation is defined in the Finance & Treasury domain.
- Currency Translation is derived from Financial Consolidation.
- Currency Translation depends on the Financial Consolidation Platform capability.

## Details
Currency Translation applies the IAS 21 method for foreign operations: assets and liabilities are translated at the closing rate, income and expenses at transaction-date (or average) rates, and the resulting exchange differences are recognised in other comprehensive income as a cumulative translation adjustment (CTA) within equity, reclassified to P&L on disposal of the operation. The capability maintains the rate sets, computes the CTA per entity and currency, and ties the translated trial balances into the consolidation.
