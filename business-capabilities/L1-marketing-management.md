# Marketing Management

**Definition.** Marketing Management is the L1 business capability for planning, executing, and measuring marketing activity to attract and retain customers, spanning brand, campaigns, segmentation, and offer management in line with the BIAN Marketing reference business area.
**Also known as:** Marketing.

## Relationships
- Marketing Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Marketing Management depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Marketing Management depends on the CRM Platform capability.
- Marketing Management is related to Customer Management.

## Details
Marketing Management covers BIAN service domains including Marketing Campaign, Customer Campaign Management, and Brand Management. It decomposes into Campaign Management and Customer Segmentation. Typical scope includes market analysis, propositions and offers, multi-channel campaign execution, and marketing performance measurement. In APQC PCF terms it maps to "Develop and Manage Marketing Plans" and related process groups, and it leans on analytics for targeting and attribution.
