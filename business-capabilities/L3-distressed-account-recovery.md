# Distressed Account Recovery

**Definition.** Distressed Account Recovery handles negotiation, restructuring and write-down booking for non-performing deposit and overdraft accounts handed over after standard servicing, realising the BIAN Account Recovery service domain.
**Also known as:** Account Recovery Capability, Distressed Deposit Handling.

## Relationships
- Distressed Account Recovery is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Distressed Account Recovery is derived from Account Servicing.
- Distressed Account Recovery depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Account Recovery service domain manages handover of impaired accounts; unrecovered overdrawn deposit balances are charged off, and any resulting collection activity on the consumer is governed by the Fair Debt Collection Practices Act (15 USC 1692).
