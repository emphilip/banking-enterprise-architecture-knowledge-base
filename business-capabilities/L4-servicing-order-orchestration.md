# Servicing Order Orchestration

**Definition.** Servicing Order Orchestration is the L4 business capability that decomposes a multi-product servicing request into ordered steps, fees and fulfilment tasks and tracks completion, realising the BIAN Servicing Order service domain.
**Also known as:** Order Orchestration, Servicing Workflow.

## Relationships
- Servicing Order Orchestration is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Servicing Order Orchestration is derived from Service Fulfilment.
- Servicing Order Orchestration depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Servicing Order Orchestration extends its parent Service Fulfilment within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Servicing Order Orchestration relies on the Workflow Orchestration technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Servicing Order Orchestration at scale.
