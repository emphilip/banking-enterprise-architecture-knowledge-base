# Outbound Engagement

**Definition.** Outbound Engagement is the Channels & Engagement capability that performs proactive outbound calling and messaging for servicing, collections support and campaigns, supporting the BIAN Contact Center Operations service domain in an outbound mode.
**Also known as:** Outbound Calling, Outbound Contact.

## Relationships
- Outbound Engagement is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Outbound Engagement is derived from Contact Center.
- Outbound Engagement depends on the Contact Center Platform capability.

## Details
Outbound Engagement runs proactive contact in the outbound side of BIAN Contact Center Operations: fraud verification calls, collections reminders, servicing follow-ups and campaign outreach. It is heavily regulated by consumer-contact law, the US TCPA (consent and prior-express-consent for autodialed calls), FDCPA limits on collections contact frequency and timing, and Do-Not-Call list scrubbing, so the dialer enforces consent flags and call windows. Channel and contact-time preferences from Consent & Preference Management gate every attempt, and right-party-contact and conversion rates drive list strategy fed by Campaign Execution.
