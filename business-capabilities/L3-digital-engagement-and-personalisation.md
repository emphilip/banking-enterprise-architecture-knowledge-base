# Digital Engagement & Personalisation

**Definition.** Digital Engagement & Personalisation is the Channels & Engagement capability that tailors content, offers and journeys to the individual within digital channels, consuming Next-Best-Action insight and aligning to the BIAN Channel Activity History service domain; it is an omnichannel CX practice extension beyond BIAN's core.
**Also known as:** Digital Personalisation, In-Channel Engagement.

## Relationships
- Digital Engagement & Personalisation is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Digital Engagement & Personalisation is derived from Digital Banking.
- Digital Engagement & Personalisation depends on the Analytics Platform capability.
- Digital Engagement & Personalisation depends on the Digital Channel Platform capability.

## Details
This capability reads BIAN Channel Activity History to assemble a unified behavioural view, then renders contextual content, nudges and offers driven by Next-Best-Action models. Real-time decisioning (Adobe Target, Salesforce Interaction Studio, Glassbox-style analytics) selects the offer at render time and writes the response back to the activity log to close the learning loop. Personalisation must honour Consent & Preference Management and GDPR/ePrivacy constraints on profiling, and any cross-channel targeting carries omnichannel context so an offer shown in-app is suppressed if accepted at the branch.
