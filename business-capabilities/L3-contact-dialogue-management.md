# Contact Dialogue Management

**Definition.** Contact Dialogue Management is the L3 business capability that conducts, routes and records inbound and outbound servicing conversations across channels, realising the BIAN Contact Dialogue and Contact Routing service domains.
**Also known as:** Contact Management, Interaction Management, Dialogue Handling.

## Relationships
- Contact Dialogue Management is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Contact Dialogue Management is derived from Customer Servicing.
- Contact Dialogue Management depends on the Conversational AI capability.

## Details
Contact Dialogue Management extends its parent Customer Servicing within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Contact Dialogue Management relies on the Conversational AI technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Contact Dialogue Management at scale.
