# Vault Float Control

**Definition.** Vault Float Control is the Channels & Engagement capability that maintains required vault float levels and orchestrates cash movements to and from central holding facilities, realising the BIAN Branch Currency Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Float Management, Currency Float Control.

## Relationships
- Vault Float Control is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Vault Float Control is derived from Cash & Vault Management.
- Vault Float Control depends on the Workflow Orchestration capability.

## Details
Vault Float Control realises BIAN Branch Currency Management by holding the vault to a target float by denomination, triggering cash orders and returns to the central bank or cash-in-transit provider when limits are breached. It enforces dual control and time-locks on vault access, runs buy/sell reconciliation with tellers, and conducts surprise counts as anti-internal-fraud controls. The optimisation minimises insured idle cash against denomination stock-out risk, and large cash flows feed Bank Secrecy Act monitoring. Workflow orchestration sequences approvals, CIT bookings and ledger postings so the physical and book positions stay reconciled.
