# Direct Debit Management

**Definition.** Direct Debit Management is the L2 business capability for handling mandate-based recurring collections, including mandate setup, collection scheduling, and exception and return processing under schemes such as SEPA Direct Debit and Bacs.
**Also known as:** Direct Debits.

## Relationships
- Direct Debit Management is defined in the Payments domain.
- Direct Debit Management is derived from Payments.
- Direct Debit Management depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.
- Direct Debit Management is related to Payment Execution.

## Details
Direct Debit Management aligns to BIAN Direct Debit and Payment Execution service domains. Typical sub-functions include mandate registration and amendment, collection file generation, indemnity and dispute handling, and return and reject processing. It supports recurring billing relationships and must comply with scheme rules such as SEPA Core/B2B and Bacs. Payment orchestration platforms process the underlying collection instructions.
