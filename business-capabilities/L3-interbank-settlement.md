# Interbank Settlement

**Definition.** Interbank Settlement discharges net or gross interbank obligations across central-bank accounts (e.g. RTGS, FedNow RTGS), realising the settlement functions in the BIAN Payment Clearing and Settlement area.
**Also known as:** Settlement Operations, RTGS Settlement.

## Relationships
- Interbank Settlement is defined in the Payments domain.
- Interbank Settlement is derived from Payment Clearing & Settlement.
- Interbank Settlement depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Interbank Settlement grounds in the settlement functions of the BIAN Payment Clearing and Settlement area. It discharges net or gross interbank obligations across central-bank accounts, including RTGS and FedNow settlement. It finalises the transfer of value between participant institutions.
