# Domestic Payments

**Definition.** Domestic Payments is the L3 business capability for executing in-country transfers over local rails such as ACH, Faster Payments, and domestic wire schemes, within a single currency and jurisdiction.
**Also known as:** ACH, Faster Payments.

## Relationships
- Domestic Payments is defined in the Payments domain.
- Domestic Payments is derived from Payment Execution.
- Domestic Payments depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.
- Domestic Payments is related to Real-Time Payments.

## Details
Domestic Payments aligns to BIAN ACH Operations and Payment Execution service domains. Typical sub-functions include batch and single credit transfers, debit transfers, scheme-specific validation, and return handling. It covers local schemes such as US ACH, UK Faster Payments and Bacs, and SEPA in-country flows. Payment orchestration platforms route and transform these instructions to the appropriate domestic rail.
