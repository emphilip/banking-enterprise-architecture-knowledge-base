# Cash & Vault Management

**Definition.** Cash & Vault Management is the Channels & Engagement capability that maintains branch and vault currency floats and orchestrates cash movements, realising the BIAN Branch Currency Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Branch Cash Management, Vault Management.

## Relationships
- Cash & Vault Management is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Cash & Vault Management is derived from Branch Banking.
- Cash & Vault Management depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
Cash & Vault Management realises BIAN Branch Currency Management: holding currency to target float levels, balancing teller drawers against the vault, and ordering or returning cash to the central bank or a cash-in-transit provider. Dual-control vault access, sealed-bag reconciliation and surprise cash counts are mandated controls; large cash movements feed Bank Secrecy Act reporting. Forecasting balances the carrying cost of idle cash against denomination stock-out risk. Smart safes and cash recyclers provide provisional credit on deposit and feed real-time vault positions back to the core for reconciliation.
