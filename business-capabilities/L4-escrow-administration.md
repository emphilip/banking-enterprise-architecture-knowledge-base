# Escrow Administration

**Definition.** Escrow Administration is the capability that manages impound accounts for taxes and insurance on secured loans, mapping to the BIAN Mortgage Loan service domain.
**Also known as:** Impound Account Management, Tax & Insurance Escrow.

## Relationships
- Escrow Administration is defined in the Lending & Credit domain.
- Escrow Administration is derived from Loan Account Maintenance.
- Escrow Administration depends on the Core Banking Processing capability.

## Details
The BIAN Mortgage Loan service domain holds the escrow/impound balances that Escrow Administration collects and disburses. A concrete banking specific: US mortgage servicers must run an annual escrow account analysis under RESPA (Regulation X), maintain only a permitted cushion, and pay property taxes and hazard-insurance premiums from the account when due.
