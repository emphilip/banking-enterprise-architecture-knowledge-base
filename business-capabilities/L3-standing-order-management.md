# Standing Order Management

**Definition.** Standing Order Management sets up and maintains recurring customer-initiated credit transfers on fixed schedules, distinct from creditor-pull direct debits, within the BIAN Payment Order service domain.
**Also known as:** Recurring Payments, Standing Instructions.

## Relationships
- Standing Order Management is defined in the Payments domain.
- Standing Order Management is derived from Payment Initiation.
- Standing Order Management depends on the Payment Orchestration capability.

## Details
Standing Order Management grounds in the BIAN Payment Order service domain. It establishes and maintains fixed-schedule, customer-initiated credit transfers, distinct from creditor-pull direct debits. Typical functions cover schedule setup, amendment, suspension and cancellation, with orchestration generating the underlying payments on each due date.
