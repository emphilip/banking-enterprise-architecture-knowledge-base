# Campaign Execution

**Definition.** Campaign Execution is the L3 business capability that operationalises marketing campaigns by selecting audiences, scheduling and delivering treatments across channels and capturing responses, realising the BIAN Customer Campaign Management service domain.
**Also known as:** Campaign Operations, Campaign Delivery, Marketing Execution.

## Relationships
- Campaign Execution is defined in the Customer Management domain.
- Campaign Execution is derived from Campaign Management.
- Campaign Execution depends on the CRM Platform capability.

## Details
Campaign Execution extends its parent Campaign Management within the Customer Management domain and is grounded in BIAN Service Domain mappings and APQC PCF alignment. Campaign Execution relies on the CRM Platform technology capability for its core processing and integrates with adjacent Customer Management capabilities. Implementations typically apply cloud-native, API-led and AI-assisted patterns to deliver Campaign Execution at scale.
