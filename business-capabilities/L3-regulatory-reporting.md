# Regulatory Reporting

**Definition.** Regulatory Reporting is the business capability that prepares, validates, and submits mandatory returns to regulators and supervisory authorities. It aligns with the BIAN Regulatory Reporting service domain.
**Also known as:** Reg Reporting.

## Relationships
- Regulatory Reporting is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Regulatory Reporting is derived from Regulatory Compliance.
- Regulatory Reporting depends on the Regulatory Reporting Engine capability.

## Details
Regulatory Reporting produces prudential, statistical, and transaction reports such as COREP, FINREP, FR Y-9C, and MiFIR submissions with full data lineage and reconciliation to source. Modern RegTech engines such as Regnology and Suade supersede legacy reporting tools, supporting granular and templated reporting and emerging machine-readable regulation.
