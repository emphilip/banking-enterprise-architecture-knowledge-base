# Online Banking

**Definition.** Online Banking is the business capability that provides browser-based banking services for retail and business customers over the web. It corresponds to the BIAN Digital Channel service domain.
**Also known as:** Internet Banking.

## Relationships
- Online Banking is defined in the Channels & Engagement domain.
- Online Banking is derived from Digital Banking.
- Online Banking depends on the Customer Identity capability.

## Details
Online Banking offers account aggregation, bill pay, transfers, secure messaging, and document access through responsive web experiences. It relies on customer identity and access management for secure authentication and session control, with progressive web apps and accessibility standards extending reach across business and corporate banking portals.
