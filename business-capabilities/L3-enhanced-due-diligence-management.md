# Enhanced Due Diligence Management

**Definition.** Enhanced Due Diligence Management applies heightened scrutiny, source-of-wealth and source-of-funds checks and senior-management approval to higher-risk relationships per FATF Recommendation 10/19 and UK Money Laundering Regulations 2017 regulation 33.
**Also known as:** EDD Management, High-Risk Diligence.

## Relationships
- Enhanced Due Diligence Management is defined in the Compliance & Financial Crime domain.
- Enhanced Due Diligence Management is derived from KYC Management.
- Enhanced Due Diligence Management depends on the Document Management capability.
- Enhanced Due Diligence Management depends on the KYC Onboarding Platform capability.

## Details
UK MLR 2017 regulation 33 mandates EDD for high-risk third countries, PEPs, complex or unusually large transactions, and any case presenting higher ML/TF risk. EDD measures include obtaining additional identity and beneficial-ownership information, establishing source of funds and source of wealth, securing senior-management approval to establish or continue the relationship, and conducting enhanced ongoing monitoring. FATF R.19 requires countermeasures for higher-risk jurisdictions identified by the FATF.
