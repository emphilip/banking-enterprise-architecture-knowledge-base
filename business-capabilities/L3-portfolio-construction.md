# Portfolio Construction

**Definition.** Portfolio Construction builds investment portfolios from an investment policy statement and target asset allocation, selecting instruments and sizing positions; it aligns with the BIAN Investment Portfolio Planning service domain.
**Also known as:** Portfolio Build, Portfolio Modelling.

## Relationships
- Portfolio Construction is defined in the Wealth & Investments domain.
- Portfolio Construction is derived from Portfolio Management.
- Portfolio Construction depends on the Portfolio Management System capability.
- Portfolio Construction depends on the Analytics Platform capability.

## Details
The BIAN Investment Portfolio Planning service domain governs the planning and design of a portfolio against a client mandate. Concretely, Portfolio Construction translates the IPS mandate limits and risk-tolerance constraints into instrument-level holdings, respecting constraints such as concentration caps, minimum lot sizes, and the client's risk capacity, before positions are sent to Order Management for execution.
