# Unauthorised Overdraft Handling

**Definition.** Unauthorised Overdraft Handling manages excess and unarranged overdraft positions, fees and customer treatment, supporting the exception behaviour of the BIAN Overdraft Facility service domain.
**Also known as:** Unarranged Overdraft Handling, Excess Management.

## Relationships
- Unauthorised Overdraft Handling is defined in the Deposits & Accounts domain.
- Unauthorised Overdraft Handling is derived from Overdraft Management.
- Unauthorised Overdraft Handling depends on the Notification Services capability.

## Details
The BIAN Overdraft Facility exception behaviour handles unarranged excess balances; non-sufficient-funds and overdraft fees and the order in which items are paid are scrutinised under Regulation DD and CFPB UDAAP guidance on representment and high-to-low posting.
